Exam Syllabus 2026

Subject-wise syllabus for all major government competitive exams in India. Click any exam to view the full topic breakdown.

UPSC

UPSC Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS) Syllabus

Prelims — GS Paper I

  • Indian History & Culture
  • Indian & World Geography
  • Indian Polity & Governance
  • Economic & Social Development
  • Environment & Ecology
  • General Science & Current Affairs

Prelims — CSAT Paper II

  • Comprehension
  • Logical Reasoning & Analytical Ability
  • Decision Making
  • General Mental Ability
  • Basic Numeracy (Class X)
  • Data Interpretation

Mains — GS Papers I–IV

  • GS I: History, Society, Geography
  • GS II: Governance, Constitution, IR
  • GS III: Economy, Environment, S&T, Security
  • GS IV: Ethics, Integrity, Aptitude
  • Essay (2 papers)
  • Optional Subject (2 papers)

SSC

SSC CGL / CHSL / MTS Syllabus

Tier I (All Posts)

  • General Intelligence & Reasoning
  • General Awareness
  • Quantitative Aptitude
  • English Comprehension

Tier II — CGL

  • Mathematical Abilities
  • Reasoning & General Intelligence
  • English Language & Comprehension
  • General Awareness
  • Computer Knowledge

CHSL / MTS

  • CHSL Tier I: Same as CGL Tier I
  • CHSL Tier II: Skill / Typing Test
  • MTS: General Intelligence, Reasoning
  • MTS: Numerical Aptitude
  • MTS: English, General Awareness

NDA / CDS / CAPF Syllabus

  • Mathematics (Paper I)

  • Paper II: General Studies, Essay & Comprehension
  • Physical & Medical Standards
