Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released an official notification for UPSSSC Lekhpal Recruitment 2025 Eligibility Result, Exam Date 2026 for 7994 Post.

The last date to apply is 28/01/2026.

Candidates must have Server I | Server II as minimum educational qualification.
