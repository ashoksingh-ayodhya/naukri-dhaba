Union Bank of India has released the Union Bank Recruitment Project 2026–27 (Officers with Domain Expertise, General Banking Officers & Specialist Officers) notification for a total of 395 vacancies spanning General Banking Officers and Specialist Officers, from Manager (MMGS-II) up to Deputy General Manager (TEGS-VI). Online registration opens on 21 July 2026 and closes on 10 August 2026. This article covers important dates, vacancy break-up, eligibility, age limit, salary, application fee, selection process, and the step-by-step application procedure, based strictly on the official notification issued by the Bank's Human Resources Vertical, Central Office, Mumbai, dated 20.07.2026.

A total of 395 posts are available for this recruitment.

Candidates must have 10.08.2026 as minimum educational qualification.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the official website www.unionbankofindia.co.in and click on the 'Recruitment' page.
  2. On the Career Overview page, click 'Click to View the Current Recruitment' to open the link "UNION BANK RECRUITMENT PROJECT 2026-27 (OFFICERS WITH DOMAIN EXPERTISE, GENERAL BANKING OFFICERS & SPECIALIST OFFICERS)".
  3. Click on "CLICK HERE TO APPLY ONLINE" to open the online application form.
  4. Choose "Click here for New Registration" and enter your Name, contact details, and Email ID. Note down the system-generated Provisional Registration Number and Password.
  5. Use the "SAVE AND NEXT" option to fill the application in stages if needed, and verify all details carefully - no changes are allowed after clicking "COMPLETE REGISTRATION".
  6. Upload photograph, signature, and left thumb impression as per the prescribed specifications.
  7. Fill in the remaining application details, preview the form, and click "COMPLETE REGISTRATION".
  8. Click on the "Payment" tab and pay the applicable application fee online.
  9. Click 'Submit' and save/print the system-generated application form and e-receipt for future reference.
  10. Application can be made for only one post; multiple appearances for a single post at interview will lead to cancellation of candidature.
  11. No change of category will be permitted at any stage after registration of the online application.
  12. Existing Union Bank of India employees may apply through proper channel (HR Department of Regional/Zonal Office) and, if selected, must resign and rejoin the Bank's service afresh.
  13. Selected candidates will be on probation for 2 years of active service.
  14. Selected candidates for posts G01–G19 must execute a Service Indemnity Bond to serve the Bank for a minimum of 3 years, or pay ₹2,00,000 plus applicable taxes if they leave before completing 3 years.
  15. Selected candidates for posts S01–S108 must execute a Service Indemnity Bond to serve the Bank for a minimum of 3 years, or pay ₹2,50,000 plus applicable taxes if they leave before completing 3 years.
