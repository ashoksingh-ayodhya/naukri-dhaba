Candidates looking for the IBPS PO/MT-XVI Notification PDF Download link can access the official detailed notification directly from the IBPS website before applying. The detailed notification titled "Detailed-Notification_CRP-PO-XVI_Final_V1_30_06_2026" was released on 01 July 2026 and signed by the Director, IBPS, Mumbai. It contains complete information on eligibility, exam pattern, vacancy distribution, and application procedure, and candidates are strongly advised to read it thoroughly before registering online since the eligibility criteria specified are basic requirements only, and final eligibility is subject to verification by the participating banks.

A total of **6715** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduation in any discipline as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website www.ibps.in and click on "CRP PO/MT" on the homepage.
2. Click "CLICK HERE TO APPLY ONLINE FOR CRP-PROBATIONARY OFFICERS/MANAGEMENT TRAINEES (CRP-PO/MT-XVI)".
3. Click "CLICK HERE FOR NEW REGISTRATION" and enter basic details to generate a provisional registration number and password.
4. Upload photograph, signature, left thumb impression, hand-written declaration, and SSC/SSLC/10th standard certificate as per the specified format and size.
5. Fill in all details carefully using the "SAVE AND NEXT" facility before final submission, since no changes are allowed after clicking "COMPLETE REGISTRATION".
6. Pay the applicable application fee/intimation charges online.
7. Take a printout of the e-receipt and the completed online application form for future reference.
8. Uploading a signature or handwritten declaration in capital letters, which is not accepted under any circumstances.
9. Entering the 'Name', 'Email ID', 'Mobile Number', or 'Nationality' fields incorrectly, since these cannot be edited after submission.
10. Waiting until the last date to pay fees, which risks failure due to heavy website traffic near the deadline.
11. Uploading blurred or smudged photographs, signatures, or thumb impressions that don't meet the prescribed file size and dimension limits.
12. Selecting the wrong category and later expecting it to be changed after the Edit Window closes.
13. Forgetting to retain multiple copies of the uploaded photograph, which is required at several later stages of the process.
14. Not indicating scribe requirements or compensatory time preferences at the time of registration, since such requests are not entertained later.
15. Apply Online: Click here
