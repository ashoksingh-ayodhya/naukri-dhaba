Bank of India has released the Bank of India Credit Officer Recruitment 2026 notification (Project No. 2026-27/01) for 779 Credit Officer (GBO Stream) vacancies across MMGS-II, MMGS-III, and SMGS-IV grades. Graduates and professionals with relevant banking/credit experience can apply online from 30 June 2026 to 20 July 2026. Selected candidates will receive pay scales ranging from ₹64,820 to ₹1,20,940 depending on the grade.

A total of **779** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 01 June 2026 as minimum educational qualification.
