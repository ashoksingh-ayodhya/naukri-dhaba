Haryana Staff Selection Commission HSSC 10+2 Common Eligibility Test CET 2026 Apply for Online Form has released an official notification for Haryana HSSC Group D Common Eligibility Test CET Online Form 2026 Date Extended. 
The last date to apply is **05/07/2026 Extended**.

## How to Apply

1. Haryana Staff Selection Commission has Released the Common Eligibility Test CET Online Form 2026 Notification. Candidate Can Apply Between 19 June 2026 to 05 July 2026.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in HSSC CET Exam 2026.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
