Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has published the Combined Graduate Level Examination Notification 2026 on 21 May 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill approximately 12,256 Group B and Group C posts across various Ministries and Departments. Candidates within the age range as specified in the official notification and holding a Graduation Degree are eligible to apply online at https://ssc.gov.in. The Online Application window has reopened from 23rd June 2026 (11:00 PM) till 25th June 2026 (11:00 PM), and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

A total of **12256** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Bachelor's Degree from a recognized University or equivalent for most posts, as on 01-08-2026. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Generate One-Time Registration (OTR) at https://ssc.gov.in (mandatory; use Aadhaar-based authentication if possible)
2. Complete OTR profile and lock it
3. Fill the Common Application Form (CAF) including live photograph capture via camera
4. Upload scanned signature (JPEG/JPG, 10–20 KB, 6.0 cm × 2.0 cm)
5. Fill the Examination Specific Module (post preferences, exam centre choices - 3 centres within same region in order of priority)
6. Pay application fee of Rs. 100/- online (if applicable) by 23-06-2026
7. Preview/Print the application to verify all details before final submission
8. Use the correction window (29-06 to 01-07-2026) if any correction is needed (chargeable)
9. Keep printout of application and all documents for at least 3 years
10. Matriculation/Secondary School Certificate (proof of date of birth)
11. Mark sheets for all years/semesters of Graduation + Provisional/Degree Certificate
12. SC/ST Certificate in prescribed format (Annexure of the notification)
13. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format (Annexure-X); must be valid as on closing date of application
14. EWS Income & Asset Certificate valid for FY 2026–27 (issued based on income for FY 2025–26) in prescribed format (Annexure-XI)
15. PwBD Disability Certificate from competent authority specifying type of disability (as per Annexure-XII to XIII)
