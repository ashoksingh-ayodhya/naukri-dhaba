Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the Combined Graduate Level (CGL) 2024 notification. This is one of the most sought-after central government examinations in India, offering **17,727 posts** across multiple departments and ministries.

The selection process involves Tier I (Computer Based Test), Tier II (Computer Based Test), and Document Verification. Candidates must be graduates from a recognized university to be eligible.
