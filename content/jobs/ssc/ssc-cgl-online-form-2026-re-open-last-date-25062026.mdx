1. OTR Instruction : SSC has started one-time registration on its new website SSC.GOV.IN. Candidates who have not yet done OTR registration on the new website will be able to apply for any recruitment only after completing the registration.
2. Staff Selection Commission SSC Are Issued Combined Graduate Level CGL Exam 2026 Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the SSC Graduate Level Recruitment 2026 Candidate Can Apply Online for 21/05/2026 to 25/06/2026.
3. Photo Instruction : SSC has made a big change in the application process. Now the photo of the candidate will be uploaded live, that too through webcam or through the official SSC App. The candidate has to take care that he looks straight ahead in the photo and the background should also be light/white.
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Staff Selection Commission SSC CGL Career Latest Various Graduate Level Recruitment 2026 . Application Form in Naukri Dhaba Recruitment Latest Job Section.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Hand Writing, Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID, Thumb, Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
9. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
