The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the tentative vacancy list for the Combined Hindi Translators Examination (CHT) 2026 on 09.07.2026, announcing 303 total posts across Senior Translator and Junior Translator/Junior Hindi Translator categories. These vacancies are spread across ministries including Defence, Finance, Home Affairs, Railways, and the Department of Posts, with the Comptroller & Auditor General of India (90 posts) and Department of Posts (76 posts) accounting for the largest share. Candidates should note that these figures are tentative and subject to revision when SSC releases the full recruitment notification with exam dates and eligibility criteria.
