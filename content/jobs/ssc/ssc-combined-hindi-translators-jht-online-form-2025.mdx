Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released an official notification for SSC Combined Hindi Translators JHT Recruitment 2025 Apply Online. 
A total of **437** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **26/06/2025 upto 11 PM**.

## How to Apply

1. SSC One Time Registration : SSC has started one-time registration on its new website SSC.GOV.IN. Candidates who have not yet done OTR registration on the new website will be able to apply for any recruitment only after completing the registration.
2. Staff Selection Commission Are Released Junior Hindi Translator, Junior Translator and Senior Hindi Translator Examination,2025. Candidate Can Apply Between 05 June 2025 to 26 June 2025.
3. SSC Live Photo : SSC has made a big change in the application process. Now the photo of the candidate will be uploaded live, that too through webcam or through the official MySSC App. The candidate has to take care that he looks straight ahead in the photo and the background should also be light/white.
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in SSC Apply Online for Junior / Senior Translator Recruitment 2025.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
