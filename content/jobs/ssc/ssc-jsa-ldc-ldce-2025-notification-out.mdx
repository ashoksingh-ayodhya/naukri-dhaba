The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the notification for the Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA) / Lower Division Clerk (LDC) Grade Limited Departmental Competitive Examination (LDCE), 2025 vide Notice No. 4/4/2026-CONF-5, to fill 149 vacancies across 12 Central Government departments and cadres. The online application window is open from 05th June 2026 to 26th June 2026 (up to 11:00 PM). Please note this is a limited departmental examination - only existing regular Group 'C' / Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) employees of the participating cadres are eligible, and every application must be forwarded through proper channel by the Head of Department/Office. The Computer Based Examination is tentatively scheduled for July–August 2026 and will be held in Delhi-NCR only. Check the complete eligibility, vacancy break-up, exam pattern, and step-by-step process below.

Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA) / Lower Division Clerk (LDC) Grade Limited Departmental Competitive Examination (LDCE), 2025 has released an official notification for SSC JSA / LDC LDCE 2025 Notification Out - Apply Online for 149 Group C Posts in 12 Departments @ ssc.gov.in. 
A total of **149** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have In-service Group 'C' / MTS of participating cadres + 12th pass + 3 years' service as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Generate OTR on the new website. Visit ssc.gov.in and click "Register Now." Any OTR created on the old site (ssc.nic.in) will not work on the new portal.
2. Opt for Aadhaar-Based Authentication during OTR (recommended). Candidates who skip Aadhaar must upload the prescribed proof documents.
3. Log in with your Registration Number and password, then open the JSA/LDC LDCE 2025 link under the "Live Exam" tab and click Apply.
4. Fill the application - service details, cadre, crucial date, length of service, and age-relaxation code (if any). Information auto-filled from OTR is non-editable; use "Modify Registration" if a correction is needed.
5. Capture your live photograph and upload your signature as per the specifications (see below).
6. Upload the required documents (listed below) and Submit. Take a printout of the submitted application.
7. Forward the hard copy through proper channel. Your Head of Department / Head of Office must forward the printed application along with service documents to: The Regional Director, Staff Selection Commission (Northern Region), Block No. 12, CGO Complex, Lodhi Road, New Delhi – 110003, so as to reach by 03-07-2026 (6:00 PM) - or 10-07-2026 for candidates abroad/A&N/Lakshadweep.
8. Certified true copy of the first page of your Service Book (attested by HoD/HoO)
9. Certified particulars of service (posts held, scale of pay, nature of appointment)
10. Class 12th certificate / educational qualification proof (if applicable)
11. Caste/Category certificate (SC/ST), if claiming reservation
12. Disability certificate (Annexure-VII / VIII) for PwBD candidates
13. A recent passport-size photo for the live capture reference, and a scanned signature
14. Photograph: Captured live via webcam/phone, in a well-lit area with a plain background. No cap, mask, or spectacles. Face must fill the on-screen red rectangle. Pre-existing/scanned photos are rejected.
15. Signature: JPEG/JPG, 10–20 KB, dimensions about 6.0 cm × 2.0 cm. Sign so the signature fills at least 80% of the box. Blurred or miniature signatures are rejected.
