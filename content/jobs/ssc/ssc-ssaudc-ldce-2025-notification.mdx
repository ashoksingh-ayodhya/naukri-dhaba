Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has published the Notice for Senior Secretariat Assistant / Upper Division Clerk Grade Limited Departmental Competitive Examination, 2025 on 05 June 2026 vide No. 4/1/2026-CONF-5. The examination will be held tentatively in July-August 2026 in Delhi NCR only for making additions to the Select List of Senior Secretariat Assistant/Upper Division Clerk Grade [Pay Level-4, Group 'C' Non-Gazetted (Rs. 25,500 - Rs. 81,100)] across 10 participating Services/Cadres. This is a Limited Departmental Competitive Examination open only to eligible serving government employees (JSA/LDC) of the specified participating cadres. The application process is active from 05 June 2026 to 26 June 2026, and candidates can access the notification PDF and apply link below.

Download SSC SSA/UDC Limited Departmental Competitive Examination 2025 Official Notification PDF has released an official notification for SSC SSA/UDC LDCE 2025 Notification Out - Apply Online for 227 Group C Posts. 
A total of **227** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Certified true copy of the first page of the Service Book duly attested by Head of Department/Head of Office (showing Name, Father's Name, Husband's Name, Nationality, SC/ST Name if applicable, Date of Birth)
2. Certified true copy of particulars of service attested by Head of Department/Head of Office (posts held, scale of pay, nature of appointment - substantive/officiating/permanent/temporary)
3. Copy of certificate of essential educational qualification, if applicable
4. Form of certificate for disabled Defence Personnel while in service, wherever applicable
5. Certificate of disability in case of PwBD candidates
6. Live photograph captured through the application module (plain background, no cap/mask/glasses, full frontal view)
7. Scanned signature in JPEG format (10 to 20 KB; image dimension about 6.0 cm width x 2.0 cm height)
8. This is a Limited Departmental Competitive Examination - only eligible serving JSA/LDC employees of the specified 10 participating cadres can apply.
9. Only one application form should be submitted; submission of multiple applications may lead to rejection of candidature.
10. After completion of online application, the printed copy must be forwarded by the Head of Department/Office through proper channel to reach SSC (NR) by 03.07.2026 (1800 hrs); applications not forwarded through proper channel will not be considered.
11. A candidate who resigns, quits service, or whose services are terminated after applying will not be eligible for appointment on the result of this examination.
12. No change in entries made in the online application form shall be allowed at any stage under any circumstances.
13. The examination shall be conducted in Delhi NCR only; no centre will be at any Indian mission abroad. Candidates serving abroad will have to appear in India at their own expense.
14. Admission Certificate for CBE will be issued online by uploading on the website of the Northern Regional Office; it will not be issued by post. Candidates are advised to regularly visit https://sscnr.nic.in.
15. Candidates must carry their Departmental Identity Card (Original) along with hard copy of Admission Certificate for appearing in the examination.
