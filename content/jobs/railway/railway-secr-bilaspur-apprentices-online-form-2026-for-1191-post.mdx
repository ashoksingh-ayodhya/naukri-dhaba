## How to Apply

1. South East Central Railway SECR Bilaspur has released Various Trade Apprentices 2026. Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the SECR Apprentice Examination 2026. Candidate Can Apply Online for 12/05/2026 to 11/06/2026.
2. Visit the official website of SECR Apprentice Bilaspur (https://secr.indianrailways.gov.in/?lang=1)
3. Click on “SECR Apprentice 2026 Apply Online” Link
4. Register using a valid email ID and mobile number
5. Login using registration ID and password
6. Fill the application form with personal, educational, and category details
7. Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, etc.)
8. Submit the form and download/print the confirmation page
9. Candidate Read the SECR Bilaspur Apprentice Notification 2026 Before Apply the SECR Bilaspur Apprentice Application Form 2026 in Rojgar Result Latest Job Section.
