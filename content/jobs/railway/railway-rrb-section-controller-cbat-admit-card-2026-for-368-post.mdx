Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released an official notification for Railway RRB Section Controller Eligibility 2025. 
A total of **368** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **14/10/2025**.
Candidates must have Bachelor Degree in Any Stream from Any Recognized University in India. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Railway Recruitment Board RRB Online Released Section Controller Recruitment Advt No. CEN 04/2025 Exam. Candidate Can Apply Between 15/09/2025 to 14/10/2025.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Railway Board Section Controller Exam 2025 Advt No. CEN 04/2025 Exam Online Form 2025.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Railway Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form. Railway RRB Section Controller Online Form 2025 for 368 Post
