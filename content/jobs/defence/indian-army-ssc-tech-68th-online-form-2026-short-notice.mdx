The Indian Army has released the official notification for the 68th Short Service Commission (Technical) Entry for Men and Women, offering a prestigious opportunity for engineering graduates to join the Indian Army as Commissioned Officers. The online application process will be conducted through the official Join Indian Army portal, and selection will be based on shortlisting + SSB interview + medical examination. This entry is one of the most sought-after defence recruitment drives for engineering aspirants.

Candidates must have B.E / B.Tech (Engineering Graduates) as minimum educational qualification.
