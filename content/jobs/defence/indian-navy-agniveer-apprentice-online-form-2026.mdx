The latest Indian Navy Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Agniveer (Apprentice) posts! Published through the Directorate of Manpower Planning & Recruitment, Naval Headquarters, New Delhi on the official website joinindiannavy.gov.in, this new recruitment drive offers vacancies to be decided based on service requirements for eligible candidates. Unmarried Indian male candidates possessing an AICTE-recognised Diploma in the relevant Engineering or Electrical discipline can apply online from 05 June 2026 to 29 June 2026. Find all details about the Indian Navy Agniveer (Apprentice) new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Candidates must have Passed Matriculation Examination with minimum 50% marks in aggregate from Boards of School Education recognised by Ministry of Education, Govt. of India AND completed a 3-year Diploma course from an AICTE-recognised Polytechnic Institute with minimum 50% marks in aggregate in any of the qualifying diploma trades (Engineering or Electrical as applicable). as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit https://joinindiannavy.gov.in → Click on "Register" → Register as AVR (Apprentice).
2. After successful registration, click on "Login" → AVR (Apprentice) → INET 2/26 → Proceed to apply.
3. Fill in all details carefully. Incorrect declaration of information identified at any stage will result in cancellation of candidature.
4. Upload a recent passport-size colour photograph (taken not before May 2026), size 10 KB to 50 KB; front portrait in light background without head gear (except Sikhs). Date of photograph must be clearly written at the bottom.
5. A live photograph will be captured through the webcam during application form filling.
6. Pay the examination fee of ₹550/- + 18% GST online via net banking / Credit Card / Debit Card / UPI.
7. Give state preference for allocation of INET exam centre. Choice of centre cannot be changed once selected or allotted by the Indian Navy.
8. Candidates will be provided a post-closing correction window. An additional correction window will be activated post INET exam for 2 days.
9. Matriculation Certificate and Marksheet (proof of date of birth and educational qualification)
10. Diploma Certificate and Consolidated Marksheet from AICTE-recognised Polytechnic Institute
11. Domicile Certificate
12. NCC 'C' Certificate (if applicable) - to be verified through DG NCC
13. Sponsorship Certificate (if applicable) - to be verified through CABS
14. Recent passport-size colour photograph (taken not before May 2026)
15. All original certificates as submitted/uploaded during online application
