## How to Apply

1. Union Public Service Commission UPSC has released Civil Service IAS Mains 2026 Notification on the official website. Those candidates who Are Interested to the UPSC Civil Service Recruitment 2026 Can Apply Online Form from 19/06/2026 to 29/06/2026.
2. Candidate Read the UPSC Civil Services IAS Notification 2026 before Apply the Recruitment UPSC Civil Services IFS 2026 Application Form
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
