1. Live Photo Required : Candidate Live Photo Required.
2. To apply for Combined Medical Services Examination CMS 2026 in Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), it will be necessary to do One Time Registration. Applications will not be accepted without OTR.
3. The application window of UPSC Combined Medical Services Examination CMS 2026 can be applied online from 11 March 2026 to 31 March 2026.
4. The number of examination centers / cities remains limited in UPSC Combined Medical Services Examination CMS 2026 recruitment, so if the candidate wants the examination city of his choice, then applies as soon as possible. After applying, the exam city which will be seen in red color means that that city is now full.
5. After completing the application in UPSC Combined Medical Services Examination CMS 2026 Recruitment, take the final print out.
