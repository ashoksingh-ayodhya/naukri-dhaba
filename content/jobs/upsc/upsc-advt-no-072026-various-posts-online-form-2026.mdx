The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released Advertisement No. 07/2026 on 25 June 2026, inviting online recruitment applications for 54 posts spread across multiple central government ministries and departments. Eligible candidates can apply online from 25 June 2026 up to 17 July 2026 at 6:00 PM through UPSC's official portal at upsconline.nic.in.
