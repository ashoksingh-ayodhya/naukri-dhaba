The latest Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for various Group A and Group B posts across multiple Central Government Ministries and Departments! Published as Advertisement No. 05/2026 at the official UPSC website, this new recruitment drive offers 194 vacancies across 32 posts in departments including DGCA, PESO, Department of Posts, IMD, Archaeological Survey of India, Ministry of Defence, Ministry of MSME, and more. Eligible Indian citizens with qualifications ranging from Bachelor's degree in Engineering to Master's degree/Ph.D. can apply online from 23-05-2026 to 12-06-2026 (up to 1800 Hrs). Find all details about UPSC Advertisement No. 05/2026 new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

A total of **194** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Candidates claiming equivalent qualifications must produce order/letter indicating the Authority (with number and date) under which it has been treated as equivalent; otherwise the application will be rejected. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Live photograph captured via webcam/camera during CAF filling (mandatory)
2. Scanned recent passport-size photograph (as per instructions on UPSC website)
3. Scanned signature (three signatures one below the other on plain white paper, black ink)
4. Matriculation/10th Standard certificate or marksheet as proof of Date of Birth
5. Degree/Diploma certificate as proof of educational qualification; if not available, provisional certificate + all year marksheets
6. Experience Certificate(s) in prescribed format from Head(s) of Organisation(s) - mentioning duration, basic pay, consolidated pay, and nature of duties
7. Caste/Community certificate (SC/ST/OBC) in prescribed proforma from Competent Authority
8. OBC Non-Creamy Layer declaration in prescribed format
9. EWS certificate in prescribed proforma
10. PwBD Disability Certificate from Medical Board constituted by Central/State Government
11. Age relaxation certificate for Ex-Servicemen/Central/UT Government Employees in prescribed proforma
12. Order/letter for equivalent qualification (if applicable) citing Authority with number and date
13. Name change documents (if applicable - marriage certificate/divorce decree/affidavit as per situation)
14. Documentary support for any other claim made (Professional Registration, Publications, GATE/NET, etc.)
15. Online applications open from 23-05-2026 and close on 12-06-2026 at 1800 Hrs - candidates must apply well in advance without waiting for the closing date.
