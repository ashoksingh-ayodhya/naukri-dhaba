Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for UPSC Indian Economic Service IES and Indian Statistical Service ISS Recruitment 2026 Apply Online for 44 Post. 
A total of **44** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **03/03/2026 upto 06:00 Pm Only**.

## How to Apply

1. Photo Instruction : Live Photo Required
2. To apply for IES / ISS Exam 2026 in Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), it will be necessary to do One Time Registration. Applications will not be accepted without OTR.
3. The application window of UPSC Indian Economic Service IES | Indian Statistical Service ISS Exam 2026 can be applied online from 11 February 2026 to 03 March 2026.
4. The number of examination centers / cities remains limited in UPSC Indian Economic Service IES | Indian Statistical Service ISS Exam 2026recruitment, so if the candidate wants the examination city of his choice, then applies as soon as possible. After applying, the exam city which will be seen in red color means that that city is now full.
5. After completing the application in UPSC Indian Economic Service IES | Indian Statistical Service ISS Exam 2026 Recruitment, take the final print out.
6. for More Details Must Read the Notification.
