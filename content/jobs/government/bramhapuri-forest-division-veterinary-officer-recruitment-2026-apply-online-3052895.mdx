Bramhapuri Forest Division (BFD) has published the Veterinary Officer Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 1 Veterinary Officer position. Candidates within the age range of up to 62 years and holding a Graduate degree in Veterinary Science & Animal Husbandry with a minimum of 50% marks from a recognized University along with registration with the Maharashtra State Veterinary Council are eligible to apply through the prescribed application process. The application process will remain active until 20-06-2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and application details below.

The last date to apply is **20-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Graduate in Veterinary Science & Animal Husbandry with minimum 50% marks from recognized University, Registration with Maharashtra State Veterinary Council as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 20-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
