Allahabad High Court, Prayagraj & Lucknow Bench Are Recently Uploaded Final Result for the Post of Law Clerk Trainee 2021. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Vacancies and Selected for Final Result.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Allahabad High Court Law Clerk Trainee Recruitment 2021 Final Result. 
A total of **94** posts are available for this recruitment.

1. There are 2 ways to fill the application for Allahabad High Court Law Clerk Recruitment 2021. First: You can get its form from Prayagraj of Allahabad High Court or Lucknow Bench for Rs. 300/- can be purchased for Rs. Or you can download the form online and send it with a demand draft.
2. All educational qualification certificates, marksheets and citations along with the application form include the application form along with the duly attested copy of Extra Curricular Activities with computer knowledge. Two self-addressed envelopes (size 5”x10”) each bearing postage stamps of Rs. 40/ -.
