National Institute of Technology Calicut (NIT Calicut) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 2 Project Associate-1 Posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply by sending their application via Email. The last date to send the application email is 10-06-2026.

The last date to apply is **10-06-2026**.
Candidates must have B.E/B.Tech in Electrical Engineering /Electrical & Electronics Engineering / Electronics & Instrumentation /Instrumentation & Control or Equivalent stream. Desirable: M.E./M.Tech in Electrical Engineering, Valid Gate Score. as minimum educational qualification.

6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 10-06-2026.
