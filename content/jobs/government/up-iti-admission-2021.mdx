Uttar Pradesh Industrial Training ITI Courses Admission SCVTUP Are Recently Uploaded Fourth Round Allotment Results for the Admission for Regular Govt. and Private ITI College 2021. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with This Admission Can Check the Allotment Results.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh Scvtup UP ITI Admission 2021 Second, Fourth Round Allotment Result. 
The last date to apply is **28/08/2021**.
Candidates must have Minimum Age : 14 Years; Candidate Born After : 31/07/2007 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. State Council of Vocation Training SCVT ITI Admission 2021. Candidate Can Apply Online Between 04/08/2021 to 23/08/2021
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the ITI Admission Application Form in UP ITI Various Govt. and Private ITI Institute in Uttar Pradesh Session August 2021-2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Are Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Pay and Complete Your Form
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
8. Candidate Can Check This Page on Regularly Bases for Merit List, Re Choice Filling and More Details.
