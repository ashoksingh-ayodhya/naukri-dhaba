APTET June 2026 notification has been officially released by the Department of School Education, Government of Andhra Pradesh on 5 June 2026. The Andhra Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test (APTET) June 2026 online application window is open from 5 June to 5 July 2026 at the official portal cse.ap.gov.in.


## How to Apply

1. 20% weightage is given to APTET score in the Teacher Recruitment Test (TRT).
2. 80% weightage is given to the TRT written test for final selection list.
3. Qualifying APTET alone does not confer a right to recruitment; it is one of the eligibility criteria.
4. The APTET certificate is valid for lifetime (NCTE guidelines, 09.06.2021; G.O.Ms.No.69, 25.10.2021).
5. Visit the official portal cse.ap.gov.in
6. Download and read the Information Bulletin carefully before filling the form.
7. Click "APTET-JUNE-2026 Online Application" → "New Register".
8. Enter basic details: Name, DOB, Aadhaar number, email ID, mobile number, category, address.
9. Upload latest photograph (3.5×3.5 cm on white paper with signature below) as JPEG ≤50 KB.
10. Complete registration to receive a Journal Number (fee confirmation only, not application completion).
11. Log in with Journal Number, fill educational qualifications, and select paper(s).
12. Preview all details → click SUBMIT (or EDIT to correct before submitting).
13. Pay ₹1,000/- per paper via Payment Gateway.
14. Take a printout of the submitted application for future reference. Do not post it to the APTET office.
15. UP Police SI DV & PST Admit Card 2026 - Download Hall Ticket @ uppbpb.gov.in
