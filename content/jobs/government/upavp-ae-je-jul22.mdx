Uttar Pradesh Sewayojan / Rojgar Sangam Portal has released the advertisement for the recruitment of Assistant Engineer and Junior Engineer in Uttar Pradesh Awas Vikas Parishad (UPAVP) through outsourcing. Any candidate who is interested in this outsourcing recruitment and fulfills the eligibility can apply online till 04 August 2022.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for UP Avas Vikas Parishad UPAVP Assistant Engineer AE, Junior Engineer JE Civil / Electrical (Out Sourcing) Online Form 2022. 
A total of **182** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **04/08/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. This recruitment of Assistant Engineer and Junior Engineer in Uttar Pradesh Avas Vikas Parishad will be done through outsourcing. All the candidates who are interested in this recruitment can apply through the Uttar Pradesh Sewayojan / Rozgar Sangam portal, the last date of which is 04 August 2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Out Sourcing Recruitment Application Form in UPAVP AE/JE Latest Outsourcing Recruitment Online Form 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
