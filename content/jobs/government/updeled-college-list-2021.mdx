Uttar Pradesh DELED 2021 College List is available in all the districts Varanasi, Prayagraj, Lucknow, Jaunpur, Fatehpur, Kaushambi, Pratapgarh, Agra, Aligarh, Mathura, SRN Bhadohi, Ghaziabad, Noida, Mahoba, Chitrakoot, banda, Ballia, Mau, Meerut, Bulandhshahar, Amethi, Raibareilly, Barabanki, Ayodhya, Firozabad, Etah, Etawah, Pilibhit and All Other District in UP, the candidates who are going to participate in the counseling can see the college information of their district in this list and can also download in the pdf file.

Exam Regulatory Authority, Allahabad (Prayagraj) UP has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh UPDELED / BTC Govt and Private College List (District Wise) 2021.
