HLL Lifecare Limited (HLL) has published the Recruitment Notification 2026 on 03 June 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 146 positions including Senior Dialysis Technician, Dialysis Technician, Staff Nurse, Central Project Coordinator, QA Coordinator-Dialysis, Lab Technician, Junior Lab Technician, Account Assistant, and IT Officer posts under its Healthcare Services Division (HCS) in Maharashtra on Fixed Term Contract basis. Candidates with relevant qualifications such as GNM, BSc Nursing, Diploma, BSc, MSc, B.Com, BE/B.Tech, MCA are eligible to attend the Walk-in Selection. The Walk-in Selection process will be held from 07 June 2026 to 14 June 2026 at various venues across Maharashtra.

A total of **146** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Proof of age
2. Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets
3. Experience certificates
4. Latest salary certificate with break-up
5. Aadhaar and PAN Card
6. Recent passport-size photograph
7. SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) candidates shall produce Community Certificate issued by the competent Revenue Authority
8. Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply.
9. The appointment will be on a Fixed Term Contract basis.
10. The prescribed application format may be downloaded from www.lifecarehll.com/careers.
11. Age relaxation will be applicable for SC/ST/OBC (Non-Creamy Layer)/PwD candidates as per Government of India rules.
12. Candidates are required to bring all original certificates along with self-attested copies for verification.
13. Candidates who fail to produce the required original documents will not be permitted to participate in the selection process.
14. The place of posting may vary depending on organizational requirements; Management reserves the right to decide the final place of posting.
15. Only candidates meeting the prescribed qualification and experience criteria will be permitted to attend the selection process.
