Armoured Vehicles Nigam Limited (AVNL), a Defence Public Sector Undertaking under the Ministry of Defence, has released Recruitment Notice AVNL/CO/HR/2026/06 inviting online applications for 1213 posts on Fixed Term Employment. This includes 1005 Non-Executive Junior Technician posts and 208 Executive posts ranging from Junior Manager to Additional General Manager and Consultant. Online applications are open from 11 July 2026 (10:00 AM) to 25 July 2026 (11:55 PM), through the AVNL careers portal.

A total of **1213** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the AVNL website and click the "Careers" tab at avnl.co.in
2. Go to Career - "Register"
3. Fill in Post name, Post Code (auto-selected), Name, Mobile Number, and Email ID
4. Generate OTP, enter Captcha, and submit
5. Enter the OTP received
6. Accept Terms & Conditions via the "I Agree" checkbox, then click re-verify
7. Select the checkboxes confirming entered values are correct, enter Captcha for re-verification, and submit registration details
8. Receive the Registration Number, Login ID, and Password on the registered Email/Mobile
9. Click "Go to Application" or log in again with the Login ID and Password
10. Fill in the complete application form
11. Upload photograph, signature, and required documents (DOB proof, Proof of Educational Qualification, Category Certificate, Apprenticeship Training Certificate, PwBD/Ex-Servicemen proof, if applicable)
12. Preview the application form before submission
13. Edit details if required (Name, Post Applied, Email ID, and Mobile Number cannot be changed once submitted)
14. Submit the application by clicking "I Agree" and entering the Captcha
15. Choose the payment mode and proceed to the Payment Gateway
