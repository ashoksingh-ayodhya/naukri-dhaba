Krishak Bharati Cooperative Limited (KRIBHCO) Apprentices Notification 2026 has been released on 19 May 2026 on the official website kribhco.net.

Candidates must have Diploma in Chemical Engineering, Diploma in Mechanical Engineering, Diploma in Instrumentation Engineering, Diploma in Electrical Engineering, Diploma in Civil Engineering, Diploma in Computer Engineering, B.Sc. (Chemistry) as minimum educational qualification.
