State Bank of India has released the official notification for SBI PO Recruitment 2026, inviting online applications for 1500 Probationary Officer (PO) vacancies under Advertisement No. CRPD/PO/2026-27/09. Online registration is open from 18 June 2026 to 8 July 2026. Graduates in any discipline aged 21 to 30 years (as on 01 April 2026) can apply through SBI's official careers portal, with selection through Prelims, Mains, and an Interview process.

A total of **1500** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduation in any discipline (as on 30.09.2026) as minimum educational qualification.
