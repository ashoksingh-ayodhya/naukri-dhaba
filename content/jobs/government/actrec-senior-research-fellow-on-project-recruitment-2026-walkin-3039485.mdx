Advanced Centre for Treatment, Research and Education in Cancer (ACTREC) has released an official notification for Walk-in Interview for 0 Senior Research Fellow (On Project) Posts. Eligible candidates can attend the Walk-in Interview on at the ACTREC office with original documents and self-attested copies.

Candidates must have M.Sc. degree in Life Sciences / Molecular Medicine / Biochemistry / Bioinformatics/ Biotechnology or equivalent subject with > 60% aggregate marks. NET-LS/GATE qualified preferred. Minimum Two years' experience in handling human samples, mammalian cell culture, molecular biology experiments, analysis of data generated by WGS/RNA seq technologies and immunohistochemistry. as minimum educational qualification.
