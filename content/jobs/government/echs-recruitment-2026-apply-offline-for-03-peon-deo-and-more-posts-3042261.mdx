Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 3 Peon, DEO and More Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for healthcare jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can download the application form from the official website and submit it offline.

Candidates must have Minimum 8th pass with relevant trade experience for Armed Forces personnel. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.echs.gov.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 04-04-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
