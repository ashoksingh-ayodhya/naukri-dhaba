Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released a notification bearing No. HQ-QB012/17/2025-QB inviting applications from eligible and experienced Language Experts for empanelment in 13 Regional Languages. The empanelled experts will be required to work at the premises of SSC Headquarters, CGO Complex, New Delhi, for assigned work on a requirement basis.

Candidates must have The applicant should be working or retired as a Faculty Member, Lecturer, or High School Teacher in the concerned regional language from any recognised School, College, or University. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Prepare a detailed resume/CV clearly indicating teaching specialization, experience in the concerned regional language, and knowledge of English and Hindi.
2. Attach all relevant supporting documents along with the resume/CV.
3. Send the complete application to [email protected] latest by 17 June 2026.
4. Educational qualification certificates (relevant to the concerned regional language)
5. Proof of current or past employment as Faculty Member, Lecturer, or High School Teacher in the concerned language (appointment letter / service certificate / retirement order)
6. Experience certificate(s) from recognised School, College, or University
7. Any other relevant supporting documents establishing expertise in the concerned regional language and knowledge of English and Hindi
8. Empanelment of Language Experts is purely temporary and on a requirement basis. Mere submission of an application or inclusion in the panel shall not confer any right for assignment of work or regular engagement by the Commission.
9. The empanelled experts will be required to come to the premises of SSC Headquarters, CGO Complex, New Delhi for the assigned work.
10. Empanelled experts shall be required to follow the instructions and timelines prescribed by the Commission from time to time.
11. Any form of misconduct, breach of confidentiality, or furnishing of false information may lead to removal from the panel without prior notice.
12. The Commission may verify the educational qualifications, experience, and other credentials submitted by applicants at any stage.
13. The Commission reserves the right to accept or reject any application without assigning any reason thereof.
14. The Commission reserves the right to modify, cancel, or withdraw the empanelment process at any stage without prior notice.
15. Remuneration shall be paid as per the rates approved by the Commission.
