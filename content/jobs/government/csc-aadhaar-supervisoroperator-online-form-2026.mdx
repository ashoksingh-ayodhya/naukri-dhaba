CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd (CSC) has published the Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill multiple Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator positions across various districts in Telangana, Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Uttar Pradesh, and Odisha. Candidates aged 18 years and above who possess 12th Pass/Intermediate, 10th Pass with 2 years ITI, or 10th Pass with 3 years Polytechnic Diploma along with a valid UIDAI Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor Certificate are eligible to apply online through the official CSC portal. The application process is active and will remain open until 20 June 2026. Candidates can easily access the notification details and apply online through the links provided below.


## How to Apply

1. Interested and eligible candidates should check their eligibility including age, qualification and Aadhaar certification before applying.
2. Visit the CSC ASK information page for details and updates: CSC Aadhaar Seva Kendra ASK.
3. Follow the application instructions issued by CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd for the respective state/district.
4. Keep all required documents and Aadhaar certification details ready while applying.
5. The last date for submission of application is 20 June 2026.
