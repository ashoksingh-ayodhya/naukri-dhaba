All India Institute of Medical Sciences Jammu has released the AIIMS Jammu Recruitment 2026 notification for 78 Senior Resident / Demonstrator posts across multiple medical and non-medical departments including Anaesthesiology, General Medicine, Surgery, Radiology, Trauma & Emergency Medicine and more. This recruitment offers an excellent opportunity for postgraduate medical professionals (MD/MS/DNB/DM/M.Ch) and non-medical candidates with M.Sc./Ph.D. qualifications to secure prestigious central government medical jobs in a premier healthcare institute.

The last date to apply is **15-04-2026**.
Candidates must have Postgraduate medical degree/ M.Sc./M.Biotech and Ph.D. as minimum educational qualification.

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 15-04-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
