National Institute for Research in Digital Health (ICMR NIRDH) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 1 Consultant (Epidemiology) Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website.

The last date to apply is **12-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Bachelor's degree in medical sciences (MBBS/BDS/BAMS/BHMS) or similar bachelor's degree with post-graduation in Public Health/Community Medicine/Epidemiology/Health Care Management/Health Administration/Clinical Research/Population Sciences/any other similar post-graduation as minimum educational qualification.
