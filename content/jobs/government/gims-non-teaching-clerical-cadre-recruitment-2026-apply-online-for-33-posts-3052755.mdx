The Government Institute of Medical Sciences (GIMS), Greater Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar has released an official advertisement for Non-Teaching Posts (Clerical Cadre) - Record Keeper and Store Keeper positions. This recruitment is through a Computer Based Test (CBT) Examination - 2026 via direct recruitment (Seedhi Bharti). The online application window opens on June 4, 2026 and the deadline to submit applications is July 3, 2026.

Department has released an official notification for GIMS Non-Teaching Clerical Cadre Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 33 Record Keeper, Store Keeper Posts. 
A total of **33** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official institute website at www.gims.ac.in from June 4, 2026 onwards.
2. Click on the Online Application link for Advertisement No. GIMS/2026/Manpower/NT-02/07 and select the desired post (Record Keeper or Store Keeper).
3. Read the User Instructions carefully before filling the application form.
4. Fill in all required details - personal information, educational qualifications, category, and other eligibility details accurately based on your original certificates.
5. Pay the applicable examination fee online using available payment modes. The application printout can only be generated after fee payment.
6. After final submission, candidates may make only one correction in the application. However, registered mobile number, email ID, and reservation category cannot be changed after submission.
7. Download and keep a printed/hard copy of the application. No documents need to be sent by post.
8. Candidates in regular government service must submit a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their employer at the time of document verification.
9. Download the Hall Ticket from www.gims.ac.in as per the exam schedule. Hall Tickets will not be sent by post.
10. For queries, contact: Helpdesk No. 9986640811 or Email: [email protected] (9:00 AM - 6:00 PM on all working days).
