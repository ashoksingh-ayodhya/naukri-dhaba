The C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026 has been announced for multiple technical and support posts on a contractual basis! Published on 29-05-2026, C-DAC Centre in North East (CINE), Guwahati, Assam is inviting eligible candidates to appear for a Walk-in Interview for 21 posts across 8 designations. Candidates with relevant engineering/graduate qualifications and experience can walk in between 16th to 18th June 2026. Find all details about C-DAC CINE Walk-in Recruitment 2026, including eligibility, vacancy details, salary, and how to apply here.

A total of **21** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Download C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026 Official Notification PDF
2. C-DAC CINE Recruitment 2026 Application Form
3. Stage 1 – Initial Screening: Based on academic credentials, experience profile, and application details submitted.
4. Stage 2 – Walk-in Interview: Candidates who clear screening will be interviewed at the venue on the respective scheduled date. Reporting time: 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM.
5. Stage 3 – Document Verification: All information submitted in the application will be verified with original documents at this stage or any stage of the recruitment process.
6. C-DAC may restrict the number of candidates called for interview based on qualifications and experience higher than the minimum prescribed. Mere fulfillment of eligibility does not guarantee being called for interview.
7. No TA/DA will be paid for attending the interview.
8. Duly filled application form affixed with latest passport-size photograph
9. Attested copies of proof of age (SSLC/SSC/ISC mark sheet or any valid document)
10. Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (with percentage proof if CGPA system is followed)
11. Experience certificates from previous and present employers clearly indicating period of employment, salary drawn, and post held
12. Caste certificate in prescribed Government of India format (for SC/ST/OBC candidates)
13. EWS certificate in the format prescribed by DoPT (for EWS candidates)
14. Disability certificate in prescribed format (for PwBD candidates)
15. No Objection Certificate (NOC) from current employer (for candidates not forwarding application through proper channel)
