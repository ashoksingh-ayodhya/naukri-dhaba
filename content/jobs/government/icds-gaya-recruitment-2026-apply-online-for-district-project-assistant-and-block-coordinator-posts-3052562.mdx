Integrated Child Development Services Gaya (ICDS Gaya) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 3 District Project Assistant and Block Coordinator Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can apply by sending their resume and documents to the official email address.

Candidates must have Graduate Degree / Post Graduate Diploma in Management Social Sciences/Nutrition, Graduate as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 16-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
