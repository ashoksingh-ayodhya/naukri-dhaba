National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (CSIR NEERI) Recruitment 2026: National Environmental Engineering Research Institute has released the official notification for 1 Senior Project Associate posts, inviting applications from eligible candidates in Maharashtra. Candidates must have Ph.D. Environmental Engineering / Chemical Engg. / Environmental Science/ Chemistry / Environmental Chemistry from a recognized University AND 2 years’ experience in Research and Development in Industrial and Academic Institutions or Science and Technology Organization and Scientific activities and services as minimum educational qualification.
