National Institute of Technology Meghalaya (NIT Meghalaya) (NIT Meghalaya) Junior Research Fellow (JRF) Notification 2026 has been released on the official website of NIT Meghalaya. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced through the NIT Meghalaya Junior Research Fellow (JRF) Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have M.Tech./M.E. in Microelectronics/VLSI/Electronics or related disciplines OR M.Sc. in related fields with GATE/NET OR B.Tech./B.E. in Electronics and Communication Engineering with GATE as minimum educational qualification.
