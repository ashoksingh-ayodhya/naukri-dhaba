Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Senior Scientific Officer posts! Published on 02-06-2026 at the official website of RPSC, this new recruitment drive offers 03 openings for eligible candidates across Toxicology, Physics, and Ballistics Divisions of the State Forensic Science Laboratory, Jaipur. Applicants within the 21 to 40 years age group and possessing a relevant M.Sc. / M.Tech. / M.E. with minimum Second Division qualification can apply online from 11-06-2026 to 10-07-2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

Department has released an official notification for RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online. 
A total of **03** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Recent photograph (as per RPSC guidelines - to be captured live via webcam/mobile at the time of online application)
2. Left thumb impression scanned and uploaded (mandatory; also required at examination centre)
3. Aadhaar Card / Janadhaar Card for identity verification (mandatory before applying)
4. Admit Card / Call Letter (downloaded from RPSC website) along with valid photo identity proof in original
5. Caste Certificate in prescribed Government format (for SC/ST candidates) - issued by competent authority
6. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed format issued before the last date of application (for OBC candidates)
7. Income and Assets Certificate (for EWS candidates) - issued in prescribed format by competent authority
8. Disability Certificate in prescribed format (for PwBD candidates) as per RPwD Act, 2016
9. Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (10th onwards, including M.Sc./M.Tech./M.E. and Computer Science qualification)
10. Experience Certificate in prescribed proforma (as per Annexure available in notification) - certifying at least 2 years of FSL/relevant experience after attainment of educational qualification
11. Last month's salary slip / proof of current employment (if applicable)
12. Character / Antecedent verification certificate from competent authority
13. Medical fitness certificate from competent authority at the time of appointment
14. Candidates must complete One Time Registration (OTR) on the SSO Portal before applying; no application can be submitted without a valid OTR.
15. The detailed advertisement and Instructions for Applicants on the RPSC website (https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in) are part of this notification - candidates must read them before applying.
