IDBI Bank Ltd. has released Advertisement No. 03/2026-27 inviting online applications for the recruitment of Specialist Officers across the Deputy General Manager, Assistant General Manager, and Manager grades. A total of 31 vacancies have been notified across Audit (Information System), Infrastructure Management, Legal, and Security functional areas. Online registration begins on July 14, 2026, and closes on July 26, 2026.

A total of **31** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Marksheets and Passing Certificates for Class 10, Class 12, Graduation, and PG/Masters (all semester/year-wise or consolidated), along with Degree/Provisional Degree Certificates as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website: idbi.bank.in/idbi-bank-careers-current-openings.aspx
2. Locate the link for Specialist Officer Recruitment 2026-27 (Advt. No. 03/2026-27)
3. Register using a valid personal email ID and mobile number
4. Fill in personal, educational, and experience details carefully
5. Upload scanned photograph (4.5cm x 3.5cm, 20-50 KB) and signature (140 x 60 pixels, 10-20 KB)
6. Upload Curriculum Vitae in PDF format, A4 size, not exceeding 500 KB
7. Pay the application fee/intimation charges online as applicable to your category
8. Click Final Submit and save the system-generated e-receipt and application printout
9. Candidates may apply for only one notified position; multiple applications will be rejected without refund.
10. Training, Internship, Apprenticeship, Teaching, Graduate Trainee, or Retainership experience will not be considered against any advertised role.
11. IDBI Bank reserves the right to offer a post one grade lower than applied for, at its discretion.
12. Candidates already employed in Government/PSU/Public Sector Banks must produce a No Objection Certificate at the time of Personal Interview.
13. Only SC/ST candidates are eligible for reimbursement of AC II tier railway fare for attending the selection process, subject to conditions.
14. Any dispute arising from this recruitment is subject to the sole jurisdiction of courts situated in Mumbai.
15. Canvassing in any form will lead to disqualification.
