The District Social Welfare Office, Majuli (DSWO Majuli) has officially announced the Case Worker, MPW and More Posts Recruitment 2026 with 7 openings. The detailed notification has been released under Advertisement No. DSWO(M)/OSC/2025-2026/01. Candidates possessing qualifications such as 10th Pass, HSLC Pass, High School Pass, or a Bachelor's Degree in the relevant discipline are eligible to apply offline. Interested applicants can submit their applications up to 17 June 2026. Direct links to download the notification and application form are provided below.

Candidates must have 10th-pass, HSLC passed, High school passed, Bachelor's degree as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: https://majuli.assam.gov.in/.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 17-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
