Himachal Pradesh Power Transmission Corporation Limited (HPPTCL) has released the official Trainee Driver Notification 2026 on 01-06-2026 at hpptcl.com. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced under the HPPTCL Trainee Driver Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 18 to 45 years with a qualification of 10+2 from a recognized Board of School Education and a valid driving license for plying of heavy/light vehicles in hilly terrain can apply offline by 30-06-2026. The official notification PDF and application form are available below.

The last date to apply is **30-06-2026**.
Candidates must have 10+2 from a recognized Board of School Education, Valid driving license for plying of heavy/light vehicles in hilly terrain as minimum educational qualification.
