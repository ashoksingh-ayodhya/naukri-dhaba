Indian Institute of Technology BHU (IIT BHU) Recruitment 2026: Indian Institute of Technology BHU has released the official notification for Project Technical Support-III posts, inviting applications from eligible candidates. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for candidates seeking research jobs.

The last date to apply is **26-06-2026**.
Candidates must have M.Pharm/M.S. (Pharm)/M.Tech(Pharm)/M.Tech in Medicinal Chemistry/Pharmaceutical Chemistry (First Class) OR Second Class with 1 year research experience. as minimum educational qualification.

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 26-06-2026.
