Those Candidates Are Interested to the Following Teacher Transfer Application Can Read the Full Notification Before Applying Online. has released an official notification for UP Teacher Transfer 2021 Apply Online Form.

## How to Apply

1. Open Apply Link for Transfer Online Form
2. Enter Your Current District
3. Enter Your Area (Rural / Urban)
4. Choose Your School Block
5. Select Your School Name
6. Enter Your Salary Bank Account No.
7. Enter Your Pan Card NO
8. Enter Your Aadhar Card
9. Enter Your Joining Date
10. Choose Preference Transfer School Name
11. For More Information Kindly See the Notification
