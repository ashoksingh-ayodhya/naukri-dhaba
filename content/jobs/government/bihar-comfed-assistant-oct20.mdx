Bihar State Milk Co-operative Federation Ltd. COMFED Sudha Milk has Uploaded the Admit Card / Hall Ticket / Call Letter for the recruitment of Various Assistant Post and other Post. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Vacancies Can Download the Admit Card.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Bihar State Milk COMFED Sudha Milk Various Assistant Post Admit Card 2021. 
A total of **142** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **07/11/2020**.

## How to Apply

1. Bihar State Milk Co-operative Federation Ltd. COMFED Sudha Milk Assistant Notification Are Issued and Various Assistant Post Online Application Form for the Bihar COMFED Recruitment 2020 Candidate Can Apply Online for 07 October, 2020 to 07 November, 2020.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Bihar Latest Recruitment 2020 Application Form in Sarkari Result Latest Job Section.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Hand Writing, Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID, Thumb, Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
