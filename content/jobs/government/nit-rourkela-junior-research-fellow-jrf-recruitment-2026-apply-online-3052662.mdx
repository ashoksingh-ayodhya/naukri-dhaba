The National Institute of Technology Rourkela (NIT Rourkela) has officially announced the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) Recruitment 2026 with 1 opening. The detailed notification has been released under Advt. No. NITR/SR/2026/Advt.-26EE018/L/035 on the official website. Candidates with a B.Tech/BE in Electrical Engineering/Electrical and Electronics Engineering and M.Tech/ME in Power Electronics/Power Electronics and Drives are eligible to apply. Applicants should not exceed 30 years of age as on the closing date, with applicable age relaxations for reserved categories. Eligible candidates can submit their applications via email on or before 21 June 2026. Direct links to download the notification and application details are provided below.

The last date to apply is **21-06-2026**.
Candidates must have B.Tech/BE in Electrical Engineering/ Electrical and Electronics Engineering and MTech/ME in Power Electronics/ Power Electronics and Drives as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 21-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
