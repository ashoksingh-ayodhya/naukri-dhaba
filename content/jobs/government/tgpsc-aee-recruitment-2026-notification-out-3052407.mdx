The latest TGPSC AEE Civil Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) posts! Published on 02-06-2026 at the official website of Telangana Public Service Commission, this new recruitment drive offers 222 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the 18 to 44 years age group and possessing a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering from a recognized University/Institute can apply online from 06-06-2026 to 13-07-2026. Find all details about the TGPSC Assistant Executive Engineer (Civil) new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for TGPSC AEE Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 222 Assistant Executive Engineer Posts. 
A total of **222** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Degrees obtained through Open Universities / Distance Education mode must have recognition from UGC / AICTE / Distance Education Bureau. Proof of recognition rests with the applicant. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Step I: Register for OTR at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in (if not already registered) to obtain TGPSC ID. Verify all bio-data details carefully.
2. Step II: Login using TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, enter OTP received on registered mobile, verify pre-filled details from OTR (Name, DOB, Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability etc.), and select examination centre.
3. Step III: Preview the application for correctness, then click "SAVE & PAYMENT" to proceed to online fee payment via Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
4. Step IV: After fee payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and save the PDF. Note the Reference ID Number for future correspondence.
5. Recent colour passport-size photograph (not older than 3 months before last date) – JPG/PNG, 100kb–200kb
6. Scanned specimen signature – JPG/PNG, 20kb–30kb
7. PDF copies of certificates as required (educational qualifications, community, OTR documents); certificates already uploaded in OTR need not be uploaded again
8. SSC / CBSE / ICSE certificate in support of Date of Birth
9. Printout of online application form (PDF)
10. School Study Certificate (1st to 7th Class) or Certificate of Residence from competent authority for the same period
11. All certificates and mark sheets of educational qualification (10th, Intermediate/12th, B.E./B.Tech Civil Engineering)
12. Community Certificate for SC/ST issued by Revenue Authority not below the rank of Tahsildar, Telangana Government, in the name of the applicant with Father's/Mother's name
13. Community Certificate for BCs (BC-A/B/C/D/E) issued by Revenue Authority not below Tahsildar, Telangana Government, obtained through Mee-seva
14. Non-Creamy Layer Certificate for BCs in Form-VIIB from Revenue Authority not below Tahsildar/RDO, Telangana Government, in the name of the applicant with Father's/Mother's name
15. Income and Asset Certificate for EWS reservation, issued by Tahsildar, Telangana Government, for the financial year prior to the year of application, in the name of the applicant with Parent's/Spouse's name
