Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission </span></a> has started the applications for ADVT.NO.A-4/E-1/2020, COMBINED STATE AGRICULTURE SERVICES (MAIN.) EXAM-2020 whichever candidate had qualified the preliminary examination. They can apply online for main exam till 03 November 2021. Other information related to recruitment such as syllabus of main examination, procedure of selection and other information and link will be available.

Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission </span></a> has started the applications for ADVT.NO.A-4/E-1/2020, COMBINED STATE AGRICULTURE SERVICES (MAIN.) EXAM-2020 whichever candidate had qualified the preliminary examination. They can apply online for main exam till 03 November 2021. Other information related to recruitment such as syllabus of main examination, procedure of selection and other information and link will be available. has released an official notification for UPPSC Agriculture Services Mains Exam 2020 Online Form 2021. 
A total of **564** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **03/11/2021**.

## How to Apply

1. UPPSC Agriculture Services 2020 Mains Exam Online Form Candidate Apply Between 22 October 2021 to 03 November 2021.
2. Candidate Read the Agriculture Services Mains Exam 2020 Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Sarkari Jobs.
3. Procedure to Fill the UPPSC Agriculture Services 2020 Mains Form.
4. Step 1 : Only those candidates who have qualified the preliminary examination can apply for Agriculture Services 2020 Main Examination, the candidate will get the link to apply in the main examination in the some useful important link section of this page,
5. Step 2 : To apply for the uppsc agriculture services 2020 main examination, the candidate has to login first: for which the candidate will have to fill his registration number along with date of birth, gender and category information.
6. Step 3 : After login, the candidate will have to choose the city of the examination, in which the candidate will get 2 options (Prayagraj and Lucknow) to choose any one city in these two cities.
7. Step 4 : After selecting the city of examination, the candidate will have to pay the main examination fee according to his category.
8. Step 5 : After the completion of the application fee process, the candidate will have to download the print out of the main examination, as well as download an address slip and some other files will also remain. The print out of all will have to be taken out by the candidate and submitted to the Public Service Commission Prayagraj on or before 10 November 2021.
9. Candidates Must Read the Full Instruction Before Apply Online Form 2021
