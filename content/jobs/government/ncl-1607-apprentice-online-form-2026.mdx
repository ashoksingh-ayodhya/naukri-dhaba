Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL), A Mini Ratna Company and subsidiary of Coal India Limited, has officially announced the Apprentice Trainee Recruitment 2026-27 with 1607 openings under Ref. No. NCL/HRD/CETI/2026-27/168. The detailed notification was published on 25-05-2026 at nclcil.in. Candidates who are Indian Nationals and have passed their Degree/Diploma/ITI/Paramedical qualification from any AICTE/BTE/UGC/SCVT/NCVT recognized Institute functional and operational in Uttar Pradesh or Madhya Pradesh can submit their online applications from 01-06-2026 to 15-06-2026 through the NATS and NAPS portals. Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

Department has released an official notification for NCL Apprentices Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1607 Graduate, Diploma, Trade Apprentice Posts. 
A total of **1607** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have PwBD candidates must submit a valid disability certificate as per Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules 2017 from the competent authority. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Valid NATS/NAPS Enrolment ID (registration reference).
2. Scanned colour photograph and scanned signature in black ink (as per portal specifications).
3. Date of Birth as per Matriculation certificate.
4. Academic details - total marks scored and maximum marks in qualifying degree/diploma/ITI trade.
5. Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) in prescribed format (Annexure-1), if applicable.
6. PwBD disability certificate as per Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules 2017 (Annexure-2), if applicable.
7. Address for correspondence and permanent address.
8. Online applications are open from 01-06-2026 to 15-06-2026 through NATS/NAPS portals only.
9. A candidate may apply for any one notified position only - multiple applications will lead to disqualification.
10. Information pertaining to Date of Birth, Caste Category, and Qualification once entered in the online application shall be considered final - no alteration will be permitted at later stages.
11. Stipend for Graduate and Diploma Apprentices is inclusive of the Government of India share paid through DBT; Trade Apprentice stipend will be paid directly by NCL.
12. Stipend will be paid directly into the AADHAAR-linked bank account of the Apprenticeship Trainee as per the signed contract.
13. Apprenticeship Training is purely temporary and statutory in nature - it does not confer any right or claim for regular employment in NCL.
14. Apprentices are eligible for 11 days Casual Leave and 15 days Medical Leave per year of training.
15. Engagement hours are between 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
