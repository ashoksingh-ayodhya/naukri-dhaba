Andaman and Nicobar Administration Lecturers and Lab Technicians Notification 2026 has been released on 02-03-2026 on the official website of Andaman and Nicobar Administration. A total of 7 vacancies have been announced through the Andaman and Nicobar Administration Lecturers and Lab Technicians Recruitment 2026.

The last date to apply is **30-06-2026**.
Candidates must have B.E/B.Tech/BS in Civil Engineering, B.E/B.Tech/BS in Computer Science and Engineering, Master’s Degree in Mathematics, Bachelor degree of Engineering, Diploma in respective field, Senior Secondary pass (10+2) with vocational course certificate as minimum educational qualification.
