## How to Apply

1. Uttar Pradesh Employment Office Released UP Rojgar Mela Recruitment 2026 Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the UP Rojgar Mela Examination 2026 . Candidate Can Apply Online Form within Last Date.
2. Visit the official website of UP Rojgar Mela (https://sewayojan.up.nic.in/)
3. Click on “UP Rojgar Mela 2026 Apply Online” Link
4. Register using a valid email ID and mobile number
5. Login using registration ID and password
6. Fill the application form with personal, educational, and category details
7. Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, etc.)
8. Submit the form and download/print the confirmation page
9. Candidate Read the UP Rojgar Mela Notification 2026 Before Apply the UP Rojgar Mela Application Form 2026 in Rojgar Result Latest Job Section.
