The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), Jaipur is set to release the Admit Card for the Rajasthan Jail Prahari Exam 2025 today, April 8. Candidates appearing for the recruitment exam scheduled on April 12, 2025, will be able to download their e-admit cards from the official website. Alongside the hall ticket, RSSB has also issued important guidelines regarding reporting time, exam day protocols, and a mandatory dress code, which candidates must follow strictly to ensure smooth entry into the examination center.
