India Post has issued an Grameen Daak Sevak GDS advertisement for the recruitment of Schedule II July 2023 posts. Those Candidate Are Enrolled with Vacancy Can Check the Result / Merit List. For recruitment details, pay scale, pet details, age limit, selection prcedure, job information and all other information, read the advertisement and then apply.

Department of Post (India Post) has released an official notification for India Post GDS Recruitment Schedule II July 2023 2nd Result / Merit List for 30041 Post. 
A total of **30041** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **23/08/2023**.

## How to Apply

1. India Post GDS Delhi Are Released Gramin Dak Sewak GDS All India Recruitment in Various Recruitment 2023. Candidate Can Apply Between 03/08/2023 to 23/08/2023.
2. Whichever candidate is registered in previous recruitment January-2023, directly Click on Step-2 : Apply Online
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in India Post GDS Jobs Recruitment May 2023.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
