1. Visit the official website www.pgimer.edu.in.
2. Click on the B.Sc. Nursing Admission 2026 link.
3. Register and fill the online application form.
4. Upload required documents.
5. Pay the application fee online.
6. Submit and take printout of the confirmation page.
7. 10th and 12th Marksheet & Certificate
8. GNM Marksheets & Certificate (for Post Basic)
