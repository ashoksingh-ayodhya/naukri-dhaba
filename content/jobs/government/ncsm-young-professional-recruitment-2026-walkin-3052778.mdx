National Council of Science Museums (NCSM) has published the Young Professional (Gallery Explainer) Notification 2026 under Advt. No. 10/2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 2 Young Professional (Gallery Explainer) positions on a contractual basis. Candidates aged up to 35 years and holding an MA/M.Sc. in Museology, Linguistics, or Anthropology with good knowledge of Linguistic Anthropology from a recognized university are eligible to participate in the recruitment process. Interested candidates can attend the walk-in interview scheduled for 10 June 2026. The official notification PDF and walk-in interview details are provided below.

Candidates must have MA/M.Sc in Museology, Linguistics or Anthropology with good knowledge on Linguistic Anthropology from a recognized university as minimum educational qualification.
