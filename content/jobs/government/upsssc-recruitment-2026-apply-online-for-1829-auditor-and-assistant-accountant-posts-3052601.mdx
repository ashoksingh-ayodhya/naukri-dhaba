Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC), Lucknow has officially announced Advertisement No. 14-Exam/2026 for Auditor and Assistant Accountant Main Examination (Pra0Ar0Pa0-2025)/15 with a total of 1829 vacancies across various departments. The notification was published on 03-06-2026 at upsssc.gov.in. Only those candidates who appeared in the Preliminary Eligibility Test-2025 (PET-2025) and have been issued a Score Card by the Commission are eligible to apply online from 14-07-2026 to 03-08-2026. Fee reconciliation and application amendment last date is 10-08-2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

Department has released an official notification for UPSSSC Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1829 Auditor and Assistant Accountant Posts. 
A total of **1829** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have PwBD candidates must possess a valid disability certificate in the updated prescribed format as per UP Government orders. Disability type and sub-type must be clearly mentioned in the application form. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. PET-2025 Registration Number for login.
2. Photograph and signature (as uploaded during PET-2025; cannot be changed — click “Continue” after viewing).
3. Details of educational qualification (Board/Institution name, year of passing, roll number, certificate date, percentage/marks).
4. Preference order of departments/posts being applied for.
5. Category/reservation certificate details (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwBD/DFF/ESM) as applicable.
6. Domicile/permanent address details (auto-populated from PET-2025 data).
7. EWS certificate (issued between 01-04-2026 and 03-08-2026 in prescribed format), if applicable.
8. Online applications are open from 14-07-2026 to 03-08-2026. Fee reconciliation and application amendment window closes on 10-08-2026.
9. Only one application is to be submitted; if a candidate submits more than one application for the same post, only the last submitted application will be considered.
10. The application form can be amended only for permitted fields (correspondence address, personal details like EWS/PwBD/DFF category, gender, outstanding sportsperson, marital status, mandatory/desirable qualification) up to 10-08-2026.
11. After fee payment and reconciliation, the application is automatically and finally submitted — candidates should download and print it immediately.
12. Candidates must indicate category/subcategory of PwBD disability in the application and update it as per the latest UP Government orders on Divyangjan empowerment.
13. All information in the application is final — no corrections to date of birth, educational qualification percentage/marks, or caste will be entertained after submission.
14. For candidates appearing in examinations at an allotted centre, change of centre will not be entertained under any circumstances.
15. Canvassing in any form will lead to cancellation of candidature and debarment from all UPSSSC examinations.
