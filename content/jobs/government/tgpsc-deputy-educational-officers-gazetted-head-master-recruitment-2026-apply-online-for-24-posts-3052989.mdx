Telangana Public Service Commission (TGPSC) has invited Online Recruitment Applications for Deputy Educational Officers / Gazetted Head Master Grade-I posts in the School Education Department, Government of Telangana, under Notification No. 03/G/SE/2026. A total of 24 vacancies have been notified across Multi Zone-I and Multi Zone-II, and candidates can apply online through the TGPSC website from 12/06/2026 to 19/07/2026 up to 5:00 PM.

Department has released an official notification for TGPSC Deputy Educational Officers / Head Master Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 24 Posts. 
A total of **24** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have PH (VH) candidates with disability not less than 40% are eligible as per reservation conditions. Disability certificate in SADAREM format from competent medical authority required. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent colour passport-size photograph (not older than 3 months, 100kb-200kb, JPG/PNG).
2. Scanned specimen signature (20kb-30kb, JPG/PNG).
3. SSC / CBSE / ICSE certificate as proof of date of birth.
4. School Study Certificate (1st to 7th Class) or Residence Certificate for local candidature.
5. Degree certificate / marksheets for Bachelor’s and Post Graduate educational qualification proof.
6. Community Certificate for BC / SC / ST candidates (issued in the name of applicant with Father’s/Mother’s name), obtained from Revenue Authority not below rank of Tahsildar under Telangana Government.
7. Non-Creamy Layer Certificate for BC candidates (Form VIIB) from Revenue Authority not below the rank of Tahsildar/RDO.
8. Latest Income Certificate for EWS reservation, obtained from Revenue Authority not below the rank of Tahsildar.
9. Disability certificate in SADAREM format from competent medical authority, if applicable.
10. Declaration by the Unemployed, if claiming exemption from examination fee.
11. No Objection Certificate from employer, if applicable.
12. Sports certificate, if claiming sports reservation.
13. Online applications are open from 12/06/2026 to 19/07/2026 up to 5:00 PM.
14. The Commission reserves the right to conduct the examination in OMR-based Offline mode or CBRT mode; mode will be notified on Hall Ticket.
15. All eligibility conditions will be determined as on 05/06/2026 (date of notification).
