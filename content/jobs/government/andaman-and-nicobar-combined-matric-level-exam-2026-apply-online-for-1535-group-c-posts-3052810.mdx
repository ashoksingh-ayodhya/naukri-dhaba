Andaman and Nicobar Administration has released a recruitment notification for 1535 Group ‘C’ Non-Ministerial posts across 194 post codes under various departments through the Combined Matric Level Examination 2026. Key facts:

A total of **1535** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Only Local Scheduled Tribe candidates under A&N Islands Scheduled Tribe Order 1959 are eligible for ST quota. Other ST candidates from outside A&N will be considered only for UR vacancies. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent passport-size colour photograph with plain white/off-white background - 20 KB to 50 KB, resolution 200x230 pixels, jpg/jpeg format; full face, front view, eyes open, no cap/mask/glasses
2. Signature - 10 KB to 20 KB, resolution 140x60 pixels, jpeg format
3. Proof of age (10th class certificate or equivalent) - 100 KB to 500 KB
4. Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets as per Annexure-I for the post applied
5. Category certificate: SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/EWS/PwBD/ESM as applicable - 100 KB to 500 KB
6. NOC from present employer (for departmental/government employees) - 100 KB to 500 KB
7. e-Admit Card (downloaded from https://erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in using Registration Number and Password) + one valid Photo ID in original (Islander Card, Pan Card, Voter ID Card, Driving License, Aadhaar Card issued by Government of India)
8. All original educational qualification certificates (as per Annexure-I for respective post)
9. Category certificate in prescribed format: ST/OBC (NCL)/EWS/PwBD/ESM (OBC-NCL not older than 2 years from closing date)
10. Age proof (original 10th certificate)
11. One recent passport-size photograph
12. NOC from present employer (for departmental candidates)
13. Candidature will be accepted only provisionally; the A&N Administration will not undertake detailed scrutiny at the time of TBT examination. Verification of eligibility, documents, and certificates will be done at the time of Online Document Verification stage.
14. Candidates must carry printed e-Admit Card and one valid government-issued photo ID (in original) to the TBT examination - entry without Admit Card will not be allowed.
15. The A&N Administration reserves the right to restrict entry to the examination venue till 09:30 AM.
