The Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) have released the Centralised Employment Notification (CEN) No. 04/2026 for recruitment to Junior Engineer (JE), Depot Material Superintendent (DMS) and Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant (CMA) posts. Eligible candidates can apply online through the official RRB website.

A total of **4098** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **13-09-2026 (Tentative)**.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official RRB application website rrb.indianrailways.gov.in.
2. Open the RRB JE CEN 04/2026 recruitment section.
3. Read the official notification carefully.
4. Complete the online registration process.
5. Fill out the application form with correct details.
6. Upload required documents and photographs.
7. Pay the application fee (if applicable).
