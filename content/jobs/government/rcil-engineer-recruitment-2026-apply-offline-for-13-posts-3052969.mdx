Candidates with Graduation/PG in Science or Engineering and required certifications with relevant SOC/cybersecurity experience are eligible to apply. The offline application process starts on 05-06-2026 and will remain open until 22-06-2026.

A total of **13** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduation / PG in Science or Engineering as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Download the application form (Annexure-I) from the official RailTel website: www.railtelindia.com
2. Fill the form completely and sign it; affix a self-attested passport size photograph.
3. Clearly mention the name of the post applied for on the application form (applications without post name will be rejected).
4. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents.
5. Superscribe the envelope as: "Application For Technical Personnel For Kerala State Information Technology Mission Project Of Railtel In Southern Region On Contract Basis."
6. Send by post/courier to reach on or before 22.06.2026 during office hours at: Territory Manager, RailTel Corporation of India Limited (Territory Office), First Floor, Eastern Entry Tower, Ernakulam Railway Station, KOCHI - 682016
7. Name in full (in block letters)
8. Father's Name
9. Mother's Name
10. Date of Birth (DD-MM-YYYY)
11. Age as on 22.06.2026 (years, months, days)
12. Category (Gen / SC / ST / OBC / EWS)
13. Whether PwBD or not
