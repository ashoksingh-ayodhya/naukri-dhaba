Indian Institute of Technology Mandi (IIT Mandi) has published the Project Scientist and Project Engineer Notification 2026 under Advertisement No. IITM/C3DAR/TT/2026/04. The recruitment drive aims to fill 3 Project Scientist and Project Engineer positions. Candidates holding M.Tech/M.E or equivalent qualifications, B.Tech in Civil Engineering, or a Bachelor's Degree in Engineering/Technology (CSE/ECE/EEE/Civil/Instrumentation) with at least 70% marks and 2 years of relevant experience are eligible to apply online through the official IIT Mandi website. The application process is open and will remain active until 15 June 2026. Candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

Candidates must have M.Tech/M.E or equivalent, B.Tech in Civil Engineering, Bachelor's degree in Engineering/Technology (CSE/ECE/EEE/Civil/Instrumentation) with 70% marks and 2 years experience as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 15-06-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of IIT Mandi to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
8. Take a printout or save a copy of the submitted application for future reference.
