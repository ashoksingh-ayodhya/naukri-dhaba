National Institute of Health Research (ICMR NIHR) has released the official Project Technical Support-III Notification 2026 on its official website. A total of 2 vacancies have been announced under the ICMR NIHR Project Technical Support-III Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged up to 35 years with a Graduate Degree in Biotechnology/Biochemistry/Microbiology/Biomedical Sciences/Genetics with 3 years post-qualification experience or a Post Graduate Degree in Biotechnology/Biochemistry/Microbiology/Biomedical Sciences/Genetics can attend the walk-in interview scheduled for 08-07-2026. The official notification PDF and application details are available below.

Candidates must have Graduate degree with Biotechnology/Biochemistry/Microbiology / Biomedical Sciences/Genetics with 3 years post-qualification experience, Post Graduate Degree in Biotechnology/Biochemistry/Microbiology/ Biomedical Sciences/Genetics as minimum educational qualification.
