Railway Recruitment Board has issued notice of online / offline form of problem / concerns / suggestion for the candidate. Any NTPC / Group D candidates of railways who have any kind of problem in railway recruitment can register online or offline or through email. This facility is available in all railway boards.

Railway Recruitment Board has issued notice of online / offline form of problem / concerns / suggestion for the candidate. Any NTPC / Group D candidates of railways who have any kind of problem in railway recruitment can register online or offline or through email. This facility is available in all railway boards. has released an official notification for RRB NTPC & Group D Level I Exam concerns/ suggestion Online / Offline Form 2022. 

## How to Apply

1. Candidates can send their concerns / suggestions to the Railways through 03 mediums.
2. First Type: The candidate himself can fill his complaint on the format given in the notice and submit it by visiting the camp office.
3. Second Type : The candidate can fill his problem with his registered email id as per the format issued and send the email of Railway Recruitment Board committee to rrbcommittee@railnet.gov.in.
4. Third Type: Candidate can also register his complaint through online form. The link of which is available in the Some Useful Important Link at the bottom of this page.
5. For more information and the address of the camp office, you can visit the official website of RRB.
