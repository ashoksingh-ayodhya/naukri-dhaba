Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL) has published the Electrical Supervisor Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 20 Electrical Supervisor positions. Candidates up to 65 years of age and who are superannuated Electrical Supervisors of NCL with an excellent professional record are eligible to apply offline. The application process is open until 23-06-2026, and candidates can access the official notification PDF and apply link below.

The last date to apply is **23-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Superannuated Electrical Supervisor of NCL with excellent professional record as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.nclcil.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 23-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
