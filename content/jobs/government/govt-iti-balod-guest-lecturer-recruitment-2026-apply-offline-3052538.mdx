Government Industrial Training Institute Balod (Govt ITI Balod) has published the Guest Lecturer Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 8 Guest Lecturer positions on a contractual basis. Candidates aged 18 years and above, possessing qualifications such as 10th pass or equivalent, NTC/NAC/ITI in relevant trade from NCVT/SCVT, valid LMV driving license (for Driver cum Mechanic and Mechanic Diesel), or BCA/PGDCA (for COPA), are eligible to apply offline. Interested candidates can submit their applications by 19 June 2026, with direct links to the official notification provided below.

Candidates must have 10th pass or equivalent, NTC/NAC/ITI in relevant trade from NCVT/SCVT, Valid LMV driving licence (for Driver cum Mechanic and Mechanic Diesel), BCA/PGDCA or equivalent (for COPA) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.balod.gov.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 19-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
