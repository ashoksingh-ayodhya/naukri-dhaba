Delhi Technological University (DTU) has released the official JRF/SRF and Field Worker Notification 2026 on its official website. A total of vacancies have been announced under the DTU JRF/SRF and Field Worker Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates with a Master's degree (M.Tech/M.E./M.S./M.Sc), B.Tech in Electronics Engineering/Electronics Science/RF & Microwave Engineering/Communication Engineering/Embedded Systems/AI & Data Science or related fields, or Graduation with relevant experience can participate in the walk-in interview scheduled for 15 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application details are available below.

Candidates must have Master's degree (M.Tech / M.E. / M.S /M.Sc)/B.Tech in Electronics Engineering /Electronics Science / RF & Microwave Engineering / Communication Engineering / Embedded Systems / AI & Data Science or related field, Graduation with some experience as minimum educational qualification.
