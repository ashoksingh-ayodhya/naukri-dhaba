Examination Regulation Authority UP, PNP Prayagraj is going to conduct the Under UPDELED (BTC) Admissions 2023. Students who want to take admission in Any DELED Courses can apply online from 02 June 2023 to 31 August 2023. UPDELED Admission 2023 Guidlines, Schedule, College List and all other information read the notification has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh UPDELEd Admissions 2023 State Rank Card / Merit List, Phase 2 Counseling Schedule, Choice Filling, Allotment Result. 
The last date to apply is **31/08/2023 upto 11:59 PM Only**.

## How to Apply

1. The Uttar Pradesh government has released the GO of 2023 this year for 2 years DELED whose old name was UPBTC.
2. This year the online applications for admission will start from 02/06/2023 the last date is 31/08/2023 for Apply Online Visit Sarkari Result.
3. The entrance exam will not be held this year, this year the admission in Uttar Pradesh DELED will be on the basis of merit.
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Applying the Recruitment Application Form in UP DELED BTC Admission 2023.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
9. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
