Indian navy (Nausena Bharti) has released the advertisement of 10+2 Sailor Entry SSR and AA Recruitment August 2022 Batch. Any candidate who wants to appear in this Navy 10+2 AA and SSR Recruitment 2022 and fulfill the eligibility can apply online from 29 March 2022 to 05 April 2022. Read the advertisement for eligibility, age limit, syllabus, selection procedure and all other information in Navy August 2022 Batch Recruitment 2022.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Join Indian Navy Sailor Entry SSR and AA 10+2 August 2022 Batch Online Form 2022. 
A total of **2500** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **05/04/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Join Indian Navy Sailor Entry AA & SSR August 2022 Batch. Candidate Can Apply Between 29/03/2022 to 05/04/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Navy Sailor Entry Recruitment 2022 Apply Online Form for Indian Navy Latest Sailor Entry Vacancies 2022-2023.
3. Kindly Check and College the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
