Konkan Railway Corporation (KRCL) has released the KRCL Chief Personnel Officer Recruitment 2026 notification for 1 prestigious deputation-based Group ‘A’ level post in the Personnel Department under Advt. No. DE/02/2026.

The last date to apply is **17-04-2026**.
Candidates must have Group 'A' Railway Service (except IRPFS and IRHS), Minimum 18 years of service, Presently working in SAG/NFSAG as minimum educational qualification.
