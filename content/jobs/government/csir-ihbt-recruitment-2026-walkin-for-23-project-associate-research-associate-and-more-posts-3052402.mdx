The Institute of Himalayan Bioresource Technology (CSIR IHBT) has officially announced the Project Associate, Senior Research Fellow, Research Associate, and Project Scientist Recruitment 2026 with 23 openings. The detailed notification was published under Advertisement No. 08/2026 on the official CSIR IHBT website. Candidates meeting the age criteria, including relaxations for SC/ST/PwBD/Women (5 years) and OBC (3 years), and holding relevant qualifications such as M.Sc., M.Pharm, Ph.D., or Master's degrees in Biological Sciences, Biotechnology, Agriculture, Food Technology, Chemistry, or related disciplines, can attend the walk-in recruitment process from 15-06-2026 to 19-06-2026. Direct links to download the notification and complete walk-in details are shared below.

Candidates must have M.Sc. in Food Technology/ Food Science/ Food and Nutrition/ Nutrition and Dietetics, Masters in Chemistry / Biochemistry/ Phytochemistry/ Organic Chemistry/ M. Pharmacy, Masters in Biochemistry or Molecular Biology or Life/ Biological Sciences/Biotechnology or Phytochemistry or M. Pharmacy, M.Sc in Biotechnology/ Plant Biotechnology/ Life Sciences/ Molecular Biology and Biotechnology/ Botany, M.Sc. in Botany, Plant Physiology with 3 years experience, M.Sc. in Botany, Biotechnology, Biochemistry, Ph.D. in Biological Science/ Biotechnology, Biochemistry/ Botany, Doctoral Degree in Science or Master's Degree in Engineering or Technology, Master's Degree in Natural or Agricultural Sciences / MVSc or bachelor's degree in Engineering or Technology or Medicine, M.Sc. in Statistics/ Agri-economics /Agriculture Extension / Agribusiness Management, Master degree in Agriculture (Agronomy, soil science, genetics & Plant Breeding) or Horticulture, Master degree in Life Science/ Biological Sciences/ Biotechnology/ Botany/ Agriculture/ Horticulture/ Chemistry/ Chemical Science /Food Science & Technology, MBA/ M.Sc. Agriculture Economics as minimum educational qualification.
