Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri National Sanskrit University has released the official Non-Teaching Posts Notification 2026 on its official website. A total of 33 vacancies have been announced under the SLBSRSV Non-Teaching Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged as per Government of India / UGC rules with qualifications such as 10th Pass, ITI, 10+2, Bachelor’s Degree, Master’s Degree, B.E./B.Tech, MCA/M.Sc (as per post) can apply online. The online application process is currently open and will remain available until 21 May 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.

Candidates must have 10th Pass, ITI Pass, 10+2, Bachelor's Degree, Master's Degree, B.E./B.Tech Computer Science/Electronics, MCA/M.Sc Computer Science, Stenography skills, Typing skills, Driving License as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 21-05-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of SLBSRSV to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
8. Take a printout or save a copy of the submitted application for future reference.
