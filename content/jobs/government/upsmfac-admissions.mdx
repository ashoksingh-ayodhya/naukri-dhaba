UPSMFAC Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty UPSMFAC Lucknow has released the information brochure of Combined Paramedical Selection Process CPSP 2024, any student who wants to take admission in any certificate / diploma course of 6 months, 01 year or 02 years in Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty can Apply Counseling. Read the information brochure for other admission related information like placement, college list, course information, fee related and all others.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty UPSMFAC Combined Paramedical Courses CPSP Admissions 2024 Counseling. 
The last date to apply is **30/06/2024**.

## How to Apply

1. Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty UPSMFAC Various Diploma Courses Admission Online Form Candidate Can Apply Between 08/06/2024 to 30/06/2024.
2. Photo Instruction : The photo should be accompanied by a black slate and the name and date of taking the photo should also be there on the photo. The photo should be after 01 March 2024.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in Uttar Pradesh State Medical Faculty Exam 2024.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
