The Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company (MAHATRANSCO) has officially announced the Electrician (Vidyut) Recruitment 2026 with 26 openings. Candidates aged between 18 and 35 years and having completed SSC along with ITI Electrician NCVT certification are eligible to apply. The detailed notification provides complete vacancy details, eligibility criteria, and instructions for online application. Interested candidates can submit their applications online from 4 June 2026 to 11 June 2026. The official notification PDF and direct apply link are provided below.

The last date to apply is **11-06-2026**.
Candidates must have SSC Pass, ITI Electrician NCVT Pass as minimum educational qualification.
