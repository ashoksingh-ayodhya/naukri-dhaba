State Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission, U.T. Chandigarh (SCDRC Chandigarh) has published the Steno-Typist (English) Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 1 Steno-Typist (English) position. Candidates aged between 18 and 28 years for OBC candidates and up to 30 years for SC candidates, holding a Bachelor's Degree or its equivalent along with proficiency in computer operations, are eligible to apply online. Interested candidates can submit their applications on or before 24 June 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are provided below.

The last date to apply is **24-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Bachelor's Degree or its equivalent with proficiency in operation of Computer (Word processing and Spread Sheets) and a speed of 80 words per minute in Stenography (English) and speed of 20 words per minute in transcripting the same. Must have a Certificate of ICT Skills course i.e. Course on Computer Concepts (CCC) 80 hours from a Govt. recognized institution OR a reputed institution which is an ISO 9001 certified OR Department of Electronics Accreditation of Computer Course (DOEACC) of Govt. of India OR from NIELIT and its authorized institutions. as minimum educational qualification.
