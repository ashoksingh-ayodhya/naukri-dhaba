The latest Gujarat Subordinate Service Selection Board (GSSSB) Recruitment 2026 has been officially updated through Sudhara Jaherat-2 (Correction Advertisement-2) for the posts of Peta Hisabnish / Sub Auditor (Class-3) and Hisabnish, Auditor / Peta Treasury Officer / Inspector (Class-3). The total number of vacancies has been significantly increased to 776. This is a golden opportunity for commerce, mathematics, statistics, and economics graduates seeking stable Class-III government jobs in the Finance Department of Gujarat. The fresh online application window is open from 02 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. Find complete details about GSSSB Sub Accountant Vacancy 2026, eligibility criteria, age limit, salary, selection process, application procedure, important instructions, and official notification PDF below.

Gujarat Subordinate Service Selection Board (GSSSB) has released an official notification for GSSSB Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Vacancies Increased to 776 Sub Accountant, Sub Auditor & Accountant Posts. 
A total of **776** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Bachelor’s Degree in BBA / BCA / B.Com / B.Sc (Mathematics or Statistics) / BA (Statistics / Economics / Mathematics) or equivalent from a recognized University. B.Sc (CA & IT) and M.Sc (CA & IT) are NOT eligible. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in
2. Click on “Online Application” and select the relevant advertisement.
3. Register with a valid mobile number and email ID.
4. Fill all personal, educational, and category details carefully.
5. Upload scanned photograph, signature, and other documents.
6. Pay the required fee online.
7. Submit the application and note down the Confirmation Number.
8. Take a printout of the submitted form for future reference.
9. Recent photograph and signature
10. Educational qualification certificates
11. Caste / Non-Creamy Layer / EWS Certificate (if applicable)
12. PwBD Certificate (if applicable)
13. Ex-Servicemen documents (if applicable)
14. Candidates can apply only once. Multiple applications will be rejected.
15. All information filled must be correct. Wrong information leads to cancellation.
