South Eastern Railway has released the detailed notification of Apprentices 1785 posts whose advertisement number is SER/P-HQ/RRC/PERS/ACT APPRENTICES/2022-23 . Candidates who have placed in various trade apprentice posts in South Eastern Railway SER, see the information of Workshop : Kharagpur, Ranchi, Etc wise trade in the notification. Interested eligible candidates can apply online from 03 January 2023 to 02 February 2023. In this new Railway SER Apprentice Recruitment 2023, read the advertisement of South Eastern Railway Apprentices for eligibility, trade information, category wise post details, selection procedure, pay scale, training time and other information.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for RRC South Eastern Railway SER Act. Apprentice Recruitment 2023 Apply Online for 1785 Post. 
A total of **1785** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **02/02/2023 upto 5 PM only**.
