## How to Apply

1. Step 1 : Candidate must first select the regional – Gorakhpur, Lucknow, Barelly, Prayagraj, Varanasi, Ghaziabad, Meerut, Agra, Jhansi, Kanpur, Ayodhya & Azamgarh
2. Step 2 : After selecting the region, the study center has to be selected in that region.
3. Step 3 : Course Type to be selected – Under Graduate / Post Graduate / Certificate / Diploma / Other
4. Step 4 :Candidate should fill his personal details, address and qualification sought in the course and upload his photo and signature.
5. Step 5 : Pay the Course Application Fee Through Online Fee Mode.
6. Uttar Pradesh Rajarshi Tondon Open University, Prayagraj UPRTOU Released UG, PG, Diploma, Certificate, Awareness Courses Admission Notification 2023. Candidate Can Apply From June 2023 to 16/08/2023.
7. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in UPRTOU Admission July 2023-2024.
8. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
9. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
10. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
11. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
12. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
