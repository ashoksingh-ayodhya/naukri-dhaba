High Explosives Factory Khadki has released the official Tenure Based Project Engineer Notification 2026 at the official website. A total of 6 vacancies have been announced under the HEF Khadki Tenure Based Project Engineer Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged up to 30 years (with applicable relaxation for OBC-NCL and SC/ST candidates) and possessing B.E./B.Tech/Diploma in Chemical Engineering or B.Sc. with Chemistry as a major subject along with the prescribed apprenticeship qualification can apply offline on or before 21 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application form are available below.

Candidates must have B.E/B.Tech/Diploma in Chemical Engineering OR B.Sc Graduate with Chemistry as one of the major subjects. Apprenticeship in Chemical Trade as per Apprenticeship Act 1961 in Ordnance Factories under Munitions India Limited OR in Factories manufacturing/handling ammunitions & explosives OR in Factories other than manufacturing handling of ammunition & explosives. as minimum educational qualification.
