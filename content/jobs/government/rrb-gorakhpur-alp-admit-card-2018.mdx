Railway recruitment board Gorakhpur has released around 1500 vacancies to recruit eligible candidates for the posts of ALP and Technician grade III. It is expected that the RRB Gorakhpur will organize the first stage CBT in May 2018. The RRB Gorakhpur will announce the ALP exam date officially @ rrbgkp.gov.in. Candidates are advised to start preparing for the exam to score good marks in the online computer-based exam.

A total of **1588** posts are available for this recruitment.
