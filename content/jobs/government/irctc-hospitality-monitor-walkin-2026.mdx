The latest Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation (IRCTC) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Hospitality Monitor posts! Published on 13 May 2026 at irctc.com, this new recruitment drive offers 92 vacancies for eligible candidates. Applicants within the age range of Max: 27 years and possessing a B.Sc. in Hospitality and Hotel Administration or BBA/MBA in Culinary Arts, or equivalent qualifications, can apply through a Walk-in Interview from 03 June 2026 to 23 June 2026. Find all details about the IRCTC Hospitality Monitor new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, walk-in interview details, and notification PDF here.

A total of **92** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 01-05-2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. 10th class Marksheet & Certificate
2. 12th class Marksheet & Certificate
3. Graduation year-wise all Marksheets & Certificate/Degree (Provisional Certificate/Degree from concerned University may also be considered). Candidates passed out from IHMs should carry all six semesters marksheets, consolidated marksheet issued from IGNOU & degree/certificate issued from IGNOU.
4. Post-Graduation or any other higher academic/education (year-wise all Marksheets & Certificate), if applicable
5. All Experience Certificates in chronological order (if any)
6. Caste certificate supporting SC/ST/OBC category (OBC certificate should be for non-creamy layer and not more than 1 year old)
7. EWS/PwBD certificate (if applicable)
8. Proof of Identity & Proof of Address
9. Two recent passport size photographs
10. Any other relevant documents
11. Only Indian Nationals are eligible to apply.
12. This engagement is purely contractual in nature and will not entitle any candidate to claim regular/permanent employment in IRCTC.
13. Freshers with relevant educational qualification are eligible; prior experience is not mandatory.
14. The cut-off date for age, qualification, experience and other purposes is 01-05-2026.
15. Candidate can appear for interview at any one convenient place only.
