Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IIT Delhi) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 1 Sr. Project Scientist Posts. Candidates must have 1st class or equivalent Master's degree with at least three years of experience in an area related to higher education, education policy or innovation. as minimum educational qualification.
