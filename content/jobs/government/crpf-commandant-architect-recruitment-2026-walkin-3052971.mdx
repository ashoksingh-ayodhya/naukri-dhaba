Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) Commandant (Architect) Notification 2026 has been released on the official website of CRPF. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced through the CRPF Commandant (Architect) Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have Degree in Architecture from a recognized University or Institution and registered with Council of Architecture under the Architecture Act, 1972 (20 of 1972), Ten years experience in execution of Architecture work, Holding analogous posts on regular basis or five years regular service in level 12 or ten years regular service in level 11 in the pay matrix as minimum educational qualification.
