Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti NVS has released the detailed notification for the Principal, Trained Graduate Teacher TGT, Post Graduate Teacher PGT and Misc Teacher Art, Music, Physical Education, Librarain Direct Teaching Post recruitment 2022. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Vacancy Can Download the Exam Date and City. For eligibility, age limit, selection procedure, pay scale, syllabus and all other information in this NVS Latest Teaching recruitment, read the notification and then apply online.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti NVS PGT Final Result 2023 – 1616 Post. 
A total of **1616** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **22/07/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti NVS Various Teaching Recruitment 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 02/07/2022 to 22/07/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Jobs Application Form in NVS Principal, TGT, PGT, Other Misc Teaching Post Recruitment 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Are Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Pay and Complete Your Form
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
