Defence Research and Development Organisation Recruitment & Assessment Centre (DRDO RAC) has officially released the Scientist Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. 157) for multiple vacancies across Scientist E, Scientist D, and Scientist C positions under the Defence Research & Development Service (DRDS) cadre. Eligible engineering and science professionals meeting the prescribed educational qualifications and experience requirements can apply online from 30 May 2026 to 19 June 2026 through the RAC recruitment portal. Selected candidates will be appointed on a regular permanent basis under the Lateral Recruitment Scheme with basic pay ranging from ₹67,700 to ₹1,23,100 per month under Pay Levels 11, 12, and 13, along with applicable allowances.

Candidates must have Post Graduate Qualification in Engineering: 2 years; PG Degree in Medicine/Physiology (Medical Post only): 3 years; Doctorate Degree in Science: 3 years; PhD in Pharmacy: 3 years; PhD in Engineering: 4 years. (Only if in the relevant discipline and acquired before closing date; period of study must not overlap with work experience.) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Date of Birth (DOB) proof - Self attested Matriculation / High School certificate / Birth Certificate
2. A recent passport size colour photograph (not exceeding 30 KB)
3. Scanned sample of candidate's signature (not exceeding 30 KB)
4. Self-attested degree certificates/testimonials regarding Essential and Higher qualification along with mark-sheets
5. Fellowship proofs mentioning start date and end date of fellowship (if applicable)
6. Duly signed CCA Declaration (if applicable)
7. Notarized affidavit in support (if name mismatch in documents)
8. Notarized/certified transcript if documents are in language other than English or Hindi
9. All experience certificates (with start and end date) and salary Proofs / Appointment letter (with pay scale or CTC), Promotion letters (with revised scales or CTC), Form 16A and Salary slips (beginning and end of each employment)
10. Requisite caste / disability certificate (for SC/ST/Divyang – for fee waiver only)
11. Requisite Release certificate (if retired Armed Forces personnel)
12. CCA Declaration (applicable) and NOC at Final Personal Interview stage
13. All candidates must apply online through https://rac.gov.in only. No other mode of application is accepted.
14. Closing Date: 19 June 2026 (1600 hrs.) - facility for online application submission will remain available on RAC website only up to this time.
15. Do not divulge any confidential information related to work experience of present or previous employer while filling the application; RAC shall not be held responsible for revelation of such information.
