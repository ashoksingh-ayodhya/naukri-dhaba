Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan KVS is going to organize All India Class 1 Admissions 2025. Any parent who wants to get their child admitted in class 01 of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan KVS and KVS Balvatika can apply online from 07 March 2025 to 21 March 2025. Read the complete information before applying and then apply.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan KVS Class 1 & Balvatika Class 1 Admissions 2025 Download Result. 
The last date to apply is **21/03/2025 Upto 10:00 Pm Only**.

## How to Apply

1. UIDAI Aadhar Card is Compulsory in KVS Nursery Admission 2025-26
2. Step 1 : Candidates Must be Read the Detailed Notification Before Apply Online Form in Any Class
3. Step 2 : Click on the Apply Online Link and Choose Your Apply School,Enter Your All Required Details
4. Step 3 : Uploaded the All Required Documents and Photo of Candidates and Parents
5. Step 4 : Review Your All Details Before Submit the Online Application Form
6. Step 5 : Take a Print out of Online Application Form and Wait for the Announced Merit List
7. Document Required for Upload : Birth Certificate and Candidate Photo Only.
