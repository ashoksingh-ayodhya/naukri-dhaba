The latest DSSSB TGT Computer Science Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Trained Graduate Teacher (Computer Science) posts! Published on 29-05-2026 at the official website of Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board, this new recruitment drive offers 675 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants not exceeding 30 years of age and possessing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Application / Computer Science / BE/B.Tech (CS/IT) or equivalent qualification can apply online from 16-06-2026 to 15-07-2026. Find all details about the DSSSB TGT Computer Science new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for DSSSB TGT Computer Science Recruitment 2026 Notification Out for 675 Vacancies, Apply Online. 
A total of **675** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Qualifications are relaxable at the discretion of the Competent Authority, for reasons to be recorded in writing, in the case of candidates otherwise well qualified. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Step I: Register on https://dsssbonline.nic.in (if not already registered) to get User ID and Password. Registration is a one-time exercise.
2. Step II: Login using User ID and Password, select Post Code 41/26 (TGT Computer Science), and fill in the application form carefully.
3. Step III: Upload a recent clear colour photograph (plain white/off-white background, full face, eyes open) and scanned signature.
4. Step IV: Pay the application fee of Rs.100/- through SBI e-Pay only (Women/SC/ST/PwBD/ESM are exempted).
5. Step V: Submit the application and take a printout of the submitted application form for records.
6. Recent clear colour photograph (plain white or off-white background, full face, front view, eyes open) - printed on photo quality paper when called
7. Scanned signature
8. Date of Birth proof (Matriculation/10th certificate)
9. All educational qualification certificates (Graduation/BCA/B.Tech marksheets and degree certificates)
10. DOEACC 'A' Level certificate (if applicable)
11. Community/Caste Certificate for SC/ST/OBC (Delhi)/EWS in prescribed format from competent authority
12. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate issued on/after 01/04/2026, not later than 15/07/2026, from Revenue Department, GNCT of Delhi
13. EWS Income and Asset Certificate valid for FY 2026-2027 (based on income for FY 2025-2026) as per DoPT OM No.36039/1/2019-Estt(Res) dated 31.01.2019
14. PwBD Disability Certificate from competent authority (for PwBD candidates)
15. SSA contractual service certificate (for SSA resource persons claiming age relaxation)
