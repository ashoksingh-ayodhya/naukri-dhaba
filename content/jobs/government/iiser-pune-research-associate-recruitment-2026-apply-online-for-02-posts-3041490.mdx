Indian Institute of Science Education and Research Pune (IISER Pune) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 2 Research Associate Posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply by sending their application via Email. The last date to send the application email is 21-03-2026.

The last date to apply is **21-03-2026**.
Candidates must have Ph.D. in Chemistry or related areas. Candidates who have submitted their thesis may also apply as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 21-03-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
