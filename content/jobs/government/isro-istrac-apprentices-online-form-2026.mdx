ISRO ISTRAC Apprentice Recruitment 2026 has been officially announced by the ISRO Telemetry Tracking & Command Network (ISTRAC), Bengaluru under the Department of Space, Government of India. A total of 95 apprentice positions - across Graduate, Diploma, and ITI (Trade) categories - are open for one-year training under Advt. No. ISTRAC:01:2026 dated 22 June 2026. The last date to apply via email is 31 July 2026. Only candidates who passed their qualifying course in 2024, 2025, or 2026 are eligible.

A total of **95** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Register on the NATS portal at nats.education.gov.in and obtain your NATS Enrolment Number.
2. Prepare scanned copies of: Final Degree/Diploma/ITI Certificate (or final year/semester mark sheet if certificate is awaited), Class X and Pre-University marks card/certificate, and NATS Enrolment Number.
3. Compose an email to the correct location-specific email ID (see table below).
4. In the Subject Line, mention the Post Code - e.g., "Application for Post Code: GA02".
5. In the body of the email, clearly state: Name, Contact Number, Degree/Diploma/ITI Details, Year of Passing, and NATS Enrolment ID.
6. Attach all required documents and send before 31 July 2026.
7. Application Scrutiny: Submitted emails are screened for eligibility and completeness.
8. Shortlisting: Eligible candidates are shortlisted based on academic percentage and other criteria.
9. Document Verification / Interview: Shortlisted candidates are called to the respective centre. Interviews are held at Lucknow (27-28 August 2026), Sri Vijaya Puram (03-04 September 2026), and Bengaluru (09-11 September 2026). Reporting time at all centres is 8:30 AM.
10. Panel Formation: A merit panel is prepared based on Degree/Diploma/ITI academic scores and interview performance.
11. Final Induction: Apprentices are inducted against vacant positions strictly based on their rank in the panel. Selected candidates are informed only via email.
12. Proof of date of birth
13. Class X and Pre-University marks card/certificate
14. Final Degree/Diploma/ITI Certificate or final year mark sheet (if certificate is awaited)
15. Mark sheets of all years/semesters of qualifying course
