## How to Apply

1. Visit the official ISRO website: www.isro.gov.in
2. Navigate to the Careers/Recruitment section and locate Advt No. ISRO:ICRB:01(A-JPA):2026
3. Read the detailed notification carefully to confirm eligibility for the post applied for
4. Register/log in and complete the online application form
5. Upload the required documents/photograph and signature as specified in the detailed notification
6. Pay the applicable processing fee online
7. Submit the application before the last date, 16 August 2026
8. Save/print the confirmation page for future reference
9. Candidates should visit www.isro.gov.in for full details on posts, eligibility, and reservation points.
10. CGPA-to-percentage conversion is not permitted for determining eligibility, even if a University prescribes a conversion formula.
11. Diploma in Commercial Practice/Diploma in Commercial and Computer Practice candidates with a NATS/BOAT Apprenticeship Training Certificate may count it as equivalent to one year of Steno-Typist/Stenographer experience.
12. The last date for online registration is 16 August 2026; the Government encourages candidates to apply well in advance to avoid last-minute technical issues.
