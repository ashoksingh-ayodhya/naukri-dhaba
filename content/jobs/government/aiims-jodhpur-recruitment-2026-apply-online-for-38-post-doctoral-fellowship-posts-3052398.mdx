All India Institute of Medical Sciences, Jodhpur (AIIMS Jodhpur) Post-Doctoral Fellowship Notification 2026 has been released on 02-06-2026 on the official website of AIIMS Jodhpur. A total of 38 vacancies have been announced through the AIIMS Jodhpur Post-Doctoral Fellowship Recruitment 2026.

The last date to apply is **30-06-2026**.
Candidates must have MD/DNB in Anaesthesiology, Physical Medicine Rehabilitation, Plastic Surgery, Pathology, Dermatology, Radiology/Radiodiagnosis, General Surgery, General Medicine, Neurosurgery, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Orthopaedics, Pediatrics, Psychiatry, Transfusion Medicine/Immunohematology & Blood Transfusion from an MCI/NMC recognized Institution with 2 year Post PG Teaching Experience (where specified). as minimum educational qualification.
