Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL), a Government of India Enterprise, has released an official advertisement for 100 posts of Junior Telecom Officer – Direct Recruitment (Telecom) [JTO(T)-DR] from the open market.

A total of **100** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the BSNL official examination website: www.externalexam.bsnl.co.in and register online only. No manual/paper applications will be entertained.
2. Keep the following ready before registering: valid mobile number and email ID (for OTP), scanned passport size colour photograph (jpg/jpeg, 20–50 kb, white background), scanned signature (jpg/jpeg, 10–20 kb), Class 10th Certificate (date of birth proof), Degree Certificate and consolidated marksheet, photo identity proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License/Voter ID), valid category/reservation certificate as applicable, and Medical Certificate for PwBD candidates.
3. Pay the requisite non-refundable examination fee online through Internet Banking, Credit Card, or Debit Card.
4. After completing registration, take a printout of the computer-generated registration form and retain it for future reference.
5. An editing window will be open for 7 days after the registration period closes (July 4–11, 2026). Selective fields can be edited during this period only.
6. Candidates currently employed in Government Departments/Public Sector Undertakings/Autonomous Bodies must produce a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from their present employer at the time of document verification.
7. Candidates must select up to 21 Circle preferences (in order of preference) from the registration portal dropdown menu. Options once exercised shall be final.
8. Admit cards can be downloaded from the BSNL website using the registered username and password well before the examination date.
