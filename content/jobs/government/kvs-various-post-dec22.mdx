A total of **13404** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **02/01/2023**.
Candidates must have Notice | Update Link as minimum educational qualification.

1. Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan KVS Teaching & Non Teaching Latest Jobs Recruitment 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 05/12/2022 to 02/01/2023
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in KVS Various Post Recruitment Exam 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Exam Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
