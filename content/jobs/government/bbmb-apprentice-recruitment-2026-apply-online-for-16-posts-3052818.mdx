Bhakra Beas Management Board (BBMB) has released the official Apprentice Notification 2026 under Advt. No. 01/2026. A total of 16 vacancies have been announced under the BBMB Apprentice Recruitment 2026. Candidates who have passed 10th Class with a minimum of 40% marks along with an ITI/NCVT/SCVT certificate for ITI trades, or 8th Class for Non-ITI trades such as Gardner and Cook, are eligible to apply online. Interested candidates can submit their applications on or before 04 July 2026. The official notification PDF and online application link are available below.

The last date to apply is **04-07-2026**.
Candidates must have 10th class with minimum 40% marks and ITI/NCVT/SCVT certificate for ITI trades, 8th class for Non-ITI trades (Gardner, Cook) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 04-07-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of BBMB to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
8. Take a printout or save a copy of the submitted application for future reference.
