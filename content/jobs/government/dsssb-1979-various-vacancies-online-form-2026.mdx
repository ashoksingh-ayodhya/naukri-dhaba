Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released Advertisement No. 03/2026, inviting online applications for 1979 vacancies across 25 posts in multiple departments of the Government of NCT of Delhi, including Forensic Science Laboratory, Directorate of Education, New Delhi Municipal Council, Information Technology Department, Labour Department, and Department of Delhi Archives. The online application window opens on 16 June 2026 (12:00 Noon) and the last date to apply is 15 July 2026 (11:59 PM). Applications must be submitted only through the official portal at https://dsssbonline.nic.in.

A total of **1979** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 15 July 2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official DSSSB registration and application portal at https://dsssbonline.nic.in.
2. If you are a new user, complete the one-time registration process. Note down your User ID and password — the same credentials are to be used for all future DSSSB examinations; do not register multiple times.
3. Log in using your registered credentials and select the relevant post code from Advertisement No. 03/2026.
4. Fill in all fields of the online application form accurately. Once submitted, no change or correction — including change of category — will be permitted under any circumstances.
5. Upload a recent, clear colour photograph (plain white or off-white background, full face, front view, eyes open) and your signature. Blurred or illegible photographs/signatures will result in rejection.
6. Pay the application fee of ₹100 through SBI ePay (exempted categories need not pay).
7. Submit the form and take a printout for your own records. Keep the registration number and application number safely.
