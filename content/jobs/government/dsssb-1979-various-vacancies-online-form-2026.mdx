The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released Advertisement No. 03/2026, inviting online applications from eligible candidates for recruitment to 1,979 posts across 25 different post codes in various departments and autonomous bodies of the Government of NCT of Delhi. This is one of the largest DSSSB recruitment cycles in 2026, covering a wide range of profiles - from Junior Scientific Assistants in the Forensic Science Laboratory to Trained Graduate Teachers (Computer Science) in the Directorate of Education, and technical posts such as Fitter, Lift Operator, Sub Station Apprentice, and Electrical Overseer in NDMC and the Labour Department. The notification was issued on 29 May 2026 and the online application window opens on 16 June 2026.

Departments: has released an official notification for DSSSB Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1979 Assistant, Trained Graduate Teacher and More Posts. 
A total of **1979** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **15/07/2026 (till 11:59 PM)**.
Candidates must have 15/07/2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the DSSSB portal: https://dsssbonline.nic.in
2. Complete one-time registration (if not already registered).
3. Login and select the relevant post code from Advertisement No. 03/2026.
4. Fill in all details carefully - no changes can be made after submission.
5. Pay the application fee of ₹100 via SBI e-Pay (exempted for Women/SC/ST/PwBD/ESM).
6. Upload a recent colour passport photo (plain white/off-white background, clear face, eyes open) and signature.
7. Submit and download the application form printout.
8. Exam date will be announced separately on the DSSSB website.
9. Only one registration is allowed per candidate on the DSSSB portal. Multiple registrations will lead to cancellation of all applications.
10. Only one application per post code is allowed. Multiple applications for the same post code will result in cancellation and debarment.
11. The eligibility cut-off date is 15 July 2026 - age, qualification, experience, and all other eligibility conditions will be determined as on this date.
12. DSSSB does not verify eligibility at the application stage; candidature is accepted on a provisional basis. Verification happens at the e-dossier stage after the written examination.
13. No calculator, mobile phone, Bluetooth device, earbud, camera, smartwatch, or any other electronic gadget is allowed in the examination hall.
14. Dress code: light clothes with half sleeves, no big buttons, brooch, or badge; salwar/trouser; slippers or sandals with low heels. Shoes are not allowed.
15. Exam centres will be in Delhi/NCR and other states as decided by DSSSB.
