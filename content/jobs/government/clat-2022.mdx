Consortium Of National Law Universities has released the notification of CLAT 2022 Under Graduate and Post Graduate Entrance Exam. Those Candidate Are Enrolled with CLAT Admission Test Can Download the Result & Score Card. Other information related to the entrance exam like list of participating universities, fee information, exam syllabus and model question paper and all other information is available in the advertisement.

Candidates must have National Law School Of India University NLSUI, Bangalore; The National Law Institute University NLIU, Bhopal; National Law University, NLU Jodhpur; Gujarat National Law University,GNLU Gandhinagar; Rajiv Gandhi National University Of Law, Punjab RGNUL ,Patiala; The National university of Advanced Legal studies, NUALS Kochi; National University of Study and Research in Law, NUSRL Ranchi; Damodaram Sanjivyya National Law University, DSNLU, Vishakhapatnam; Maharashtra National Law University, MNLU Mumbai; NALSAR University of Law, Hyderabad; The West Bengal NU of Juridical Sciences NUJS, Kolkata; Hidayafullah National Law University HNLU, Raipur; DR. Ram Manohar Lohiya RMLNLU, Lucknow; Chanakya National Law University, CLNU Patna; National Law University Odisha, NLUO Cuttack; NLU and Judicial Academy, Assam, Guwahati; The Tamil Nadu national Law School, Tiruchirappalli as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. CONSORTIUM OF NATIONAL LAW UNIVERSITIES Are Released CLAT UG / PG Admission 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 01/01/2022 to 31/03/2022
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in LLB / BA LLB Admission 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Application Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
