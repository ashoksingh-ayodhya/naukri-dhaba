National Aluminium Company Limited (NALCO) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Non-Executive Personnel posts! Published on 14-05-2026 at NALCO nalcoindia.com, this new recruitment drive offers 268 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the 27 to 45 Years age group (post-wise) and possessing a 10th / ITI / Diploma / B.Sc qualification can apply online from 21-05-2026 to 10-06-2026. Find all details about the NALCO Non-Executive Personnel new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Department / Location has released an official notification for NALCO Non-Executive Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 268 Technician, Operator and More Posts. 

## How to Apply

1. Left Thumb Impression
2. Hand Written Declaration (in English, blue/black ink)
3. Proof of Date of Birth (Matriculation Certificate / School Leaving Certificate / Matriculation mark sheet) (PDF)
4. Photo Identity Proof (PAN Card / Passport / Driving Licence / Voter's Card / Bank Passbook with photograph / Photo ID issued by Gazetted Officer) (PDF)
5. Educational Certificates - All mark sheets and certificates (Final or Provisional) of Class 10th, ITI, Graduation, Diploma (as applicable) (PDF)
6. Ex-Servicemen eligibility proof with declarations as per latest Government guidelines (if applicable) (PDF)
7. Valid Employment Exchange Registration Card
8. Valid Heavy Vehicle Driving Licence (for Sl. No. 1 - Operator HEMM post)
9. Valid Mining Foreman Certificate of Competency / Electrical Supervisor's Certificate of Competency / Mining Mate Certificate of Competency issued by DGMS (as applicable for the post applied for)
10. National Apprenticeship Certificate (NAC) with requisite endorsement
11. Latest SC / ST / Disability Certificate / OBC (NCL) Certificate / EWS - Income & Asset Certificate, issued on or after 01-04-2026, in prescribed format (PDF)
12. Experience Certificate (if applicable) in the format given at Annexure-IV, on letterhead of the Organisation, along with offer of appointment and last pay slip
13. Land Ousted candidates - all supporting documents including land documents, legal heir certificate, and affidavit in prescribed format (Annexure-III) on non-judicial stamp paper of Rs. 25/-
14. A candidate is allowed to apply for only one post - applications for multiple posts will result in summary rejection of all applications.
15. Only full-time qualifications from recognized institutes will be considered; ITI/Diploma/B.Sc. must be in the specific branch/trade prescribed for the post.
