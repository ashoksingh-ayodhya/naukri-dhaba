WCD Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 – Apply online for the latest Anganwadi Worker, Anganwadi Helper and Supervisor vacancies through the official WCD/ICDS portals such as wcd.nic.in and state ICDS portals. Candidates can check state-wise and district-wise Anganwadi vacancy lists, eligibility, qualifications, age limit, salary, and online application dates, and download the Anganwadi Application Form 2026. Start your career in Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) and stay updated with the latest Anganwadi job notifications from all major states including Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra, Bihar and more.

A total of **244** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduation (any stream) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official portal: Go to your state's official WCD / ICDS website or the direct link provided in the vacancy table above. For central information, visit wcd.nic.in.
2. Find the recruitment notification: Click on the "Recruitment" or "Anganwadi Recruitment 2026" section.
3. Download and read the notification: Download and read the official notification PDF carefully before applying.
4. Register / Login: Create a new account using your mobile number and Aadhaar. If already registered, log in with your credentials.
5. Fill the application form: Enter personal details, educational qualification, address, and select your preferred district/ward. Ensure you apply only for a ward where you are a permanent resident.
6. Upload documents: Upload scanned copies of your passport-size photograph, signature, 10th/12th marksheet, Aadhaar card, and any caste/domicile certificate if required.
7. Review and submit: Check all entries carefully before final submission. Download and print the application confirmation / acknowledgement slip for your records.
8. Aadhaar Card (Mandatory)
9. 10th Marksheet & Certificate
10. 12th Marksheet (for AWW)
11. Graduation Certificate (Supervisor)
12. Domicile / Residence Certificate
13. Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC)
14. Passport-size Photograph
15. Signature (scanned)
