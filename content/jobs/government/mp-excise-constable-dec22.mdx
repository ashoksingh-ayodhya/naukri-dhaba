Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board MPESB Which was earlier named Professional Examination Board PEB has released Excise Constable Recruitment 2022 Notification. Those Candidates Are Enrolled in this MP ESB Excise Constable (Aabkari Sipahi) 2022 Vacancy Can Download the Result. See the advertisement for information related to age limit, syllabus, institute wise post, selection procedure, pay scale in MP Abkari Constable Recruitment 2022. has released an official notification for Madhya Pradesh MPESB / PEB Excise Constable (Abkari Sipahi) Recruitment 2022 Result 2023 for 462 Post. 
A total of **462** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **29/12/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Recruitment of Madhya Pradesh PEB for the posts of Excise Constable (Karyapalik) Direct and Backlog post for Excise Department M.P. Recruitment Test – 2022 The applications of Recruitment will be online from 10-24 December 2022.
2. Along with Madhya Pradesh, candidates from other states can also apply (As per Excise Constable Recruitment 2022 Rules) .
3. To apply in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, the candidate has to first create a profile of himself. To create a profile, the candidate wants to have Aadhar card number and also want to have registered mobile number in it, only then there will be E-KYC in the profile, those whose mobile number is not registered, they will have to create a profile through biometric through MP Online Kiosk. .
4. For the application of recruitment in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, along with the profile, all the candidates will also have to register in the Madhya Pradesh Employment Office.
5. Candidates who have registered their profile and employment, now they have to apply which will run from 10/12/2022 to 24/12/2022.
6. For More Details Read the Full Notification.
