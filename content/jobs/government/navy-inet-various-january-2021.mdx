Join Indian Navy Nausena Bharti Are Recently Recently Released Admit Card for the INET Batch January 2021 Various Post of Technical, Education and Executive Batch. Those Candidate Are Enrolled with INET Vacancies Can Download the Admit Card

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Indian Navy INET Various Post Admit Card 2020. 
A total of **144** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **19/12/2019**.
Candidates must have First Class in M.Sc. (Maths/Operational Research) with Physics in B.Sc. OR First Class in M.Sc. (Physics/Nuclear Physics) with Maths in B.Sc. OR First Class in M.Sc. (Meteorology/Oceanology/ Atmospheric Science) OR BE / B.Tech Degree 60% Marks Computer Science/Information Technology/Computer Technology//Information Systems/Computer Engineering / Mechanical Engineering; For Post Eligibility Details Read Notification. as minimum educational qualification.
