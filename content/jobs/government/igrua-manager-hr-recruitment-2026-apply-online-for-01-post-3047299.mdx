Candidates up to 57 years of age who have MBA in HR/PM/IR, or PG Diploma in HR with relevant experience, or Defense background with HR experience are eligible to apply for these Manager-HR vacancies. The application process for IGRUA Manager-HR Recruitment 2026 is offline via email and will remain open until 13 May 2026.

The last date to apply is **13-05-2026**.
Candidates must have MBA with specialization in Personnel Management / Industrial Relations / Human Resource Management (PM/IR/HRM) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 13-05-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
