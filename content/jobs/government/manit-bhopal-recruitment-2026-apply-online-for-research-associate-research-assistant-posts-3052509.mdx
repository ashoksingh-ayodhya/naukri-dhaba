Candidates who have Post Graduation in Social Science or related subjects with a minimum of 55% marks, along with the prescribed research experience and desirable qualifications such as NET/M.Phil./Ph.D. for the respective posts, are eligible to apply for these Research Associate and Research Assistant vacancies. The application process for MANIT Bhopal Recruitment 2026 has started on 01 June 2026 and will remain open until 22 June 2026.

The last date to apply is **22-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Post Graduation in Social Science (or related subjects) with minimum 55% marks, NET/M.Phil/Ph.D. preferred, Minimum 2 years research experience (for Research Associate) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 22-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
