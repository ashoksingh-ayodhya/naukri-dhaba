Directorate of Medical Education and Training, Uttar Pradesh Online counseling / registration for admission in Uttar Pradesh UPNEET PG courses has started. Any candidate who wants to take admission in Uttar Pradesh through UPNEET can do online registration. To know more about counseling registration, choice filling, documents required, college list, allotment result, merit list and more, read the counseling guideline.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh UP NEET PG Admission 2022 Online Counseling, Registration, Merit List, Choice Filling & Allotment Result. 
The last date to apply is **28/09/2022 upto 02 PM Only.**.
Candidates must have OBC : 27 Percent; SC : 21 Percent; ST : 02 Percent; EWS : 10 Percent; UR / General : 40 Percent as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Online counseling of Uttar Pradesh NEET PG Admission 2022 will start from 26 September 2022 at 02 PM.
2. Those who want to get admission in the medical college of Uttar Pradesh Domicile Candidates and have passed NEET examination conducted by NBE, they can register Uttar Pradesh NEET in online counseling.
3. For More Details Must Read the UP NEET PG Counseling Notification.
