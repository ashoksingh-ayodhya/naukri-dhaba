Sainik School Jhansi has published the PGT Computer Science, TGT Social Science, Art Master, Lab Assistant Physics Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 4 positions across these posts. Candidates within the age range of 18 to 50 years (as per post-specific limits) and holding qualifications from 12th Pass to Post Graduate degrees are eligible to apply offline. The application process will be active until 26 June 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and application details below.

Candidates must have 12th-pass, Any Graduate, Post Graduate as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.sainikschooljhansi.com.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 26-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
