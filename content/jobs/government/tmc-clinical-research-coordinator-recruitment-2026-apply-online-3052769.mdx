The Tata Memorial Centre (TMC) has officially announced the Clinical Research Coordinator Recruitment 2026 with 1 opening under Advt. No. TMC/AD/97/2026. Candidates who are not more than 30 years of age and possess qualifications such as B.Sc. in Life Science/B.Pharm with PGDCR, M.Sc. Clinical Research, or M.Sc. in Life Science/M.Pharm with PGDCR/M.Sc. Clinical Research are eligible to apply. Interested candidates can submit their online applications on or before 12 June 2026. Selected candidates will receive a salary ranging from ₹29,000 to ₹34,000 per month on a contractual basis. Direct links to download the official notification and apply online are provided below.

The last date to apply is **12-06-2026**.
Candidates must have BSc in Life Science/B.Pharm with Post Graduate Diploma in Clinical Research(PGDCR) or BSc in Life Science/B.Pharm with MSc Clinical Research or MSc in Life Science/M.Pharm with PGDCR or MSc in Life Science/M.Pharm with MSc in Clinical Research as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 12-06-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of TMC to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
