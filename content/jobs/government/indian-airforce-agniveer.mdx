Indian Air Force has released the detailed notification for the Agniveer recruitment under Agneepath scheme. Any youth whose age is between 18 to 23 years and come from any state / city in India can apply in this Indian Airforce Agniveer Recruitment 2022. For more details read the full notification and apply only after that.

The last date to apply is **05/07/2022 upto 05 PM**.

## How to Apply

1. Indian Airforce Are Released the Notification for Agniveer Vayu Intake 01/2022 Recruitment 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 24/06/2022 to 05/07/2022.
2. Photo Instruction : Passport size recent colour photograph (taken not before April 2022) of size 10 KB to 50 KB (front portrait in light background without head gear except for Sikhs). The photograph is to be taken with candidate holding a black slate in front of his chest with his name and date of photograph taken, clearly written on it with white chalk in capital letter.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Agniveer Vayu Intake 01/2022 Online Form 2022.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
