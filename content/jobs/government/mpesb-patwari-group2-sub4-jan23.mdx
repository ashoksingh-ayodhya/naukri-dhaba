A total of **9073** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **23/01/2023**.

## How to Apply

1. Recruitment of Madhya Pradesh PEB for the posts of Group-2 (Sub Group -4) Sahayak Samparikshak and other equivalent Post Combined 2022 and Patwari & other post Combined Recruitment Test – 2022 The applications of Recruitment will be online from 05-23 January 2023.
2. Along with Madhya Pradesh, candidates from other states can also apply (As per Patwari & Other Group 2 Sub Group 4 Recruitment 2022-2023 Rules) .
3. To apply in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, the candidate has to first create a profile of himself. To create a profile, the candidate wants to have Aadhar card number and also want to have registered mobile number in it, only then there will be E-KYC in the profile, those whose mobile number is not registered, they will have to create a profile through biometric through MP Online Kiosk. .
4. For the application of recruitment in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, along with the profile, all the candidates will also have to register in the Madhya Pradesh Employment Office.
5. Candidates who have registered their profile and employment, now they have to apply which will run from 05/01/2023 to 23/01/2023.
6. For More Details Read the Full Notification.
