Maharashtra State Electricity Transmission Company (MAHATRANSCO) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 50 Apprentice Electrician Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website. The online application process starts from 11-03-2026 and will remain open until 20-03-2026, so applicants are advised to submit their forms well before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues.

The last date to apply is **20-03-2026**.
Candidates must have Maharashtra State SSC Passed, NCVT Approved ITI Electrician Passed as minimum educational qualification.
