District Health Society Ramanathapuram (DHS Ramanathapuram) has released the official ICTC Counsellors and ICTC Lab Technician Notification 2026 on 02 June 2026. A total of 8 vacancies have been announced under the DHS Ramanathapuram Recruitment 2026, including 4 posts for ICTC Counsellors and 4 posts for ICTC Lab Technicians. Eligible candidates up to 35 years of age with a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology, Sociology, or Social Work for Counsellor posts, and CMLT/DMLT from Government-approved institutions for Lab Technician posts, can apply through offline mode. The application process must be completed before 16 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application instructions are available below.

The last date to apply is **16-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Bachelor Degree in Psychology, Sociology, Social Work, CMLT, DMLT in Government approved institutions. as minimum educational qualification.
