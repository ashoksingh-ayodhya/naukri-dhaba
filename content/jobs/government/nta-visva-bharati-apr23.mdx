National Testing Agency NTA & Visva Bharati has released the LDC, Deo, Assistant, MTS, JE, AE & Other Post Non Teaching Post recruitment advertisement for 709 posts. Those Candidate Are Enrolled with Vacancy Can Check the Exam Admit Card. Read the notification for recruitment eligibility, post information, selection procedure, age limit, pay scale and all other information.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for NTA Visva Bharati Non Teaching Recruitment 2023 Result, MTS CBT Exam Admit Card for 709 Post. 
A total of **709** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **16/05/2023**.

## How to Apply

1. National Testing Agency NTA Conducted Visva Bharati MTS, LDC, DEO & Other Various Post Recruitment Examination 2023 Online Form Candidate Apply Between 17 April 2023 to 16 May 2023.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in NTA Visva Bharti Non Teaching Latest Career Jobs.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Allahabad High Court Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
