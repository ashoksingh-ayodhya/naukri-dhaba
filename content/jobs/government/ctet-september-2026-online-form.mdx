Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has released an official notification for Central Teacher Eligibility Test CTET September 2026 Apply Online Form. 
The last date to apply is **10/06/2026**.

## How to Apply

1. Central Board of Secondary Education CBSE Are Released the Central Teacher Eligibility Test CTET Paper I to V & VI to VIII Exam September 2026. Candidate Can Apply Online 11/05/2026 to 10/06/2026.
2. Note: This year the exam centers are limited in every city, the information of which will be seen live by the candidate while filling the form. All the candidates who want their nearest exam center / city should apply as soon as possible because the slots of the exam city are limited.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Teacher Eligibility Test Application Form in Central TET CTET Exam Latest Online Form for September 2026.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
