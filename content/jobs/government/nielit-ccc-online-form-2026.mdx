National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology NIELIT</span></a> is a basic IT literacy program by the National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology (Government of India) to teach essential computer skills like document preparation, internet browsing, emailing, presentations, and digital services. Duration is 80 hours (30 theory + 60 practicals). The online exam is held every month, with registration typically starting on the 1st of the preceding month and the last date being the last day of every month. Read the notification for CCC Course eligibility, post information, selection procedure, Practice Test, pay scale and all other information.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for NIELIT CCC Exam 2026 Apply Online Form for Course on Computer Concepts CCC. 

## How to Apply

1. Candidate Can Click on the Apply Online Form
2. Fill the All Basic Details Name, Father Name, Date of Birth and Aadhar Card, Contact Details
3. Upload Candidate Passport Size Photo, Signature and Thumb Impression
4. Take A Print Out of Application Form
5. Next Month First Week You have Download Admit Card to Enter Your Application No. and DOB
