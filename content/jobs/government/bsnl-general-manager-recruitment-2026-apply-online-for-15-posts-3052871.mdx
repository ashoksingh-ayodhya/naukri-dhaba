The Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has officially announced the General Manager in Finance & Accounts Recruitment 2026 with 15 openings. The detailed notification was published on the official BSNL website. Candidates who meet the age criteria of up to 50 years and hold a Chartered Accountant (CA), Cost & Management Accountant (CMA), MBA (Finance), or PG Diploma in Management (Finance) qualification can now submit their online applications. The application process will remain open until 24-06-2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

The last date to apply is **24-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Chartered Accountant (CA), Cost & Management Accountant (CMA), MBA (Finance), or PG Diploma in Management (Finance) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 24-06-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of BSNL to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
