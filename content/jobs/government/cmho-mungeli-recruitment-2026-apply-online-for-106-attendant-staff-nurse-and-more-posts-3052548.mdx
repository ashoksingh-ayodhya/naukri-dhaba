District Health Society, National Health Mission, Mungeli (Chhattisgarh) has published the NHM Mungeli Recruitment Notification 2026 on 01 June 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 106 contractual positions including Laboratory Technician, Physiotherapist, Pharmacist, Staff Nurse, Nursing Officer, Community Health Officer, Audiologist, Counsellor, Radiographer, RMA, and various other posts under NHM programs. Candidates with qualifications such as 8th Pass, 10th Pass, 12th Pass, Diploma, BSc, MSc, and Graduation are eligible to apply online at mungelinhms.indicompute.com. The application process will be active from 02 June 2026 to 20 June 2026 (up to 5:00 PM), and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

A total of **106** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 65% weightage (for most posts) / 90% weightage (for Class-IV posts) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. 8th standard marksheet (for Post Nos. 5 and 6)
2. 10th standard marksheet / Date of Birth proof
3. 12th / Graduation / Post-Graduation / all required educational qualification marksheets for all years
4. Computer Diploma/Degree marksheet from a recognized institution
5. CG Council Registration Certificate (wherever applicable)
6. Original Domicile Certificate issued by a competent authority (Chhattisgarh residents only)
7. Permanent Caste Certificate issued by a competent authority (for SC/ST/OBC candidates)
8. PwD Certificate from a Medical Board with minimum 40% disability (if applicable)
9. Identity proof (Aadhaar Card/Voter ID/PAN Card/Driving Licence with name, address and photo)
10. Experience Certificate (from Government/Semi-Government/Government-funded institutions only)
11. NOC/No Objection Certificate from current employer (for candidates working in Government/Semi-Government organizations)
12. Live employment registration certificate from Employment Office
13. Last 6 months' salary slips (mandatory for experience marks)
14. Appointment letter for claimed work experience (mandatory)
15. Online applications only - no other mode of application will be accepted; applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected without prior notice.
