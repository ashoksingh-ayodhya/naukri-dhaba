The latest C-DAC Recruitment 2026 has been announced for various contractual positions under the JIT (Just-In-Time) June 2026 Cycle! Published on 01-06-2026 at the official C-DAC careers portal, this massive pan-India recruitment drive covers multiple centres including Bengaluru, Chennai, Noida, Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Delhi, Mohali, Kolkata, Thiruvananthapuram, and the North East (Guwahati/CINE). Eligible candidates with BE/B.Tech, ME/M.Tech, MCA, Ph.D., or relevant graduation/post-graduation in ICT&E domains can apply online from 01-06-2026 to 20-06-2026. Find all details about C-DAC JIT June 2026 Recruitment including eligibility, vacancies, salary, and notification PDFs here.

Candidates must have BE/B.Tech or equivalent degree (min 60% or equivalent CGPA for most posts; 50% for Project Associate at some centres) OR PG in Science/Computer Application/relevant domain with requisite percentage OR ME/M.Tech OR Ph.D. in relevant discipline – from AICTE/UGC-approved institution or recognized university. For support/admin posts: Graduation with 50% + relevant experience. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Passport-size photograph in .jpg format (not more than 400 KB)
2. Resume / CV in PDF format (not more than 500 KB)
3. All educational mark sheets and degree certificates from Class 10 onwards
4. CGPA-to-percentage conversion certificate issued by the university (if applicable)
5. Experience certificates from all previous and current employers clearly indicating period of employment, salary drawn, and post held
6. Caste/category certificate in prescribed Government of India format (SC/ST/OBC Non-Creamy Layer/EWS) where applicable
7. Disability certificate in prescribed format issued by the District Medical Board (for PwBD candidates)
8. SSLC/SSC/ISC mark sheet or other valid document as proof of date of birth
9. No Objection Certificate (NOC) from current employer (if not forwarded through proper channel)
10. Any other document evidencing eligibility as declared in the application
11. These are CONTRACTUAL positions - no right to regular or permanent employment in C-DAC is created by engagement under this recruitment.
12. Candidates may be deputed or transferred from one project to another at any C-DAC project location across India based on organizational requirement.
13. The contract is initially for up to 3 years (1 year for Kolkata centre) or project completion, whichever is earlier. Extension is possible based on performance and project requirement.
14. All correspondence including interview call letters will be sent by email only - candidates must keep their registered email ID and mobile number active throughout the process.
15. C-DAC reserves the right to increase/decrease the number of posts, change eligibility criteria, cancel any or all positions, or modify/cancel the selection process at any stage without notice.
