HSSC TGT Teacher recruitment 2023 see the notification for eligibility, age limit, selection procedure, pay scale, subject wise posts and other information. has released an official notification for Haryana HSSC TGT Teacher Recruitment 2023 Exam Final Result 2024 for 7471 Post. 
A total of **7471** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **20/03/2023**.
Candidates must have Bachelor / Diploma in Physical Education B.P.Ed / D.P.Ed.; HTET / STET Certificate in Related Subject.; Hindi / Sanskrit Subject in 10+2 / BA / MA Level. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Haryana Staff Selection Commission HSSC Recruitment 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 23/02/2023 to 20/03/2023.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in HSSC Trained Graduate Teacher TGT Exam 2023.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
