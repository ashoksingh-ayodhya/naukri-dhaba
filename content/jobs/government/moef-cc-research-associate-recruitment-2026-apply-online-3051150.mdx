Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEF&CC) has released the official Research Associate Recruitment 2026 notification for 4 vacancies under Advt. No. F. No. 17/56/2022-FPD. Candidates holding a Master’s Degree in Chemistry or B.E./B.Tech in Chemical Engineering with a minimum of 3 years of relevant experience are eligible to apply. The recruitment is being conducted on a contractual basis with a monthly salary of ₹40,000. Interested candidates can submit their applications through email mode on or before 12 June 2026.

The last date to apply is **12-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Master’s Degree in Chemistry OR B.E./B.Tech in Chemical Engineering, Minimum three (03) years relevant experience as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 12-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
