Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC) has officially announced the Driver Recruitment 2026 with 4599 openings. The detailed notification was published on 06/06/2026 at https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in and https://gsrtc.in. Candidates who meet the age criteria and hold a valid Heavy Vehicle Driving Licence with PSV Badge and minimum 4 years of experience in driving heavy vehicles including diesel buses can now submit their online applications from 06/06/2026 to 05/07/2026 (up to 23:59 hours). Direct links to download the notification and apply online are shared below.

Department has released an official notification for Gujarat GSRTC Driver Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 4599 Posts. 
A total of **459** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Standard 12 Pass (10+2). Diploma after Std. 10 (10+3 or more years) is also accepted. ITI (2-year course after Std. 10) with NCVT exam passed and English paper from GSHSEB / Gujarat Open School is also equivalent. Polytechnic 3-year Diploma after Std. 10 is also equivalent. Equivalence certificate from Education Board must be produced. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Scanned passport-size photograph (JPG, max 10 KB, 5 cm H x 3.6 cm W).
2. Scanned signature (JPG, max 10 KB, 2.5 cm H x 7.5 cm W).
3. SSC (Std. 10) Board certificate as proof of date of birth (Birth certificate / extract not acceptable).
4. Std. 12 Mark Sheet / Leaving Certificate or equivalent qualification certificate.
5. Computer Knowledge certificate / marksheet as per Gujarat Government resolution Dt. 13/08/2008.
6. Valid Smart Card Driving Licence (HGV/HPV/PSV) - manual/book-type not accepted at document verification.
7. PSV Badge (mandatory at the time of online application).
8. Experience certificate from transporter / company / travels with owner’s signature and seal (minimum 4 years; for the exact same period as mentioned in the application form).
9. Caste certificate for SC/ST/SEBC/EWS from competent authority (issued in applicant’s name along with father’s/mother’s name).
10. SEBC Non-Creamy Layer Certificate (Parishisht-4 in Gujarati or Annexure-A in English) issued between 01/04/2024 and 05/07/2026.
11. EWS Income and Asset Certificate (Annexure-KH in English or Parishisht-Ga in Gujarati) issued between 06/07/2023 and 05/07/2026.
12. Discharge Book and Identity Card / Certificate for Ex-Serviceman candidates.
13. Marriage Certificate / Notarized Affidavit / Gazette copy (for married female candidates who have shown husband’s name in place of father’s name).
14. Sports Certificate (if claiming 5% bonus marks) - from competent authority in prescribed format.
15. Widow Certificate, husband’s death certificate, and affidavit of no re-marriage (for widow candidates claiming 5% bonus marks).
