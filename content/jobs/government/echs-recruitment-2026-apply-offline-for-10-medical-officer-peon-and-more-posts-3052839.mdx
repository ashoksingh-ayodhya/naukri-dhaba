Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme (ECHS) Medical, Para Medical & Non-Medical Staff Notification 2026 has been released on the official website. A total of 10 vacancies have been announced through the ECHS Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have MD/MS in specialty concerned/ DNB, MBBS, BDS, BSc Nursing/ GNM diploma/ Class I Nursing Assistant course (Armed Forces), Literate, Class 8 or GD trade for Armed Forces as minimum educational qualification.

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: echs.gov.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
