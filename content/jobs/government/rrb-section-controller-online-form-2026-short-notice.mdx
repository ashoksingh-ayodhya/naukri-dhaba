Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) under the Ministry of Railways have released the Centralised Employment Notification (CEN) No. 03/2026 for the recruitment of Section Controller posts. This recruitment offers a great opportunity for graduates seeking a prestigious government job in Indian Railways. A total of 119 vacancies have been announced across various RRB zones. Eligible candidates can apply online through the official RRB portal before the last date. The selection will be conducted through Computer Based Test (CBT), followed by other stages as per the official notification.

A total of **119** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduate Degree in any discipline as minimum educational qualification.
