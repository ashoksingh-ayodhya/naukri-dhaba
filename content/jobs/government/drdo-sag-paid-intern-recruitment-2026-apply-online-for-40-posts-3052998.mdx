The Scientific Analysis Group (DRDO SAG) has officially announced the Paid Intern Recruitment 2026 with 40 openings. The detailed notification was published on 06 June 2026 at the official DRDO SAG website. Candidates who meet the age criteria as specified in the official notification and hold B.E./B.Tech (IV Year) full-time, M.E./M.Tech/M.Sc (II Year) full-time qualifications can now submit their applications via email from now until 15 June 2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply are shared below.

The last date to apply is **15-06-2026**.
Candidates must have B.E./B.Tech (IV Year) full time, M.E./M.Tech/M.Sc (II Year) full time as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 15-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
