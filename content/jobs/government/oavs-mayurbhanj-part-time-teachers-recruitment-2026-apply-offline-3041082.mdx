Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya Sangathan (OAVS Mayurbhanj) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of Part-Time Teachers Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for education jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can download the application form from the official website and submit it offline.

Candidates must have PGT: Integrated post-graduate course or Master degree with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PH/SEBC) in the concerned subject, B.Ed. (a course prescribed by NCTE), proficiency in teaching Odia & English. TGT: Bachelor degree in Arts & Science with at least 50% marks (45% for SC/ST/PH/SEBC) in the concerned subjects along with degree in bachelor in education/ 3 years integrated B.A. B.Ed (a course prescribed by NCTE with 50% in aggregate (45% for SC/ST/PH/SEBC candidates) from an institution recognised by NCTE and affiliated to a recognised university), proficiency in teaching Odia & English. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: mayurbhanj.odisha.gov.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 30-03-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
