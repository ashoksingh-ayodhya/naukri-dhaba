The latest Tamilnad Mercantile Bank (TMB) Recruitment 2026 has been officially announced for Agricultural Officer positions in the Assistant Manager cadre. Released in May 2026 on the bank's career portal, this prestigious recruitment drive by one of South India's leading old-generation private sector banks offers excellent career opportunities for agriculture graduates with relevant banking experience. The online application window is open from 22 May 2026 to 06 June 2026. This is a regular employment opportunity with posting anywhere in India. Find complete details about TMB Agricultural Officer Vacancy 2026, eligibility criteria, selection process, roles & responsibilities, salary, application procedure, and official notification PDF below.

Candidates must have Graduation / Degree in Agriculture or allied subjects from a recognized University with minimum 50% aggregate marks. Preference will be given to candidates having specializations in Horticulture, Animal Husbandry, Veterinary Science, Dairy Science, or Agricultural Engineering. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official career portal: www.ib.tmbonline.bank.in/tmb_careers/
2. Click on the link for “Recruitment Advertisement – Agricultural Officer” and download the detailed advertisement.
3. Ensure you meet all eligibility criteria before proceeding.
4. Click on “Apply Online” and register using a valid email ID and mobile number.
5. After registration, you will receive an activation link on email and credentials on mobile.
6. Login using the Registration Number / Username and Password.
7. Fill all personal, educational, and experience details carefully.
8. Upload scanned copies of required documents within the prescribed size limits.
9. Verify the preview of the application and submit.
10. Take a printout of the final submitted application for future reference.
11. Recent passport-size photograph (390×520 pixels, max 120 KB)
12. Signature (240×240 pixels, max 60 KB)
13. SSLC / Higher Secondary Mark Sheet for age proof
14. All Degree Certificates and Mark Sheets
15. Experience Certificates from previous employers
