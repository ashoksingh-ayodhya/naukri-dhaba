The Indian Premier League, popularly known as IPL is a professional cricket league in India. The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007 when the Indian cricket team won the World T20. The IPL is the most attended cricket league in the world and ranks sixth among all sports leagues. The first season of the IPL was held in 2008, with eight teams competing for the title. Since then, the IPL has undergone several changes, including an expansion to 10 teams in 2011. The IPL winners list covers all the winners and runners-up from the very first season to the latest IPL season, i.e., IPL 2022. Find out who won the title in each season and which team made it to the finals. In 2022, Gujarat Titans bagged the IPL winner title against Rajasthan Royals at Narendra Modi stadium, Ahmedabad.
