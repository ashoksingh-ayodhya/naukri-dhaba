National Health Mission Chandigarh (NHM Chandigarh) Medical Specialists Notification 2026 has been released on the official website of NHM Chandigarh under Advertisement No. NHM-UT/06/2026-27. A total of 6 vacancies have been announced through the NHM Chandigarh Medical Specialists Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have MBBS Degree with MD/DNB/DCH/DGO/Diploma in respective specialty from recognized University, Registration with State Medical Council or Medical Council of India-New Delhi as minimum educational qualification.
