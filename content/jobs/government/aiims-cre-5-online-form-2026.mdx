## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website: www.aiimsexams.ac.in
2. Register through the OTR (One-Time Registration) portal if you have not already done so.
3. Log in and select the CRE-5 application link.
4. Fill in all details carefully - name, date of birth, category, qualification, experience, and NOC details if applicable.
5. Upload photographs, signature, and left thumb impression in the format specified under "Upload Image Instructions."
6. Select your examination centre preference (state/UT).
7. Pay the applicable examination fee via Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
8. Submit the application and download the registration slip for your records.
9. If you are working in a Government/PSU/Autonomous Body, upload the No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your current employer by 6 July 2026 - failing to do so will prevent you from appearing in the examination.
10. Check your application status on 9 July 2026 at www.aiimsexams.ac.in.
11. Downloaded Registration Slip and Admit Card
12. Date of Birth certificate (Matriculation certificate or equivalent)
13. Category certificate (SC/ST/OBC/EWS) - valid and current; OBC NCL certificate must be valid for Financial Year 2026-27 (issued on or after 01 April 2026)
14. EWS certificate issued between 01 April 2026 and 30 June 2026
15. Disability certificate (for PwBD candidates, with minimum 40% benchmark disability)
