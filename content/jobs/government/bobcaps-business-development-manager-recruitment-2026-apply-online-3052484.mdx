BOB Capital Markets Ltd. (BOBCAPS), a subsidiary of Bank of Baroda, has released an opening for the role of Business Development Manager (Off Roll) on a full-time, on-site basis. The selected candidate will be responsible for promoting and facilitating the opening of Demat & Trading (2-in-1) accounts, new client acquisition, maintaining client and bank relationships, and meeting sales targets.

Candidates must have Minimum Graduation OR 12th Pass from a recognised Board/University. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Prepare an updated resume/CV highlighting educational qualifications, Capital Market experience, experience in Demat & Trading account opening, broking product sales experience, and key skills.
2. Compose an email to [email protected].
3. Mention "Application for the post of Business Development Manager (Off Roll)" in the subject line of the email. Applications with any other subject line will not be accepted.
4. Attach the resume/CV and any relevant supporting documents.
5. Send the email at the earliest as no specific last date has been mentioned.
6. This is an Off Roll, full-time, on-site position. Selected candidates will not be on the regular payroll of BOB Capital Markets Ltd.
7. Only candidates with minimum 6 months of experience in the Capital Market sector should apply. Candidates without the requisite experience need not apply.
8. Applications must be submitted via email at [email protected] only. Applications through any other mode will not be accepted.
9. The subject line of the email must read exactly: "Application for the post of Business Development Manager (Off Roll)". Applications with any other subject line will not be accepted.
10. Candidates with experience in opening Demat & Trading accounts and selling broking products will be given preference during shortlisting.
11. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for further stages of the selection process. No separate communication will be sent to non-shortlisted candidates.
12. Candidates are advised to apply at the earliest as no specific last date has been mentioned in the notification.
