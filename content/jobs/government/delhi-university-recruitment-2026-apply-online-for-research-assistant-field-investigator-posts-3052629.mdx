Delhi University (DU) has officially released the Research Assistant & Field Investigator Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. 100/2024-25/ICSSR/RP/MJ/OBC) for 2 vacancies at Delhi University, New Delhi. Candidates holding an M.A. in Linguistics, Applied Linguistics, or allied Social Science disciplines with a minimum of 55% marks are eligible to apply. Additional NET/M.Phil./Ph.D. qualifications are required for the Research Assistant post. Interested candidates can submit their applications via email on or before 15 June 2026. Selected candidates will be appointed on a project-based basis with a consolidated monthly remuneration of ₹37,000 for Research Assistant and ₹20,000 for Field Investigator.
