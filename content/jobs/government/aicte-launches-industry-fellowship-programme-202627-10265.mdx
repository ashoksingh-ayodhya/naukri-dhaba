The All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) officially launched the AICTE Industry Fellowship Programme (IFP) 2026–27 on May 26, 2026 - a one-year structured fellowship that embeds college faculty members inside real industries. The programme was unveiled by AICTE Chairman Prof. Yogesh Singh, alongside Member Secretary Prof. Shyama Rath, in New Delhi.


## How to Apply

1. First, get a No Objection Certificate (NOC) from your Principal or Director. Without this, your application will not be accepted.
2. Open the official AICTE Faculty Registration Portal.
3. Register using your email ID and mobile number, then verify your account via email.
4. Fill in the application form completely.
5. Upload all required certificates and documents.
6. Check all details carefully and submit the form.
7. After submission, save your application number safely.
