The IndianOil Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is a simultaneous notification issued by four IOCL refinery units on 29 June 2026 under the Apprentices Act, 1961/1973. The following table provides a unit-wise overview of the IOCL Refinery Apprentice Vacancy 2026.

A total of **582** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 30 June 2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit www.iocl.com and go to the 'What's New' section.
2. Click on the relevant refinery unit's apprentice engagement notification link.
3. Read the detailed advertisement carefully.
4. Ensure your profile is registered and 100% correctly updated on the relevant NAPS or NATS portal.
5. Log in to the NAPS/NATS portal using your User ID/Email ID and apply for the apprenticeship opportunity under the applicable refinery's Establishment ID.
6. After successfully applying on NAPS/NATS, go back to www.iocl.com and click on the Microsoft Forms link for your refinery unit.
7. Submit the additional information form with the same Enrolment No./Student ID/Registration No. used in Step I.
8. Ensure all details in both steps are identical and correct.
9. Completing only Step I or only Step II: Both NAPS/NATS portal application and Microsoft Forms submission are mandatory. Completing only one will make the application invalid.
10. Mismatched details across Step I and Step II: The Enrolment No., email ID, and mobile number used in NAPS/NATS must exactly match those submitted in the Microsoft Forms. Any mismatch leads to rejection.
11. Applying for more than one trade/discipline: The notification explicitly states that candidates applying for more than one trade/discipline will be summarily rejected.
12. Applying with qualifications acquired through distance/correspondence/part-time mode: Only regular full-time courses are eligible. Distance education qualifications are not accepted.
13. Candidates with higher qualifications (BE/B.Tech/MBA/CA etc.) applying: Those possessing or having completed higher professional qualifications before joining are not eligible and will be rejected at document verification.
14. Not converting CGPA to percentage before applying: Candidates with CGPA/Grade-based marksheets must mention the equivalent percentage as per their institute's norms and carry a conversion certificate to document verification.
15. Waiting until the last minute: The portal closes at 17:00 hrs on 28 July 2026. Technical issues near the deadline are common - apply well in advance.
