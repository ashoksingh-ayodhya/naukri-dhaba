The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released Centralised Employment Notification (CEN) No. 03/2026 inviting online applications for 119 vacancies of Section Controller across various Zonal Railways of Indian Railways. Eligible graduate candidates aged 20 to 33 years can apply online from 15 July 2026 to 14 August 2026.

Graduate Degree from a recognized University or its equivalent has released an official notification for RRB Section Controller Recruitment 2026 (CEN 03/2026): 119 Vacancies, Apply Online from 15 July. 
A total of **119** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduate Degree from a recognized University or its equivalent as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website of the chosen RRB (list provided below)
2. Click on the CEN 03/2026 application link and complete "Create an Account" (details cannot be modified later, including mobile number and email ID)
3. Authenticate identity using DigiLocker or Aadhaar during account creation (recommended)
4. Log in and fill out the online application form with personal, educational, and category details
5. Capture a live photograph through webcam or mobile camera as per specifications (pre-existing photos are not accepted)
6. Upload scanned signature (JPG/JPEG, 30–49 KB) and SC/ST certificate if applicable (PDF, under 400 KB)
7. Select RRB and Railway Zone preference (final and cannot be changed later)
8. Pay the applicable examination fee online
9. Submit the application and save the confirmation for future reference
10. Candidates awaiting final results of the prescribed educational qualification are not eligible to apply
11. Application modification (except "Create an Account" details and chosen RRB) is allowed from 17.08.2026 to 26.08.2026 on payment of Rs. 250 modification fee
12. Center/city allocation for CBT/CBAT depends on technical and logistical feasibility; requests for change of exam centre are not entertained
13. Banned items in the exam hall include mobile phones, watches, calculators, pens, bags, and other electronic devices
14. Free Sleeper Class Railway Pass is admissible only to SC/ST candidates on request
15. Candidates should regularly check the official RRB website for CBT/CBAT dates and e-call letter downloads
