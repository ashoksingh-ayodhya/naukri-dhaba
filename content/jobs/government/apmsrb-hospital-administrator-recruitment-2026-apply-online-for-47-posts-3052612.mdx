Andhra Pradesh Medical Services Recruitment Board (APMSRB) Hospital Administrator Notification 2026 has been released on 01 June 2026 on the official website of APMSRB. A total of 47 vacancies have been announced through the APMSRB Hospital Administrator Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have MBBS / MDS, Masters Degree in Hospital Administration or Hospital Management or its equivalent, At least 2 years post-qualification experience in Hospital Administration, At least 1 year work experience in Administration or Leadership or Managerial roles, Computer Literacy in MS Word, Excel, Power point & Web surfing, Fluency in Telugu and English, Knowledge of Govt. Legislations and Hospital administration, Good Communication and presentation skills as minimum educational qualification.
