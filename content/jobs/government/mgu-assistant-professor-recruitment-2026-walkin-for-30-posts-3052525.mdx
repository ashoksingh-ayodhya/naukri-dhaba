Mahatma Gandhi University (MGU) has published the Assistant Professor on Contract Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 30 Assistant Professor positions on a contract basis. Candidates up to 70 years of age for retired teachers, and holding qualifications as per UGC norms, are eligible to attend the walk-in process. Interested applicants can participate in the walk-in from the dates mentioned in the official notification, with direct links to download the PDF and details provided below.

Candidates must have As per UGC norms as minimum educational qualification.
