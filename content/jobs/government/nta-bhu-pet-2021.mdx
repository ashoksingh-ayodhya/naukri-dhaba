National Testing Agency NTA & Banaras Hindu Universit BHU Are Recently Recently Uploaded Entrance Exam Result for the Admission for BHU Post Graduate PET Various Courses Admission 2021. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Admission Test Can Download the Test Result.

Banaras Hindu University BHU PET 2021 Short Details of Notification has released an official notification for NTA BHU Post Graduate PET Entrance Exam Result 2021. 

## How to Apply

1. Banaras Hindu University BHU, Varanasi Admission 2021 for the PET MA, M.Com, MBA, M.Sc, M.Ed and Other PET Various Courses. Candidate Can Apply Between 14/08/2021 to 05/09/2021
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in BHU PET 2021 .
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
