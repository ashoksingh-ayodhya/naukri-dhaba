Railway Recruitment Board, Bhubaneswar is going to issue an online RRB Bhubaneswar ALP Admit card 2018 for all the candidates who have submitted application for RRB Bhubaneswar ALP posts. If you have applied in response to notification issued by RRB Bhubaneswar ALP and have paid the examination fee then you will be eligible to download your RRB Bhubaneswar ALP Admit card 2018.

Railway Recruitment Board Bhubaneswar has released an official notification for RRB ALP Bhubaneswar Admit card 2018, RRB ALP Bhubaneswar Admit Card. 
Candidates must have 10TH (Matriculation), 12th, SSC, ITI as minimum educational qualification.
