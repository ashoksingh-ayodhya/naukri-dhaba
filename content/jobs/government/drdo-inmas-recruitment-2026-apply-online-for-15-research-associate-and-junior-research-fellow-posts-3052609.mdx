The Institute of Nuclear Medicine and Allied Sciences (INMAS), a specialized laboratory under the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), Timarpur, Delhi has released an official advertisement for Research Associate (RA) (04 Posts) and Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) (11 Posts).

A total of **15** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Download the prescribed application form (Annexure 'A') from the official DRDO website: www.drdo.gov.in.
2. Fill out the application form completely (Typed, not Handwritten) and attach supporting documents - educational qualifications, matriculation certificate (for date of birth), GATE/NET/GPAT score card, and caste certificate (if applicable).
3. Scan all documents into a single combined PDF file.
4. Name the PDF precisely using this format: POST CODE_YOUR NAME (e.g., RA-01_James).
5. Send the email with all documents to [email protected] before June 30, 2026.
