The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) has released Advertisement No. 734 / Notification No. 04/2026 inviting online applications for the Combined Technical Services Examination (Non-Interview Posts). A total of 461 vacancies across 45 posts in various Government Departments and Public Sector Organisations are being filled through this recruitment.

A total of **461** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 01.07.2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official TNPSC website: www.tnpscexams.in.
2. Complete One Time Registration (OTR) if not already registered, then log in to fill the online application.
3. Select the subject paper(s) for which you are eligible and specify them clearly in the application.
4. Upload scanned copies of all required documents including photo, signature, educational certificates, eye fitness certificate (where applicable), and Tamil knowledge proof.
5. Pay the examination fee of ₹100/- per subject paper online via Net Banking / Credit Card / Debit Card / UPI.
6. Submit the application before 25.06.2026 at 11:59 PM. Use the correction window from 29.06.2026 to 01.07.2026 for any edits if needed.
