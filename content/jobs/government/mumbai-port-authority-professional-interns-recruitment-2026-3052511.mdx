Candidates must have 01 June 2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official MbPA website at www.mumbaiport.gov.in and go to 'People & Career / Jobs / Advertisements' menu.
2. Download the Application Format (Annexure I) from the website.
3. Fill the application format with the required details and declaration.
4. Attach self-attested copies of documents/certificates relating to Age, Qualification and Experience along with the application.
5. Candidates serving in Government/Semi Government/PSUs/Autonomous Bodies must attach a "No Objection Certificate" from their employer.
6. Super-scribe the envelope as "Application for engagement of Professional Associates (Name of the Post) or Professional Assistants (Name of the Post) on Contract Basis".
7. Send the filled application to: Chief Medical Officer, Medical Department, MbPA Hospital, Nadkarni Park, Wadala (East), Mumbai – 400037 before 29 June 2026.
8. Separate applications must be submitted for each post if applying for more than one position.
