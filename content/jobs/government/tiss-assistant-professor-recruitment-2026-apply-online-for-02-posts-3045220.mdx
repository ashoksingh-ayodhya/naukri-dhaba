The Tata Institute of Social Sciences has released the TISS Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026 notification for 2 contractual Assistant Professor posts under Advt No. ADVT/TISS/AP/SMCS/APRIL/2026. This is a great opportunity for candidates in Humanities, Social Sciences, Media, Film Making, Design, and allied fields looking for teaching and academic careers in a reputed institution.

The last date to apply is **30-04-2026**.
Candidates must have Master's degree with 55% marks in Humanities and Social Sciences or Media and Cultural Studies as minimum educational qualification.

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 30-04-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of TISS to complete the application.
