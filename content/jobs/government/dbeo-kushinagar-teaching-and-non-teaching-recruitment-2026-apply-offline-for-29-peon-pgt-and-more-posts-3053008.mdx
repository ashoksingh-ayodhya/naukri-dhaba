District Basic Education Office Kushinagar has released the official Various Posts Notification 2026 at the official website. A total of 29 vacancies have been announced under the DBEO Kushinagar Various Posts Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 25 and 45 years with qualifications ranging from 8th Pass to Post Graduate along with B.Ed, D.El.Ed, TET, and other prescribed qualifications can apply offline on or before 20 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application form are available below.

The last date to apply is **20-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Various qualifications from 8th pass to Post Graduate with B.Ed/D.El.Ed/TET etc. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: http://kushinagar.nic.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 20-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
