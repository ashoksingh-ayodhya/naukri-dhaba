CSIR-National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (CSIR-NEERI) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 4 Project Associate-I Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website.The last date to apply online is 13-03-2026, so applicants are advised to submit their forms well before the deadline to avoid last-minute issues.

Candidates must have M.Sc. Chemistry, M.Sc. Environmental Science, M.Sc. Biotechnology, M.Sc. Microbiology as minimum educational qualification.
