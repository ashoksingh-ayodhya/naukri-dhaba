The Advanced Centre for Treatment, Research and Education in Cancer (ACTREC) has published the Junior Research Fellow (JRF) Notification 2026 under Advt. No. ACTREC/ADVT-101/2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 1 JRF position on a contractual basis. Candidates not exceeding 28 years of age and holding a B.S. 4-year programme, B.Pharm, MBBS, Integrated BS-MS, M.Sc., B.E., B.Tech, or an equivalent degree with at least 55% marks and having qualified NET/GATE are eligible to apply. Interested candidates can submit their applications via email on or before 18 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application details are provided below.

The last date to apply is **18-06-2026**.
Candidates must have B.S. 4 years programme /BPharm/MBBS/Integrated BS-MS/MSc/BE/BTech or equivalent degree, with 55% marks and passing of NET/GATE test. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 18-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
