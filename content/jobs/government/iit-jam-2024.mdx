GATE 2024 Indian Institute of Technology IIT Madras will conduct Joint Admission Test for Masters this year, candidates who are Enrolled in the JAM 2024 Entrance Exam Can Download Result with Score Card and Cutoff . Read the information brochure on JAM 2024 for eligibility, age limit, exam date, syllabus, previous year question paper and all other details.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for IIT Madras Joint Admission Test for Masters JAM Admissions Result / Score Card with Cutoff 2024. 
The last date to apply is **13/10/2023**.
Candidates must have M.Sc. Two Year, M.Sc PHd Dual Degree Course, Other Post Bachelor Degree Courses in Various IIT in India and Integrated PHD Course. JAM 2023 Exam Conducted by IIT Guwahati. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Passed / Appearing Both Interested Candidates Are Filled the JAM 2024 Application
2. Step 1 : Open the Registration Link and Filled the Basic Details
3. Step 2 : Note Down Your Registration / Enrollment No.
4. Step 3 : Login to Portal and Complete the Form, Upload Photo Signature and Paid the Examination Fee to Complete the Form
5. Instruction of Photo : Clear Recently Passport Size Photo with White Background
