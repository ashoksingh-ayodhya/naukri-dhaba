The Cotton Corporation of India Ltd (CCI) provides marketing support to cotton growers across the country through more than 590 market yards and 19 branches. CCI has invited applications for direct recruitment against various posts. Below is a quick overview of the notification.

A total of **148** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Download CCI Recruitment 2026 Notification PDF Here
2. Direct Link to apply online for CCI Recruitment 2026
3. The written test will consist of 120 objective-type MCQs to be completed in 120 minutes.
4. 1 mark will be awarded for each correct answer, with 0.25 marks deducted for each wrong answer.
5. The test covers General English, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, General Knowledge, Computer Proficiency (post-wise), and Subject Knowledge specific to the post.
6. Recent passport size color photograph
7. Recent scanned signature
8. 10th class certificate/mark sheet
9. 12th class certificate/mark sheet
10. Essential qualification degree certificate
11. Additional qualification degree certificate (if any)
12. Experience certificate (if applicable)
13. Visit the official website www.cotcorp.org.in and click on the "Recruitment" link.
14. Read the advertisement carefully and verify eligibility for the post.
15. Click "Enter" and fill in the online application form with personal, educational, and other required details.
