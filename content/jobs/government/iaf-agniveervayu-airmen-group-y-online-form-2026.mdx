The Indian Air Force has released two separate recruitment notifications inviting online applications for Agniveervayu Intake 02/2027 (under the Agnipath Scheme) and Airmen Group 'Y' (Non-Technical) Medical Assistant Trade for Intake 02/2027. Online registration for both notifications opens on 06 July 2026 and closes on 26 July 2026, with the online examination scheduled for 22-23 September 2026. While Agniveervayu is open to unmarried male and female Indian candidates for a four-year engagement, the Airmen Medical Assistant trade is open only to male Indian/Gorkha (Nepal) candidates for a 20-year regular cadre tenure. Applications must be submitted through the official CASB portal at iafrecruitment.edcil.co.in.


## How to Apply

1. Visit the official portal: iafrecruitment.edcil.co.in
2. Step I (Registration): Enter personal and contact details, then click "Generate OTP." Verify both mobile number and email ID using the OTPs received.
3. Agree to the terms & conditions, re-verify entered details, and click "Submit" to receive your User ID and Password via email/SMS.
4. Step II (Application Form): Log in and click "Go To Application" to fill in personal, communication, and qualification details.
5. Upload scanned photograph, signature, parent's signature (if under 18), and left thumb impression as per specified size/format guidelines.
6. Capture a live photo via webcam for face-match verification.
7. Preview the form, correct any errors, and click "Submit."
8. Pay the examination fee of ₹550 + 18% GST through the payment gateway.
9. Print and retain a copy of the final submitted application form for future reference.
10. Colour printout of Phase-II Admit Card and the filled application form
11. Eight passport-size colour photographs (same as used in registration)
12. Matriculation passing certificate and marksheet
13. 10+2/Intermediate/Diploma/Vocational passing certificate and marksheets
14. Diploma/B.Pharma/B.Sc Pharmacy certificate (Medical Assistant Diploma route only)
15. Domicile/COAFP Certificate (Agniveervayu) or SOAFP Certificate, if applicable (Medical Assistant)
