Rashtriya Chemicals and Fertilizers Limited (RCF), A Navratna Company and Government of India Undertaking, has announced recruitment for Honorary Doctors (31 posts) and Panel Doctors (15 posts) for its Trombay Unit Hospital and panel areas across Mumbai/Navi Mumbai. The detailed notification is available at the Human Resource Department, Trombay Unit, Chembur, Mumbai - 400 074. Specialist Doctors and MBBS practitioners meeting the prescribed qualifications and experience criteria can send their applications by post to reach the Deputy General Manager (HR) U on or before 31-07-2026. Direct links to download the notification and apply are shared below.

Department has released an official notification for RCFl Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline for 46 Honorary Doctors & Panel Doctors Posts. 
A total of **46** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Duly filled and signed application in the prescribed format (separate for Honorary Doctor / Panel Doctor).
2. Recent passport-size photograph affixed and duly signed on the application form.
3. Attested photostat copies of all educational qualification certificates.
4. Attested photostat copies of experience certificates from all organizations/hospitals.
5. Copy of valid Medical Council Registration certificate (with number, date, and state).
6. For Panel Doctors: details of clinic/dispensary including address, telephone, timings, facilities available, and details of any existing empanelment with other organizations.
7. Research papers / articles published, if any (for Honorary Doctors).
8. Last date for receipt of application is 31-07-2026 - applications received after this date will not be considered.
9. The post applied for must be written at the top of the left-hand corner of the envelope.
10. Separate applications are required for Honorary Doctor and Panel Doctor posts.
11. Engagement is purely temporary in nature and will not constitute an employer-employee relationship with RCF Ltd.
12. Engagement may be terminated by giving one month’s notice by either side.
13. The honorarium/reimbursement rates will be conveyed only at the time of personal Interview.
14. Management reserves the right to consider applications of doctors with lesser experience than advertised, if applications received are less than three times the number of advertised posts.
15. Management reserves the right to modify the number of posts and to cancel/alter this advertisement partially or completely without communicating the same to candidates.
