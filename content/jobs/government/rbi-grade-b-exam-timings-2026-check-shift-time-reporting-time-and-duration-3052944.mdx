The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Grade B 2026 exam is conducted in a single shift across all exam centres in India. The exam starts at 2:00 PM and ends at 4:00 PM, with a mandatory reporting time of 1:00 PM. Candidates who miss the gate closure at 1:45 PM will not be permitted entry under any circumstances.
