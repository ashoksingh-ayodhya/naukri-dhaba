National Institute of Translational Virology and AIDS Research (ICMR NITVAR) Recruitment 2026: National Institute of Translational Virology and AIDS Research is conducting a Walk-in Interview for 1 Consultant (Medical) posts. This recruitment provides an excellent opportunity for eligible candidates to attend the interview directly without any prior online application.

Candidates must have MBBS or BDS or BVSc or Equivalent degree in relevant subject with at least 12 years of post-qualification experience. OR Retired Government employee with requisite qualification and who were in regular Pay level 10 and above and having at least 10 years of experience in the required specialization. as minimum educational qualification.
