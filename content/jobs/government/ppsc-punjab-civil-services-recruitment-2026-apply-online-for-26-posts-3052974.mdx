Punjab Public Service Commission (PPSC) has released the notification for Punjab Civil Services (Executive Branch) Recruitment 2026 for posts to be filled from Register A-II (Ministerial Cadre - Group A and B) and Register-C (Group A and B - Non-Ministerial) vide Advertisement No. 20263 and Advertisement No. 20264 respectively, dated 05 June 2026. A total of 26 vacancies have been announced - 21 posts under Register A-II and 05 posts under Register-C.

1. Certificate from Head of Department (Annexure-A - Original): has released an official notification for PPSC Punjab Civil Services Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 26 Posts. 
A total of **26** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 01 November 2025 as minimum educational qualification.
