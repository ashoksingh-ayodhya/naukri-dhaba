Telangana Public Service Commission (TGPSC) will recruit 49 Assistant Executive Engineer (Electrical) posts in Transport Roads and Buildings Department, which will be filled through TGPSC AEE Electrical Recruitment 2026. Interested candidates can apply online from 08 June 2026 to 15 July 2026. Check details like eligibility, salary, apply online, vacancy, syllabus & more from the article.

A total of **49** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in and complete the One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain your TGPSC ID (if not already registered).
2. Login with your TGPSC ID and Date of Birth and enter the OTP received on the registered mobile number.
3. Verify the details fetched from the OTR database (Name, Date of Birth, Educational Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability, etc.).
4. Select the examination centre and fill in all required details in the online application form.
5. Upload a recent colour passport size photograph (not older than 3 months, 100-200kb) and scanned signature (20-30kb) in JPG/PNG format.
6. Preview the application carefully and click on SAVE & PAYMENT button to proceed with online fee payment through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card or UPI.
7. After payment, click CONFIRM & SUBMIT. Download the PDF application form and note the Reference ID Number for future reference.
