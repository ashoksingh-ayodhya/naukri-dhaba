Andaman and Nicobar Administration has published the Combined Higher Secondary Level (CHSL) Recruitment Notification 2026 vide Advertisement No. 02/HSL/2026 dated June 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 716 Group B (NG) and Group C vacancies across 38 posts in various departments including Lower Grade Clerk, Stenographer (OG), Forester, Tally Clerk, IT Analyst, Marine Radio Operator, and many more. Candidates within the age range as per post-wise eligibility criteria and holding qualifications such as 10th Pass, 12th Pass, ITI, Diploma, or Graduation are eligible to apply online at erecruitment.andamannicobar.gov.in. The application process will be active from 15 June 2026 to 05 July 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

A total of **716** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have (1) Bachelor's Degree in Science of the subject concerned. (2) Two years experience as Laboratory Assistant in any Govt. recognized Institution/School. (3) Knowledge of Hindi. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent passport size colour photograph in jpg/jpeg format (20 KB to 50 KB; resolution 200x230; plain white or off-white background; full face, front view, eyes open; without cap, mask or glasses/spectacles)
2. Signature in jpeg format (10 KB to 20 KB; resolution 140x60)
3. Other documents such as proof of age, qualifications, category, NOC etc. should be between 100 KB to 500 KB as instructed in the online portal
4. Educational qualification certificates and marksheets
5. Age proof certificate
6. ST Certificate in the prescribed format (Local A&N Islands ST only for reserved quota)
7. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate - valid OBC-NCL certificate not older than two years from the closing date of the vacancy notice
8. EWS Certificate issued by the Revenue Department, A&N Administration
9. PwBD Certificate in the prescribed format (minimum 40% disability) issued by a Competent Authority as per form V, VI and VII of rule 18(1) under Chapter 7 of Rights of Persons with Disabilities Rules, 2017
10. Ex-Serviceman discharge/service certificate
11. NOC from employer (for departmental candidates working in Government Departments)
12. Online applications only - no other mode of application will be accepted; applications incomplete in any respect will be summarily rejected.
13. No documents are to be uploaded at the time of submission of online application; candidature will be accepted only provisionally and eligibility will be verified at the time of Online Document Verification.
14. Candidates must have their own active personal E-mail ID and Mobile Number which should be kept active throughout the entire recruitment process - admit cards, registration numbers, e-Call letters will be sent only on the registered e-mail ID.
15. Candidates are advised to check spam/junk box for e-mails; A&N Administration will not be responsible for bouncing back of any e-mail or undelivered SMS.
