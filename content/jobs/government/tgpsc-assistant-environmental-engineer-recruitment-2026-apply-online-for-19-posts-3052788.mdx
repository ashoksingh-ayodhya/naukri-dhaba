The Telangana Public Service Commission (TGPSC), Hyderabad has released an official notification for the post of Assistant Environmental Engineer in the Telangana Pollution Control Board (TGPCB). This is a State Level direct recruitment through an Objective Type Written Examination (CBRT/OMR based) under Notification No. 02/OG/PC/2026, Dated: 02/06/2026. Online applications open on June 10, 2026 and the last date to apply is July 17, 2026 by 5:00 PM.

Department has released an official notification for TGPSC Assistant Environmental Engineer Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 19 Posts. 
A total of **19** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official TGPSC website at https://www.tgpsc.gov.in.
2. If applying for the first time, complete One Time Registration (OTR) to obtain a TGPSC ID. Note: OTR alone does not constitute an application.
3. Login using your TGPSC ID and Date of Birth, and enter the OTP received on your registered mobile number.
4. Verify details fetched from OTR - Name, Date of Birth, Educational Qualifications, Community, Gender, Disability, Ex-Servicemen status, Sports, etc.
5. Select your preferred examination centre from the available options.
6. Upload a recent passport-size colour photograph (not older than 3 months; File size: 100KB–200KB) and scanned specimen signature (File size: 20KB–30KB) in JPG/PNG format.
7. Preview the application carefully, then click SAVE & PAYMENT to proceed to fee payment.
8. Pay the prescribed fee online through Net Banking, Debit Card, Credit Card, or UPI.
9. After payment, a PDF application will be generated. Download and retain the PDF. Note your Reference ID Number for future correspondence.
10. Click CONFIRM & SUBMIT to complete the submission. No changes will be made by the Commission to any submitted application.
11. For technical issues: Call 040-23120301 / 040-23120302 / 040-22445566 / 040-67445566 (10:30 AM to 5:00 PM on working days) or email [email protected].
