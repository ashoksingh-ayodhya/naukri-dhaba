The All India Institute of Ayurveda (AIIA) has officially announced the Research Associate, Panchkarma Technician Recruitment 2026 with 5 openings under Advt. No. O-15/2/2026-AIIA. Candidates aged up to 40 years and holding a Postgraduate degree in Ayurveda with specialization in Panchakarma/Kayachikitsa or 12th pass with a One-Year Certificate Course in Panchkarma Technician are eligible to participate. Interested candidates can attend the walk-in interview scheduled for 11 June 2026. The official notification PDF and walk-in details are provided below.

Candidates must have Postgraduate degree in Ayurveda with specialization in Panchakarma/Kayachikitsa OR 12th passed with One Year Certificate Course in Panchkarma Technician as minimum educational qualification.
