Indian Army ASC Centre (South) – 2 ATC Civilian Recruitment 2023. Those candidates who are interested in this ASC Centre (South) – 2 ATC recruitment can apply Offline from 22 April 2023 to 13 May 2023. Read the notification for recruitment eligibility, post information, selection procedure, age limit, pay scale and all other information.

A total of **236** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. ASC Center South ATC 2 Are Issued Various Civilian Jobs Recruitment 2023. Candidate Can Apply Between 22/04/2023 to 13/05/2023
2. Send Application Form to : The Presiding Officer, Civilian Direct Recruitment Board, CHQ, ASC Centre (South) – 2 ATC/ASC Centre (North)-1 ATC Agram Post, Bangalore -07
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in ASC Center South ATC 2 Various Post Vacancy 2023.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
