UP Joint Entrance Examination has released the Uttar Pradesh Engineering Diploma Admission JEECUP 2025 online application for admission 2025 will start from 15 January 2025 and the last date to apply will be 20 May 2025. Various Polytechnic Institute are participating in JEECUP 2025, candidates should read the information brochure for all the information related to the entrance exam like syllabus, eligibility, age limit, placement, fees, college information and other types of information and then apply.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for UPJEE UP Polytechnic JEECUP 2025 Admissions Test Exam Admit Card 2025. 
The last date to apply is **20/05/2025**.

## How to Apply

1. Photo Instruction : Upload Color Passport Size Photo with Candidates Name and Photo Taken Date (Photo Not Be Older Than 3 Month)
2. Thumb Impression : Uploaded Left Hand Thumb Impression
3. Signature Instruction : Candidates Signature in Running Hand (Capital Letter Signature Not Accepted)
4. No Need to Send Confirmation Page to JEECUP 2025
5. For More Details Must Read the Notification.
