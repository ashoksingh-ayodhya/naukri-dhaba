University of ALlahabad is going to conduct the Various UGAT Courses Admissions 2023. Students who want to take admission in Uttar Pradesh Allahabad Universit AU Under Graduate BA, B.Com, B.Sc, BA LLB, IPS, Etc Course can apply online 12 July 2023 to 31 July 2023. Allahabad University CUET Admission 2023 Guidlines, Schedule, Fees List and all other information read the notification.

How to Fill:Allahabad University UG Courses Online Registration 2023 has released an official notification for Allahabad University UG Courses Admissions Through CUET 2023 Online Counseling, Cutoff. 
The last date to apply is **31/07/2023**.
Candidates must have Click Here as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. University of Allahabad UGAT UG Courses Admission 2023 Through CUET UG 2023 for the BA, B.Com, B.Ed, B.Sc, BA LLB and Other Various UG Courses. Candidate Can Apply Between 12/07/2023 to 31/07/2023
2. NTA CUET UG 2023 Application Number. Registration for AU UGAT 2023 will be based on the CUET UG Application Number.
3. Scanned Document Required : Passport Size Photograph, Signature, 10th Marksheet,12th Mark sheet, Birth Certificate and Other relevant Documents such as Caste, Income, TC, CC.
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in University of Allahabad UGAT Admissions 2023.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
