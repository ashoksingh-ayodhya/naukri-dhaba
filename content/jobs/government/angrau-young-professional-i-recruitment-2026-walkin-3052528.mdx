Candidates aged between 21 and 35 years who have a 4-year Bachelor's Degree in Agriculture or a Diploma in Agriculture from an ICAR-accredited University/College are eligible to apply for this Young Professional-I vacancy. The walk-in interview for ANGRAU Young Professional-I Recruitment 2026 is scheduled to be held on 11 June 2026.

Candidates must have 4 years Bachelor's Degree in Agriculture or Diploma in Agriculture from ICAR accredited University/College as minimum educational qualification.
