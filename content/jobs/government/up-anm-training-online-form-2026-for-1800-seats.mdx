Short Description : Uttar Pradesh Department of Medical Health & Family Welfare has released an official notification for UP ANM Training Online Form 2026 for 1800 Seats. 
The last date to apply is **31/07/2026**.

## How to Apply

1. Cast Certificate Instruction : OBC and EWS certificate should be issued after 01/04/2026.
2. Photo Instruction : The photograph should be full face, from front, with white background and without spectacles along with the Name of the candidate and date of photograph should be written on the photograph.
3. Director General Training Medical Health and Family Welfare, UP For New Application for ANM Training 2026. Candidate Can Apply Between 01/07/2026 to 31/07/2026
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University, ABVMU Lucknow Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
