The offline application process for ECHS Medical Specialist Recruitment 2026 has started on 24 May 2026 and will remain open until 05 June 2026.

Candidates must have MD/ MS in Specialty concerned/DNB with minimum 03 years work experience after post graduation as minimum educational qualification.
