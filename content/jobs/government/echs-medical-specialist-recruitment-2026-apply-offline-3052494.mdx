Candidates as per ECHS norms who have MD/MS in the concerned specialty or DNB with a minimum of 03 years of work experience in the relevant field after post-graduation are eligible to apply for these Medical Specialist vacancies. The offline application process for ECHS Medical Specialist Recruitment 2026 has started on 24 May 2026 and will remain open until 05 June 2026.

Candidates must have MD/ MS in Specialty concerned/DNB with minimum 03 years work experience after post graduation as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.echs.gov.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 05-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
