The Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) has published the Research Officer and Administrative-cum-Accounts Officer Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 3 project-based positions. Candidates holding an MA in Social Work, Women's Studies, or Development Studies, or a Bachelor's degree in Commerce, Finance, or Business Administration with relevant experience, are eligible to apply. Interested applicants can submit their applications via email on or before 08 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application details are provided below.

The last date to apply is **08-06-2026**.
Candidates must have MA in Social Work/Women's Studies/Development Studies or Bachelor's degree in Commerce/Finance/Business Administration with relevant experience. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 08-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
