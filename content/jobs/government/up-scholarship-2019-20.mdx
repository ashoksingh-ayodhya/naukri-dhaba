Uttar Pradesh Samaj Kalyan Vibhag UP Scholarship for Class 9-10, Class 11-12 adn Dashmottar UG, PG and Other Diploma, Certificate Courses Fresh and Renewal Scholarship 2019. Those Candidates Are Interested to the Completed the All Eligibility Criteria Can Read the Full Notification and Apply Online

Social Welfare Department, Uttar Pradesh has released an official notification for UP Scholarship Online Form Date Extended 2019. 
The last date to apply is **20/11/2019**.
Candidates must have Last Qualifying Exam Mark Sheet; Cast Certificate; Income Certificate; Bank Passbook; Fee Receipt Number; Annual Non Refundable Amount; Enrollment Number; Aadhar Card Number; Latest Passport Size Scan Photo; For Renewal Candidates : Kindly Use Last Year Registration Number to Login to Renewal Section and Enter the Fresh Details as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Fresh Candidates : Those Candidates Are Enrolled in This Year OR Not Filled the Scholarship Form in Last Year Are Registered Yourself to as a Fresh Candidates.
2. Renewal Candidates : Those Candidates Are Enrolled in Previously Years and Required to Re Fill the Scholarship Form in This Year Kindly Use as a Renewal Candidates
3. Kindly Collect the All Document Before Apply Online
4. If You have Dont Know Annual Non Refundable Amount Kindly Contact to Enrolled College
5. After the Complete the Application Process Submit the Form within 3 Days of Complete Form
6. For More Information Must Read the Full Instruction
7. Aadhar Card is Compulsory for the Scholarship Application 2018-19
