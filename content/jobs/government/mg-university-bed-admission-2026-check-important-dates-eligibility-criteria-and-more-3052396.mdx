MG University BEd Admission 2026 has started. Mahatma Gandhi University (MG University) has commenced the online registration for B.Ed CAP 2026 on 26 May 2026. The last date for online registration is 10 June 2026. This is the Centralized Allotment Process (CAP) for admission to B.Ed programmes in affiliated teacher training colleges for the academic year 2026-27.

MG University BEd Admission 2026 has released an official notification for MG University BEd Admission 2026: Check Important Dates, Eligibility Criteria and More. 

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website https://cap.mgu.ac.in/bedcap2026/.
2. Click on the “Registration” link.
3. Fill in the minimum personal details carefully (Name, Date of Birth, Mobile Number, Email ID).
4. Create a password and note down the 8-digit Application Number.
5. Fill the application form with academic details and provide up to 20 college/course options.
6. Pay the application fee online using Debit Card, Credit Card, or Internet Banking.
7. Upload photo, signature, and supporting documents.
8. Take a printout of the confirmation page for future reference.
9. SSLC / Plus Two certificates
10. Degree / PG Marksheets and Certificates
11. Category / Community Certificates (if applicable)
12. Photo and Signature
13. Other relevant supporting documents as mentioned in the prospectus
