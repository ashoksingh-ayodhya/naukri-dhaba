Uttar Pradesh State Rural Livelihood Mission (UPSRLM) has issued an BC Sakhi Recruitment 2023 Notification. Any candidate who is interested in this UP BC Sakhi Recruitment 2023 can apply Through BC Sakhi App Till Last Date 05 February 2023. For District / Panchayat Wise details, pay scale, age limit, selection prcedure, job information and all other information, read the advertisement and then apply.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh BC Sakhi Recruitment 2023 Apply Offline for 3808 Post. 
A total of **3808** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **05/02/2023**.

## How to Apply

1. UPSRLM BC Sakhi Recruitment 2023. Candidate Can Apply Till 05 February 2023.
2. In Uttar Pradesh BC Sakhi Recruitment 2023, only women whose age is 18 to 50 years can apply and they should also be a resident of the concerned Gram Panchayat.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in UPSRLM Apply Through BC Sakhi App for BC Sakhi Jobs 2023.
4. Candidates applying for Uttar Pradesh BC Sakhi Recruitment 2023will have to download the BC Sakhi App from Android Store and apply from the same.
5. If you apply in more than one Gram Panchayat, all the applications will be rejected.
6. For More Details Read the Notification
