The latest Hindustan Copper Limited (HCL) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Deputy Manager (E2), Manager (E3), and Senior Manager (E4) posts! Published on 16-05-2026 at Hindustan Copper Limited Official Website, this new recruitment drive offers 70 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the minimum 18 years and above age group and possessing a relevant Bachelor’s Degree / Post Graduate Degree / CA / CMA / MBA / MBBS qualification as per post requirement can apply online from 05-06-2026 to 25-06-2026. Find all details about the HCL Executive new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for Hindustan Copper HCL Executive Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 70 Posts. 
A total of **70** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 1 Mark each, subject to maximum 2 Marks as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Date of Birth proof – Xth Standard Passing Certificate or School Leaving Certificate / Birth Certificate
2. XIIth Standard Passing Certificate
3. Mark Sheets and Degree/Diploma Certificates of Graduate and/or Post-Graduate and/or Professional Qualification as applicable
4. Experience Certificates for each organization (with designation, department, date of joining/leaving, job description)
5. If working in PSU/Govt.: documentary evidence of working in next below grade and scale of pay
6. No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer if working in PSU / Government organization
7. Salary Slips (last 3 months), Bank Statement, and latest Form-16 (for private/bank employees)
8. Caste/Category Certificate – SC/ST/OBC (NCL)/EWS/PwBD as applicable, in prescribed Government of India format
9. Original Photo ID proof (Aadhar / Driving License / Voter-ID)
10. CGPA to Percentage conversion certificate (if grades awarded) duly issued by University/Institute
11. Only Indian Nationals of 18 years and above are eligible to apply.
12. Date of reckoning for age, experience, and qualification is 01-05-2026.
13. Experience acquired in Training / Consultancy / Teaching / Fellowship / Internship / Apprenticeship / Academic Project shall not be counted.
14. Experience in relevant field only shall be considered; HCL's decision on relevance is final.
15. Candidates from private organizations must be from companies with turnover of Rs. 250 Crores or more.
