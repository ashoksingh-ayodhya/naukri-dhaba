All India Institute of Medical Sciences Patna (AIIMS Patna) has released an official notification for the recruitment of 2 Head (Incubation Centre) and Coordinator Posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply Offline by sending the application form to the official address. The last date to submit the application is 22-06-2026.

Candidates must have Ph.D. in Life Sciences /MBBS/First class in BTech/ M.Sc degree in Life Sciences/ Electrical/ Electronics/ Mechanical/ Computer science/ Biomedical/IT or equivalent, Ph.D./First class in Postgraduate in Life Sciences /MBBS/BDS/Business Administration/Technology or equivalent as minimum educational qualification.
