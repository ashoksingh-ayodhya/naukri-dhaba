All India Institute of Medical Sciences Jodhpur (AIIMS Jodhpur) has officially announced a Walk-in Interview for the recruitment of 1 Research Assistant Posts. This walk-in drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Candidates with Master’s in Public Health / Sociology / Social Work / Anthropology / Microbiology / Zoology / Health Science/Msc in zoology life science OR equivalent Graduate in Science / Sociology / Social Work / Anthropology / Microbiology / Zoology / Health Science from a recognized University with three years of work experience from a recognized institution qualification can directly attend the interview on 09-06-2026.

Candidates must have Master’s in Public Health / Sociology / Social Work / Anthropology / Microbiology / Zoology / Health Science/Msc in zoology life science OR equivalent Graduate in Science / Sociology / Social Work / Anthropology / Microbiology / Zoology / Health Science from a recognized University with three years of work experience from a recognized institution as minimum educational qualification.
