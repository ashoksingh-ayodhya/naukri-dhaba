Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IIT Hyderabad) has published the Non-Teaching Positions Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 21 Non-Teaching Positions. Candidates within the age range of up to 35 years (age relaxation applicable as per rules) and holding a Diploma or Bachelor's Degree are eligible to apply online at the official IIT Hyderabad website. The application process will be active until 20 July 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

Candidates must have Diploma, Bachelor's degree as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 20-07-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of IIT Hyderabad to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
8. Take a printout or save a copy of the submitted application for future reference.
