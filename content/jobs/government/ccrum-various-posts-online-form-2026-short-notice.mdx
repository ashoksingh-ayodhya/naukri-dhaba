Those searching for the CCRUM Ayush Vacancy 2026 official notification should note that the PDF contains the complete post-wise eligibility criteria, age limits, category-wise reservation breakup, detailed syllabus for all 23 posts, application fee structure, selection procedure, and the full examination schedule. The online application window opens on 1 July 2026 at 10:00 AM and closes on 31 July 2026 at 23:59 hrs, with a Modification Window available from 3 to 5 August 2026. A Hindi version of the notification was also indicated to follow shortly after the English version was published. Candidates are strongly advised to read the full notification PDF carefully before applying, as eligibility is determined as on the closing date of application (31 July 2026) and no subsequent relaxation or correction is permitted.

Ministry has released an official notification for CCRUM Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 179 Staff Nurse, MTS and More Posts. 
A total of **179** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit www.ccrum.res.in or www.ccrum.ayush.gov.in and go to the "Advertisement/Vacancy" tab.
2. Create a new account (Step I): Enter your full name (as per Matriculation Certificate), father's name, mother's name, gender, date of birth, mobile number, valid email ID, and Government ID proof. Verify via OTP.
3. Complete your profile (Step II): Log in with your Reference Number and Password. Upload photograph, signature, caste certificate, disability certificate (if applicable), basic qualification certificates, and other relevant documents.
4. Apply for the post (Step III): Select the post(s) you wish to apply for, fill in post-specific information, and upload post-specific documents.
5. Pay the fee: You will be redirected to the payment gateway. Pay via Debit Card, Credit Card, Net Banking, or UPI. Download the payment acknowledgement slip.
6. Download your application form in PDF format from the Dashboard for future reference.
7. Latest passport-size colour photograph (50–100 KB, JPEG/JPG), taken in 2026
8. Signature on white paper with black ink pen (50–100 KB, JPG/JPEG)
9. Caste/Category Certificate in Central Government format (SC/ST/OBC/PwBD/EWS)
10. Disability Certificate issued by Competent Authority (for PwBD candidates)
11. Discharge Certificate (for Ex-Servicemen)
12. Educational qualification certificates with mark sheets
13. Valid Government photo ID proof (Voter ID, Passport, PAN Card, or Driving License)
14. No Objection Certificate (for candidates already in Government service)
15. Each document file: between 100 KB and 1000 KB in PDF/JPG/JPEG format
