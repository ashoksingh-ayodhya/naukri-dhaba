Indian Institute of Management Lucknow (IIM Lucknow) has officially released the Academic Assistant for Finance & Accounting Area Recruitment 2026 notification for 2 vacancies at its Lucknow campus. Candidates holding a Postgraduate degree such as M.Com., MBA (Finance), B.Com. with MBA, CA, or CWA from a recognized institution with a minimum of 60% marks and aged below 38 years as on 31 May 2026 are eligible to apply online. Selected candidates will be appointed on a contractual basis and will receive a salary of ₹40,000 per month along with HRA as per institute norms. Interested applicants can submit their online applications on or before 15 June 2026 through the official IIM Lucknow recruitment portal.

Candidates must have Post Graduate (M.Com. or M.B.A. with specialization in Finance, or B.Com. with an M.B.A., or CA, or CWA) from a recognised institute/ university in regular mode with minimum 60% marks. as minimum educational qualification.
