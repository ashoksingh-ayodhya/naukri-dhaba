UP Ayush NEET UG 2024 Admissions has released the Information Brochure of Uttar Pradesh UP Ayush NEET UG 2024 BAMS / BUMS / BHMS Admissions 2024. Those Candidates Are Interested with UP Ayush NEET UG 2024 Can Apply Online Counseling Registration from 25/09/2024 to Schedule Date. For other information related to UP Ayush NEET UG 2024 Admissions College List, Information Brochure, Eligibility, GO, Choice Filling, Important Dates, and all other information read the advertisement and then apply.


## How to Apply

1. For registration in the Uttar Pradesh Ayush course, the candidate will have to enter his NEET 2024 roll number and his date of birth.
2. All the information of the candidate will be visible according to the form of NEET 2024 , the candidate will have to fill the other information that has been asked, he will have to submit it later.
3. After the completion of the registration process, the candidate will also have to pay the registration fee of Rs.2000/- then only the registration process will be completed.
4. When the merit list will be released : Candidates will have to check their name in that whose name will be there in the merit list they can do choice filling of their college.
5. For other information, read the entire notification and apply only after that.
