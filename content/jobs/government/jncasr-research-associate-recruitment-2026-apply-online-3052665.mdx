Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR) has released the official Research Associate Notification 2026 under Advt. No. CPMU/EMR/62/26-27. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced under the JNCASR Research Associate Recruitment 2026. Candidates possessing a Ph.D. degree in Chemical Science or an equivalent qualification and aged up to 35 years are eligible to apply. Interested candidates can submit their applications through email on or before 12 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application details are provided below.

The last date to apply is **12-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Ph.D. degree in Chemical Science or equivalent as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 12-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
