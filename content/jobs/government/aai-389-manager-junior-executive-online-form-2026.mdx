AAI Recruitment 2026 OUT! The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has released the AAI Manager and Junior Executive Recruitment 2026 notification for 389 vacancies across 15 disciplines. The recruitment includes posts of Manager and Junior Executive in departments such as Engineering, Finance, IT, Law, Operations, Commercial, Fire Services, and other disciplines. Eligible candidates with the required educational qualifications and experience can apply online through aai.aero.

A total of **389** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website: www.aai.aero and go to the "CAREERS" tab.
2. Keep ready a valid personal email ID, mobile number, scanned photograph, signature, left thumb impression, and hand-written declaration as per specifications.
3. Fill in the online application form with accurate personal, educational, and experience details.
4. Upload all required documents as per the eligibility criteria for the post.
5. Pay the application fee of Rs. 1,000 online (if applicable), using the integrated payment gateway.
6. Verify all details carefully before final submission, as no changes are possible after submitting.
7. Take a printout of the e-Receipt and the online application form containing fee details, for your own record (no need to send it to AAI).
8. Regularly check your registered email (including spam folder) and the AAI website for admit card and further updates.
9. Only Indian Nationals fulfilling the eligibility criteria can apply.
10. Age, qualification, experience, and all other eligibility criteria will be reckoned as on 07 September 2026 (cut-off date).
11. Candidates should apply only if the final result of their required qualification has already been declared; there is no relaxation on this account.
12. OBC (NCL), EWS, SC/ST, and Disability certificates must be valid and in the prescribed Government of India format, produced at the time of Application Verification/Interview.
13. Candidates can apply for multiple posts, with separate registration and fee payment for each post.
14. Furnishing wrong or false information will lead to disqualification, and AAI will not be responsible for any consequence of such information.
15. No TA/DA will be paid for appearing in the Computer Based Test.
