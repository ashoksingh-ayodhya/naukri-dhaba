Candidates aged up to 40 years for Research Scientist-II posts and up to 25 years for the Lab Technician post who possess qualifications such as MBBS, M.Sc. with Ph.D., or B.Sc./Intermediate with Diploma in MLT and relevant experience are eligible to apply for these vacancies. The walk-in interview for AIIMS Jodhpur Research Project Recruitment Notice 2026 is scheduled to be held on 15 June 2026, while requests for online interviews must be submitted by 14 June 2026.

Candidates must have MBBS, M.Sc. + Ph.D, B.Sc. / Intermediates + Diploma in MLT as minimum educational qualification.
