All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh will recruit 75 Faculty posts including Professor, Associate Professor and Assistant Professor on Regular and Contractual basis through AIIMS Bilaspur Faculty Recruitment 2026. Interested candidates can submit the Google Form online before 20 June 2026 and send the hard copy application before 25 June 2026. Check details like eligibility, salary, apply online, vacancy & more from the article.

A total of **75** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Pay the application fee through NEFT to the designated SBI account and save the transaction proof.
2. Download the soft copy of Application Form (Appendix-I), Career Graph (Appendix-II in MS Word/PDF format) and Presentation (Appendix-III in PPT format) from the official website.
3. Fill in the Application Form and Career Graph in the prescribed format. Rename files as per the prescribed naming convention (e.g. "Name Post Department.pdf").
4. Submit the Google Form at https://forms.gle/F262hswPdmZ6j2st5 by 20 June 2026 (05:00 PM) along with scanned copies of all required documents and fee transaction proof.
5. After submitting the Google Form, send the duly filled and signed hard copy of the application (Appendix-I) along with self-attested copies of all certificates (age, qualification, experience, caste/EWS/PwBD certificate, NOC, fee payment proof) to: Deputy Director (Administration), Administrative Block, 3rd Floor, AIIMS, Kothipura, Bilaspur, Himachal Pradesh – 174037 so as to reach on or before 25 June 2026 (05:00 PM).
6. Super-scribe the envelope as "Application for the post of ………, Department of ……… for AIIMS, Bilaspur (H.P.)"
7. Candidates applying for more than one post must submit a separate application for each post.
8. Scrutiny of Applications by Scrutiny Committee
9. Shortlisting based on experience, patient care, teaching/research, publications, and administrative experience
10. Personal Interview by Standing Selection Committee (SSC)
11. 5-Minute PowerPoint Presentation before Selection Committee
12. Document Verification & Medical Fitness by Institute Medical Board
13. DLSA Indore Vehicle Driver Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline
14. GVHSS Rayagada Guest Faculty Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline
15. CSIR IGIB Technician Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 15 Posts
