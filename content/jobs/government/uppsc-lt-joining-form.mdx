Secondary Education Department, Uttar Pradesh Are Invited to Online Online Posting Form, Choice Filling, Subject Wise School List Online Registration 2020. Those Candidates Are Qualified in UPPSC LT Grade Teacher Recruitment 2018 Written Exam Can Fill the Posting Form.

Secondary Education Department, Uttar Pradesh Are Invited to Online Online Posting Form, Choice Filling, Subject Wise School List Online Registration 2020. Those Candidates Are Qualified in UPPSC LT Grade Teacher Recruitment 2018 Written Exam Can Fill the Posting Form. has released an official notification for UPPSC LT Grade Teacher Online Posting Form 2020. 
A total of **10768** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 168 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Only Written Exam Qualified Candidates Are Fill the Choice Filling.
2. Those Subject Are Not Released Till Date Kindly Wait for the Released Result.
3. First You Have See the School List and Choose the School Preference.
4. Registration to Choice Filling Portal with Registered Application No. and OTP.
5. If Facing Any TOP Related Issue Kindly Contact to Office.
6. Must Read the Complete Notification Before Filling Online Form.
