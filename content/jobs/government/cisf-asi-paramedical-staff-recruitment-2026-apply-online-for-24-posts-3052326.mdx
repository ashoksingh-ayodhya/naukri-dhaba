Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) has invited online applications from eligible Male and Female Indian citizens for filling up temporary posts of Para Medical Staff in Pay Level-5 of 7th CPC (Rs. 29,200 – 92,300/-) through CISF ASI Para Medical Staff Recruitment 2026. All interested and eligible candidates can apply online at the official CISF recruitment website cisfrectt.cisf.gov.in from 08 June 2026. This article highlights all major details like eligibility, selection process, application dates and more.

A total of **24** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official CISF recruitment website at cisfrectt.cisf.gov.in.
2. Click on the "NEW REGISTRATION" button and complete the One-Time Registration process using your Mobile Number and Email ID.
3. Login using your Registration ID and auto-generated password and click on "Recruitment of Paramedical Staff in CISF – 2026" tab.
4. Fill in the essential details such as Registration ID, Password and Captcha and click "SUBMIT".
5. Fill the required personal and educational details in the online application form.
6. Upload your recent passport size photograph (not more than 3 months old), signature, Date of Birth Certificate and educational qualification certificate as per specified format.
7. Pay the application fee through UPI, Net Banking, Credit/Debit card or SBI Challan (cash at SBI branches).
8. Review all the details carefully, click "Final Submission" and take a printout of the submitted application form for future reference.
9. Written Examination (OMR/CBT Mode – Objective Type)
10. Physical Standard Test (PST) & Documentation
11. Basic Vocational Aptitude Test (BVAT)
12. Detailed Medical Examination (DME) / Review Medical Examination (RME)
13. Objective Multiple Choice type written examination.
14. Total Marks: 100
15. Duration: 2 Hours
