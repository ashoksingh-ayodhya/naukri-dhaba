GPSC AE Recruitment 2026: The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has released official notifications on June 5, 2026 announcing a combined total of 235 vacancies for the post of Assistant Engineer (Civil) and Assistant Engineer (Mechanical), Class-2. The vacancies span two separate departments - the Road and Building Department (200 posts) and the Gujarat Maritime Board (35 posts across Mechanical and Civil branches).

Important - Gujarat Maritime Board posts: has released an official notification for GPSC AE Recruitment 2026 for 235 Assistant Engineer Vacancies in Civil & Mechanical, Apply Online by June 19. 
A total of **235** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have B.E./B.Tech in Civil or Mechanical Engineering as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit gpsc-ojas.gujarat.gov.in and complete One Time Registration (OTR) if not done already. OTR is mandatory but is not itself an application - you must separately apply for each advertisement.
2. On the Menu Bar, click "Online Application" → "Apply" and select the relevant advertisement.
3. Enter your OTR Registration Number and Date of Birth, then click Apply.
4. Fill in Personal Details, Communication Details, Other Details, Language Details, Education Details, and Experience Details carefully. Red asterisk (*) fields are mandatory.
5. Upload your passport-size photograph (white background, with date, 5cm × 3.6cm, JPG, max 10 KB) and signature (white paper, black/blue ink, 2.5cm × 7.5cm, JPG, max 10 KB).
6. Review all details before clicking SAVE/SUBMIT. Note your Application Number and Confirmation Number that appear on screen immediately after.
7. Pay the application fee (if applicable) and take a printout of the confirmed application.
8. Applications remain editable until June 19, 2026 at 11:59 PM. Use the "Edit" option to make corrections - do not submit a fresh application.
9. Complete the Consent Form and Consent Deposit (separate process) before June 20, 2026 at 10:00 AM.
10. Medium of exam: Gujarati (unless the Commission decides otherwise)
11. Minimum qualifying marks: 15% of total (below 15% = not eligible for Interview)
12. Both papers are held on different dates. Absence from either paper means automatic disqualification.
13. Preliminary Test: Marks obtained (out of 300) × 50/300
14. Interview: Marks obtained (out of 100) × 50/100
15. Combined total = out of 100
