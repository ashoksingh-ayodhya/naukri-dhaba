## How to Apply

1. Union Public Service Commission UPPSC Will be Start the One Time Registration Process on 03/01/2023.
2. Following the Simple Step to Registered Online.
3. Step 1 : Open the Website uppsc.up.nic.in OR otr.pariksha.nic.in or Direct Link Will be in this Page Some Useful Important Links Section
4. Step 2 : Click on Registration Tab
5. Step 3 : Students will have to give their basic information such as: Name, Father’s Name, Mother’s Name, Mobile Number, Email Id and Aadhar Card Last 6 Digit.
6. Step 4 : Verify Your UPPSC OTR Profile Details & Submit Registration.
7. Step 5 : An OTP will be received on the registered email and registered mobile number of the candidate, he will have to enter it.
8. Step 6 : The registration process will be completed, the candidate will see the OTR ID on the screen. Now candidate can take print and screen shot of OTR profile.
9. Step 7 : Now the candidate will have to login, to login, the candidate will have to enter his mobile number / email or OTR id along with an OTP which will be received on the registered email and mobile.
10. Step 8 : Change UPPSC OTR ID Password.
11. Step 9 : Now the candidate UPPSC OTR registration process will be completed, now whatever new application comes out, the candidate can apply from the same, can also get the history of the old filling application from the same.
12. For more information, you have to complete the notice issued to UPPSC OTR.
