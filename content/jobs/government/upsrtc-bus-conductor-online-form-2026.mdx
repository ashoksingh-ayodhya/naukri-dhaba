Uttar Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation (UPSRTC) has released an official notification for UP Uttar Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation UPSRTC Bus Conductor Recruitment 2026 Apply Online District Wise Vacancy Details. 
A total of **10** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Conductor recruitment in Uttar Pradesh State Road Transport Corporation, which is being done this time through outsourcing.
2. Candidates who are eligible and interested in UPSRTC Conductor Recruitment will have to apply through Uttar Pradesh Sevayojan Portal (Rojgar Sangam).
3. After registering in Uttar Pradesh Sevayojan Portal (Rojgar Sangam) and updating their qualification as well as CCC information in the profile, the candidate will get the option of Apply Job in UPSRTC Conductor Recruitment, read the information and apply.
4. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
