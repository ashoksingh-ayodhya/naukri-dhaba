Vardhman Mahavir Medical College & Safdarjung Hospital (VMMC & SJH) has released an official notification for Walk-in Interview for 84 Senior Resident Posts. Eligible candidates can attend the Walk-in Interview on at the VMMC & SJH office with original documents and self-attested copies.

Candidates must have MBBS with PG Degree/Diploma/DNB in relevant specialty, registered with NMC and Delhi Medical Council. For Dental specialties, MDS recognized by DCI. as minimum educational qualification.
