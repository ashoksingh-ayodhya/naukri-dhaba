Navy Short Service Commission Officers Various Post January 2025 Batch has released an official notification for Indian Navy Short Service Commissions SSC Executive, Education and Technical January 2025 Branch Online Form 2024. 
A total of **254** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **10/03/2024**.
Candidates must have Total Post as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Join Indian Navy Are Released for Various Executive / Technical and Education Branch Entry Recruitment Indian Navy Vacancies. Candidate Can Apply Between 24/02/2024 to 10/03/2024
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Indian Navy SSCO January 2025 ST Course Recruitment 2024.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Columns Carefully.
6. No Application Fees in Indian Navy Recruitment But. Candidate Must Final Submit the Form.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
