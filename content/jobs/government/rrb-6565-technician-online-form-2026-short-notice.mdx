Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) under the Ministry of Railways have released an indicative Centralised Employment Notification (CEN No. 02/2026) for the recruitment of Technician Grade I Signal and various Technician Grade III posts. Applications will be accepted through online mode only, and a total of 6,565 Technician vacancies are expected to be filled across all RRBs for the 2026-27 recruitment cycle.


## How to Apply

1. Visit your regional RRB website (e.g., Ahmedabad, Ajmer, Bengaluru, Chennai, Secunderabad, etc.).
2. Open the link for Centralised Employment Notification CEN 02/2026 – Technician.
3. Read the detailed notification carefully including post-wise eligibility, instructions and list of RRBs.
4. Register by providing basic details like name, mobile number and email ID.
5. Login using the registration credentials and fill the online application form for the desired post and pay level (only one RRB and one pay level allowed per candidate).
6. Upload required documents, photograph and signature as per specifications. >Pay the prescribed application fee through online mode, if applicable.
7. Submit the application form and take a printout / save PDF for future reference.
8. Each candidate can apply to only one RRB for a specific pay level and only one common online application is allowed for all notified posts in that pay level.
9. Separate applications must be submitted for each pay level, if eligible.
10. Candidates are strongly advised to verify name, date of birth and other details using Aadhaar to avoid mismatches at later stages.
11. RRBs caution candidates to be aware of touts, brokers and job racketeers and to rely only on official RRB websites for genuine information.
