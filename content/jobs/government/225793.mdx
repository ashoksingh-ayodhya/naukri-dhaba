Manipur TET - Notification, Online Application, Call Letter, Result & Other Complete Details: Board of Secondary Education Manipur (BSEM) conducts Manipur Teacher Eligibility Test also known as Manipur TET. Manipur TET is for the candidates aspiring to become teachers in primary (Class I-V) and upper primary (VI-VIII) schools in state of Manipur. TET is a compulsory examination for all the B.Ed qualified candidates to be considered for teacher jobs in schools run by the government and private managements. Senior Secondary/ Graduation is the minimum educational qualification to apply for this examination.
