Join Indian Navy has released the notification of 10th Pass Matric entry MR Agniveer Recruitment 01/2022. Any youth whose age is 17.5 to 23 years and class 10 passed and they want to appear in this Agniveer SSR Recruitment of Navy. Enrolled Candidate Are Check the Cutoff and Eligibile Candidate Can Download the Admit Card. Read the notification for eligibility, age limit, physical details, selection procedure, pay scale, and all other information in recruitment.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Join Indian Navy 10+2 SSR Agniveer Admit Card with Cutoff 2022 for Male / Female 2800 Post. 
A total of **200** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **01/08/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. The recruitment process of Indian Navy Sailor Entry MR December 2022 batch will be online.
2. Any high school pass candidates who want to participate in this Navy Agnipath Scheme Agniveer MR Examination can apply online from 25 July 2022 to 01 August 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
