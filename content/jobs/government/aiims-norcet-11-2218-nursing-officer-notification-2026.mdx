The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi has released the AIIMS NORCET 11 Recruitment 2026 notification for 2,218 tentative Nursing Officer vacancies across AIIMS New Delhi and other participating AIIMS institutes/hospitals. The recruitment will be conducted through the Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test (NORCET)–11. To apply for the Nursing Officer post, candidates must possess the prescribed educational qualifications in Nursing and be between 18 and 30 years of age as specified in the notification.

A total of **2218** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official AIIMS Examination website: www.aiimsexams.ac.in
2. Locate and click on the NORCET-11 online registration link.
3. Register with basic details, or log in using your existing One Time Registration (OTR) ID if previously registered.
4. Fill in the online application form carefully with personal, educational, and category details.
5. Upload photograph, signature, and thumb impression as per the prescribed image guidelines.
6. Pay the applicable application fee online via Debit Card, Credit Card, or Net Banking.
7. Review all entered details in the preview screen, as corrections are not permitted after submission.
8. Submit the application and download/save a copy of the confirmation and fee payment proof for future reference.
9. Candidates already registered under OTR or a previous examination must apply using the same old ID/OTR registration; applying with a different ID will lead to rejection.
10. Candidates employed in Government/Semi-Government/PSU/Autonomous bodies must upload a valid NOC by 18 August 2026 (5:00 PM); failure will lead to cancellation of candidature.
11. No correction is permitted after the specified dates, including changes to category (SC/ST to UR/OBC/EWS) once the registration fee is submitted.
12. No extension of dates will be provided under any circumstances.
13. Any change of allotted examination centre will be summarily rejected.
14. Candidates should rely only on official notices published on www.aiimsexams.ac.in; AIIMS does not authorize any agent, coaching centre, or third-party website for recruitment-related activities.
15. The OBC-NCL certificate must be valid for financial year 2026–2027 and issued between 01.04.2026 and 13.08.2026.
