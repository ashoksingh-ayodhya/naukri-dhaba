Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Vacancy Can Download the Result. The CLAT exam will be held on 18 December 2022. For all other information read the notification.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for CLAT UG / PG Admission Test 2023 Result.

Ram Manohar Lohiya RMLNLU, Lucknow; Chanakya National Law University, CLNU Patna; National Law University Odisha, NLUO Cuttack; NLU and Judicial Academy, Assam, Guwahati; The Tamil Nadu national Law School, Tiruchirappalli as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. CONSORTIUM OF NATIONAL LAW UNIVERSITIES Are Released CLAT UG / PG Admission Test 2023. Candidate Can Apply Between 08/08/2022 to 13/11/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in LLB / BA LLB Admission 2023.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Application Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
