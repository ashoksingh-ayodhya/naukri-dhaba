The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi has released the Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test (NORCET) 11 Notification 2026 for Nursing Officer posts. The recruitment process is conducted for filling Nursing Officer vacancies in AIIMS New Delhi and other participating AIIMS institutes.

The last date to apply is **13-08-2026 (Up to 05:00 PM)**.
Candidates must have B.Sc Nursing / Post Basic B.Sc Nursing / Diploma in General Nursing Midwifery as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official AIIMS Exams website.
2. Open the NORCET 11 online application link.
3. Complete the registration process using required details.
4. Fill out the application form carefully.
5. Upload required documents, photograph and signature.
6. Pay the application fee as applicable.
7. Submit the application form before the last date.
