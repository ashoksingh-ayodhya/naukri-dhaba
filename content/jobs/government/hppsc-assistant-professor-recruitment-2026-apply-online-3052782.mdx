The Himachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (HPPSC) has officially announced the Assistant Professor Recruitment 2026 with 8 openings under Advt. Nos. 7/6-2026, 5/6-2026, and 11/6-2026. Candidates who are not more than 45 years of age and possess a recognized medical qualification as per the IMC Act/NMC Act 2019, along with the required postgraduate and post-doctoral degree in the concerned super-speciality and a minimum of three years of teaching experience after post-graduation, are eligible to apply. Interested candidates can submit their online applications on or before 01 July 2026. Selected candidates will receive a pay scale of ₹37,400–67,000 with Grade Pay ₹8,900 or ₹1,29,700 (Cell-1, Level-30) as per applicable rules. Direct links to download the official notification and apply online are provided below.

The last date to apply is **01-07-2026**.
Candidates must have Recognised medical qualification as per IMC Act/NMC Act 2019, Postgraduate and post-doctoral degree in concerned Super-Speciality, Minimum 3 years teaching experience after post-graduation as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 01-07-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of HPPSC to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
