Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026 has been announced for various contractual posts under State Child Protection Society (SCPS), District Child Protection Units (DCPUs), Juvenile Justice Boards, Child Welfare Committees, and Special Juvenile Police Units! Published vide F. No. DWCD-E021/15/2026-CPU-HQ-WCD- by Department of Women and Child Development (State Child Protection Society), Government of NCT of Delhi, ISBT Building, Kashmiri Gate, Delhi – 110006, this recruitment drive offers vacancies for 10 post categories on a purely contractual basis initially for a period of one year as per provisions of Mission Vatsalya Scheme by Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India. Candidates can apply online from 05-06-2026 (12:01 AM) to 26-06-2026 (11:59 PM). Find all details about the Mission Vatsalya Delhi WCD Recruitment 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

DISTRICT CHILD PROTECTION UNITS (DCPUs) / JUVENILE JUSTICE BOARDS / CHILD WELFARE COMMITTEES / SPECIAL JUVENILE POLICE UNITS has released an official notification for Delhi Anganwadi Recruitment 2026 - Apply Offline for 62 Assistant cum Data Entry Operator, Accountant and More Posts. 

## How to Apply

1. Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (from 12th onwards, including graduation/post-graduation degree, as applicable per post)
2. LLB degree certificate (for Legal cum Probation Officer post)
3. PG Diploma in Counseling and Communication certificate (if applicable for Counselor post)
4. 1-Year Diploma/Certificate in Computer Applications (for ADEO post)
5. Typing speed certificate (minimum 35 WPM in English on computer - for ADEO post)
6. Experience certificates from previous employers certifying post-qualification experience (specifying period, nature of work, and whether regular paid capacity)
7. Age proof (10th certificate or birth certificate)
8. Category certificate - SC/ST/OBC/EWS as applicable (as per Delhi State Government format)
9. Identity proof (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, etc.)
10. Recent passport-size photograph
11. NOC from present employer (for candidates currently employed in Government/WCD, GNCTD)
12. This recruitment is purely on a contractual basis, initially for a period of one year. It is NOT a permanent job with the Government of NCT of Delhi or the Government of India.
13. A candidate shall be eligible to apply for only one post. If a candidate applies for more than one post, ALL applications submitted by the candidate shall be summarily rejected.
14. The Department reserves the right to decrease or increase the number of posts in each category mentioned in the table at any stage/point of time.
15. The Department further reserves the right to terminate this contract work at any stage/point of time.
