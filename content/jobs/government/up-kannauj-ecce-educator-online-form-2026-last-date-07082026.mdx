UP Rojgar Sangam (Sewayojan) Portal has released a new Outsourcing Recruitment 2026 for ECCE Education in the उत्तर प्रदेश बेसिक शिक्षा विभाग. Candidates who are interested in this outsourcing vacancy and are registered on the UP Rojgar Sangam Portal can apply online from 22 July 2026 to 07 August 2026. Read the official notification for eligibility, vacancy details, age limit, monthly remuneration, selection process, and other important information before applying.

A total of **195** posts are available for this recruitment.
