Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (GSRTC) has published the Conductor Notification 2026 on 06 June 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 4318 Conductor positions. Candidates within the age range of 18 to 45 years and holding Class 12 Pass qualification along with a valid Conductor Licence and Badge are eligible to apply online at ojas.gujarat.gov.in. The application process will be active from 06 June 2026 to 05 July 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

A total of **4318** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Class 12 Pass (10+2) from Gujarat Secondary and Higher Secondary Education Board or any equivalent recognized board. Diploma (10+3 or more years after Class 10) is also accepted. ITI (2-year or more NCVT-passed course after Class 10 + English subject passed from GSEB or Gujarat Open School Examination) and 3-year Polytechnic Diploma after Class 10 are also treated as equivalent to Class 12 for government service purposes (with equivalence certificate from Education Board). as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Step 1: Visit https://ojas.gujarat.gov.in → Click "Apply Online" → Click on "Conductor" post to view vacancy details.
2. Step 2: Click "Apply Now" → Fill Personal Details (fields with * are mandatory).
3. Step 3: Click "Educational Qualification" → Fill minimum educational details. Click "Computer Knowledge" → Fill computer qualification details.
4. Step 4: Click "Self Declaration" → Accept terms by clicking "Yes" → Click "Save" to complete online application submission.
5. Step 5: Upload Photo (JPG, max 10 KB; size 5 cm height x 3.6 cm width) and Signature (JPG, max 10 KB; size 2.5 cm height x 7.5 cm width).
6. Step 6: Click "Confirm Application" → Review application via "Show Application Preview" → Edit if required → Click "Confirm Application" to finalize. Note the Confirmation Number generated for all future reference.
7. Step 7: Click "Online Payment of Fees" → Enter Confirmation Number and Date of Birth → Pay fee through Online Payment Gateway.
8. Step 8: Click "Print Application" → Enter Confirmation Number and Date of Birth → Print and save the application for your records.
9. Recent passport-size photo and signature (JPG format as per specifications above)
10. Valid photo ID proof at the exam venue: Election Card / Driving Licence / PAN Card / Aadhaar Card / Government-issued ID Card (any one)
11. Original Hall Ticket (must be brought to the examination venue)
12. Class 12 marksheet and pass certificate
13. Date of birth proof: SSC (Class 10) Board Certificate or School Leaving Certificate (Birth extract/certificate not accepted)
14. Valid Conductor Licence and Badge issued by Regional Transport Authority
15. Computer knowledge certificate/marksheet
