National Testing Agency NTA & Delhi University DU, New Delhi Are Recently Uploaded DUET Admit Card for the Admission for DU Post Graduate Master Degree Various Courses Admission 2021. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Admission Test Can Download the DUET 2021 Admit Card.

National Testing Agency NTA & Delhi University DU, New Delhi Are Recently Uploaded DUET Admit Card for the Admission for DU Post Graduate Master Degree Various Courses Admission 2021. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Admission Test Can Download the DUET 2021 Admit Card. has released an official notification for NTA Delhi University DU PG Course Admission Admit Card 2021. 
The last date to apply is **21/08/2021**.

## How to Apply

1. Delhi University has started online application for admission to Master Degree Courses. Any candidate who wants to apply should first complete the advertisement and then apply only after that.
2. Delhi University Admission online form is available from 27 July 2021 and the last date is 21 August 2021. between these two dates, you can fill the online application form.
3. Kindly Check and College the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
