The Railway recruitment board of Chennai will conduct the online written test (CBT) for the positions like ALP, Group D, ASM, Goods Guard, Traffic Assistant, Commercial Apprentice, lineman and Gagman in the months of May/June 2018. The exam dates will be updated on the official website of the Railway Recruitment Board, Chennai and RRB Chennai ALP Admit card 2018 will be issued online 15 days before the exam.

The Railway recruitment board of Chennai will conduct the has released an official notification for Exam Result. 
A total of **3408** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 10TH ( Matriculation), 12th, SSC, ITI and relevant Qualification as minimum educational qualification.
