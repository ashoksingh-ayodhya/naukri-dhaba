Government Medical College & Hospital Chandigarh (GMCH Chandigarh) Senior Resident / CMO / Resident Anaesthetist / Resident Pathologist Notification 2026 has been released on 01 June 2026 on the official website of GMCH Chandigarh. A total of 64 vacancies have been announced through the GMCH Chandigarh Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have Postgraduate Medical Degree i.e. (M.D./M.S./DNB/M.Ch./D.M) OR equivalent in the concerned specialty (recognized by MCI and registered either with Medical Council of India or State Medical Council) as minimum educational qualification.
