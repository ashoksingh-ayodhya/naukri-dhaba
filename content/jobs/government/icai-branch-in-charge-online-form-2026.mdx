ICAI Branch In-Charge Recruitment 2026 has been announced by The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) for 129 Branch In-Charge posts across its five Regional Councils. The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) invites online applications from graduates for the position of Branch In-Charge under the Branch Human Resource Scheme (BHRS) 2022, vide Advertisement No. 01/2026/HRD/BHRS/07. Online applications start on 3rd July 2026, and the last date to submit the application is 19th July 2026. This article covers the complete overview, important dates, region-wise vacancy details, eligibility criteria, age limit, salary, application fee, selection process, and how to apply for ICAI Branch In-Charge Recruitment 2026.

A total of **129** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Graduate from a recognized University as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Strong communication and interpersonal skills with proficiency in spoken and written English.
2. Proficiency in MS Office and other relevant computer applications.
3. Effective managerial, administrative, and organizational abilities.
4. Strong relationship management and conflict resolution skills.
5. Category-I Branches: Minimum 5 years of experience - Compensation of approximately ₹7.45 lakhs per annum.
6. Category-II Branches: Minimum 5 years of experience - Compensation of approximately ₹6.33 lakhs per annum.
7. Category-III Branches: Minimum 5 years of experience - Compensation of approximately ₹5.59 lakhs per annum.
8. Category-IV Branches: Minimum 3 years of experience - Compensation of approximately ₹4.84 lakhs per annum.
9. Category-V & VI Branches: Minimum 3 years of experience - Compensation of approximately ₹4.10 lakhs per annum.
10. Visit the official ICAI website and locate the Branch In-Charge Recruitment 2026 notification (Advertisement No. 01/2026/HRD/BHRS/07).
11. Click on the online application / "Apply Here" link provided in the notification.
12. Register with a valid, active email ID and mobile number.
13. Fill in the online application form with personal, educational, and experience details.
14. Select your preferred region and give up to three branch location preferences.
15. Upload all required documents in the prescribed format.
