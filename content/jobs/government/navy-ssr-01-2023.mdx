Join Indian Navy has issued advertisement for the recruitment of 1400 posts for Sailor Entry 10+2 Agniveer SSR 01/2023 May 2023 batch. Both male and female candidates can apply for this recruitment. Those Candidates Are Enrolled with Vacancy Can Download the Result, Phase II Admit Card. Read the notification for eligibility, age limit, selection procedure, syllabus and all other details of Navy SSR 1/2023 Recruitment. </td>

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Join Indian Navy Agniveer SSR 01/2023 Batch May Recruitment 2022 Result 2023 for 1400 Post. 
A total of **1400** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **28/12/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Join Indian Navy Sailor Entry Agniveer SSR 01/2023 May Batch. Candidate Can Apply Between 08/12/2022 to 28/12/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Navy Sailor Entry Agniveer SSR 01/2023 May Batch Sailor Recruitment 2022 Apply Online Form for Indian Navy Latest Sailor Agniveer 01/2023 Entry Vacancies 2022-2023.
3. Photo Instruction : Passport size recent colour photograph (taken not before November 2022) of size 10 KB to 50 KB (front portrait in light background without head gear except for Sikhs). The photograph is to be taken with candidate holding a black slate in front of his/her chest with his her name and date of photograph taken, clearly written on it with white chalk in capital letters. Change in appearance like growing beard, head gear, etc in comparison to the photograph may result in cancellation of candidature.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
