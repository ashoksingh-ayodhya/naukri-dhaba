The Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) has announced the admission schedule for the upcoming session 2014-15 for all the candidates who wish to participate in the entrance exams of the university. The university will be officially inviting the applications for the university admissions through online registrations available at the website of the university from 7th February 2014. The candidates can also purchase offline applications forms from the university offices from the same date. The application forms will not be available after 26th March 2014 for the candidates to apply for the university course programmes. The entrance examinations for all the university courses will be conducted in May 2014 respectively.
