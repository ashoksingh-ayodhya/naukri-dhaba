## How to Apply

1. Recruitment of Madhya Pradesh ESB for the posts of Group-5 Staff Nurse , Paramedical and Other Post Combined Recruitment Test – 2024 The applications of Recruitment will be online from 30/12/2024 to 16/01/2025
2. Along with Madhya Pradesh, candidates from other states can also apply (As per Group V Recruitment 2024 Rules) .
3. To apply in Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board, the candidate has to first create a profile of himself. To create a profile, the candidate wants to have Aadhar card number and also want to have registered mobile number in it, only then there will be E-KYC in the profile, those whose mobile number is not registered, they will have to create a profile through biometric through MP Online Kiosk. .
4. For the application of recruitment in Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board, along with the profile, all the candidates will also have to register in the Madhya Pradesh Employment Office (If Required) .
5. For More Details Read the Full Notification.
