Air Force Join Indian Air Force has released the advertisement of rally recruitment to the post of Medical Assistant Group Y Rally Recruitment 2024 Intake 01/2025. All the candidates who enrolled this recruitment can download the admit card / hall ticket. See advertisement for other recruitment related information like syllabus, qualification, age limit, selection procedure.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Indian Airforce Medical Assistant Group Y Rally Recruitment 2024 Admit Card. 
The last date to apply is **05/06/2024**.

## How to Apply

1. Indian Airforce Are Released the Notification for Medical Assistant Y Group Intake 01/2025 Recruitment 2024. Candidate Can Apply Online Between 22/05/2024 to 05/06/2024.
2. Photo Instruction : Passport size recent colour photograph (not taken before six months)) of size 10 KB to 50 KB (front portrait in light background without head gear except for Sikhs). The photograph is to be taken with candidate holding a black slate in front of his chest with his name and date of photograph taken, clearly written on it with white chalk in capital letter.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Rally Group Y Intake 01/2025 Online Form 2024.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
