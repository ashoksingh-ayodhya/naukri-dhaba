Hindustan Shipyard Limited (HSL), Visakhapatnam - 530 005, a Government of India Undertaking functioning under the Ministry of Defence and pioneer in Shipbuilding and Ship Repair, has officially released Advertisement No. HR/ES(O)/0102/04/2026 dated 03 June 2026 for recruitment of suitably qualified and experienced personnel for various posts on Permanent Absorption and Fixed Term Contract (FTC) basis. HSL has completed eighty-five years of service to the Nation. Candidates are strongly advised to download and read the full advertisement before applying online.

A total of **34** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. SSC (10th) Marks Lists / Certificate
2. Intermediate / Diploma Marks Lists
3. Degree / PG Marks Lists and Provisional/Degree Certificate
4. Proof of Date of Birth
5. Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC in prescribed format) / PH Certificate (if applicable)
6. Experience Certificates (for past employment: including date of joining and relieving; for current employment: Appointment/Offer Letter + Latest Pay Slip)
7. Most recent salary certificate / CTC proof
8. Annual Turnover proof of present working company (for Private Sector candidates)
9. No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer (mandatory for Permanent post applicants working in Government organizations)
10. Annexure-I (Experience Details as given in the notification)
11. Photograph (20-50 KB) and Signature (10-20 KB) in prescribed format
12. Only Indian Nationals need to apply. Applications sent other than through the prescribed online method will be rejected.
13. All vacancies are for Visakhapatnam except F2 (Dy. Project Superintendent - Delhi Region). Officers selected may be transferred to other stations for projects up to 2 years in one stretch and may need to undertake outstation duties to Delhi/Yards/Vendor Premises.
14. Aggregate percentage of marks shall NOT be rounded off (e.g., 64.99% will not be treated as 65%).
15. Training period, Teaching experience, Apprenticeship/Internship, Sabbatical, and Extra-ordinary leave will NOT be counted towards work experience.
