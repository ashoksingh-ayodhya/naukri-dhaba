The CSIR Institute of Genomics & Integrative Biology (CSIR IGIB) has officially announced the Technician (Gr.-II) Recruitment 2026 with 15 openings under Advt. No. 01/2026. Candidates who are not more than 28 years of age and possess a Graduate degree in Genomics, Molecular Medicine, Bioinformatics, Proteomics, or related disciplines are eligible to apply. Interested applicants can submit their online applications on or before 04 July 2026. Selected candidates will receive a salary in the pay scale of ₹19,900–₹63,200. Direct links to download the official notification and apply online are provided below.

The last date to apply is **04-07-2026**.
Candidates must have Graduate in Genomics, Molecular Medicine, Bioinformatics and Proteomics. as minimum educational qualification.
