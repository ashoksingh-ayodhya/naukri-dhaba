Bihar State Cooperative Bank has issued the Admit Card for phase III selection process i.e. Interview for the post of Assistant (Multipurpose). Admit Cards are now available at the official website of Bihar State Co-operative Bank Limited. Candidates who have applied for the post of Assistant (Multipurpose) are advised to visit the official website of Bihar State Cooperative Bank in order to download the Admit Card. The last date for downloading the Admit Card is 24-09-2016.
