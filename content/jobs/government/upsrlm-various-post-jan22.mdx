;”>Uttar Pradesh State Rural Livelihoods Mission has released the advertisement for the recruitment of various posts of State / District and Block level. Eligible candidates who want to appear in this recruitment can apply online from 25 January 2022 to 25 February 2022. For other information related to recruitment, candidates should see and read the advertisement and apply only after that.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for UPSRLM Various Post Recruitment 2022 Online Form 2022. 
A total of **1736** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **25/02/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Candidates Must be Enrolled in UP Sewayojan Karyalaya (Rojgar Sangam) Portal.
2. Uttar Pradesh State Rural Livelihoods Mission UPSRLM State / District & Block Level Recruitment 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 25 January 2022 to 25 February 2022.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in UPSRLM Latest Jobs 2022.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
