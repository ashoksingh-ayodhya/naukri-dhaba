Candidates aged between 18 and 28 years who possess a graduate degree in Library & Information Science or any discipline from a recognized university are eligible to apply for these Graduate Apprentice vacancies. The offline application process for IIMC Graduate Apprentice Recruitment 2026 is currently open and will remain available until 22 June 2026.

Candidates must have Graduate degree in Library & Information Science or any discipline. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.iimc.gov.in.
3. Applications will be considered who opted Delhi preference for written test / personal interview / skill test.
4. Candidates claiming reservation must submit certificate as per Central Government format.
5. Mandatory to enroll on National Graduate Apprenticeship Training Scheme (NATS) portal (https://nats.education.gov.in) before applying.
6. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
7. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
8. Check all details carefully before submission.
9. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
10. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 22-06-2026.
11. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
