Candidates who possess qualifications such as MBA/ PG Degree in Management/ Administration, Company Secretaryship (ICSI), LLB, M.Com (Regular), or CA/CMA Intermediate along with the prescribed work experience for the respective posts are eligible to apply for these Administrative Officer and Accounts Executive vacancies. The online application process for CMD Kerala Recruitment 2026 has started on 02 June 2026 and will remain open until 15 June 2026.

The last date to apply is **15-06-2026**.
Candidates must have MBA/PG in Management/Administration, Company Secretaryship (ICSI), LLB, M.Com (Regular), CA/CMA Intermediate as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 15-06-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.

5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
