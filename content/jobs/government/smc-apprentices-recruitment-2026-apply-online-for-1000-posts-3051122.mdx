Surat Municipal Corporation - SMC has officially released the Apprentice Recruitment 2026 notification (Advt. No. PRO/48/2026-27) for 1000 vacancies across Surat, Gujarat. Eligible ITI pass, diploma holders, and graduates can apply online from 01 June 2026 to 10 June 2026 via the official portal suratmunicipal.gov.in after mandatory registration on apprenticeshipindia.gov.in. Selected candidates will be appointed on an apprenticeship basis with a monthly stipend of ₹9,600 to ₹12,300 depending on the trade and qualification level.

Department has released an official notification for SMC Apprentices Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 1000 Posts. 
A total of **1000** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have ITI Trade Pass (for trades 1–9) OR relevant graduation/degree as specified trade-wise (for trades 10–14). Refer vacancy table for trade-specific qualification details. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Printed copy of the online application form
2. All original educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (ITI Trade Certificate / Degree Certificate as applicable to the trade applied for)
3. Date of Birth certificate / School Leaving Certificate
4. NAPS portal registration and eKYC completion proof
5. Caste/category certificate as applicable
6. Valid Photo ID proof
7. Self-attested copies of all above documents
8. THIS IS NOT A PERMANENT EMPLOYMENT. The apprenticeship period is as per the contract generated on the Government website and candidates will be immediately discharged on completion of the apprenticeship period.
9. Mandatory NAPS portal registration with eKYC update must be completed before applying on the SMC website. Applications submitted without prior NAPS registration will not be processed.
10. Candidates who have already completed an apprenticeship in any trade are not eligible — online contract generation will not be possible for such candidates.
11. Stipend payable to apprentices is subject to Government policy and guidelines as amended from time to time.
12. Document verification date and intimations will be sent only to the registered mobile number and email ID provided in the application. In case of change in mobile number or email, SMC will not be responsible for any future communication gaps.
13. Candidates must appear for document verification with both original documents and self-attested copies. Absence from document verification will result in automatic cancellation of candidature.
14. If, during scrutiny of application form and documents, any candidate is found not meeting the eligibility criteria under the Apprentices Act or the notification provisions, or if any discrepancy is found between the application and original certificates, candidature will be cancelled at any stage.
15. If a candidate leaves the apprenticeship before completion of the period, the contract will be terminated.
