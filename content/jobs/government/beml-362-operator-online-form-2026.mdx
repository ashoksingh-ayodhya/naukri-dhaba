BEML Limited has invited online applications for 362 Operator posts on a tenure basis under Advt No. KP/S/10/2026, dated 17 June 2026, for candidates holding ITI certificates in Fitter, Turner, Welder, Machinist, and Electrician trades. The online application closes at 18:00 Hrs on 10 July 2026, and eligibility (age, qualification, experience) is reckoned as on the same date. This BEML Non-Executive Recruitment 2026 is open to Indian Nationals only, with category-wise vacancies and age limits as detailed below.

A total of **362** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 10 July 2026 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the BEML career page at www.bemlindia.in
2. Locate the online application form under Advt No. KP/S/10/2026
3. Register using a valid personal e-mail ID and mobile number that will remain active for at least one year
4. Fill in personal, educational, and category details accurately
5. Upload all mandatory documents (listed below) in the prescribed format
6. Pay the applicable application fee online (GEN/EWS/OBC candidates only)
7. Submit the form and note down the generated Registration Number for future reference
8. Recent photograph and signature (on clear white background, signature in black ink)
9. 10th Standard (X-th) marks card
10. ITI - National Trade Certificate (NTC); provisional certificate accepted if final certificate is not yet issued
11. All ITI marks cards as a single PDF (CGPA-to-percentage conversion certificate required, if applicable)
12. National Apprenticeship Certificate (NAC) issued by DGT
13. Experience certificate(s) clearly indicating start and end dates, combined as a single PDF
14. Identity card issued by the Government of India (Aadhaar, Passport, Driving Licence, PAN Card, etc.)
15. Caste/Category Certificate (SC/ST/OBC-NCL/EWS) and PwD Certificate, as applicable
