NKGSB Bank Clerk Recruitment 2026: NKGSB Co-operative Bank Ltd., a leading multi-state scheduled cooperative bank with over 108 years of legacy, has released an official notification inviting applications for 30 vacancies for the post of Clerk (Branch Operations). The vacancies are spread across branches in Mumbai, Thane, Navi Mumbai, and Gujarat.

A total of **30** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Maximum Age as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Prepare your updated resume.
2. Collect the following details in a plain format (not just resume - the bank wants this data separately): Full Name Residential Address Qualification Percentage Scored Date of Birth Email ID Mobile Number
3. Residential Address
4. Qualification
5. Percentage Scored
6. Date of Birth
7. Mobile Number
8. Compose an email with both your resume and the above data pasted in the email body or as an attachment.
9. In the email subject line, clearly mention: 'Post applied for: CLERK'
10. Send to: [email protected]
11. Ensure you send before June 11, 2026.
12. Conducted by an external agency (not directly by the bank)
13. Test format, syllabus, and exam date will be communicated to shortlisted candidates via their registered email ID after application scrutiny
