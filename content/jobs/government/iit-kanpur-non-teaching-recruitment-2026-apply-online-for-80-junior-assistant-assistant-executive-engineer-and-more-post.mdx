IIT Kanpur Recruitment 2026 has been announced for various Non-Teaching Staff posts on Regular basis! Published on 02-06-2026 vide Advt. No. 1/2026, this new recruitment drive offers 80 openings across 12 post categories (Group A, B, and C) in Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur - an Institute of national importance under the Institutes of Technology Act, 1961. Applicants with qualifications ranging from Bachelor's Degree to Master's Degree / M.Tech. / MBBS (post-wise) can apply online up to 03-07-2026 (11:59 PM). Find all details about the IIT Kanpur Non-Teaching Staff new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for IIT Kanpur Non Teaching Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 80 Junior Assistant, Assistant Executive Engineer and More Posts. 
A total of **80** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Closing date of online application (03-07-2026) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent passport-size photograph (scanned, clear and legible)
2. Signature (scanned)
3. Proof of age (10th/Matriculation/Secondary School Leaving Certificate or equivalent)
4. Educational qualification certificates and mark sheets (all degrees from qualifying degree onwards)
5. Experience Certificates (on official letterhead, in chronological order, with designation, period, nature of duties, and seal/stamp) - post-qualification experience only
6. SC/ST Caste Certificate in Central Government prescribed format (for SC/ST applicants)
7. OBC (NCL) Certificate in prescribed format, clearly specifying validity period and valid as on the closing date (for OBC NCL applicants)
8. Income and Asset Certificate (EWS) issued by Competent Authority, valid as on the closing date (for EWS applicants)
9. Valid UDID/Disability Certificate issued by Competent Authority (for PwD applicants)
10. All original certificates and mark sheets (educational qualifications, age proof, experience)
11. Original category/disability certificates (SC/ST/OBC/EWS/PwD)
12. No Objection Certificate (NOC) from present employer (for candidates in Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body)
13. Vigilance Clearance Certificate from present employer (for candidates in Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body)
14. Only Indian Nationals are eligible. All qualifications must be from a recognized University/Institution.
15. Experience claimed must be post-qualification, full-time work experience. Only full-time work experience will be considered; the Institute's decision on relevance of experience is final.
