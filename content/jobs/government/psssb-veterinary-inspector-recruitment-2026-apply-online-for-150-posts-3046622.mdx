The Subordinate Services Selection Board, Punjab (SSSB Punjab) has re-opened the recruitment process for Veterinary Inspector (Group-C) posts under SSSB Punjab Recruitment 2026. Originally published on 24 April 2026 at sssb.punjab.gov.in, this recruitment drive offers 150 vacancies for eligible candidates. Applicants meeting the age limit prescribed under Punjab Government rules and possessing the required Veterinary/Animal Husbandry qualification as per departmental service rules can apply online through the official PSSSB career portal. Registration re-opened on June 6, 2026, and the last date to apply and pay the application fee is June 19, 2026. Check complete details about the SSSB Punjab Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026, including eligibility, application process, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for PSSSB Veterinary Inspector Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 150 Posts. 
A total of **150** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 25.05.2026 (last date of application) as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official SSSB Punjab website: https://sssb.punjab.gov.in and click on the “Apply Online” link.
2. For new users, click on “Registration” and fill in the required details to generate User ID (Email ID) and Password.
3. After successful registration, log in using your User ID (Email ID) and Password to access the online application form.
4. Fill in personal, educational and category details carefully and upload a recent passport size photograph and scanned signature.
5. Click on “Attach Annexure” and upload scanned copies of Matric (10th) certificate, educational qualification certificates and other mandatory documents in PDF format.
6. Click on “Make Payment” and pay the application fee online as per your category.
7. After successful fee payment, click on “Submit” to complete the application process and download/print the submitted application form for future reference.
8. Fill the online form strictly as per your correct category and category code; category and category code cannot be changed after final submission.
9. Candidates will be solely responsible for any mistake made while filling the online form; once submitted, the data cannot be edited.
10. Scan and upload all required certificates (Matric/10th, educational qualification and other relevant documents) in PDF format only as per the portal instructions.
11. No fee exemption or concession is admissible; non-payment of application fee will render the application incomplete and liable to rejection.
12. If the online fee transaction fails for any reason and the application is not submitted, the fee once paid will be non-refundable.
13. Applications submitted and fees paid through any mode other than the prescribed online method will not be accepted and such applications will be rejected.
14. SSSB Punjab reserves the right to increase or decrease the number of vacancies, withdraw the requisition or cancel the advertisement at any stage of the recruitment process; such decisions will be binding on all candidates.
