National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (CSIR NEERI) has released the official CSIR NEERI Project Associate I Recruitment 2026 notification for 2 Project Associate I posts on a project basis. This recruitment offers an excellent opportunity for candidates with M.Sc. in Chemistry / Environmental Science or B.E / B.Tech in Civil, Environmental, or Chemical Engineering to work in a reputed government research institute under the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR).

Candidates must have M.Sc, B.Tech,B.E as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 03-04-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of CSIR NEERI to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
