Defence Research & Development Organization DRDO Online Are Invited Online Application Form for the Trade Apprentice Recruitment 2020. Those ITI Candidates Are Interested to the Following Vacancy and Completed the All Eligibility Criteria Can Read the Full Notification and Apply Online.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for DRDO Apprentice Online Form 2020. 
A total of **116** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **03/04/2020 upto 05 PM**.

## How to Apply

1. DRDO Apprentice Various Post Recruitment Online Form Candidate Can Apply Between March 2020 to 03/04/2020
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in DRDO RAS Career Section.
3. Kindly Check and College the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form. and Do Not Send to Conducted Authorities.
