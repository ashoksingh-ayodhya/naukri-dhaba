Indira Gandhi Institute of Medical Sciences (IGIMS) (IGIMS) Junior Resident (Non Academic) Notification 2026 has been released on the official website IGIMS. A total of 18 vacancies have been announced through the IGIMS Junior Resident (Non Academic) Recruitment 2026.

Candidates must have MBBS including completion of Internship or equivalent degree recognized by NMC, Appeared in NEET-PG 2025 but did not get admission to any Medical College as per rules & regulations of Govt. of Bihar as minimum educational qualification.
