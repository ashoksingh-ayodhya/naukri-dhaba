Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar (IIT Gandhinagar) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 1 Program Associate-I (Grants Management) Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website.
