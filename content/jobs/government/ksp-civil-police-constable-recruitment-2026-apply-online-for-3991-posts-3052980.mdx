The Karnataka State Police Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Police Constable (Civil) posts! Published on 05-06-2026 by the Karnataka State Police, Bengaluru, this recruitment drive offers a total of 3991 vacancies - 596 posts under the Kalyana Karnataka (371(J) area) cadre and 3395 posts under the Mikkili (Okkalida) cadre - for eligible candidates. Applicants within the 18 to 33 years age group (with relaxations) possessing a PUC or equivalent qualification can apply online from 08-06-2026 to 03-07-2026. Find all details about Karnataka Police Constable Recruitment 2026, including eligibility, vacancy details, exam pattern, and notification PDF here.

A total of **3991** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have PUC (Pre-University Certificate) or equivalent qualification (as on last date of application). Candidates appearing in PUC exam and awaiting results are NOT eligible. All subjects and marks must have been secured by the last date. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent passport-size photograph in JPEG format (50 KB to 200 KB)
2. Signature in black ink on white paper in JPEG format (50 KB to 70 KB)
3. Valid mobile number and e-mail ID (must remain active until recruitment process is complete)
4. Aadhaar Card (mandatory)
5. Marks cards and pass certificates of all subjects/semesters of the qualified post (as on last date of application)
6. Date of birth proof: SSLC / 10th standard marks card or relevant document
7. Discharge certificate and pension book / pension payment order (for Ex-Servicemen candidates)
8. Caste / Reservation certificates in prescribed Government of India formats (SC/ST: Form 'D'; Cat-1: Form 'E'; Cat-2A/2B/3A/3B: Form 'F') - must be currently valid and issued by Tahsildar or competent authority with seal, signature, and bar code/hologram
9. Rural reservation certificate (Form 1 or Form 2 as applicable) - signed by school Headmaster and Field Education Officer
10. Kannada Medium Study certificate (for KMS reservation), if applicable
11. Project Displaced Person (PDP) certificate, if applicable
12. Income and caste certificate from Tahsildar in prescribed format (for OBC categories)
13. NOC / Service certificate from employer (for candidates currently in service)
14. One original photo identity proof: Passport / Driving License / PAN Card / Service ID Card issued by State/Central Govt., PSU or Private Companies / Bank Passbook with Photo / Registered Deeds with Photo / Caste Certificate with Photo / Ex-servicemen's Pension Book / NREGS Job Card / Voter ID (EPIC) / Aadhaar Card
15. Candidates applying online must verify all information carefully before final submission; take a printout of the application and retain the fee challan until completion of the recruitment process. No changes/modifications to the submitted application will be permitted thereafter.
