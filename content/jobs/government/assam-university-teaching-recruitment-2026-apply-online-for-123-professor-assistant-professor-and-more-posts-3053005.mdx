The Assam University Silchar Recruitment 2026 has been officially announced for Teaching Positions (Professor, Associate Professor, and Assistant Professor) at Silchar HQ and Diphu Campus. Published on 26 May 2026 via Advertisement No. 2/2026, this major recruitment drive offers excellent opportunities for eligible Indian Citizens and Overseas Citizens of India (OCIs) with strong academic credentials. Online applications are invited from 06 June 2026 to 10 July 2026. Find all details about Assam University Teaching Vacancy 2026, eligibility, specializations, pay matrix, application process, and official notification PDF here.

Download Assam University Silchar Recruitment 2026 Official Notification PDF has released an official notification for Assam University Teaching Recruitment 2026 - Apply Online for 123 Professor, Assistant Professor and More Posts. 
A total of **123** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Closing date of online application as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit https://curec.samarth.ac.in/ and register.
2. Fill the online application form carefully (Part-A & Part-B).
3. Upload photograph, signature, and all relevant documents.
4. Pay the application fee (if applicable).
5. Download the PDF of the submitted application.
6. Submit Hard Copy (mandatory) along with self-attested documents to: Deputy Registrar, Room No. 103, Raja Rammohan Roy Administrative Building, Assam University, Silchar – 788011, Cachar, Assam by Speed Post / Registered Post.
7. Proof of age, educational qualifications, experience certificates, caste/disability certificates, publications, etc.
8. Hard copy submission is mandatory.
9. Separate application for each post.
10. Canvassing in any form leads to disqualification.
11. University reserves the right to withdraw/cancel posts.
12. No correspondence regarding rejection will be entertained.
13. Jurisdiction for disputes: High Court, Guwahati.
14. Candidates with strong academic records, Ph.D., and publications in reputed journals.
15. Those meeting UGC norms and specific departmental specializations.
