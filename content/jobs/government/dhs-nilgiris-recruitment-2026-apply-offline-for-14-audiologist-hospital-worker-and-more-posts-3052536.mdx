District Health Society Nilgiris (DHS Nilgiris) has officially released the Audiologist, Hospital Worker and More Posts Recruitment 2026 notification for 14 vacancies in The Nilgiris district of Tamil Nadu. Eligible candidates possessing qualifications ranging from 8th Standard to a Bachelor's Degree in relevant disciplines can apply offline on or before 15 June 2026. Selected candidates will be appointed on a contractual basis with a monthly salary ranging from ₹11,000 to ₹32,000.

Candidates must have Minimum 8th standard to Bachelor's degree in relevant fields. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: nilgiris.nic.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 15-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
