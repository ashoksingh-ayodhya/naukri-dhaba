## How to Apply

1. The first is to fill the application of up learning license online.
2. After that fees have to be paid the UP Learning License Fees.
3. Then you have to select the slot where the learning test is to be held.
4. After booking the slot, print out all Document (Receipt / Online Form / Form)
5. Now you have to take the exam by going to the respective RTO office on the day you book the Slot for UP Learning License.
6. You will get your result immediately after the exam
7. Now after a few days you can download your learning license online
