District Magistrate Birbhum has released the official Technical Assistant and Multi-Tasking Staff Notification 2026 at the official website. A total of 22 vacancies have been announced under the District Magistrate Birbhum Technical Assistant and Multi-Tasking Staff Recruitment 2026. Eligible candidates aged between 21 and 40 years with a qualification of Graduation in any discipline along with computer knowledge, MS Office proficiency, typing skills, and relevant work experience can apply offline on or before 12 June 2026. The official notification PDF and application form are available below.

The last date to apply is **12-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Graduate in any discipline with knowledge of Computer and MS Office Packages, Typing Speed, and minimum 1 year of experience for TA. Graduate in any discipline with basic knowledge of Computer and MS Office Packages, Typing Speed, English writing ability, and minimum 1 year of experience for MTS. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.birbhum.nic.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 12-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
