District Programe Officer, Shamli with Bal Vikas Seva Evam Pustahar Vibhag, Govt. Of Uttar Pradesh Are Invited to Online Application Form for the Recruitment Post of Aganwadi Bharti Worker, Mini Worker and Helper in Shamli District Recruitment 2021. Those Candidates Are Interested to the Following Recruitment Can Read the Full Notification Before Apply Online.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Uttar Pradesh Shamli District Aganwadi Bharti Online Form 2021. 
A total of **122** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **11/06/2021**.

## How to Apply

1. Bal Vikas Seva Evam Pustahar Vibhag ICDS UP Shamli District Recruitment for Various Post Candidate Can Apply Between 21/05/2021 to 11/06/2021.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in UP Shamli Aganwadi Bharti Recruitment 2021.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
