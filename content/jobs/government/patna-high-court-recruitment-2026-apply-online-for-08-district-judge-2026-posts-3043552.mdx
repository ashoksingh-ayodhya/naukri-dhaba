Patna High Court has released the official notification for the District Judge (Entry Level), Direct From Bar Exam-2026 Recruitment to fill 8 permanent District Judge posts through direct recruitment from eligible Advocates and Judicial Officers. This is a prestigious judiciary opportunity for candidates with 7 years or more of legal practice or judicial service, offering a high-level pay scale under Level J-5 of the judicial pay matrix.

The last date to apply is **25-05-2026**.
Candidates must have Advocates/Judicial Officers with 7 years of practice or more. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Applications must be submitted online on or before 25-05-2026.
2. To apply directly, use the Apply Online link provided in the Important Links section.
3. Alternatively, candidates may visit the official website of Patna High Court to complete the application.
4. Fill out the application form carefully with correct personal, educational, and contact details.
5. Upload required documents such as photograph, signature, and certificates in the prescribed format.
6. Pay the application fee online, if applicable.
7. Review all details carefully before submitting the form.
8. Take a printout or save a copy of the submitted application for future reference.
