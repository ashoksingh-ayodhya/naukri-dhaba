ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Faridabad, NIT-3 (under Employees' State Insurance Corporation, Ministry of Labour & Employment, Government of India) has released a Recruitment Advertisement dated 02.06.2026 (File No: 134/A/19/11/91/2025/Admin (Part-1)) for recruitment of Senior Residents in Broad Speciality Departments on a contractual basis. A total of 83 vacancies have been announced across 19 departments including Anesthesia, ICU, NICU, PICU, General Medicine, Radiology, OBGY, Pediatrics, and others.

Senior Residents in Broad Speciality Departments has released an official notification for ESIC Medical College Faridabad Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 – Walk in for 83 Posts. 
A total of **83** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have As per the latest guidelines of the National Medical Commission (NMC/MCI). MBBS with MD/MS/DNB or equivalent Post Graduate Degree in the concerned speciality from a recognised institution. All qualifications to be supported by Marks Sheets, Attempt Certificate, and Degree Certificate. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Download the prescribed application form from the official notification.
2. Fill in the application form completely and correctly for each post applied for. A separate application must be submitted for each post.
3. Prepare self-attested copies of all required documents as specified in Clause (J) of the notification.
4. Prepare a Demand Draft of ₹500/- (for applicable categories) in favour of "ESI Fund A/c No. 1" payable at Faridabad.
5. Report at Faculty Reading Hall, Academic Block, ESIC Medical College & Hospital, Faridabad on 08.06.2026 between 9:00 AM and 10:00 AM with the completed application form, self-attested document copies, Demand Draft, and all original certificates for document verification.
6. Certificate of passing Class 10th (for Date of Birth)
7. All Marks Sheets, Attempt Certificate, and Degree Certificate of MBBS
8. All Marks Sheets, Attempt Certificate, and Degree Certificate of MD/MS/DNB or Other Examination
9. Valid EWS/OBC-NCL/SC/ST/PwD Certificate issued in format for appointment in Central Government job (wherever applicable); failing which candidate will not be allowed to appear for reserved category but may be treated as UR candidate
10. State/NMC Medical Registration Certificate (updated)
11. Experience Certificate
12. Recent Passport Size Photograph
13. Relieving/NOC Certificate from previous/current employer (if applicable)
14. Aadhaar Card
15. These vacancies are provisional and may increase or decrease as per actual patient care requirements. The Competent Authority reserves the right of any amendment, cancellation, or changes to this advertisement without prior notice.
