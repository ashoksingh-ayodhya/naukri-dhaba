VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Senior Resident posts on Adhoc basis through Walk-in-Interview! Published on 03-06-2026 vide File No. ACDM-13016/1/2026-ACAD-VMMC, this recruitment drive offers 179 total openings across 38 specialties/departments of VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital and its satellite centre RHTC, Najafgarh, New Delhi. Candidates possessing MBBS with PG Degree/Diploma/DNB in the relevant specialty recognized by NMC and registered with Delhi Medical Council (or applied for registration) can appear for Walk-in-Interview scheduled from 15-06-2026 to 25-06-2026. Find all details about the VMMC Safdarjung Hospital Senior Resident new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, walk-in schedule, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital Senior Resident Recruitment 2026 – Walk in for 179 Posts. 
A total of **179** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Duly filled Application Form as per Annexure-I with one recent passport size photograph affixed
2. Secondary School Certificate (10th class) - for age proof
3. MBBS Degree / Provisional Pass Certificate
4. MD/MS/DNB/MDS/Diploma Degree / Provisional Pass Certificate
5. Permanent DMC Registration Certificate for both MBBS and PG (or acknowledgement of registration applied for)
6. SC/ST/OBC Caste Certificate (if applicable) in prescribed Central Government format
7. OBC Certificate issued on or after 01-04-2026 in DOPT prescribed format for Central Govt. posts (as per Annexure-II) - OBC candidates must submit the latest certificate
8. EWS Income & Asset Certificate dated on or after 01.04.2026 (valid for year 2026-27) as per Annexure-III + Copy of Form 16 / ITR for FY 2025-26 + income proof for parents, siblings (below 18 yrs), spouse, and children (below 18 yrs)
9. Experience certificate, if any
10. No Objection Certificate from present employer (if currently employed in Central Govt./State Govt./PSU/Autonomous Body)
11. PwBD Certificate, if applicable
12. This is an Adhoc appointment - initially for 89 days, extendable for further 89 days subject to satisfactory work & conduct report from concerned HOD. Maximum tenure is 3 years including all prior SR service in any Govt. of India/State Government hospital/institution.
13. All Senior Residents recruited, including those recruited against VMMC & SJH posts, will be posted in RHTC, Najafgarh on brief rotational basis as per requirement. Teaching experience certificate will be issued by VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital.
14. Candidates must appear for only one specialty/department - appearing in more than one specialty will lead to cancellation of candidature.
15. The crucial date for determination of eligibility with reference to age, educational qualification, and experience is the date of walk-in-interview for the respective specialty.
