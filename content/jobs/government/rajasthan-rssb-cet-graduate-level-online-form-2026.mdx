1. Rajasthan Staff Selection Board Released RSSB CET Graduate Level Recruitment 2026 Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the RSSB CET Graduate Level Examination 2026 . Candidate Can Apply Online for 04/07/2026 to 02/08/2026.
2. Visit the official website of RSSB CET Graduate Level (https://rssb.rajasthan.gov.in/)
3. Click on “RSSB CET Graduate Level 2026 Apply Online” Link
4. Register using a valid email ID and mobile number
5. Login using registration ID and password
6. Fill the application form with personal, educational, and category details
7. Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, etc.)
8. Submit the form and download/print the confirmation page
9. Candidate Read the RSSB CET Graduate Level Notification 2026 Before Apply the RSSB CET Graduate Level Application Form 2026 in Rojgar Result Latest Job Section.
