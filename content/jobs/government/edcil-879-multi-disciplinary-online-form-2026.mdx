EdCIL (India) Ltd. (A Mini Ratna Category-I CPSE, Government of India) has published the Multidisciplinary Staff Notification 2026 on 22 May 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 879 Multidisciplinary Staff and PMU Consultant positions. Candidates within the age range of up to 45 years and holding the prescribed qualifications from recognized institutions are eligible to apply online at the official EdCIL recruitment portal. The application process will be active from 22 May 2026 to 04 June 2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and apply link below.

A total of **875** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the online recruitment portal at https://tsgrecruitment.edcil.co.in/login.
2. Register and fill in the online application form with accurate and complete details of personal information, qualifications and experience.
3. Upload all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, resume and other supporting documents in PDF format only.
4. Verify all details before final submission, as screening and shortlisting will be based only on the information and documents submitted.
5. Submit the online application on or before 04 June 2026.
