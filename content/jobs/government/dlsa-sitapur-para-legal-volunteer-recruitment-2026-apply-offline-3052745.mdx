District Legal Services Authority, Sitapur (DLSA Sitapur) Para Legal Volunteer Notification 2026 has been released through the official website of DLSA Sitapur. Applications are invited from eligible candidates for engagement as Para Legal Volunteers (PLVs) under the DLSA Sitapur Para Legal Volunteer Recruitment 2026.

The last date to apply is **25-06-2026**.
Candidates must have No specific educational qualification required; interest in social service for weaker sections as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: www.district.ecourts.gov.in/sitapur.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 25-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
