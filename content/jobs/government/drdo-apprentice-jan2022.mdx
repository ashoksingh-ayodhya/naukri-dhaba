DRDO Research Center Imarat – (RCI) has released the advertisement for Engagement of Graduate, Diploma and ITI Trade Apprentices 2022. Any candidate who wants to apply in this apprentice recruitment can do it from 25 January 2022 to 07 February 2022. For other information related to recruitment, see the advertisement and apply only after that.

A total of **150** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **07/02/2022**.

1. DRDO GTRE Apprentice Degree / Diploma / ITI Trainee Recruitment 2022-23. Candidate Can Apply Between 25/01/2022 to 07/02/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in DRDO Apprentice Trainee 2022 in Various Trade Vacancy 2022-2023.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Columns Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
