The latest State Bank of India (SBI) Recruitment 2026 has been announced for Apprentice posts! Published on 19-05-2026 at the official website of SBI, this new recruitment drive offers 7150 openings for eligible candidates. Applicants within the 20 to 28 years age group and possessing a Graduation degree from a recognized University/Institute can apply online from 19-05-2026 to 08-06-2026. Find all details about the SBI Apprentice new vacancy 2026, including eligibility, online form, and notification PDF here.

Department has released an official notification for SBI Apprentice Recruitment 2026 Notification Out - Apply Online for 7150 Posts. 
Candidates must have Graduation from a recognized University / Institute. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent passport-size colour photograph (4.5 cm × 3.5 cm; 200×230 pixels; 20 KB–50 KB; JPEG)
2. Signature with black ink on white paper (140×60 pixels; 10 KB–20 KB; JPEG)
3. Left Thumb Impression on white paper with black or blue ink (240×240 pixels; 20 KB–50 KB; JPEG/JPG)
4. Handwritten Declaration in English on white paper with black ink (800×400 pixels; 50 KB–100 KB; JPEG/JPG) — in candidate's own handwriting
5. Live photograph captured via webcam or mobile phone (to be uploaded at the time of online application)
6. Call Letter (downloaded from Bank's website) along with photo identity proof (Passport / Aadhaar / PAN Card / Driving License / Voter's Card / Bank Passbook with photograph / Identity Card issued by School or College / Gazetted Officer in official letterhead) in original and self-attested photocopy
7. Caste Certificate in prescribed Government of India format (for SC/ST candidates) — to be submitted at the time of engagement if called for
8. OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate in prescribed Government of India format, issued during the period 01-04-2026 to the date of joining (for OBC candidates)
9. Income and Asset Certificate issued by Competent Authority in the format prescribed by Government of India, valid for the year 2026-27 (for EWS candidates)
10. Disability Certificate in prescribed format issued by a Competent Authority as per Government of India guidelines (for PwBD candidates)
11. Educational qualification certificates and marksheets (Graduation onwards)
12. THIS IS NOT AN EMPLOYMENT IN THE BANK. NOR IS THE SAME CONTRACT EMPLOYMENT. Candidates engaged as Apprentice will NOT be treated as "employees" of SBI and will not be entitled to receive any benefits available for employees of the Bank.
13. SBI shall have no obligation to offer regular employment to Apprentices during and/or after the completion of the Apprenticeship period. After completion, candidates will be relieved from the respective work area.
14. Candidates can apply for engagement in one State only and can appear for the examination only once under this engagement project.
15. Mandatory registration on NAPS portal (https://apprenticeshipindia.gov.in) before applying online on SBI website is required.
