Uma UG Higher Secondary School, Boudh has released an official notification for the recruitment of Guest Faculty Posts. Interested and eligible candidates can apply Offline by sending the application form to the official address. The last date to submit the application is 12-06-2026.

The last date to apply is **12-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Master's degree in the concerned subject with at least 50% marks or equivalent grade, and B.Ed. or equivalent degree recognized by NCTE, New Delhi. Basic computer knowledge is also required. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
3. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
4. Check all details carefully before submission.
5. Apply through a Bio-Data form along with Self Attested EDUCATIONAL QUALIFICATION DOCUMENTS beginning from HSC to Master degree in the above-mentioned subject with having B.Ed. as professional qualification and basic knowledge in computer Application, in the O/O Principal UMA UGHSS on all working days from 7.00am-11.00 am.
6. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 12-06-2026.
7. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
