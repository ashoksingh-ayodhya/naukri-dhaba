PM Shri Government Higher Secondary School Podagada (PM Shri HSS Podagada) Guest Faculty Notification 2026 has been released on 04 June 2026 on the official website of the institution. The recruitment is being conducted for Guest Faculty positions on a contractual basis through a Walk-in Interview.

Candidates must have Master's Degree in the concerned subject with at least 50% marks or equivalent grade, B.Ed. or equivalent degree recognized by NCTE, New Delhi (except in Logic/Philosophy, Sociology subjects) as minimum educational qualification.
