## How to Apply

1. After the release of rank card in Uttar Pradesh DELED 2024 admission, the process of counseling has also started, first of all the schedule of counseling has been issued after other information links have also become active.
2. Uttar Pradesh DELED 2024 , whose rank according to the proposed schedule, they can do it online.
3. Step 1 : First of all, the candidates who had applied online for Uttar Pradesh DELED 2024 admission can check their rank card by entering their registration number and date of birth.
4. Step 2 : Uttar Pradesh DELED 2024 First round counseling schedule has been released, candidates should see the schedule according to their rank and when their turn comes, get your registration online.
5. Step 3 : Uttar Pradesh DELED 2024 will get a link to Generate One Time Password for registration, to register the candidate, login by entering his online registration number and date of birth, after that an OTP will be sent to the candidate’s mobile, after that after entering a counseling Password will have to be created by the candidate himself.
6. Step 4 : After Uttar Pradesh DELED 2024 registration, the candidate will have to fill the choice of the college, the candidate will have to fill the choice of the college according to his own order, after that the choice will have to be locked, only then this process will be completed.
7. Step 5 : Candidates will have to wait for the allotment result, according to the schedule of counseling the result will be declared. After the declaration of the result, if a college is allotted to the candidate, then the candidate will also have to pay a Remaining fee.
8. Candidates can check other information such as counseling schedule, documents required and other information on the official website.
