Sahara Refund Portal On the orders of the Hon’ble Supreme Court, the depositor’s money is also deposited in Sahara Credit Cooperative Society Limited, Lucknow, Saharayan Universal Multipurpose Society Limited, Bhopal, Humara India Credit Cooperative Society Limited, Kolkata, Stars Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited, Hyderabad. A portal has been created for refund of money. Which started on 18 July 2023.


## How to Apply

1. Sahara Refund Portal’s official website mocrefund.crcs.gov.in has to be opened, its direct link will be found on some useful Important Link in this page.
2. Step 1 : In the Sahara Refund Portal, first of all you have to click on Depositor Registration, after that you have to register by entering the last 4 digits of your Aadhaar card along with your mobile number.
3. Step 2 : After depositor registration in Sahara Refund Portal, the candidate will have to click on depositor login and will have to provide the last 4 digits of his Aadhaar along with the registered mobile number, after which OTP verification will have to be done.
4. Step 3 : After OTP verification in Sahara Refund Portal, the candidate will have to fill his/her personal information like name, date of birth, father’s name etc.
5. Step 4 : For refund in Sahara Refund Portal, the candidate will have to update the details of his/her deposited money along with uploading the documents.
6. Step 5 : After updating the details of all the claim details in the Sahara refund portal, the information will have to be submitted, after that a form will be downloaded, its print will have to be taken out and then it will have to be uploaded along with the photo / signature and other information.
7. For More Details & Step by Step Guidance Must Read the Sahara Refund Portal User Manual 2023
