Directorate General of Assam Rifles has released detailed advertisement of Technical and tradesman Rally Recruitment 2024. Any candidate who is interested in the recruitment rally for various posts in different states and fulfills the eligibility can apply online between 21 October 2023 to 19 November 2023. There are a total of 167 posts in Assam Rifles Rally Recruitment 2024 which are according to different posts and states. Candidates should read the advertisement for more information. Read the notification for eligibility, age limit, selection procedure, pay scale and all other information in Assam Rifles Recruitment 2024.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Assam Rifles Technical and Tradesman Rally Recruitment 2024 Apply Online for 167 Post. 
A total of **161** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **19/11/2023**.

## How to Apply

1. Assam Rifles Tradesman Various State Rally Recruitment 2024 Candidate Can Apply Between 21/10/2023 to 19/11/2023
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in All India Assam Rifles Tradesman Latest 10th, 12th & Degree Pass Jobs 2024 Various Post Tradesman Rally Recruitment 2024.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
