National High Speed Rail Corporation Limited (NHSRCL) has released vacancy notices for 175 posts across two grades - Deputy Engineer / Deputy Chief Controller (34 posts, Vacancy Nos. 21–25/2026) and Junior Engineer / Controller / Train Manager / Station Manager (175 posts, Vacancy Nos. 26–32/2026) - on absorption basis from Regular/Contractual employees of Indian Railways, Government-owned or operated Railways, Metro Railways, and RRTS. These roles support India's first high-speed rail corridor between Mumbai and Ahmedabad. Eligibility criteria are reckoned as on 31 May 2026. Candidates must check the official NHSRCL website for the application portal link and closing date.

A total of **175** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 31 May 2026 as minimum educational qualification.
