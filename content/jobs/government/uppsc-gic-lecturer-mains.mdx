Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission UPPSC has released ADVT. NO. Advertisement for A-3/E-1/2020, LECTURER (MALE/FEMALE) GOVERNMENT INTER COLLEGE,(MAINS) EXAM-2020. Any candidate who has qualified the preliminary exam of UPPSC GIC Lecturer can only apply in the main exam. The online applications for the main examination will run from 24 December 2021 to 10 January 2022. For other information read the advertisement only then apply.

## How to Apply

1. Open the UPPSC GIC Lecturer Mains Conventional Form Apply Online Link.
2. Enter Your Login Details (Registration No, DOB, Gender, Domicile & Category) as per Your Application Form
3. Enter Your Registered Mobile No. (As per Pre Exam)
4. Enter OTP Mobile Number OR EMail ID
5. Choose the Exam Center and Fill the Required Information.
6. Take a Print Out of Application Form and Submit / Send to UPPCS Office, Prayagraj Before 18/01/2022
