Candidates can now check their application status in the Railway recruitment board Guwahati whether they are eligible to appear in 1st stage computer-based test and if application status is successful, RRB Guwahati ALP Admit card 2018 can be downloaded.

A total of **2800** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 10th, 12th/ITI/ Graduation from Govt. Recognized Institution. as minimum educational qualification.
