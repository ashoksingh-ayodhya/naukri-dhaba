Vardhman Mahavir Medical College and VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital (VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital) Junior Resident (Non-PG) MBBS Notification 2026 has been released on the official website vmmc-sjh.nic.in. A total of 151 vacancies have been announced through the VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital Junior Resident Recruitment 2026.

A total of **151** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have MBBS Degree from a Recognized University / Institute. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Recent passport-size colour photograph
2. Duly filled Application Form (Annexure-I) with recent passport size photo affixed and signed
3. Permanent Registration Certificate from Delhi Medical Council (Original + Self-attested copy)
4. Internship Completion Certificate (on or after 01.07.2024) (Original + Self-attested copy)
5. EWS Certificate / OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) Certificate / SC / ST Certificate / PwBD Certificate (as applicable) issued on or after 01.04.2026 (Original + Self-attested copy)
6. Proof of Identity (Aadhaar / Voter ID / PAN Card / Driving License etc.) (Original + Self-attested copy)
7. All original documents for verification
8. Self-attested photocopies of all required certificates
9. Application Form (Annexure-I) completely filled
10. THIS IS PURELY ADHOC ENGAGEMENT FOR 89 DAYS OR TILL 30.09.2026, WHICHEVER IS EARLIER. It is NOT a regular or permanent employment in VMMC & Safdarjung Hospital / VMMC.
11. Selected candidates will NOT be treated as regular employees and will not be entitled to any benefits available to regular employees of the Hospital.
12. Hospital shall have no obligation to offer regular employment after completion of the Adhoc period.
13. Candidates who have already completed one year Junior Residency (Non-PG) MBBS in any Government Hospital are NOT ELIGIBLE and need not apply.
14. Candidates must bring all original documents along with self-attested photocopies for verification. Failure to produce original documents will result in disqualification.
15. EWS / OBC candidates must produce valid certificates issued on or after 01.04.2026 mentioning Non-Creamy Layer for Central Government posts.
