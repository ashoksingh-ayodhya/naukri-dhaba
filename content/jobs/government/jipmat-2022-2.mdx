National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the notification for Join Integrated Program in Management Admission Test JIPMAT 2022. Those Candidate Are Enrolled with JIPMAT Entrance Exam Can Download the Result with Score Card. For Course list, syllabus, procedure and other information in this year’s JIPMAT exam, read the notification.

Delhi University PG / B.Ed Admission 2022 has released an official notification for NTA JIPMAT 2022 Admissions Test Result 2022. 
The last date to apply is **09/06/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. National Testing Agency NTA Are Released Notification for JIPMAT Entrance Test 2022 , Candidate Can Apply Between 10/05/2022 to 09/06/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form in JIPMAT Admission 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
