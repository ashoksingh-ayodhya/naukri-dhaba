Indian Coast Guard has released the advertisement of AAssistant Commandant 02/2022 Batch, any candidate who wants to join the GD and technical recruitment of Indian Coast Guard can apply online from 06 December 2021 to 17 December 2021. The admit cards for the exam will be issued on 28 December 2021 and the exam will be held in January 2022.

All Board Exams has released an official notification for Join Indian Coast Guard Assistant Commandant Exam 02/2022 Batch Exam Admit Card 2021 for 50 Post. 
A total of **50** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **17/12/2021 upto 05:00 PM Only Last Date Complete Form : 17/12/2021**.
