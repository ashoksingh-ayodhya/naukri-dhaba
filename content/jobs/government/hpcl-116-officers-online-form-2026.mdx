Before diving into details, candidates should review this quick overview of the HPCL Officer Recruitment 2026, which summarizes the organization, total vacancies, application dates, and mode of application in one glance.

A total of **116** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official website: www.hindustanpetroleum.com
2. Go to the "Careers" section and click on "Current Openings."
3. Read the detailed advertisement (HPCL/OPEN/HR/1/2026-27) carefully.
4. Register using a valid, personal email ID and mobile number (must remain active for at least one year).
5. Fill in the online application form with accurate personal, educational, and experience details.
6. Upload photograph and signature as per specified format.
7. Pay the applicable application fee online (if not SC/ST/PwBD).
8. Submit the form and take a printout of the acknowledgment/payment receipt for future reference.
9. Direct Link to apply HPCL Officers Recruitment 2026
10. Do not wait until the last day (20 July 2026) to submit your application, as heavy website traffic can cause payment or submission failures.
11. Double-check your email ID and mobile number before submission - the notification clearly states no changes will be entertained after submission.
12. Ensure your photograph and signature meet the prescribed format; wrong or missing uploads can lead to cancellation of candidature.
13. Verify your category (UR/EWS/SC/ST/OBCNC/PwBD) carefully before submitting, as category changes are not permitted later.
14. Keep scanned copies of caste certificates, income & asset certificates, and educational documents ready in advance for document verification.
15. Candidates currently employed with HPCL are not eligible to apply for lateral (experienced) positions.
