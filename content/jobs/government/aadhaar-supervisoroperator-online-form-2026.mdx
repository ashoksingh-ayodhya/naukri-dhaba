CSC e-Governance Services India Ltd. has released the CSC Aadhaar Supervisor Operator Recruitment 2026 notification for the post of Aadhaar Supervisor/Operator – District across multiple states including Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, and Maharashtra. These posts are to be filled on a purely contract basis for a period of 1 year. Candidates with an Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor certificate issued by a UIDAI-authorized agency can apply before the last date of 20 August 2026 through the CSC Job Portal.

A total of **125** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Details as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Visit the official CSC Job Portal at career.csccloud.in.
2. Navigate to the relevant job post for your state – Bihar, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, or Maharashtra.
3. Click on the "Apply Now" button on the job post page.
4. Fill in the online application form with your personal, educational, and certification details.
5. Upload the required documents including your Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor certificate.
6. Submit the application form before 20 August 2026.
7. Applying without a valid UIDAI certificate: The Aadhaar Operator/Supervisor certificate from a UIDAI-authorized agency is a mandatory requirement. Applying without it will result in disqualification.
8. VLEs applying despite ineligibility: VLEs are explicitly barred from applying. Submitting an application as a VLE wastes your time and will be rejected at the verification stage.
9. Applying for the wrong district: Each vacancy is district-specific. Ensure you apply for the correct district as per your location and preference.
10. Incomplete LMS Certification: Candidates sometimes have the Supervisor exam cleared but not the LMS Certification. Both are required before applying.
11. Submitting incorrect documents: Upload clear, legible scans of your educational certificates and Aadhaar Operator certificate. Blurry or mismatched documents can cause rejection.
12. Missing the last date: The portal closes applications on 20 August 2026. Do not wait until the last day — technical issues near the deadline are common.
13. Confusing CSC e-Governance with state government jobs: This is a contractual, not permanent government post. Ensure you understand the terms of engagement before applying.
