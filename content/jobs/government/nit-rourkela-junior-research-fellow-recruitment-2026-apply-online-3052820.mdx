National Institute of Technology Rourkela (NIT Rourkela) Junior Research Fellow (JRF) Notification 2026 has been released on 02 June 2026 on the official website of NIT Rourkela under Advertisement No. NITR/SR/2026/Advt.-26ER026/L/034. A total of 1 vacancy has been announced through the NIT Rourkela JRF Recruitment 2026.

The last date to apply is **26-06-2026**.
Candidates must have M.Sc. or M.Tech in Meteorology or Atmospheric Science or Oceanography with at least 60% marks, National Eligibility Tests (CSIR-UGC NET, GATE) or equivalent, Working experience with satellite products and AI/ML models, Skills in analysis of tropical cyclones or extreme weather events, Strong background in Unix/Linux with Python and shell scripting as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 26-06-2026.
