Broadcast Engineering Consultants India Limited (BECIL) has officially released a notification for the recruitment of 5 Office Assistant Posts. This recruitment drive offers a great opportunity for candidates looking for government jobs. Interested and eligible candidates can apply online through the official website.

The last date to apply is **13-05-2026**.
Candidates must have Graduate in any discipline with 50% or more marks, 2 years’ experience as Office Assistant in a reputed organization, Proficiency in MS Office and other computer applications as minimum educational qualification.
