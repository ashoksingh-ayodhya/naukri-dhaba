Directorate of Urban Land Transport Karnataka (DULT Karnataka) has published the Associate Transport Planner, CAD Operator Notification 2026. The recruitment drive aims to fill 3 Associate Transport Planner, CAD Operator positions. Candidates holding a Postgraduate degree or equivalent in Transport Planning/Engineering, or a Civil Engineering degree or diploma are eligible to apply via email. The application process will remain active until 15-06-2026, and candidates can easily access the notification PDF and application details below.

The last date to apply is **15-06-2026**.
Candidates must have Postgraduate degree or equivalent in Transport Planning/Engineering, Civil Engineering degree or diploma as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Prepare your application with complete bio-data including personal details, educational qualifications, and experience.
3. Attach self-attested copies of all required documents such as educational certificates, experience certificates, ID proof, and recent passport-size photographs.
4. Clearly mention the post applied for in the subject line of the email.
5. Send the complete application to the official email address mentioned in the notification.
6. Ensure the email is sent before the last date: 15-06-2026.
7. Keep a copy of the sent email for future reference.
