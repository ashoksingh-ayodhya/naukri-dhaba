The offline application process for ECHS Medical Officer Recruitment 2026 has started on 20 May 2026 and will remain open until 30 June 2026.

Candidates must have MBBS with preferable additional qualifications in Medicine/Surgery and minimum 03 years work experience after internship as minimum educational qualification.
