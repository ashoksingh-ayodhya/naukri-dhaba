Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) has published the Visiting Consultant (Specialist Doctor) Notification 2026 under Advertisement No. AD-KORWA/VC/2026/. The recruitment drive aims to fill 2 Visiting Consultant positions. Candidates up to 64 years of age and holding D. Ortho, MS/DNB, or MD/DCH qualifications are eligible to apply through offline mode. The application process will remain active until 16 June 2026. Candidates can access the official notification PDF and application instructions below.

Candidates must have D. Ortho/MS/DNB, MD/DCH as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Read the official notification carefully before applying.
2. Download the application form from the official website: hal-india.co.in.
3. Fill the application form carefully with correct information.
4. Attach self-attested copies of required documents such as photograph, signature, ID proof, and certificates.
5. Check all details carefully before submission.
6. Send the completed application form to the address mentioned in the notification through Speed Post / Registered Post or as instructed.
7. Ensure the application reaches before the last date: 16-06-2026.
8. Keep a photocopy of the submitted application form for future reference.
