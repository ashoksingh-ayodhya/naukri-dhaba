Short Information : Central Bank of India (CBI) has released the Exam Date for the post of Apprentice. This recruitment was released for 4500 posts. Applications for the Central Bank of India Apprentices Recruitment 2026 were accepted from 12 June 2026, to 28 June 2026. The online examination is going to be conducted on 05 July 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their SBI Apprentice Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **393** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 1903 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Candidates must go to the Important Links section below
2. Then the candidates must click on the Exam Date link.
3. After clicking on the link, the candidates will be redirected to a new page.
4. In this page, candidates have to enter the following details:
5. Registration no.
6. Birth Date/Password
7. Captcha Code
8. Then the candidates have to submit the details by clicking on the Login button.
9. After successful login, candidates will be able to download the exam date.
10. Alternatively, the candidates can download the exam date from the official website of CBI also.
