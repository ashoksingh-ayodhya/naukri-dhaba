Short Information : Central Bank of India has released the Admit Card for the recruitment of Foreign Exchange Officer in Scale III & Marketing Officer in Scale I in Specialist Category On Regular Basis. This recruitment was released for 350 posts. Applications for the Central Bank Specialist Officer SO Recruitment 2026 were accepted from January 20, 2026, to February 15, 2025. The online examination is going to be conducted on 14 March 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Central Bank Specialist Officer SO Admit card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **300** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Full time Graduation from All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)/ University Grants Commission (UGC) approved University/College and Two years Full time Master of Business Administration (MBA)/Post- Graduate Diploma in Business Analytics (PGDBA)/ Post- Graduate Diploma in Business Management (PGDBM)/ Post Graduate Programme in Management (PGPM)/ Post-Graduate Diploma in Management (PGDM) with Major specialization in Marketing.; Post Qualification experience of two years out of which one-year continuous experience in marketing domain in a organization (Banking financial services and Insurance- BFSI) and should be currently employed in marketing domain as minimum educational qualification.
