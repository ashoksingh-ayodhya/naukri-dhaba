Punjab Police Constable Admit Card 2026 has been officially released on 23rd June 2026 on the Punjab Police recruitment portal. Candidates who applied for the 3,298 Constable vacancies under District Police Cadre and Armed Police Cadre can now download their hall ticket PDF using their registration number and date of birth. The Computer-Based Test (CBT) is scheduled to begin from 1st July 2026 onwards. Carrying a printed copy of the admit card to the examination centre is mandatory candidates without it will not be permitted to enter the exam hall.

A total of **3298** posts are available for this recruitment.
