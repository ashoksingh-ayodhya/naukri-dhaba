The Delhi Police Constable Physical Admit Card 2026 for the Physical Endurance and Measurement Test (PE&MT) is expected to be released on 20 July 2026. This is ahead of the physical test, which is officially scheduled to begin on 25 July 2026 (Saturday). The admit card will apply to candidates shortlisted for Constable (Executive) Male & Female, Constable (Driver), Head Constable (Ministerial), and Head Constable (AWO/TPO) posts under the Delhi Police Examination 2025.
