The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the Bihar Police BPSSC SI Prohibition Admit Card 2026 on its official website on 21 June 2026. Candidates who applied for the Sub-Inspector (Prohibition) Recruitment Examination under Advt. No. 03/2026 can now download their prelims hall ticket using their Registration Number/Enrollment Number and Date of Birth. The preliminary exam is scheduled for 12 July 2026, and the direct admit card download link is available below.

A total of **78** posts are available for this recruitment.
