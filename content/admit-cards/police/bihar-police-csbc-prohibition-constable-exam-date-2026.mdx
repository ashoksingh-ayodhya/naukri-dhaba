Short Information : Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC) has released the Exam Date, Admit Card & Exam City Details notice for the post of Constable posts in Prohibition, Jail Warder, and Mobile Squad units. Candidates who have applied for this recruitment can download their Bihar Police CSBC Prohibition Constable Exam Date 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **108** posts are available for this recruitment.
