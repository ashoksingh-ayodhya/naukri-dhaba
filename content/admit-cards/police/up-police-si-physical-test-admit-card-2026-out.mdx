The UP Police Sub Inspector Physical Efficiency Test (PET) Admit Card 2026 has been officially released by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB/UPPBPB), Lucknow. Candidates who cleared the Written Exam and DV/PST rounds of the UP Police SI Recruitment 2025 (Advt. No. PRPB-4(SI)-3/2025) can now download their PET Admit Card directly from the official exam portal at siupexam25.com. The Physical Efficiency Test is scheduled on June 29 and 30, 2026, with a reserve date of July 1, 2026.

A total of **4543** posts are available for this recruitment.
