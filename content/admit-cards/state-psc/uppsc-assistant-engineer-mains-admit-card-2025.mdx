Short Information : Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the mains admit card for the Combined State Engineering Services Examination 2025 (Assistant Engineer) Post. This recruitment was released for 604 posts. The applications for UPPSC Assistant Engineer Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 17 December 2024 to 17 January 2025. The mains examination was held on 28, 29 September 2025. Candidates who qualified for this pre examination can download their UPPSC Assistant Engineer Mains Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **604** posts are available for this recruitment.
