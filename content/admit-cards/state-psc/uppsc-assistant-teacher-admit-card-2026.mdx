The UPPSC LT Grade Sangeet admit card 2026 has been made available by the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) for candidates appearing in the Music subject paper of the Assistant Teacher, Trained Graduate Grade (LT Grade) Main Examination-2025. Candidates can download their hall ticket from the official website using their OTR number ahead of the exam scheduled on 11 July 2026 in Prayagraj.
