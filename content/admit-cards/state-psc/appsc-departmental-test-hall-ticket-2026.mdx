The Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) will be released the Hall Ticket for the Departmental Tests May 2026 Session on June 30, 2026. All 49,117 registered candidates can now download their APPSC Departmental Tests Admit Card 2026 from the official website psc.ap.gov.in. The examinations are scheduled from July 6, 2026 to July 11, 2026, at 21 District Centres across Andhra Pradesh. Candidates must download and carry a printed copy of their Hall Ticket to be allowed entry into the examination hall.
