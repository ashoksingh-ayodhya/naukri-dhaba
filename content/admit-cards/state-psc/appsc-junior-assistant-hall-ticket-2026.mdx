The Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) releases hall tickets for the Junior Assistant cum Computer Operator exam through its dedicated hall ticket portal at portal-psc.ap.gov.in/Download_HallTickets/LOGINFORM_OTHERS. Candidates need their OTPR (One Time Profile Registration) ID and password to log in and download the admit card, which is mandatory for entry into the exam hall. As of now, APPSC has been progressively releasing hall tickets notification-wise for various 2025-26 recruitment drives, so Junior Assistant candidates should keep checking the official portal regularly for their specific release date.
