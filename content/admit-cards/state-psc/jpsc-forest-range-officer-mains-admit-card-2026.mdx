Applications for the Jharkhand JPSC ACF & FRO Recruitment 2024 were accepted from July 29, 2024, to August 30, 2024. The mains examination is going to be conducted on 22 ,23 & 24 January 2026. Candidates who appeared for this examination can download their Jharkhand JPSC Forest Range Officer Mains Admit Card 2026 from the link given below

A total of **170** posts are available for this recruitment.
