Short Information : Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the mains admit card for the post of Combined State Agriculture Services. This recruitment was released for 268 posts. Applications for the UPPSC Agriculture Services Recruitment 2024 were accepted from April 10, 2024, to May 05, 2024. The mains examination is scheduled to be conducted on 23-26 March 2025. Candidates who have applied for this recruitment can download their UPPSC Agriculture Services Mains Admit Card 2025 from the link provided below.

A total of **55** posts are available for this recruitment.
