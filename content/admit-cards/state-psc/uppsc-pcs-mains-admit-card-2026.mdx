Short Information : Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has released the mains Admit Card for the Combined State Upper Subordinate Services Examination 2025. This recruitment was released for 210 posts. Applications for the UPPSC Pre Recruitment 2025 were accepted from February 20, 2025, to April 02, 2025. The mains examination is going to be conducted From 29 March 2026 to 01 April 2026. Candidates who have applied for this mains form can download their UPPSC PCS Mains Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **210** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Bachelor Degree in Economics OR Commerce with 55% Marks. as minimum educational qualification.
