The SSC CPO PET PST Admit Card 2026 was released by the Staff Selection Commission on 13th July 2026 for candidates who qualified in Paper 1 of the SSC CPO (Sub-Inspector in Delhi Police and Central Armed Police Forces) Examination. The Physical Endurance Test (PET) and Physical Standard Test (PST) are scheduled from 21st to 28th July 2026. Candidates can download their hall ticket online from the official SSC website using their registration number and date of birth.
