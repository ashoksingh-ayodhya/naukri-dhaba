Short Information : Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the Admit Card for the recruitment of Assistant Sub Inspector Operation Posts on the official website of the Bihar Police BPSSC. As per the notification, Online application process for Bihar Police BPSSC ASI Operation Recruitment 2026 were accepted from 04 February 2026 to 04 March 2026. This recruitment has been issued for 462 posts. The Examination is going to conducted on 22 July 2026. Candidates must check the details for Bihar Police BPSSC ASI Operation Admit Card 2026 which has given below.

A total of **05** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 185 as minimum educational qualification.
