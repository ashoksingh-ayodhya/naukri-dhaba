Short Information : Uttarakhand Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UKSSSC) has recently release the Admit Card for the recruitment of Additional Private Secretary, Personal Assistant, DEO, Stenographer Posts. This recruitment was done by UKSSSC for 257 posts. Applications for UKSSSC PA, Steno & Other Post Recruitment 2024 were filled from 24th September 2024 to 14th October 2024. Candidates who have done 12th Exam & Bachelor Degree in Any Stream are eligible for this recruitment. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Admit Card from the link given below.

A total of **02** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Passed Class 12th Exam Form Any Recognized Board in India with 2 Years House Keeping Experience.; Hindi Stenographer: 80 WPM; Computer Hindi Typing : 3000 Key Depressions as minimum educational qualification.
