Short Information : Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the admit card of SSC Constable GD in BSF, CISF, ITBP, CRPF, NCB, SSF, Assam Rifles on the official website of the SSC. This recruitment was released for 25,487 posts. Candidates can also check all the details on the official Naukri Dhaba website. Applications for the SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 01 December 2025, to 31 December 2025. The written examination is going to be Conducted from 27 April to 30 May 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their SSC GD Constable Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **2020** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Male: 1.6 KM in 06 Minute , 30 Sec (For Ladakh Region); Female: 800 Meter in 04 Minute (For Ladakh Region) as minimum educational qualification.
