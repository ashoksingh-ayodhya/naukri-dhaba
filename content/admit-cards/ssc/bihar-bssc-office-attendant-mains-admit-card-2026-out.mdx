The mains examination is going to be conducted on 26 May 2026. Candidates who qualified this pre exam can download their Bihar BSSC Office Attendant Mains Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **02** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 99 as minimum educational qualification.
