UPSSSC PWD Shikshak Samvarg Mukhya Pariksha Admit Card 2026: The Uttar Pradesh Adhinasth Seva Chayan Aayog (UPSSSC) has released the main examination admit card for the recruitment against Advertisement No. 03-Pariksha/2026, for Teacher Cadre (Shikshak Samvarg) posts under the Divyangjan Sashaktikaran Nideshalaya, Uttar Pradesh, Lucknow. As per the official notice dated 8 July 2026, the main exam is proposed to be held on 12 July 2026 from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM in Lucknow district. Candidates can download their admit card from the official website, upsssc.gov.in, from 8 July 2026 onward. The commission has clarified that the admit card can be downloaded only after the required main exam fee has been paid.
