The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the admit card for the UP Pollution Control Board Various Post Mains Examination 2026 (Advt. No. 02-Exam/2026). Candidates can download their admit cards from the official website upsssc.gov.in starting 25 June 2026. The Mains exam is scheduled for 29 June 2026, from 3:00 PM to 5:00 PM, in Lucknow district, for the remaining vacant posts under various eligibility categories of the UP Pollution Control Board. Carrying a valid admit card to the exam centre is compulsory.
