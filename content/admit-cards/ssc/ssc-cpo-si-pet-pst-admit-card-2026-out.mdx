Short Information : Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released the PET/ PST Admit Card for the post of Sub Inspector in Delhi Police and Central Armed Police Forces Examination, 2025 on the official website of SSC. This recruitment was released for 3073. posts. Candidates Can Also Check All Details on Official Naukri Dhaba2026, Applications for the SSC CPO SI Recruitment 2025 were accepted from September 26, 2025, to October 16, 2025. The PET/PST examination will be held on 21-28 July 2026. Candidates who appeared for this examination can download their SSC CPO SI PET/ PST Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **210** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 210 as minimum educational qualification.
