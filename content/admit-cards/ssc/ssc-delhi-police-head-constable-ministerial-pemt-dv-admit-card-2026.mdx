Short Information : Staff Selection Commission, SSC has released the PE&MT DV Admit Card for the post of Head Constable Ministerial on the official website of SSC. This recruitment was released for 456 posts. Applications for the Delhi Police Head Constable Ministerial Recruitment 2025 were accepted from September 29, 2025, to October 20, 2025. The CBT examination was held on 07-12 January 2026. Candidates who appeared for this examination can download their SSC Delhi Police Constable Ministerial PE&MT DV Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **168** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 2.3 Feet as minimum educational qualification.
