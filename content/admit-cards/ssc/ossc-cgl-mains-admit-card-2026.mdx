The Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC) will release the OSSC CGL Mains Admit Card 2026 in the last week of June 2026 on the official website, ossc.gov.in. This hall ticket is for the 8,037 candidates who cleared the prelims stage and are now eligible to appear for the OSSC Combined Graduate Level Recruitment Examination (CGLRE) 2025-26 Mains, scheduled for 6th, 7th, and 8th July 2026. Candidates will need their registered username, mobile number, or email ID along with their password to download the admit card.
