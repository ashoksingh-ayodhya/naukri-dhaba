The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has released the admit card for the Assistant Sub Inspector (Technical) recruitment exam 2026. The e-admit card is available from 24 July 2026 on the official BPSSC website for candidates who applied for the post of ASI (Technical) in Bihar Police Radio (Wireless), Government of Bihar, under Advertisement No. 07/2026. The written examination is scheduled for 5 August 2026 (Wednesday).

A total of **22** posts are available for this recruitment.
