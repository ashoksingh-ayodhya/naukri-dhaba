Short Information : Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released the admit card for the recruitment of BCG Technician posts on the official website of UPSSSC. This recruitment was released for 255 posts. Applications for the UPSSSC BCG Technician Recruitment 2024 were accepted from 08 July 2024, to 07 August 2024. The written examination is going to be conducted on 30 June 2026. Candidates who have applied for this recruitment can download their UPSSSC BCG Technician Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **04** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 111 as minimum educational qualification.
