Bihar Police Subordinate Service Commission (BPSSC) has released the Admit Card for the Havildar Instructor Recruitment 2026 (Advt. No. 05/2026) on 13 July 2026. This recruitment is for 122 Havildar Instructor posts in the Bihar Home Guard under the Home Department (Special), Government of Bihar. The written examination will be held on 29 July 2026 in offline (OMR-based) mode. Candidates can download their admit card from the official BPSSC portal using their Registration Number/Mobile Number and Date of Birth/Password.

A total of **122** posts are available for this recruitment.
