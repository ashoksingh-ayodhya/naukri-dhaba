Short Information : West Bengal School Service Commission (WBSSC) has released the admit card for the post of Group C (Clerk) & Group D. This recruitment was released for 8477 posts. Applications for the WBSSC Group C and D Recruitment 2025 were accepted from November 03, 2025, to December 03, 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 01 & 08 March 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their WBSSC Group C and D Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **5488** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Candidates who have completed their VIII level of Examination or equivalent from any Recognized Board in India. as minimum educational qualification.
