The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC) has announced that the e-admit card for the Assistant Sub-Inspector (Operator) recruitment exam in Bihar Police Radio (Wireless), Patna will be available for download from July 7, 2026 on the official website, bpssc.bihar.gov.in. The preliminary written competitive examination is scheduled for July 22, 2026 (Wednesday) in a single shift. This article covers the complete admit card download process, exam schedule, required documents, and reporting guidelines based on the official notice issued by BPSSC.
