The Indian Army Agniveer Physical Admit Card 2026 download link has been activated for candidates who cleared the Agniveer CEE Result 2026, declared on 12 July 2026. The Physical/Rally Admit Card is being released zone-wise and ARO-wise, and candidates must download it from the official website joinindianarmy.nic.in to appear for the Physical Fitness Test (PFT), Physical Measurement Test (PMT), Document Verification and Medical Examination. The first confirmed schedule is for ARO Rohtak under HQ Recruiting Zone Ambala, where the admit card is available for download from 14 July 2026 and the rally will be held from 20 to 27 July 2026 at Rajiv Gandhi Sports Complex, Rohtak.
