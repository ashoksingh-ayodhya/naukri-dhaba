The Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC) officially released the Admit Card for the LTR Teacher Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 15-05-2025 to 23-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website ossc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Odisha.
