The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has officially released the DSSSB MTS Admit Card 2026 for Advertisement No. 07/25 on 29 June 2026. Candidates who applied for the Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) recruitment can now download their hall ticket directly from the official website, dsssb.delhi.gov.in, by logging in with their registration number and date of birth. The DSSSB MTS exam will be conducted as a Computer-Based Test (CBT) from 25 July to 1 September 2026. This article covers the direct download link, step-by-step download process, exam schedule, exam pattern, and all the essential details printed on the admit card.
