The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the RSSB LDC Admit Card 2026 for the Clerk Grade-II (Lower Division Clerk) and Junior Assistant Combined Direct Recruitment Examination. The admit card went live on 1 July 2026 on the official portal, ahead of the written exam scheduled for 5 July 2026. This recruitment drive covers 10,644 vacancies across various Rajasthan government departments, making it one of the largest exams conducted by RSSB this year.

A total of **10644** posts are available for this recruitment.
