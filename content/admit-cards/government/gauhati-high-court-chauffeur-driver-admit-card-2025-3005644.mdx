The Gauhati High Court (Gauhati High Court) has officially released the Admit Card for the Chauffeur (Driver) Driving Test 2025. Candidates appearing for the Driving Test scheduled on 22-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website ghconline.gov.in. The Driving Test will be held at various centres across the state of Assam.
