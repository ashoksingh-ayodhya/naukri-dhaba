The High Court of Judicature at Patna, Bihar has confirmed that the Admit Card for Computer Operator-cum-Typist Recruitment Examination 2026 will be released on 15 July 2026 from 12:00 PM onwards on the official website, patnahighcourt.gov.in. This admit card is for candidates who applied under Advertisement No. PHC/02/2026 for 48 posts of Computer Operator-cum-Typist. The written examination is scheduled for 22 July 2026 (Wednesday). Candidates should download their admit card in advance and carefully check all exam-day instructions printed on it.

A total of **48** posts are available for this recruitment.
