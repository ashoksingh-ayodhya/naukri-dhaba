Short Information : Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the mains exam city/ admit card for the post of Assistant Engineer. This recruitment was released for 1014 posts. Applications for the RPSC Assistant Engineer Recruitment 2024 were accepted from August 14, 2024, to September 15, 2024. The mains examination is going to be conducted on 22-23 March 2026. Candidates who qualified for this pre examination can check their RPSC Assistant Engineer Mains Exam City/ Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **240** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Degree in Civil / Agriculture Engineering from a University established by law in India or qualifications declared equivalent thereto by the State Government.; Working Knowledge of Hindi written in Devanagari Script.; Knowledge of Rajasthani Culture.; For More Details regarding Educational Qualifications, Candidates are advised to must read the official notification before applying for this posts. as minimum educational qualification.
