New Update: Admit Card 2025 will be release by the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) Authority on 09-05-2025 at 6pm and the candidates will be able to download it from the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) official Website and it will be release by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The MET Phase 2 Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
