Short Information : Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has released the mains admit card for the recruitment of Civil Services Examination 2026. This recruitment was released for 102 posts. Applications for the HPSC Haryana Civil Service HCS Recruitment 2026 were accepted from February 06, 2026, to February 26, 2026. The mains examination is going to be conducted on 27, 28 & 29 June 2026. Candidates who qualified for this pre examination can download their HPSC Civil Service HCS Mains Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **07** posts are available for this recruitment.
