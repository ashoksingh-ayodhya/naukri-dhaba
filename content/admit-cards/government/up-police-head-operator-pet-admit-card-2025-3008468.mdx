The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment Board (UP Police) has officially released the Admit Card for the Head Operator Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 20-05-2025 to 24-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website uppbpb.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Uttar Pradesh.
