The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has officially released the Admit Card for the Junior Assistant Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 29-05-2025 (Expected), can now download their hall ticket from the official website aai.aero. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of West Bengal, Bihar, Odisha, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Andaman & Nicobar Islands and Sikkim.
