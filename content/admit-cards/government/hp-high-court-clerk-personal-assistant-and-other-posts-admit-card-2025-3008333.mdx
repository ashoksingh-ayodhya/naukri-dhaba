The High Court of Himachal Pradesh (HP High Court) has officially released the Admit Card for the Clerk, Personal Assistant and Other Posts Exam 2025 on 09-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 16-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website highcourt.hp.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Himachal Pradesh.
