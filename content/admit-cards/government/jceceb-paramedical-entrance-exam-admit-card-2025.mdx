Short Information : Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) has released the admit card for the Para Medical Entrance Competitive Examination. Applications for the JCECEB Paramedical Admission Online Form 2025 were accepted from 16 May 2025, to 16 June 2025. The online examination is going to be conducted on 20 July 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their JCECEB Paramedical Entrance Exam Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

Candidates must have Passed Matriculation/ Secondary/ 10th/ equivalent examination. as minimum educational qualification.
