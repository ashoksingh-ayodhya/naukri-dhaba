The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the Forester (Vanpal) and Forest Guard (Vanrakshak) Admit Card 2026 on 24 June 2026. Candidates who registered for this recruitment drive can download their provisional e-admit card from the official website, rssb.rajasthan.gov.in, using their SSO ID or registration number along with their date of birth. The written examination for these posts is scheduled for 28 June 2026 and will be conducted in CBT (Computer-Based Test) mode across various centres in Rajasthan.
