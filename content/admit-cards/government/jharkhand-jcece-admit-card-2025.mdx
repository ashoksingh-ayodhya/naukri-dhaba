Short Information : Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) has released the admit card for the Agriculture and Other Allied Courses for academic year 2025-26. Applications for the Jharkhand JCECE Admission Online Form 2025 were accepted from 20 April 2025, to 20 May 2025. The online examination is going to be conducted on 15 June 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Jharkhand JCECE Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

Candidates must have 10+2/ Intermediate with PCB, PCMB, PCM, PC Forestry or Inter (Agriculture) (P- Physics, C- Chemistry, M- Mathematics, B- Biology). as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. AIBE 21st Exam Admit Card 2026
2. BPSSC Bihar Police Havildar Clerk Admit Card 2026
3. Jharkhand JSSC Swasthya Karyakarta Field Worker Exam Date 2026
4. LIC HFL Junior Assistant Admit Card 2026 – Out
5. BPSC 33rd Judicial Services Pre Exam Postponed 2026
