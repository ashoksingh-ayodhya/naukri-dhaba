AIIMS CRE 5 Admit Card 2026 has been released by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) on 21st July 2026. The admit card is for the AIIMS Common Recruitment Examination-5 (CRE-5), which is scheduled to be conducted between 25th and 27th July 2026 for Group B and Group C non-faculty posts. Candidates who applied online can download their hall ticket at aiimsexams.ac.in using their application number and password.
