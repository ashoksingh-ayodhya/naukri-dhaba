The Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the AEE Exam 2025 on 08-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 11-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website opsc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Odisha.
