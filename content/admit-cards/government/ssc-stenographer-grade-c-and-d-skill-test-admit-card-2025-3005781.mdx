The Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Stenographer Grade C and D Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 16-04-2025 and 17-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website ssc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Delhi.
