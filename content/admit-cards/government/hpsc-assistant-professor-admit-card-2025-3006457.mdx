The Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Assistant Professor Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 25-04-2025 to 27-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website hpsc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Haryana.
