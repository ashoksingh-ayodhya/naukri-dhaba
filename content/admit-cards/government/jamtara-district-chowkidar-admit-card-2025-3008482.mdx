The Jamtara District has officially released the Admit Card for the Chowkidar Exam 2025 (12-05-2025). Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 14-05-2025 to 15-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website jamtara.nic.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Jharkhand.
