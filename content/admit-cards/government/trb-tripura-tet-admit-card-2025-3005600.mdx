The Teachers Recruitment Board (TRB), Tripura (TRB Tripura) has officially released the Admit Card for the TET Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 20.04.2025 (Paper-I) and 27.04.2025 (Paper-II), can now download their hall ticket from the official website trb.tripura.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Tripura.
