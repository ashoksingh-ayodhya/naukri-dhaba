The State Level Police Recruitment Board (Assam Police) has officially released the Admit Card for the Constable Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 09-05-2025 to 16-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website slprbassam.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Assam.
