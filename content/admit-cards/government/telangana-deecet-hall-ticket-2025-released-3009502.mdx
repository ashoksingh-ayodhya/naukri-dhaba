New Update: Telangana Diploma in Elementary Education Common Entrance Test Hall Ticket 2025 released by the Department of School Education Government of Telangana (TS DEECET) Authority on 20-05-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Department of School Education Government of Telangana (TS DEECET) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Department of School Education Government of Telangana (TS DEECET) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Telangana Diploma in Elementary Education Common Entrance Test Hall Ticket 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The Telangana DEECET Hall Ticket 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
