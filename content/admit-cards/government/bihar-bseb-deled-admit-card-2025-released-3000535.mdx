Bihar BSEB DELED Admit Card 2025 - Commissioner State CET Cell hosts the Bihar BSEB DELED Admit Card 2025 for the BSEB Bihar D.El.Ed 2025 online and field test on the official website i.e. biharboardonline.bihar.gov.in. The officials of Bihar School Examination Board, Patna released the Bihar BSEB DELED Admit Card 2025 on February 11, 2025. Candidates can download it by entering their registration number/roll number, password/date of birth. Candidates are required to affix a recent recognizable photograph on the Bihar BSEB DELED Admit Card 2025 preferably the same as provided during the registration process. Candidates have to carry the Bihar BSEB DELED Admit Card 2025 along with the original ID proof to the exam centre. Candidates must preserve the admit card with the original photo and signature of the candidate and invigilator carefully to present it at the time of admission process. No entry shall be allowed without valid Bihar BSEB DELED Admit Card 2025 and photo identity. For more details scroll down the article.
