Short Information : Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) has released the Trade Test & DV Admit Card for the post of Constable Tradesmen. This recruitment was released for 1161 posts. Candidates Can Also Check All Details on Official Naukri Dhaba2026, Applications for the CISF Constable Tradesmen Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 05 March 2025, to 03 April 2025. The Trade Test & DV examination is going to conduct on December 11, 2025. Candidates who appeared for this examination can download their CISF Constable Tradesmen Trade Test & DV Admit Card2025 from the link provided below.

A total of **1161** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 103 as minimum educational qualification.
