Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL), a subsidiary of Coal India Limited (CIL), has officially released the NCL Staff Nurse Admit Card 2026 on 26 June 2026. Candidates who applied for the 577 vacancies under the HEMM Operator, Paramedical Staff, and Overseer (Civil) Recruitment 2026 can now download their hall ticket from the official NCL candidate portal using their registered login credentials. The Computer-Based Test (CBT) for this recruitment will be held in a single shift on 6 July 2026.

A total of **577** posts are available for this recruitment.
