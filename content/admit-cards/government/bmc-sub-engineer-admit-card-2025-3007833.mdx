The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Sub Engineer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 13-05-2025 to 14-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website mcgm.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Maharashtra.
