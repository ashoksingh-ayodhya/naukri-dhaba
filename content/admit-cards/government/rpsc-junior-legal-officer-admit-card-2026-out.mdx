Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), Ajmer has announced the exam schedule for the Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Recruitment 2025-26 (for the Jaipur Development Authority). The written exam is scheduled for 26 and 27 July 2026, and the RPSC JLO Admit Card 2026 is set to be released on 23 July 2026 on the official RPSC website and the SSO portal. Candidates who applied against Advertisement No. 08/2025-26 will be able to download their hall ticket using their Application Number and Date of Birth.

A total of **12** posts are available for this recruitment.
