CG Vyapam Lab Assistant Admit Card 2026: The Chhattisgarh Staff Selection Board (CGSSB), formerly known as CG Vyapam, has released the admit card on 20 July 2026 for the Laboratory Assistant (Prayogshala Paricharak) written recruitment examination, referenced as PSLA26. This recruitment is being conducted for the Chhattisgarh Environment Conservation Board. Candidates who applied for this post can download their hall ticket from the official websites vyapamcg.cgstate.gov.in or cgssb.cgstate.gov.in through the profile login page. The written exam is scheduled for 26 July 2026 (Sunday).
