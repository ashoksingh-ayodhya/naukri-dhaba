The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) will release the RSSB Female Supervisor Admit Card 2026 on 20 July 2026 for the written examination scheduled on 24 July 2026. Candidates who applied under Advertisement No. 02/2026 for 72 Female Supervisor posts will be able to download their admit card from the official website, rssb.rajasthan.gov.in once the download link is activated.

A total of **72** posts are available for this recruitment.
