The High Court of Himachal Pradesh (HP High Court) has officially released the Admit Card for the Clerk and Proof Reader posts 2026 on its online recruitment portal at hphcrecruitment.in. Candidates who applied for the Clerk/Proof Reader - High Court-HC-Misc./2026 (1) recruitment can now download their hall ticket directly from the portal using their registered mobile number. The admit card is an essential document that every candidate must carry to the examination centre - no candidate will be permitted entry into the exam hall without it.
