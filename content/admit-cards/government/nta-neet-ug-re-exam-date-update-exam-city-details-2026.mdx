Short Information : National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the NEET UG 2026 re-examination date on its official website for candidates appearing in the undergraduate medical entrance exam. According to the notification, the NEET UG 2026 Re-Exam is scheduled to be held on 21 June 2026. The last date for submitting the online application form was 11 March 2026. Candidates can access their re-exam details by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Official updates also suggest that the NEET UG 2026 Admit Card is expected to be released by 14 June 2026. Additionally, candidates appearing for the re-exam may receive an extra 15 minutes to complete the examination. All registered applicants are advised to regularly check official announcements for updates regarding admit cards, examination guidelines, and other important instructions related to the NEET UG 2026 re-examination.
