RRB Isolated Categories Admit Card 08/2025 has been released by the Railway Recruitment Boards (RRBs) on 10th July 2026 at the official website rrb.indianrailways.gov.in. Candidates who registered for the RRB Isolated Categories CEN 08/2025 recruitment and are scheduled to appear for the Computer Based Test (CBT) on 14th and 15th July 2026 can now download their hall ticket using their registration number and date of birth.
