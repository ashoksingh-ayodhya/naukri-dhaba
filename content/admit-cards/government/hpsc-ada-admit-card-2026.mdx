The Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) is set to release the HPSC ADA Admit Card 2026 on 25 June 2026. This hall ticket is mandatory for all candidates appearing in the Assistant District Attorney (ADA) Screening Test, scheduled for 5 July 2026. Candidates can download their admit card from the official HPSC website, hpsc.gov.in, by logging in with their registration number and date of birth. This article covers the exact download steps, important dates, and the documents you must carry to the exam centre.

A total of **255** posts are available for this recruitment.
