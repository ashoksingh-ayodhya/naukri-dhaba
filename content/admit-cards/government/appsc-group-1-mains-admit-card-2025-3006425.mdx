The Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Group 1 Mains Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 03-05-2025 to 09-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website psc.ap.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Andhra Pradesh.
