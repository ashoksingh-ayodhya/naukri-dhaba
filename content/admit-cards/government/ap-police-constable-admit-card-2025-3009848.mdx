The State Level Police Recruitment Board (SLPRB), Andhra Pradesh officially has released the Admit Card for the Constable Exam 2025 on 23-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the Final Written Examination for SCT Police Constable (Civil) (Men & Women) and SCT Police Constable (APSP) (Men) for 01-06-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website slprb.ap.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Andhra Pradesh.
