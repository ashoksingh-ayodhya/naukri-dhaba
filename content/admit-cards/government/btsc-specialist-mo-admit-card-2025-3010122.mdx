The Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Specialist Specialist Medical Officer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 31-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website btsc.bihar.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Bihar.
