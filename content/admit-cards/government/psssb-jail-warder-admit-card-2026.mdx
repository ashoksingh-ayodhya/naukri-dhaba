The Punjab Subordinate Services Selection Board (PSSSB) will release the PSSSB Jail Warder Admit Card 2026 for the Warder and Matron written examination on its official website, sssb.punjab.gov.in. As per the board's standard practice, the admit card is issued 4 to 5 days before the exam date. According to the latest PSSSB Jail Warder Exam Date 2026 update, the written examination is scheduled for 26 July 2026. Candidates who applied under Advertisement No. 08/2026 will need their application number and date of birth to download the hall ticket once the link is activated.

A total of **532** posts are available for this recruitment.
