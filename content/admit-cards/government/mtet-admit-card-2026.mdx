The Directorate of Educational Research and Training (DERT), Meghalaya has officially released the Meghalaya Teacher Eligibility Test (MTET) Admit Card 2026 on 10 July 2026. Candidates who have registered for the MTET 2026 examination can now download their hall ticket from the official website dert.megeducation.gov.in using their registration number and password. The MTET 2026 examination is scheduled to be held on 24 July 2026 in offline (pen-and-paper) mode. Candidates must carry a printed copy of their admit card along with a valid photo ID proof to the examination centre.
