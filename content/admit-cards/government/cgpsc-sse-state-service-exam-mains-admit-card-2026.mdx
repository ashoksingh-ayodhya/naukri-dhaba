Short Information : Chhattisgarh Public Service Commission (CGPSC) has released the Result for the CGPSC State Service Prelim Exam 2025 on the official website of the CGPSC on 11th February 2026. This recruitment was released for 238 posts. Applications for CGPSC SSE PCS Pre Recruitment 2025 were filled from 01 December 2025 to 30 December 2025. The written examination was conducted on 22 February 2026. Candidates who had qualified for this pre examination can download their CGPSC SSE State Service Exam Mains Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **06** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 103 as minimum educational qualification.
