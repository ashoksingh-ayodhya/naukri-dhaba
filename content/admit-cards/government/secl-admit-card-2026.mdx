South Eastern Coalfields Limited (SECL) has released the SECL Admit Card 2026 on 29 June 2026 for candidates appearing in the Computer-Based Test (CBT) scheduled on 2 July 2026. The admit card is available for the posts of Mining Sirdar, Deputy Surveyor, and Assistant Foreman (Electrical), covering a total of 1,055 vacancies. Candidates can download their hall ticket in online PDF format from the official website, secl-cil.in, by logging in with their registration number and password/date of birth.
