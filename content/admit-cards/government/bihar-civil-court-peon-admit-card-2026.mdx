Short Information : Civil Courts of Bihar (District Court) has released the Admit Card for the post of civil court Peon. This recruitment was released for 7692 posts. Applications for the Bihar Civil Court Clerk Recruitment 2022 were accepted from September 20, 2022, to October 20, 2022. The Exam Is Going to Conducted on 15 March 2026. Candidates who Applied this Form can download their Bihar Civil Court Peon Admit Card 2026 from the link provided below.

A total of **1673** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Class 10th Matric Exam Passed. as minimum educational qualification.
