Short Information : Delhi Subordinate Service Selection Board (DSSSB) has released the admit card for the post of Assistant Teacher Nursery. This recruitment was released for 1455 posts. Applications for the Delhi DSSSB Assistant Teacher Nursery Recruitment 2024 were accepted from 09 January 2024, to 07 February 2024. The written examination is going to be conducted on 10-14 August 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Delhi DSSSB Assistant Teacher Nursery Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **1455** posts are available for this recruitment.
