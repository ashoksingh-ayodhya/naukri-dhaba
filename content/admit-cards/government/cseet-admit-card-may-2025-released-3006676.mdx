New Update: Admit Card 2025 released by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) Authority on 24-04-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The CSEET Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
