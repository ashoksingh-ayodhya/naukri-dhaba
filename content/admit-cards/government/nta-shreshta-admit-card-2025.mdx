Short Information : National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the admit card for the National Entrance Test for Scheme for Residential Education for Students in Higher Classes in Targeted Area (SHRESHTA) 2025 for admission into Class 9th and 11th for Schedule Caste students for the academic session 2025-26. Applications for the NTA SHRESHTA Online Form 2025 were accepted from April 15, 2025, to May 05, 2025. The online examination is going to be conducted on 01 June 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their NTA SHRESHTA Admit Card 2025 from the link given below..
