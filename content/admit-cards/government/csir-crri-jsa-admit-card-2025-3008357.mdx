The CSIR Central Road Research Institute (CSIR CRRI) has officially released the Admit Card for the Junior Secretariat Assistant and Stenographer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 13-05-2025 to 20-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website crridom.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of New Delhi.
