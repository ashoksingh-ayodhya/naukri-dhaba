The LBS Centre for Science & Technology, on behalf of the Government of Kerala, has released the Kerala SET Hall Ticket 2026 for the July session of the Kerala State Eligibility Test (SET). The hall ticket is available for candidates who have successfully registered, and can be downloaded by logging in with a Registration ID or Registered Mobile Number along with the Site Access Key. The Kerala SET July 2026 exam is scheduled for 2 August 2026 across examination centres in Kerala.
