Uttarakhand Public Service Commission Admit Card 2026 has released an official notification for UKPSC Admit Card 2026 for Mains Upper Subordinate Service Exam. 

## How to Apply

1. First Open Uttarakhand Public Service Commission Official Website
2. Go to Download Admit Card Section.
3. Enter All Necessary Details Such as Application Number / Registration Number / Password / Date of Birth.
4. Login through with Required Details.
5. After entering the details your admit card will be displayed.
6. Finally Download your 2026 Admit Card.
7. Take a Printout for Colour or B&W as per Examination Instructions.
