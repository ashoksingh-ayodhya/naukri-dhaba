The IGM Noida Admit Card 2026 will be released on 28 June 2026 at 10 AM for candidates appearing in the Computer Based Test (CBT) scheduled on 1 July 2026 (Wednesday). This admit card is for the recruitment drive conducted by India Government Mint (IGM), Noida, a unit of the Security Printing and Minting Corporation of India Limited (SPMCIL), under Advertisement No. IGMN-01/2026, covering 94 vacancies for Supervisor, Junior Office Assistant, Junior Technician, and other posts. Once released, candidates will be able to download their hall ticket using their registration number and date of birth or password through the official CBT portal. This article covers everything you need to know the release timeline, download steps, exam day documents, and frequently asked questions.
