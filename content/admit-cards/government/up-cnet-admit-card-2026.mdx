Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University (ABVMU), Uttar Pradesh has released an official notification for UP CNET Admit Card 2026. 
The last date to apply is **20/05/2026**.

## How to Apply

1. Cast Certificate Instruction : OBC and EWS certificate should be issued after 01 April 2026.
2. Photo Instruction : The photograph should be full face, from front, with white background and without spectacles along with the Name of the Candidate and Date of Photograph should be written on the photograph.
3. Uttar Pradesh Common Nursing Entrance Test CNET 2026 Notification Released. Candidate Can Apply From 28/03/2026 to 20/05/2026.
4. Candidate Read the Full Notification Before Apply the Admission Application Form.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Form – Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
