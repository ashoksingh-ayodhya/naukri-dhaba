The Rajasthan Subordinate and Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB) officially Will release the Admit Card for the JTA Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 18-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website rsmssb.rajasthan.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Rajasthan.
