The Directorate of Educational Research and Training (DERT) has officially released the Admit Card for the MTET Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 25-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website megeducation.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Meghalaya.
