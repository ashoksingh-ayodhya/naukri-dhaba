New Update: Polytechnic Entrance Competitive Examination Admit Card 2025 released by the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) Authority on 14-05-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (JCECEB) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Polytechnic Entrance Competitive Examination Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The JCECEB Jharkhand Polytechnic Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
