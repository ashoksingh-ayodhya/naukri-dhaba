The RRB JE CBT 2 Admit Card 2026 has been officially released on 28 June 2026 by the Railway Recruitment Board at rrb.digialm.com. Candidates who successfully cleared the CBT 1 stage under CEN 05/2025 can now log in and download their RRB Junior Engineer hall ticket using their Registration Number and Date of Birth. The CBT 2 exam is scheduled for 2 July 2026 and will be held in three shifts across examination centres spread all over India. A total of 39,035 candidates have been shortlisted for this stage, competing for 2,588 vacancies across the posts of Junior Engineer, Depot Material Superintendent, and Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant. This article covers the direct download link, step-by-step process, shift timings, exam pattern, documents required, prohibited items, and all exam day guidelines in one place.

A total of **2588** posts are available for this recruitment.
