The Punjab Subordinate Services Selection Board (PSSSB), also known as SSSB Punjab, has released the admit card for the Junior Scale Stenographer and Stenotypist Skill Test 2026. The admit card went live on 24 June 2026, and the Stenography Skill Test will be conducted on 26 June 2026. Candidates who applied under Advertisement Numbers 7/2023, 11/2024, and 14/2025 can download their roll number/admit card directly from the official portal using their Application Number and Date of Birth.
