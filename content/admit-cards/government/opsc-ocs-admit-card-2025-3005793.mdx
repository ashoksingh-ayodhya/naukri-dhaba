The Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC) officially Released the Admit Card for the OCS Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 19-04-2025 to 27-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website opsc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Odisha.
