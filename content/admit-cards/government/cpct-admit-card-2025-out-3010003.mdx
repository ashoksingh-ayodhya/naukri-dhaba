New Update: Computer Proficiency Certification Test Admit Card 2025 released by the Madhya Pradesh Agency for Promotion of Information Technology (MAP_IT) Authority on 23-05-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Madhya Pradesh Agency for Promotion of Information Technology (MAP_IT) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Madhya Pradesh Agency for Promotion of Information Technology (MAP_IT) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Computer Proficiency Certification Test Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The CPCT Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
