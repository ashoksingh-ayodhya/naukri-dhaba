New Update: UG and PG Courses Admit Card 2025 released by the Odisha University of Agriculture and Technology (OUAT) Authority on 20-05-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Odisha University of Agriculture and Technology (OUAT) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Odisha University of Agriculture and Technology (OUAT) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the UG and PG Courses Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The B.Arch/B.Voc/M.Arch/M.Com/Other Exams Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
