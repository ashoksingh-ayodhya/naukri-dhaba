The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has issued an official notice for the online download of the personal interview call letter (entry pass) under Advertisement No. 47/2023-24, covering the Gujarat Administrative Service Class-1, Gujarat Civil Service Class-1 and Class-2, and Gujarat Municipal Chief Officer Service, Class-2 posts. Eligible candidates for the eighth phase of personal interviews can now download their call letter and annexure from the GPSC OJAS portal. This article explains the important dates, the step-by-step download process, required documents, and key instructions exactly as stated in the official notification.
