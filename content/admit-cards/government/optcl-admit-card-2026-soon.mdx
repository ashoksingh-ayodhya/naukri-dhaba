The Odisha Power Transmission Corporation Limited (OPTCL) is set to release the OPTCL Admit Card 2026 in the 3rd week of June 2026 for candidates appearing in the Computer-Based Test (CBT) for Management Trainee, Junior Management Trainee, and Office Assistant Grade-III Trainee posts. The CBT exam is scheduled for 29th and 30th June 2026, and candidates can download their hall ticket from the official website, optcl.co.in, using their Application Number and Date of Birth.

A total of **50** posts are available for this recruitment.
