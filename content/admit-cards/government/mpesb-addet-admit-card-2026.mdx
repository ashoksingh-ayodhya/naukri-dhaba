The admit card is available on the official MPESB portal, and the entrance exam is scheduled for 10 July 2026. Candidates can download their admit card using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. This article covers the complete download process, important dates, eligibility, fee details, and the direct link to access the admit card.
