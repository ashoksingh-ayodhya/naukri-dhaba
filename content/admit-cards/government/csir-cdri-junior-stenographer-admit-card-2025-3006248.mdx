The CSIR Central Drug Research Institute (CSIR CDRI) has officially released the Admit Card for the Junior Stenographer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 27-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website cdri.res.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Uttar Pradesh.
