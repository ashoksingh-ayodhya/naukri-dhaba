The GPSSB Additional Assistant Engineer Admit Card 2026 has been officially released by the Gujarat Panchayat Service Selection Board (GPSSB) on its official website, gpssb.gujarat.gov.in. Candidates who applied for the posts of Additional Assistant Engineer (Civil), Tracer, Research Assistant, and Statistical Assistant can now download their call letters online using their Confirmation Number and Date of Birth. The admit card is a mandatory entry document for the Computer-Based Response Test (CBRT) and must be printed and carried to the examination hall on exam day.
