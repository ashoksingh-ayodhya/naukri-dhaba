The Reserve Bank of India has officially released the RBI Grade B Phase 1 Admit Card 2026 on its official portal at rbi.org.in. Candidates who applied for the Grade B Officer (General Cadre) post can download their Phase 1 call letter using their Registration Number and Date of Birth. The Phase 1 exam for the General post was conducted on 13th June 2026, while the Phase 2 Mains exam is scheduled for 25th July 2026 and the Mains admit card is now the next major release that candidates are eagerly waiting for.

A total of **60** posts are available for this recruitment.
