The Andhra Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Assistant Chemist Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 28-04-2025 to 29-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website psc.ap.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Andhra Pradesh.
