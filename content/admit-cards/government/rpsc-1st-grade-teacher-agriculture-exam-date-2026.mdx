Short Information : Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the exam date for the post of 1st Grade Teacher (Agriculture). This recruitment was released for 500 posts. Applications for the Rajasthan RPSC School 1st Grade Teacher (Agriculture) Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 04 September 2025, to 03 October 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 31 May 2026 to 16 June 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can check their RPSC 1st Grade Teacher (Agriculture) Exam Date 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **500** posts are available for this recruitment.
