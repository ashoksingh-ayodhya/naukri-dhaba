Candidates must have Degree in Any Stream with 50% Marks and 2 Year Diploma in Education / Elementary Education OR; Graduation Degree with 50% Marks and B.Ed / Special B.Ed Degree OR; Intermediate with 50% Marks and 4 Year BA B.Ed / B.Com B.Ed Degree.; For More / Full Eligibility Details Kindly Read Notification / Advertisement. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test 2025 Notification Released and Candidate Apply within Last Date.
2. Candidate Read Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test Notification 2025 very carefully before Apply the Application Form.
3. While applying, the candidate will have to fill all the columns carefully so that no mistake can be made like – Name of the candidate, Father’s name, Mother’s name, Date of birth, Address, Qualification details.
4. If upload is asked in the application form, then upload all the documents in the correct size and correct format, be it PDF or JPEG.
5. Before submitting the form, re-check all the columns and documents and submit only if everything is correct.
6. After submitting the form of Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test Application Form 2025, take a print of it or save it in PDF.
