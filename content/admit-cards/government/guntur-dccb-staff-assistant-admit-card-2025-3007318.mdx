The Guntur District Cooperative Central Bank Ltd (Guntur DCCB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Staff Assistant Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 11-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website gunturdccb.com. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Andhra Pradesh.
