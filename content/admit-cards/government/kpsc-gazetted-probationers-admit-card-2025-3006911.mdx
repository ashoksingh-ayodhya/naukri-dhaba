The Karnataka Public Service Commission (KPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Gazetted Probationers Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 03-05-2025 to 09-05-2025 can now download their hall ticket from the official website kpsc.kar.nic.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Karnataka.
