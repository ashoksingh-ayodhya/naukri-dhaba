The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially released the RSSB LDC Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the Clerk Grade-II and Junior Assistant Combined Direct Recruitment Examination. The admit card went live on the official portal on 1 July 2026, ahead of the written exam scheduled for 5 July 2026. Candidates can log in with their application credentials to download their hall ticket, which carries their exam date, shift, reporting time, and centre details.

A total of **10644** posts are available for this recruitment.
