Short Information : Indo Tibetan Border Police Force (ITBP) has released the PET / PST admit card for the post of Constable Driver. This recruitment was released for 712 posts. Applications for the ITBP Constable Driver Recruitment 2025 were accepted from October 08, 2024, to November 06, 2024. The PET/PST examination is scheduled to be conducted on November, December 2025. Candidates who have applied for this recruitment can download their ITBP Constable Driver PET / PST Admit Card 2025 from the link provided below.

A total of **48** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 275 as minimum educational qualification.
