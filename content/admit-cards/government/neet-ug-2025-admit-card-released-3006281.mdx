New Update: National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test Admit Card 2025 released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) Authority on 20-04-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the National Testing Agency (NTA) Official Website. If candidate faces any query regarding the National Eligibility Cum Entrance Test Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The NEET UG 2025 Admit Card will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
