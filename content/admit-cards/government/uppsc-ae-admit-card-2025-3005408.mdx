The UPPSC AE Admit Card 2025 has been released on the official website of the Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission at uppsc.up.nic.in. Candidates appearing for the Assistant Engineer Preliminary Examination, scheduled for April 20, 2025, must download their admit cards through the official portal once released. This year, a total of 604 vacancies have been announced under the UPPSC AE recruitment drive. Additionally, the Mains Examination is slated to take place on September 28, 2025.
