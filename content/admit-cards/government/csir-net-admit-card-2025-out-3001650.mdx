New Update: National Eligibility Test Admit Card 2025 released by the National Testing Agency (NTA) Authority on 26-02-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the National Testing Agency (NTA) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the National Testing Agency (NTA) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the National Eligibility Test Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The CSIR NET Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
