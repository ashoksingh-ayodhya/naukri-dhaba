The CSIR Central Road Research Institute (CSIR CRRI) officially will release the Admit Card for the JSA and Junior Stenographer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 13-05-2025 to 20-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website crridom.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of New Delhi.
