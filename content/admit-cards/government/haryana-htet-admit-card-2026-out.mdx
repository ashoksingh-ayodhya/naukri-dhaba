The Board of School Education Haryana (BSEH) has released the Haryana HTET Admit Card 2026 on its official website for candidates appearing in the Level-1 (PRT), Level-2 (TGT), and Level-3 (PGT) examinations. Candidates can download their hall ticket by logging into the official portal at htet.eapplynow.com or bseh.org.in using their enrollment number, registration number, or date of birth. The HTET 2026 exam will be conducted on 4 and 5 July 2026.
