Short Information : Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the Admit Card for the posts of Hospital Assistant. This recruitment has been issued for 1200 posts. The online application process for MPESB Hospital Assistant Recruitment 2026 were accepted from 07 May 2026, to 23 May 2026. The Examination is going to conducted on 24 June 2026. Candidates must check the complete details for MPESB Hospital Assistant Admit Card 2026 given below.

A total of **240** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 324 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Check the Important Links section.
2. Then click on the Admit Card link.
3. After clicking on the link, candidates will be redirected to the Dashboard page.
4. In this page candidates have to fill up the following fields with proper details: Candidate’s Registration Number Date of Birth Gender Verification Code
5. Then the candidates will be required to submit the details in order to Check the Admit Card.
6. The candidate can also Check their Admit Card from the official Website.
