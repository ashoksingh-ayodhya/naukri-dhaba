The Chhattisgarh Public Service Commission (CGPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Boiler Inspector Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 04-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website psc.cg.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Chhattisgarh.
