Assam University, Silchar will be released the admit card for the B.Ed Entrance Examination 2026 on 24th July 2026. This admit card is mandatory for candidates appearing in the entrance test for admission to the 2-year B.Ed programme (2026–2028 session) at Teacher Training Colleges and Institutes affiliated with Assam University. Candidates can download their hall ticket from the official portal, ausexamination.ac.in, using their registered login credentials.
