The Department of Biotechnology (DBT) has released the admit card for the Scientist 'C' recruitment exam 2026. Candidates who applied for the 10 vacant Scientist 'C' (Group A, Gazetted) posts can now download their hall ticket from the official recruitment portal, rcbdbt26.rcb.ac.in/RCBDBT/. The admit card carries the candidate's exam date, reporting time, test city, and venue details, and must be carried to the exam hall along with a valid photo ID.

A total of **10** posts are available for this recruitment.
