The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) has released the Memorandum of Admission (Hall Ticket) for candidates appearing in the Main Examination for the post of Assistant Public Prosecutor, Grade-II. This update is based on Press Release No. 84/2026, dated 17.07.2026, issued by the Controller of Examinations. Candidates who cleared the Preliminary Examination can now download their hall tickets through the OTR Dashboard on the official TNPSC websites.
