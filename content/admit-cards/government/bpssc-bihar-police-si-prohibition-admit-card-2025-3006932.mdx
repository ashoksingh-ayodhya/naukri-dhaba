The Bihar Police Subordinate Services Commission (BPSSC has officially released on 03 May 2025 the Admit Card for the SI Prohibition Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 18-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website bpssc.bihar.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Bihar.
