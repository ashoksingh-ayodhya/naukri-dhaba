New Update: 1st and 2nd year Hall Ticket 2025 released by the Telangana Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE) Authority on 03-03-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Telangana Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Hall Tickets that will be available in the Telangana Board of Intermediate Education (TSBIE) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the 1st and 2nd year Hall Ticket 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The TSBIE Inter 1st and 2nd year Hall Ticket 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
