The Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC) has issued a notification titled "Admit Card Download 3 of 2026-27" (No. A.12033/1/2026-MPSC(EA)), dated 22 July 2026. This notice covers the admit card download schedule and exam dates for 13 different posts, including Assistant under DP&AR (SSW) and SEC and Upper Division Clerk (UDC) under Various Departments and under H&FW. Candidates can download their admit cards from the official MPSC Online Portal within the specified window for each post.
