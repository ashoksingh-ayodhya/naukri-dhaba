Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited (BPCL) has officially released the BPCL Entry Level Admit Card 2026 for the online Computer-Based Test (CBT). The admit card has been released for recruitment to 250 vacancies of Junior Executive (Engineering, Accounts, Wayside Amenities), Secretary, and Associate Executive (Quality Assurance, Official Language Implementation) posts. The examination is scheduled to be held on 05 July 2026. Registered candidates can now download their BPCL hall ticket 2026 from the IBPS portal using their Registration Number / Roll Number and Password / Date of Birth. No hard copy of the admit card will be sent to candidates - the BPCL Hall Ticket 2026 is available in online mode only.

A total of **250** posts are available for this recruitment.
