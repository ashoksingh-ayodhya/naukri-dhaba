The Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) officially released the Admit Card for the Non Teaching Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 14-05-2025 to 19-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website navodaya.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across India.

A total of **1377** posts are available for this recruitment.
