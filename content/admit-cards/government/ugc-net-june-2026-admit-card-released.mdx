The University Grants Commission (UGC) has released an official notification for UGC NET June 2026 Admit Card – Released. 

## How to Apply

1. Union Grant Commission NET 2026 Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the UGC NET Examination 2026 . Candidate Can Apply Online for 29/04/2026 to 20/05/2026.
2. Register using a valid email ID and mobile number
3. Login using registration ID and password
4. Fill the application form with personal, educational, and category details
5. Upload scanned documents (photo, signature, etc.)
6. Submit the form and download/print the confirmation page
7. Candidate Read the RBI Apprentice Notification 2026 Before Apply the Reserve Bank Officer Application Form 2026 in Sarkari Result Latest Job Section.
