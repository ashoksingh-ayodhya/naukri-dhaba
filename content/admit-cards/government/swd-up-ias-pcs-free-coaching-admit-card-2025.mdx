Short Information : Social Welfare Department, SWD Uttar Pradesh, has released the admit card for the SWD UP IAS / PCS Free Coaching Admission 2025 on the official website of the SWD. Applications for the SWD UP IAS / PCS Free Coaching Online Form 2025 were accepted from February 25, 2025, to March 31, 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 27 April 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their SWD UP IAS / PCS Free Coaching Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.


## How to Apply

1. DRDO CEPTAM-11 Tier-II Exam Date 2026
2. UPSSSC Pharmacist Eligibility Revised Exam Date 2026
3. RBI Assistant Mains Admit Card 2026
4. Bihar Police CSBC Prohibition Constable Exam Date 2026
5. BSNL Senior Executive Trainee SET Exam Date 2026
