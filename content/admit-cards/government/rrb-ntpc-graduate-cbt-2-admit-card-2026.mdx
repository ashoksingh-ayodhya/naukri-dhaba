The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) will be released the RRB NTPC Graduate CBT 2 Admit Card 2026 today, on 6th July 2026, four days ahead of the CBT 2 exam scheduled for 10th July 2026. This admit card is available for the 88,037 candidates who qualified in the CBT 1 exam for CEN 06/2025 Graduate Level posts. Candidates can now download their hall ticket from their respective regional RRB website using their Registration Number and Date of Birth.
