The Bihar Home Guard Department (Bihar) has officially released the Admit Card for the Home Guard Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 30-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website onlinebhg.bihar.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Bihar.
