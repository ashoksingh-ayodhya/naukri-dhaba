Short Information : Madhya Pradesh Power Generating Company (MPPGCL) has released the admit card for the Assistant Engineer (Mechanical/ Electrical/ Electronics) Post. This recruitment was released for 40 posts. Applications for MPPGCL Assistant Engineer AE Recruitment 2025 were filled from 22 October 2024 to 20 November 2024. The written examination is going to be conducted on 23 March 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their admit card from the link given below.

A total of **16** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Candidates Having BE/ B.Tech or AMIE Degree in Electronics/ Electronics & Instrumentation/ Electronics & Telecommunication/ Electronics & Communication/ Instrumentation/ Instrumentation & Control Engineering from a University/ Institute recognized by AICTE/ UGC. as minimum educational qualification.
