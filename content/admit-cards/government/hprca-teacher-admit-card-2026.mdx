The Himachal Pradesh Revenue and Common Service Centre Authority (HPRCA) has officially released the admit card for the Computer Teacher Recruitment Exam 2026. Candidates who applied for the HPRCA Computer Teacher post can now download their hall ticket from the official TCS iON portal at cdn3.digialm.com. The admit card download window is open from 26 June 2026 to 3 July 2026 till 6:00 PM. All eligible candidates must download and carry their admit card to the examination centre without fail.
