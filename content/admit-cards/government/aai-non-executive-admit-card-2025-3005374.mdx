The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has officially released the admit card for the 2025 recruitment exam for the posts of Junior Assistant and Senior Assistant. The examination is set to take place on April 21 and 22, 2025. Candidates who have applied for these Non-Executive roles can now download their call letters by visiting the official website at aai.aero.

A total of **224** posts are available for this recruitment.
