Short Information : Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) has released the Admit Card for the post of Senior Executive Trainee (Telecom & Finance). This recruitment was released for 120 posts. Applications for the BSNL Senior Executive Trainee SET Recruitment 2026 were accepted from 05 February 2026, to 30 April 2026. The written examination is going to be conducted on 05 July 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their BSNL Senior Executive Trainee SET Exam City Details 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **120** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Charted Accountant (CA); Cost and Management Accountancy (CMA) as minimum educational qualification.
