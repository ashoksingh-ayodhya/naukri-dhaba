The UPTET Admit Card 2026 has released 29 June 2026, by the Uttar Pradesh Education Service Selection Commission (UPESSC). Once activated, candidates can download their hall ticket using their registration number and date of birth or OTP from the official portals upessc.up.gov.in and admitcards.upessc.org. The admit card is mandatory for entry into the UPTET 2026 examination, which will be held on 2, 3, and 4 July 2026 across multiple districts of Uttar Pradesh.
