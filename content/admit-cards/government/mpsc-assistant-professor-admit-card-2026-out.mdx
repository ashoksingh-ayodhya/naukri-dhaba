The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) released the MPSC Assistant Professor Admit Card 2026 on 17th July 2026 on its official website. Candidates who applied for the Assistant Professor recruitment can now download their hall ticket online. The computer-based screening exam is scheduled for 23rd July 2026 at exam centres in Mumbai and Pune. Every candidate must carry the admit card along with a valid photo ID to enter the exam centre. This article covers the download link, step-by-step process, exam schedule, required documents, and important instructions for the MPSC Assistant Professor exam.
