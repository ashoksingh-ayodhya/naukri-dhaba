The Central Electronics Engineering Research Institute (CSIR CEERI) has officially released the Admit Card for the JSA and Junior Stenographer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 27-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website ceeri.res.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Rajasthan.
