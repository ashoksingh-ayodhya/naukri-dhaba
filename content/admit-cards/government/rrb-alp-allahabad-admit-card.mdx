Aspirants who want to join the Railway Recruitment Board have started submitting application forms through online. RRB Allahabad will hold the Computer Based Test for the ALP recruitment in upcoming days. As per the official data, more than 1.4 crore candidates have submitted the online applications across India. As the RRB Allahabad recruitment is being conducted zone wise. Every zone has the responsibility of managing and organizing the RRB Allahabad ALP CBT appropriately.
