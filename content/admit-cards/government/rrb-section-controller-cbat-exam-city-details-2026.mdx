Applications for the Railway RRB RRB Section Controller Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 15 September 2025, to 15 October 2025. The CBAT examination examination is going to be conducted on 06 June 2026. Candidates who qualified for this CBT I examination can download their Railway RRB Section Controller CBAT Exam City Details 2026 from the link provided below.

Candidates must have 174 as minimum educational qualification.
