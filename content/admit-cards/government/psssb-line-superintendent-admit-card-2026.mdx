The Punjab Subordinate Services Selection Board (PSSSB) has activated the admit card download portal for the Line Superintendent Recruitment 2026 (Advertisement No. 11 of 2026). The written examination for this 6-vacancy Group-C post in the Electricity Department (Energy Branch) is scheduled for 19 July 2026, as per the official PSSSB Exam Date Notice dated 16 June 2026. Candidates can generate their hall ticket using their Application Number and Date of Birth. The exact date on which the admit card link went live has not been officially confirmed with a specific date in the notices reviewed for this article PSSSB's public communication states only that admit cards, exam dates, and results will be published exclusively on the official website, with no individual communication sent to applicants.

A total of **6** posts are available for this recruitment.
