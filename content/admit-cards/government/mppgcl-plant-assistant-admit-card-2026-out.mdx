Short Information : Madhya Pradesh Power Generating Company (MPPGCL) has released the Admit Card for the Plant Assistant. This recruitment was released for 90 posts. Applications for MPPGCL Plant Assistant Recruitment 2025 were filled from 01 December 2025 to 30 December 2025. The CBT examination is going to be conducted on 26 February 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their MPPGCL Plant Assistant Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **37** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 10th Class Passed, ITI in the Electrician / Wireman / Electronics / Instrumentation Trade. as minimum educational qualification.
