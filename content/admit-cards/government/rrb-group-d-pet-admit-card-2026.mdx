The RRB Group D PET Admit Card 2026 has started releasing for candidates who qualified in the Computer-Based Test (CBT) under CEN No. 08/2024. The Railway Recruitment Cell (RRC), Prayagraj released its Physical Efficiency Test (PET) admit card on 18 July 2026, with the Prayagraj PET scheduled from 25 July to 28 July 2026. Admit cards for the remaining RRB/RRC zones will be released in a phased manner as each zone's PET approaches.

A total of **22195** posts are available for this recruitment.
