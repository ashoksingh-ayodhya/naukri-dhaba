Short Information : India Post Payment Bank IPPB has released the admit card for the Specialist Officer SO Pre Examination 2024. This recruitment has been issued for 68 posts. Applications for India Post IPPB Specialist Officer SO Recruitment 2024 were filled from 21 December 2024 to 10 January 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 09 February 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their admit card from the link given below.

A total of **07** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have BSc. Electronics, Physics, Computer Science, Information Technology. OR; B.Tech /B.E- Electronics, Information Technology, Computer Science. OR; MSc. Electronics, Physics, Applied Electronics/Computer Science/Information Technology.; Certifications in any of the following domain will be given preference CISSP, CISA, CISM, CEH, Cyber Law etc. as minimum educational qualification.
