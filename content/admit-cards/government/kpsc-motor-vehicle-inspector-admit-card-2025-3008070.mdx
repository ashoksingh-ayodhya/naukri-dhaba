The Karnataka Public Service Commission (KPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Motor Vehicle Inspector Exam 2025 on 06-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 13-05-2025 to 14-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website kpsc.kar.nic.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Karnataka.
