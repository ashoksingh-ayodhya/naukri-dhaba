The Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the ANM, Junior Laboratory Technician, Pharmacist and Staff Nurse Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 01-05-2025 to 02-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website ossc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Odisha.
