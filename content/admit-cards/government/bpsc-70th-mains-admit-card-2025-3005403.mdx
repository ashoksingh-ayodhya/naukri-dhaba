The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) will conduct the 70th Combined Competitive Examination (CCE) Mains 2025 on April 25, 26, 28, 29, and 30 at various examination centers across the state. Candidates appearing for the exam can expect the BPSC 70th Mains admit card to be released on April 12, 2025. Aspirants are advised to regularly check the official website for updates and download their admit card once available.
