Short Information : Indo Tibetan Border Police Force (ITBP) has released the PET / PST admit card for the post of Constable & Head Constable (Motor Mechanic) Group-C (Non-Gazetted). The PET/PST examination is scheduled to be conducted on November, December 2025. Candidates who have applied for this recruitment can download their ITBP HC/ Constable Motor Mechanic PET / PST Admit Card 2025 from the link provided below.

A total of **44** posts are available for this recruitment.
