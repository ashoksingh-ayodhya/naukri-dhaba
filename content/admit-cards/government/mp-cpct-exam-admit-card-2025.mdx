Short Information : Madhya Pradesh State Electronics Development Corporation (MPSEDC) has released the Admit Card for the exam of MP Computer Proficiency Certification Test (MP CPCT 2025). Applications for the MP CPCT Online Form 2025 were accepted from February 10, 2025, to February 24, 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 15 – 16 March 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their MP CPCT Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.
