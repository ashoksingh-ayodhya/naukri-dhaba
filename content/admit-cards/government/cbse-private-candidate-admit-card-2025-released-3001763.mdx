New Update: 10th and 12th Class Admit Card 2025 released by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) Authority on 28-02-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the 10th and 12th Class Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The CBSE Private Candidate Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
