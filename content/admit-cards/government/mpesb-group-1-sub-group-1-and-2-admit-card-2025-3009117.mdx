The Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Group 1 (Sub Group 1 and 2) Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 25-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website esb.mp.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Madhya Pradesh.
