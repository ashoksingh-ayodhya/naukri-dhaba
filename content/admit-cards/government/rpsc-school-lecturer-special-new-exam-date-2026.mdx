Short Information : Rajasthan Public ervice Commission (RPSC) has released the New Exam Date for the post of School Lecturer (Special). This recruitment was released for 121 posts. Applications for the RPSC School Lecturer Special Recruitment 2026 were accepted from 01 June 2026 to 30 June 2026. The online examination is going to be conducted on 08 December 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their RPSC School Lecturer Special New Exam Date 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **14** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 46 as minimum educational qualification.
