The Supreme Court of India (Supreme Court of India) has officially released the Admit Card for the Personal Assistant Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 01-05-2025 and 07-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website sci.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of New Delhi.
