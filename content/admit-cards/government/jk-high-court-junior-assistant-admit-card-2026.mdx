The High Court of Jammu & Kashmir and Ladakh has released the admit card for the Junior Assistant Offline OMR-Based Written Examination 2026. Candidates who applied under Advertisement Notification No. 02 of 2025/RR/RC dated 26 May 2025 can download their hall ticket from the official website, jkhighcourt.nic.in, starting 13 July 2026. The written exam will be held on 19 July 2026 (Sunday) at centres in Srinagar and Jammu.
