The Gujarat Panchayat Seva Pasandgi Mandal (GPSSB) has officially announced the release of the online admit card (call letter-cum-attendance slip) for Advertisement No. 04/2026-27 for the Laboratory Technician cadre. The exam will be conducted through the Computer-Based Response Test (CBRT) method using Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Candidates can download their admit card from the official GPSSB website within the specified time window before the exam date.
