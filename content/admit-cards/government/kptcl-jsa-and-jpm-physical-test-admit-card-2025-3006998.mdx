The Karnataka Power Transmission Corporation (KPTCL) officially released the Admit Card for the JSA and JPM Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 05-05-2025 to 08-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website kptcl.karnataka.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Karnataka.
