The Karnataka State Police (KSP), through the Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA), is conducting the Kannada Language Test on 19 July 2026 from 10:30 AM to 12:30 PM as part of the KSP Civil Police Constable Recruitment 2026 process. This test applies to candidates who applied for Group A and Group B departmental posts as well as Civil Police Constable posts. The hall ticket for this stage must be downloaded from the official KEA exam portal before appearing for the test.

A total of **3991** posts are available for this recruitment.
