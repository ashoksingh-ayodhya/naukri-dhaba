The High Court of Jharkhand (Jharkhand High Court) has officially released the Admit Card for the District Judge Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 27-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website jharkhandhighcourt.nic.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Jharkhand.
