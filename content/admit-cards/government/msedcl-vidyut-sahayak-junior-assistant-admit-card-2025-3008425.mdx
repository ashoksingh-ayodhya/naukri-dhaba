The Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Company Limited (MSEDCL) has officially released the Admit Card for the Vidyut Sahayak (Junior Assistant) Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 20-05-2025 to 22-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website mahadiscom.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Maharashtra.
