The Bharat Electronics Ltd (BEL) has officially released the Admit Card for the Trainee Engineer and Project Engineer Exam 2025 on 14-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 25-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website bel-india.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Haryana.
