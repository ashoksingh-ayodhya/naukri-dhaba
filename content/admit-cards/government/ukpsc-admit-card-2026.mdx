The Uttarakhand Public Service Commission (UKPSC) has released the admit card for the Assistant Review Officer, Assistant Librarian, Translator and Typist Examination-2025 on 4 July 2026. This examination is being conducted for filling 24 vacancies in the Establishment Office of the Hon'ble High Court, Nainital. Candidates who applied against Advertisement No. A-5/E-3/DR(H.C.)/2025 can now download their hall ticket from the official website, psc.uk.gov.in or ukpsc.net.in. The preliminary examination (objective type) is scheduled for 19 July 2026 (Sunday) and will be held at exam centers in Haridwar, Haldwani and Dehradun.

A total of **24** posts are available for this recruitment.
