REET Admit Card 2025 - The officials of Board of Secondary Education Rajasthan released the REET Admit Card 2025 on 19-02-2025. Candidates have to carry the REET Admit Card 2025 along with the original ID proof to the exam centre. Candidates must preserve the admit card with the original photo and signature of the candidate and invigilator carefully to present it at the time of admission process. No entry shall be allowed without valid REET Admit Card 2025 and photo identity. For more details scroll down the article.
