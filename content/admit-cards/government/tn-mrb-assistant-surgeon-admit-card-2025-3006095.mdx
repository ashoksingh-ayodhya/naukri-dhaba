The Tamil Nadu Medical Recruitment Board (TN MRB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Assistant Surgeon Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 24-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website mrb.tn.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Tamil Nadu.
