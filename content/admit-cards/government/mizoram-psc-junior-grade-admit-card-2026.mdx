The Mizoram PSC Junior Grade Admit Card 2026 for the Junior Grade of Mizoram Civil Service (Limited Competitive Examination), LCE No. 1 of 2025-26, will be released by the Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC) . As per the official notification dated 23 July 2026, the admit card will be released for download between 30 July 2026 and 9 August 2026 on the MPSC Online Portal (mpsconline.mizoram.gov.in).
