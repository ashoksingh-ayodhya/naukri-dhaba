The Uttarakhand Public Service Commission (UKPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Upper PCS Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 14-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website psc.uk.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Uttarakhand.
