The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has released the Typing Test Admit Card for Post Code 802/23 (Advt. No. 05/2023) covering DASS Grade-4 / Junior Assistant, Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Stenographer, and Assistant Grade-I posts. The admit card was released on 23 July 2026, and the Typing Test will be conducted from 27 to 31 July 2026. Candidates who qualified in the written examination and are shortlisted for the typing/skill stage can download their admit card from the official DSSSB exam portal.
