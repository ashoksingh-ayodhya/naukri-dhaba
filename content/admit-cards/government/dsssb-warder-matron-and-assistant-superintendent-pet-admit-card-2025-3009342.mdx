The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Warder, Matron and Assistant Superintendent Exam 2025 On 19-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on Warder: 26-05-2025 to 05-06-2025, Matron: 09-06-2025 to 10-06-2025, Assistant Superintendent: 11-06-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website dsssb.delhi.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Delhi.
