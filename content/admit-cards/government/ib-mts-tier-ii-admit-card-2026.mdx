The Ministry of Home Affairs (MHA) has released the IB MTS Tier-II Admit Card 2026 on 11 July 2026 for candidates shortlisted after the Tier-I examination of the Intelligence Bureau (IB) Multi-Tasking Staff (MTS) Recruitment 2026. The Tier-II exam is scheduled to be conducted on 12 July 2026. Eligible candidates can download their hall ticket from the official recruitment portal using their User ID/Registration Number and Password.
