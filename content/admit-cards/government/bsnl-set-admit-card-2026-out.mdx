The BSNL SET Admit Card 2026 has been officially released by Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) on 23rd June 2026. All registered candidates appearing for the BSNL Senior Executive Trainee (SET) Computer-Based Examination 2026 can now download their hall ticket from the official portal at ibtexamination.com. The exam is scheduled for 5th July 2026 (Sunday). Candidates must download and print at least 2-3 copies of the BSNL SET Hall Ticket 2026 well in advance to avoid any last-minute technical issues. Without a valid admit card, no candidate will be permitted to enter the examination venue.
