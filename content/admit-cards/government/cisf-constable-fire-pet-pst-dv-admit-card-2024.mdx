Short Information : Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) has released the PET / PST / DV Admit Card for the post of Constable Fire. This recruitment has been issued for 1130 posts. Applications for CISF Constable Fire Recruitment 2024 were filled from 31 August 2024 to 30 September 2024. Candidates who have done 12th in Any Stream are eligible for this recruitment. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Admit Card from the link given below.

A total of **161** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 236 as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Bihar Vidhan Parishad Driver Phase-II Admit Card 2026
2. Union Bank of India Apprentices Exam Date 2026
3. Bihar BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Phase 5th Admit Card 2026
4. DSSSB June Admit Card 2026
5. UP Police SI DV & PST Date 2026
