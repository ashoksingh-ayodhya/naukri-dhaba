Short Information : High Court of Delhi has released the mains admit card for the post of Higher Judicial Service (HJS) Examination 2024. This recruitment was released for 16 posts. Applications for the Delhi High Court HJS Recruitment 2024 were accepted from 27 December 2024, to 10 January 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on March 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Bank of Delhi High Court HJS Mains Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **6** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 5 as minimum educational qualification.
