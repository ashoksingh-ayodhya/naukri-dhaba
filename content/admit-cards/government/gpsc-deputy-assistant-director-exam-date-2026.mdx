The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has announced the date and time of the main written examination for two posts under Advertisement No. 45/2025-26: Deputy Director of Information (Nayab Mahiti Niyamak), Class-1 and Assistant Director of Information (Editing) (Sahayak Mahiti Niyamak, Sampadan), Class-2. The exam will be held over two days, 20 and 21 September 2026, covering three papers - Gujarati Language, English Language, and Journalism. This notice was issued by the Joint Secretary, GPSC, and is dated 22 July 2026.
