HPRCA Proof Reader and Copy Holder Admit Card 2026 has been released by Himachal Pradesh Rajya Chayan Aayog (HPRCA) on 02 June 2026. Candidates who applied for Proof Reader (Post Code-25005) and Copy Holder (Post Code-25006) can download their admit card from the official website hprca.hp.gov.in. The Computer Based Test (CBT) is scheduled for 05 June 2026 (Proof Reader in morning shift & Copy Holder in afternoon shift) across various centers in Himachal Pradesh. This hall ticket is mandatory for appearing in the exam.
