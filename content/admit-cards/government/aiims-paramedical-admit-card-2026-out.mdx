The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi has officially released the AIIMS Paramedical Admit Card 2026 on its examination portal aiimsexams.ac.in. Candidates who registered for the AIIMS BSc Paramedical Entrance Examination 2026 can now download their hall ticket by logging in with their Registration ID and Password. The entrance exam is scheduled on 4 July 2026 (Saturday) in Computer-Based Test (CBT) mode. Since the admit card is mandatory for entry into the exam centre, candidates must download and print it well in advance.
