Short Information : National Seeds Corporation Limited (NSCL) has recently uploaded Trainee, Management Trainee, Sr. Trainee, Assistant Manager & Deputy General Manager Exam Date for the Examination scheduled on 05 January, 2025 for aforesaid recruitment. Examination will be organized in Online Mode. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Exam Date from the link given below..

A total of **13** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have ITI certificate in relevant trade of Fitter/ Electrician/ Auto Electrician/ Welder/ Diesel Mechanic/ Tractor Mechanic/ Machineman/ Blacksmith with minimum 60% marks and one year trade apprenticeship training in any industry and passed NAC Examination conducted by National Council for Vocational Training (NCVT). as minimum educational qualification.
