Candidates who had applied this form can download their NABARD Development Assistant Grade-B Mains 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **03** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Candidates having Bachelor’s Degree from a recognized University in English / Hindi medium with Hindi and English as a compulsory or elective subject with a minimum of 50% marks (pass class for SC / ST / PWBD / EXS candidates) in the aggregate OR Bachelor’s Degree with Hindi and English as main subjects with a minimum of 50% marks (pass class for SC / ST / PWBD / EXS candidates) in the aggregate.; Candidate must be able to translate from English to Hindi and vice-versa.; Knowledge of word processing on PC. as minimum educational qualification.
