Short Information : Defence Research & Development Organisation (DRDO CEPTAM) has released the Tier-II Exam Date for the recruitment of Senior Technical Assistant-B, Technician-A Posts on the official website of the DRDO. This recruitment was released for 764 posts. Applications for the DRDO CEPTAM-11 Recruitment 2026 were accepted from December 11, 2025, to January 11, 2026. The Tier-II examination is going to conducted on 15 June 2026. Candidates who had appeared in Tier-I Examination can download their DRDO CEPTAM-11 Tier-II Exam Date 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **203** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 10th Class Passed & ITI in the Related Trade. as minimum educational qualification.
