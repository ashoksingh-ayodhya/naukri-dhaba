Short Information : Join Indian Coast Guard has released the Stage-III Admit Card for the post of Navik GD & Yantrik 01/2026 & 02/2026 Batch on the official website of the Indian Coast Guard. This recruitment was released for 630 posts. Applications for the Coast Guard Navik & Yantrik 01, 02/2026 Recruitment 2025 were accepted from June 11, 2025, to June 29, 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their Coast Guard Navik CGEPT 02/2026 Batch Stage-III Admit Card from the link given below.

A total of **50** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Class 10th passed from an education board recognized by Council of Boards for School Education (COBSE); Diploma in Relevant Engineering of duration 03 or 04 years as minimum educational qualification.
