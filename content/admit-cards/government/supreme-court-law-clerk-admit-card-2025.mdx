Applications for Supreme Court Law Clerk Recruitment 2025 were filled from 14 January 2025 to 07 February 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 09 March 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their admit card from the link given below.

A total of **90** posts are available for this recruitment.
