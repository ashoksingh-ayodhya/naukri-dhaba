Short Information : Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) has released the Trade Test / DV Admit Card for the post of Constable Driver / Constable Driver Cum Pump Operator. This recruitment was released for 1124 posts. Applications for the CISF Constable Driver Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 03 February 2025, to 04 March 2025. The Trade Test / DV examination is scheduled to be conducted on 07-22 November 2025. Candidates who have applied for this recruitment can download their CISF Constable Driver PET/ PST Trade Test / DV 2025 from the link provided below.

A total of **20** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 27 as minimum educational qualification.
