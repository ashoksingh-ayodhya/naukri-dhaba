The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UP Police) has officially released the Admit Card for the Constable (Skilled Players) Exam 2025 on 19-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 26-05-2025 to 28-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website uppbpb.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Uttar Pradesh.
