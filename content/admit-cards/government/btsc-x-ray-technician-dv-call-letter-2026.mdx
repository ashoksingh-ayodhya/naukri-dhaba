Short Information : Bihar Technical Services Commission (BTSC) has released the DV Call Letter for the post of X Ray Technician. This recruitment was released for 9757 posts. Applications for the BTSC OT Assistant, Lab, ECG, X-Ray Technician & SMO Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 04 March 2025, to 01 April 2025. The written examination was held on 26 April 2025. Candidates who appeared for this examination can download their BTSC X Ray Technician DV Call Letter 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **3623** posts are available for this recruitment.
