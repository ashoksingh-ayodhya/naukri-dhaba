Short Information : Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has recently released the exam date for the post of Computer Instructor on the official website of RSSB. This recruitment was released for 3951 posts. Applications for the RSSB Computer Instructor Recruitment 2026 were accepted from January 25, 2026, to June 23, 2026. The online examination is going to be conducted on 22-23 June 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their RSSB Computer Instructor Exam Date 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **3951** posts are available for this recruitment.
