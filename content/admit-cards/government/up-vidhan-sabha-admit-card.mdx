Uttar Pradesh Vidhan Sabha has issued the Admit Card for the Typing Test against the recruitment notification for the post of RO/ARO. The Typing Test is scheduled to be held on 16-09-2016. Admit Cards are now available in the official website of UP Vidhan Sabha. Candidates who have qualified the Re Exam are requested to view and download the Admit Card from the official Website of UP Vidhan Sabha. Candidates are requested to download the Admit Card before the commencement of the selection process i.e. 16-09-2016.
