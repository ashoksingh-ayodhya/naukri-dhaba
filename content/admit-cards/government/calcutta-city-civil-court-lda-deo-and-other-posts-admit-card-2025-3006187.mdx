The Calcutta City Civil Court has officially released the Admit Card for the LDA, DEO and Other Posts Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on April 2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website citycivilcourtcalcutta.dcourts.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of West Bengal.
