Short Information : National Testing Agency (NTA) has released the NEET UG 2026 Re-Exam Admit Card on its official website for candidates appearing in the undergraduate medical entrance exam. According to the notification, the NEET UG 2026 Re-Exam is scheduled to be held on 21 June 2026. The last date for submitting the online application form was 11 March 2026. Candidates can access their re-exam Admit Card by logging into the portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. Additionally, candidates appearing for the re-exam may receive an extra 15 minutes to complete the examination. Candidates who had applied this form can download their NTA NEET UG Re-Exam Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.


## How to Apply

1. Check the Important Links section.
2. Then click on the Re-Exam Admit Card link.
3. After clicking on the link, candidates will be redirected to the Dashboard page.
4. In this page candidates have to fill up the following fields with proper details: Candidate’s Registration Number Date of Birth Gender Verification Code
5. Then the candidates will be required to submit the details in order to fill the Re-Exam Admit Card.
6. The candidate can also download their Re-Exam Admit Card from the official Website.
