Short Information : Sanjay Gandhi Post Graduate Institute of Medical Science (SPPGI) has released the admit card for the Nursing Officer & Other Post. This recruitment was released for 1479 posts. Applications for the SGPGI Nursing Officer & Other Post Recruitment 2025 were accepted from June 18, 2025, to July 31, 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 19-23 June 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their SGPGI Nursing Officer & Other Post Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **81** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have B.Sc. (Anesthesia / Operation Theatre Technologist). as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Check the Important Links section below
2. Open the Download Admit Card link
3. After that, a new page will be opened in which candidates have to provide Roll Number / Application Number & DOB.
4. Candidates are suggested to download the admit card & save the pdf file for the future reference.
5. Candidates can download the admit card also from the official website of SGPGI.
