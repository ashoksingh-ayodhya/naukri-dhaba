Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL) has issued an important notice for candidates appearing in the upcoming Computer-Based Test (CBT) for direct recruitment to the posts of HEMM Operator (Trainee), Paramedical Staff, and Overseer (Civil). Candidates who were originally allotted the Lucknow exam centre at Pawan Online Solutions have now been shifted to a new venue in Lucknow due to unavoidable circumstances. All affected candidates must download their revised admit card before the exam date of 06.07.2026.
