MPSC Admit Card Download 2 of 2026-27 has been announced by the Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC), allowing candidates to download their admit cards for the written examinations of Tourist Officer, Librarian, and Jr. Grade of MHS posts. As per the official notification No. A.12033/1/2026-MPSC(EA) dated 1st July 2026, candidates who applied for these three posts can download their admit cards through the MPSC Online Portal within the specified download windows given below.
