The Uttarakhand Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UKSSSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Senior Milk Inspector Exam 2025 on 19-05-2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 25-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website sssc.uk.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Uttarakhand.
