The Patna High Court (Patna High Court) has officially released the Admit Card for the Translator and Translator cum Proof Reader Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 17-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website patnahighcourt.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Bihar.
