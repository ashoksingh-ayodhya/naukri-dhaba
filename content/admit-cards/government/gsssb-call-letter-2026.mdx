Gujarat Subordinate Service Selection Board (GSSSB), Gandhinagar has released an important notice dated 10 July 2026 for downloading call letters (admit cards) for the competitive MCQ-CBRT examinations of several technical cadres under its special recruitment drive for Divyang (Persons with Disabilities) candidates, along with one general direct recruitment post. This article covers everything mentioned in the official notice, including exam dates, call letter download steps, exam-day rules, and the scribe/compensatory time facility for eligible disabled candidates.
