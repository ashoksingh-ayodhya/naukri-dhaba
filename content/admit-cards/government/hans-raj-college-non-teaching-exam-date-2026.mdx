Hans Raj College, University of Delhi, has released the written examination schedule for its Non Teaching Recruitment 2026 drive. As per the official notice dated 15 July 2026, the college will conduct the Stage 1 Preliminary Test for five posts - Laboratory Assistant, Administrative Officer, Junior Assistant, Laboratory Attendant, and Library Attendant - between 24 and 26 July 2026. This recruitment is being conducted against Advertisement No. HRC/R(NTS)/005/2025, dated 16 January 2026, for appointment to various Non Teaching posts on a permanent basis.

A total of **41** posts are available for this recruitment.
