New Update: Joint Entrance Examination Admit Card 2025 released by the Tripura Board of Joint Entrance Examination (TBJEE) Authority on 12-04-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Tripura Board of Joint Entrance Examination (TBJEE) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Admit Cards that will be available in the Tripura Board of Joint Entrance Examination (TBJEE) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Joint Entrance Examination Admit Card 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The TBJEE Admit Card 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
