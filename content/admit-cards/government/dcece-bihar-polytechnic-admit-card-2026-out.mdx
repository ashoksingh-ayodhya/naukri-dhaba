Short Information : Bihar Combined Entrance Competitive Examination Board (BCECEB) will release the Admit Card for the Polytechnic/Diploma Certificate Entrance Competitive Examination (DCECE-PE, PPE, PMD & PM) 2026 on its official website. Applications for the DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026 were accepted from 19 March 2026, to 27 April 2026. The examination is going to be Conducted from 23-24 May 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their DCECE Bihar Polytechnic Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.


## How to Apply

1. Open the Download Admit Card link from the Important Links box placed below.
2. Candidates will be redirected to a new page.
3. In this page, candidates have to fill up the required fields given as following:
4. Registration ID/ Roll Number/ Name
5. Date of Birth
6. Father’s Name (In case of searching by name)
7. Captcha code
8. And Click on Search
9. After submitting the correct details, applicants will be able to download the admit card.
