The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Group B C Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 06-05-2025 to 07-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website dsssb.delhi.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Delhi.
