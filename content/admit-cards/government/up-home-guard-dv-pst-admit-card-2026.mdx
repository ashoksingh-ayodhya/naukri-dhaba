Short Information : Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) will release the UP Home Guard DV / PST Admit Card 2026 on its official website. This recruitment was released for 41,424 posts. The applications for UP Home Guard Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 18 November 2025 to 17 December 2025. The PST examination will conducted on 13 July to 07 August 2026. Candidates who appeared for this examination can download their UP Home Guard DV / PST Admit Card 2026 Soon from the link given below.

Candidates must have 16650 as minimum educational qualification.
