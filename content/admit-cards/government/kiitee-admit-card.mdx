The KIIT has proclaimed to conduct many examinations each passing year for which the participants have to be in possession of their admit card without which the entry of student in the examination hall is banned. All students who are enrolled in the respective degree courses can download their examination schedule and admit card thereon accordingly from the registered web portal of the University.
