The KIITEE University is also known as the Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology Entrance Examination was held for taking admittance to the enormous programmes that are offered by the KIIT University. The University is one of the finest Universities of India under section declared 3 of UGC Act 1956. The KIIT has proclaimed to conduct many examinations each passing year for which the participants have to be in possession of their admit card without which the entry of student in the examination hall is banned. All students who are enrolled in the respective degree courses can download their examination schedule and admit card thereon accordingly from the registered web portal of the University.
