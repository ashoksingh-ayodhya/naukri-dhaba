Navi Mumbai Municipal Corporation (NMMC) has released an official notice confirming that admit cards (hall tickets) for the Saral Seva Bharti 2025-2026 online examination will be available for download from 10 July 2026. The exam covers vacant Group-A, Group-B and Group-C posts advertised under Advertisement Nos. Aastha/02/2025, Aastha/03/2025 and Aastha/04/2025, and will be held on 21 and 22 July 2026. Eligible candidates must download their admit card from the official NMMC website, nmmc.gov.in, before appearing for the exam.
