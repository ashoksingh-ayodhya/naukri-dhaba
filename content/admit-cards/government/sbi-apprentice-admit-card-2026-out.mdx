The State Bank of India has released the SBI Apprentice Admit Card 2026 on 30 June 2026 for candidates appearing in the recruitment drive for 7,150 Apprentice posts. Candidates who applied under notification CRPD/APPR/2026-27/07 can now download their hall ticket from the official SBI careers portal using their registration number and password or date of birth. The written examination is expected in the first week of July 2026, with several reports indicating the exam is scheduled around July 11, 2026 candidates should confirm their exact date and shift timing on their individual admit card, as these can vary by exam center.

A total of **7150** posts are available for this recruitment.
