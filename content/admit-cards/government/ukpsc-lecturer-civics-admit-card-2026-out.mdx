UKPSC Lecturer Civics Admit Card 2026 has been released by the Uttarakhand Public Service Commission (UKPSC) for the Uttarakhand Special Subordinate Education (Lecturer Cadre 'Group-C') Service, General Branch and Women Branch, Main Written (Objective Type) Examination-2025 for the subject Civics (Nagrik Shastra). As per official Notification No. 147/81/06/D.R./(Ma.Shi.)/Seva-1/2025-26 dated 2nd July 2026, the exam earlier scheduled for 10th May 2026 has now been rescheduled to 18th July 2026, and eligible candidates can provisionally download their admit card from 4th July 2026 onwards.
