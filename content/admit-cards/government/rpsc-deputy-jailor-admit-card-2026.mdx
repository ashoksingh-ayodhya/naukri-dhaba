The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), Ajmer has released an official press note confirming the schedule for the Physical Efficiency Test and Physical Standards Test (PET/PST) for candidates who cleared the written examination of the Deputy Jailor Direct Recruitment Exam 2024. As per the notice dated 02-07-2026, the PET/PST will be conducted by the Directorate of Prisons, Rajasthan, Jaipur, starting 15-07-2026 at the Prison Training Institute (Karagar Prashikshan Sansthan), Ajmer. Eligible candidates can download their admit card from the departmental website, jail.rajasthan.gov.in, starting 01-07-2026.
