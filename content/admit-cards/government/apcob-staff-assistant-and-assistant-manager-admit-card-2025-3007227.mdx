The Andhra Pradesh State Co-operative Bank (APCOB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Staff Assistant and Assistant Manager Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 11-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website apcob.org. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Andhra Pradesh.
