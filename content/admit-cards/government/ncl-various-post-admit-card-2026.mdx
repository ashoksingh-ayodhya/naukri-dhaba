Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL) has officially released the NCL Various Post Admit Card 2026 for the Computer Based Test (CBT). Candidates who applied for HEMM Operator (Trainee), Staff Nurse, Pharmacist, Technician, and Overseer (Civil) posts can now download their NCL hall ticket 2026 from the official candidate login portal at cdn.digialm.com. The NCL CBT exam is scheduled to be held on 06 July 2026. All eligible applicants must download and print their admit card before the exam date, as it is a mandatory document for entry into the examination hall.

A total of **577** posts are available for this recruitment.
