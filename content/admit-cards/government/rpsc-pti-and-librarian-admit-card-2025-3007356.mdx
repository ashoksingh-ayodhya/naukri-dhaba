The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the PTI and Librarian Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 03-05-2025 to 06-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Rajasthan.
