Q. When will Apply Online Link will Start for Bihar SHSB CHO Sarkari Result Recruitment 2024 Answer. Online applications for this recruitment have started from 01 November 2024.

A total of **4500** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Female as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Candidates can apply through link https://shs.bihar.gov.in/ or provided below in link section or they can also apply through official site of the Bihar SHSB before Last Date.
2. You Can Check other Sarkari Result 2025 Notification Here – Check Now.
