OPSC OCS Admit Card 2026 will be released by the Odisha Public Service Commission on 30 May 2026. Candidates who applied for the Odisha Civil Services (OCS) Preliminary Examination can download their admit card from the official website opsc.gov.in. The written examination (Prelims) is scheduled for 07 June 2026 across various centers in Odisha. This hall ticket is mandatory for appearing in the exam.
