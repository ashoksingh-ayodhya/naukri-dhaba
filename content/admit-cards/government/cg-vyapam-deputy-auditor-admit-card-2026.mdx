Chhattisgarh Karamchari Chayan Mandal (CG Vyapam) has issued an official notice regarding the admit card for the Deputy Auditor (Up Ankeshak) recruitment written examination, known as COSA26. This exam is being conducted for vacant Deputy Auditor posts under the Department of Cooperation and Registrar, Cooperative Societies, Chhattisgarh. Candidates can download their admit cards from the official website starting June 25, 2026, ahead of the exam scheduled for July 5, 2026.
