For all the candidates who applied for RRB Chandigarh ALP posts, the Railway Recruitment Board Chandigarh is going to conduct the Computer-based test (CBT) examination. RRB Chandigarh will conduct the CBT examination in the month of April / May 2018 for all those candidates whose application status is eligible. Hence candidates need to login online and check their application status in their respective regional/zonal official websites of the Railway recruitment board.

A total of **1546** posts are available for this recruitment.
