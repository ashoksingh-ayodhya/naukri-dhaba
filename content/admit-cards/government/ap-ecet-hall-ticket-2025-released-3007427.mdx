New Update: Andhra Pradesh Engineering Common Entrance Test Hall Ticket 2025 released by the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Authority on 01-05-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Hall Tickets that will be available in the Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Andhra Pradesh Engineering Common Entrance Test Hall Ticket 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The AP ECET Hall Ticket 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
