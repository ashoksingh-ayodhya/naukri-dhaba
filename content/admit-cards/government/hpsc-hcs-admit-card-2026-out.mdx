The Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has released the HPSC HCS Admit Card 2026 for candidates appearing in the Haryana Civil Service Mains Examination. The admit card was made available on 22 June 2026, and the Mains exam will be conducted on 27, 28, and 29 June 2026 across multiple centres in Haryana. Candidates can download their hall ticket using their registered login credentials on the official HPSC portal.
