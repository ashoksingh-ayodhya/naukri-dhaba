The UKPSC Group C Admit Card 2026 has been officially released by the Uttarakhand Public Service Commission (UKPSC). Candidates who have successfully submitted their online application for the Lecturer Recruitment 2026 can now download their hall ticket from the official portal. The admit card is available at psc.uk.gov.in and also through the direct admit card login portal at ukpscnet.in. The admit card is a mandatory document no candidate will be allowed to enter the examination hall without it.

A total of **808** posts are available for this recruitment.
