The Odisha Staff Selection Commission (OSSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the JE Certificate Verification 2025. Candidates appearing for the Certificate Verification scheduled on 22-04-2025 to 28-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website ossc.gov.in. The Certificate Verification will be held at various centres across the state of Odisha.
