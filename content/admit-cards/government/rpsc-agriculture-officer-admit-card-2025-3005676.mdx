The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has officially released the Admit Card for the Agriculture Officer Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 20-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Rajasthan.
