The Rajkot Municipal Corporation (RMC) has officially released the Call Letter for the Junior Clerk Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 04-05-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website rmc.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Gujarat.
