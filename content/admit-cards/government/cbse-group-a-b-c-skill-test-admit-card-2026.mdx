Short Information : Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has released the Skill Test Admit Card for the Various Group A, B & C Posts. This recruitment was released for 124 posts. Applications for the CBSE Group A, B, C Recruitment 2025 were accepted from December 02, 2025, to December 27, 2025. The skill test examination is going to conducted on 11 July 2026. . Candidates who had appeared in this Examination can download their CBSE Group A, B, C Skill Test Admit Card 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **35** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have Post Graduate Degree in any discipline with 55% marks (or equivalent grade) from a recognized University/ Institute with provision of applicable concession to the respective categories. as minimum educational qualification.
