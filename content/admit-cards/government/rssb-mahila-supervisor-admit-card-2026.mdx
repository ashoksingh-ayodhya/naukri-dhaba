The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the RSSB Mahila Supervisor Admit Card 2026 today, 20 July 2026, on the official Rajasthan Recruitment Portal for candidates appearing in the Mahila Supervisor (Supervisor Female / Mahila Paryavekshak) written examination under Advertisement No. 02/2026. Candidates can now download their hall ticket using their Application Number and Date of Birth. Note: an earlier report of a 17 July 2026 release referred to the separate Exam District / City Slip facility, not the admit card itself the admit card link has gone live today as scheduled. The exam is set for 24 July 2026. This article explains how to download the hall ticket, what to carry to the exam centre, and other essential details.

A total of **72** posts are available for this recruitment.
