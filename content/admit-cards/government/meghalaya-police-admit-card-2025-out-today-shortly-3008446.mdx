The Central Recruitment Board, Meghalaya (Meghalaya Police) officially released the Admit Card for the UBSI, Constable and Other Posts Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 18-05-2025 to 01-06-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website megpolice.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Meghalaya.
