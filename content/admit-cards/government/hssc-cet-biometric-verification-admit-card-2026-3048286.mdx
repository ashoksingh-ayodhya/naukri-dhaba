HSSC CET Biometric Verification Admit Card 2026 has been released by the Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) for candidates of CET-2025 (Group-C) whose biometric capturing remained incomplete during the written examination. Candidates can download their admit card from the official portal cet2025groupc.hryssc.com. The biometric verification process will begin from 11 May 2026 in phases. This admit card/schedule is mandatory for appearing in the biometric session.
