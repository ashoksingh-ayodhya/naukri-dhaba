New Update: Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test Hall Ticket 2025 released by the Anna University (Anna University) Authority on 08-03-2025 and the candidates will be able to download it from the Anna University (Anna University) Official Website. All the candidates who had successfully submitted their Application Forms will be receiving their Hall Tickets that will be available in the Anna University (Anna University) official Website and it has been released by the Authority. If candidate faces any query regarding the Tamil Nadu Common Entrance Test Hall Ticket 2025, then he or she has to contact the concerned Authority without fail. The TANCET Hall Ticket 2025 will be present in Online mode only and will not be provided to candidates through any other offline modes.
