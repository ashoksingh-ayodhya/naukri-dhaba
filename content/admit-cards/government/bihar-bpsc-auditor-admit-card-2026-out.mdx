The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has released the admit card for the Auditor recruitment exam (Advt. No. 09/2026). Candidates who applied for the 102 Auditor posts can now download their admit card from the official BPSC portal using their Enrollment Number, Registration Number, or Date of Birth. The written examination is scheduled for 5 July 2026. This article covers the direct download link, important dates, eligibility criteria, vacancy details, and a step-by-step download guide.

A total of **102** posts are available for this recruitment.
