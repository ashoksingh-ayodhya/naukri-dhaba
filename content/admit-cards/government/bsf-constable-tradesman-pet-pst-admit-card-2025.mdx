Short Information : Border Security Force (BSF) has released the Admit Card for the post of Constable (Tradesman). This recruitment was released for 3588 posts. Candidates Can Also Check All Details on Official SarkariResult2026, Applications for the BSF Constable Tradesman Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 24 July 2025, to 26 August 2025. The PET/ PST examination is going to be conducted on December 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their BSF Constable Tradesman PET/ PST Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

Candidates must have Height : 155 Cms (Gen/OBC/EWS/SC), 148 Cms (ST); Race : 1.6 KM in 8 minutes 30 seconds as minimum educational qualification.
