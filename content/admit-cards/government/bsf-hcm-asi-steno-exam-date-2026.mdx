Short Information : Border Security Force (BSF) has released the exam date for the Probationary Assistant Sub Inspector ASI Stenographer and Head Constable HC Ministerial Post. This recruitment was released for 1526 posts. Applications for BSF HC Ministerial and ASI Steno Recruitment 2024 were filled from 09 June 2024 to 08 July 2024. The written examination is going to be conducted in September 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can download their BSF HCM & ASI Steno Exam Date 2026 from the link given below.

A total of **1496** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 12th Intermediate Exam from Any Recognized Board in India; Typing Test (English : 35 WPM OR Hindi : 30 WPM) as minimum educational qualification.
