Short Information : Uttarakhand Public Service Commission (UKPSC) has released the mains Admit Card for the post of Combined State Civil Lower Subordinate Services Examination. This recruitment was released for 113 posts. Applications for the UKPSC Lower PCS Recruitment 2024 were accepted from December 13, 2024, to January 04, 2025. The mains examination is going to be conducted on 13-14 September 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their UKPSC Lower PCS Mains Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **03** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 06 as minimum educational qualification.
