Short Information : Central Institute of Petrochemicals Engineering & Technology (CIPET) has released the admit card for the Diploma / Post Diploma & PG Diploma Courses Admission 2025 for which the online application process has started from 07 February 2025. Any candidate who is interested to fill this form, they should check eligibility, age limit & must read the Full Notification Before applying for the CIPET Admit Card 2025. Those Candidates Are Interested in the following recruitment can apply online from the link given below.

Candidates must have CIPET Course Details as minimum educational qualification.
