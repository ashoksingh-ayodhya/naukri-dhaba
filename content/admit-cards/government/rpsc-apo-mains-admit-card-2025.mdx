Short Information : Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the Mains Admit Card for the Assistant Prosecution Officer APO Post. This recruitment was released for 181 posts. Applications for the RPSC APO Recruitment 2024 were accepted from March 14, 2024, to April 12, 2025. The mains examination is going to be conducted on 29 May 2025. Candidates who had applied this form can download their RPSC APO Mains Admit Card 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **181** posts are available for this recruitment.
