Delhi Higher Judicial Service (DHJS) Prelims Admit Card 2026: The High Court of Delhi has released the Delhi Higher Judicial Service (DHJS) Preliminary Examination Admit Card 2026 for recruitment to 27 posts under the 25% Direct Recruitment Quota from the Bar. Candidates appearing for the DHJS Preliminary Examination, scheduled to be held on 26 July 2026, can now download their hall ticket from the official website using their login credentials. Applicants are advised to verify all details mentioned on the admit card and carry a printed copy along with a valid photo ID to the examination centre.

A total of **27** posts are available for this recruitment.
