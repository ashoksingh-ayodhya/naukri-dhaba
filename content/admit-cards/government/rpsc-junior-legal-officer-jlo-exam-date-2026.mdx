Short Information : Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the exam date for the post of Junior Legal Officer (JLO). This recruitment was released for 12 posts. Applications for the RPSC School Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Recruitment 2025 were accepted from 27 August 2025, to 25 September 2025. The written examination is going to be conducted on 26-27 July 2026. Candidates who had applied this form can check their RPSC Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Exam Date 2025 from the link given below.

A total of **12** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. In order to download their RPSC Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Exam Date candidates are required to go to the important link section provided below.
2. After getting the link candidates need to click it for downloading their RPSC Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Exam Date.
3. Candidates need to provide their following details-:
4. Registration No. /Roll No.
5. DOB/Password
6. Captcha Code(if specified)
7. After providing their details appropriately candidates will be able to check their RPSC Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Exam Date.
8. Candidates can also check their RPSC Junior Legal Officer (JLO) Exam Date from official site of the RPSC.
