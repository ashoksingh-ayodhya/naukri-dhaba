Short Information : All India Bar Examination (AIBE) has released the admit card for the exam of All India Bar Examination AIBE XXI (21st) for law graduates willing to start practice of profession as Lawyer. Online applying process for AIBE 21st Online Form 2026 has started from 11 February 2026 & the candidates can apply till 30 April 2026. Candidates must check the complete details for AIBE 21st Online Form 2026 which has given below.
