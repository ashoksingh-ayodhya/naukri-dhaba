The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has officially released the Admit Card for the Teacher Exam 2025. Candidates appearing for the examination scheduled on 20-04-2025, can now download their hall ticket from the official website esb.mp.gov.in. The exam will be held at various centres across the state of Madhya Pradesh.
