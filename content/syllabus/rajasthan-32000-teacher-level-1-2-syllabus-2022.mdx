Elementary Education Department Rajasthan Bikaner has released an official notification for Rajasthan Primary / Upper Primary 32000 Teacher Level 1 & Level 2 Syllabus 2022. 
A total of **32000** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **16/02/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Elementary Education Department Rajasthan Bikaner Online Released Primary TGT & Upper Primary PGT Recruitment Exam 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 10/01/2022 to 16/02/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Rajasthan Elementary Education 32000 Post Teacher Exam Online Form 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
