Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for UPSC IAS / IFS Recruitment 2021 Marks 2022. 
A total of **822** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **24/03/2021 Upto 06:00 PM Only**.
Candidates must have Minimum Age : 21 Years; Maximum Age : 32 Years; Age Relaxation Extra as per Rules as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Union Public Service Commission UPSC Civil Services / Forest Services IAS / IFS Prelim Exam Latest Jobs Recruitment 2021. Candidate Can Apply Between 04/03/2021 to 24/03/2021
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in UPSC 2021.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Exam Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
