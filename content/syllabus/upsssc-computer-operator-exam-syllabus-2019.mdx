UPSSSC Computer Operator Admit Card 2020 | Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released an official notification for UPSSSC Computer Operator Admit Card 2020 | Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC). 
The last date to apply is **02/01/2017**.
Candidates must have Diploma in Computer Science or DOEACC O Level Certificate Hindi Typing : 25 WPM English Typing : 40 WPM Knowledge of MS Office, Smart Suit, Networking as minimum educational qualification.
