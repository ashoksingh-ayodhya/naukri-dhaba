Candidates must have Good Quality Passport Size Color Photo with White Background, Must Be Taken After 30/08/2022.; Face Should Occupy at Least 75% of Photograph as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Indian Institute of Technology IIT Kanpur Are Issued Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the GATE 2023 Candidate Can Apply Online for 30 August 2022 to 30 September 2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the IIT Kanpur GATE 2023 Application Form in Sarkari Result Admission Section.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Hand Writing, Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID, Thumb, Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
