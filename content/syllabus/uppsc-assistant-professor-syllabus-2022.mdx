Candidates must have 05 as minimum educational qualification.

1. Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission UPPSC Assistant Professor 02/2020 Direct Recruitment 2020. Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the Latest UPPSC Jobs 2020-2022 Candidate Can Apply Online for 24 November, 2020 to 28/02/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the UPPSC Latest Recruitment 2022 Application Form in Naukri Dhaba Latest Job Section.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Hand Writing, Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID, Thumb, Proof, Etc.
