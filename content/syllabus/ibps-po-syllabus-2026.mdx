The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) will conduct IBPS PO 2026 in three stages - Prelims, Mains and Interview - with a Prelims screening test of 100 questions for 100 marks in 60 minutes, a Mains exam combining 145 objective questions for 200 marks plus a 25‑mark English descriptive paper, and a final Interview round, all carrying 0.25 negative marking for wrong answers in the objective sections as per the latest exam pattern.
