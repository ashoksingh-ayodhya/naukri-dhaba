Allahabad University UG PG Admission Online Form 2021 has released an official notification for IIT JEE Advanced Answer Key 2021. 
The last date to apply is **20/09/2021 upto 05 PM Only.**.
Candidates must have Eligible Candidates Must be Born After : 01/10/1995 for General / OBC; Candidate Must be Born After : 01/10/1990 for SC / ST / PH Candidates; Age Relaxation As per JEE Advance Rules as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. All JEE Mains Eligible Candidates Are Filled to JEE Advanced 2021 Application Form.
2. Click on the Apply Online Link and Enter Your Registration Details.
3. SCAN Document Required for Filling Online : Class 10 & Class 12 Certificate
4. Photo & Signature Scan Copy
5. Check the Application and Pay the Examination Fees to Complete Form.
6. Read the Full Instruction Carefully Before Apply Online.
