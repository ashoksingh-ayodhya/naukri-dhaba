Madhya Pradesh Professional Examination Board (MPPEB) has released an official notification for Madhya Pradesh PEB Group-03 Sub Engineer, Draftsman and Other Post Combined Recruitment Test Online Form 2022. 
A total of **3435** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **Notified Soon**.

## How to Apply

1. Madhya Pradesh government has issued notice for the recruitment of Group-03 Sub Engineer, Draftsman and Other Post Combined Recruitment Test - 2022.
2. Candidates, whether they are resident of Madhya Pradesh or belong to another state, will be required to register their employment in Madhya Pradesh only then they will be able to fill the online application.
3. All the candidates are required to register the profile in the PEB portal in Madhya Pradesh, only then they will be able to fill the online form and for that the mobile number should be registered in your Aadhaar card. If it is not so, you can get your form filled by biometric from any kiosk in Madhya Pradesh.
