The Jammu & Kashmir Public Service Commission (JKPSC) conducts recruitment for Assistant Professor posts under the Medical Education Department across multiple subjects, including Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Microbiology, Pharmacology, Forensic Medicine, and Community Medicine. Candidates preparing for this recruitment need to understand both the syllabus and the exam pattern together, since the syllabus tells you what to study while the pattern tells you how you will be assessed. This article covers the subject-wise JKPSC Assistant Professor Syllabus 2026 based on the official syllabus document released by JKPSC.
