Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released an official notification for UPSSSC Preliminary Examination Test PET 2022 Result, Score Card, Certificate Download 2023. 
The last date to apply is **31/07/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission UPSSSC Are Released PET Exam Form 2022. Candidate Can Apply Between 28/06/2022 to 31/07/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Vacancies Application Form in Apprentice Jobs of UPSSSC PET 2022 for Upcoming Latest Govt. Vacancies Recruitment Online 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Apply Online / Submit Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
