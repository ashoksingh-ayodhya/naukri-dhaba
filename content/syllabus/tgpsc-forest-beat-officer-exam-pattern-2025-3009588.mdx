TGPSC Forest Beat Officer Exam Pattern 2025: For the Forest Beat Officer post, the examination will consist of a total 4 of subjects with a maximum score of 200 marks. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
