The Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations (CISCE) has updated the syllabus for select subjects in Classes 11 and 12. The revised Indian School Certificate (ISC) syllabus, along with its detailed scope, is now available on the Council’s official website cisce.org under the 'Library (Publications)' section. For the ISC Class 12 2025 exams, changes have been made to subjects such as Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Commerce, Accounts, History, Geography, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, and Legal Studies.
