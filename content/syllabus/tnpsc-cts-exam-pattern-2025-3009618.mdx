TNPSC CTS Exam Pattern 2025: For the CTS post, the examination will consist of a total 3 of subjects with a maximum score of 450 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Studies, Aptitude and Mental Ability, and a specific Subject Paper. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
