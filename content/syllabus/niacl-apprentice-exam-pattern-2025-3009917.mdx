NIACL Apprentice Exam Pattern 2025: For the Apprentice post, the examination will consist of a total 4 of subjects with a maximum score of 100 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General/Financial Awareness, General English, Quantitative and Reasoning Aptitude, and Computer Knowledge. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
