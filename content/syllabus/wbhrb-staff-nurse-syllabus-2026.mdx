The West Bengal Health Recruitment Board (WBHRB) conducts a written exam as part of its selection process for the post of Staff Nurse, Grade II. Since the board may shortlist candidates through academic merit, experience, and interview with a written exam added only when a large number of applications are received knowing the exact syllabus and exam pattern in advance is the first step to a focused preparation plan. This guide covers the complete WBHRB Staff Nurse Syllabus 2026, subject-wise topics, exam pattern, marking scheme, and selection process.
