Punjab Police Constable Exam Pattern 2025: For the Constable post, the examination will consist of a total 6 of subjects with a maximum score of 150 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude and Numerical Skills, Mental Ability and Logical Reasoning, Language Test (English and Punjabi), Digital Literacy and Awareness, Mandatory Qualifying Paper of the Punjabi Language... Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
