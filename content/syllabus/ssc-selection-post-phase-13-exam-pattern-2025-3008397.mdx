SSC Selection Post Phase 13 Exam Pattern 2025: For the Selection Post Phase 13 post, the examination will consist of a total 4 of subjects with a maximum score of 200 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Intelligence, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude (Basic Arithmetic Skill), English Language (Basic Knowledge)... Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
