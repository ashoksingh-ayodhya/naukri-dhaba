GSSSB Technical Assistant Exam Pattern 2025: For the Technical Assistant post, the examination will consist of a total 7 of subjects with a maximum score of 130 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are Reasoning & Data Interpretation, Quantitative Aptitude, Constitution of India, Current Affairs, Comprehension (Gujarati), Comprehension (English), Concerned Subject. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
