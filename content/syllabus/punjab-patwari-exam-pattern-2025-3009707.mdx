Punjab Patwari Exam Pattern 2025: For the Patwari post, the examination will consist of a total 6 of subjects with a maximum score of 150 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Knowledge, Current Affairs, Logical Reasoning, Mental Ability, Languages (English and Punjabi), and ICT. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
