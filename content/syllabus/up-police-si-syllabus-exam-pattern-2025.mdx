Short Information : The Uttar Pradesh Police has released 4,543 new vacancies for the posts of Sub Inspector (Civil Police), Sub Inspector (Civil Police – Women), and Sub Inspector/Platoon Commander (Special Security Force). Cracking the SI examination demands well-planned preparation, proper study materials, and a thorough grasp of the latest UP Police SI Syllabus and Exam Pattern 2025. A detailed understanding of the syllabus and pattern helps candidates clearly know the subjects covered and the distribution of questions across each section. The UP Police SI syllabus is divided into four major parts — General Hindi, General Knowledge/Current Affairs/Basic Law/Constitution, Numerical & Mental Ability Test, and Mental Aptitude/IQ/Reasoning Ability.
