A detailed understanding of the syllabus and pattern helps candidates clearly know the subjects covered and the distribution of questions across each section. The UP Police SI syllabus is divided into four major parts — General Hindi, General Knowledge/Current Affairs/Basic Law/Constitution, Numerical & Mental Ability Test, and Mental Aptitude/IQ/Reasoning Ability.
