OPSC ASCO Exam Pattern 2025: For the ASCO post, the examination will consist of a total 4 of subjects with a maximum score of 200 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Awareness, Reasoning and Mental Ability, Mathematics (Matriculation Level), and Language (English). Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
