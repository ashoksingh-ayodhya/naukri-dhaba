UPSSSC Forest Guard Exam Pattern 2025: For the Forest Guard post, the examination will consist of a total 5 of subjects with a maximum score of 100 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are Elementary Mathematics, Biology, Computer and IT Knowledge, and General Information related to Uttar Pradesh Also includes General Studies, Aptitude, and Mental Ability Test.. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
