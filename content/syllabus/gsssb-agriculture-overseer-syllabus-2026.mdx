The Gujarat Subordinate Service Selection Board (GSSSB) conducts the written examination for the recruitment of Agriculture Overseer (Class-3) posts. Candidates preparing for this exam need a clear picture of both the syllabus and the exam pattern, since the two together decide how study time should be split between general aptitude sections and the technical Agriculture/Horticulture portion. This article lays out the GSSSB Agriculture Overseer Syllabus 2026, the section-wise exam pattern, and a subject-wise topic list based on the official exam structure.
