Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has released an official notification for Madhya Pradesh MPESB / PEB High School Teacher Eligibility Test MPHSTET Varg I Result 2023. 
The last date to apply is **27/01/2023**.
Candidates must have Master Degree in Related Subject with Second Division Marks and Passed / Appearing B.Ed Exam Passed.; For More Information Read the Notification as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board for the High School Teacher Eligibility Test- 2023 The applications of Recruitment will be online from 12 January 2023 to 27 January 2023.
2. Along with Madhya Pradesh, candidates from other states can also apply (As per MP High School TET Varg I Recruitment Rules) .
3. To apply in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, the candidate has to first create a profile of himself. To create a profile, the candidate wants to have Aadhar card number and also want to have registered mobile number in it, only then there will be E-KYC in the profile, those whose mobile number is not registered, they will have to create a profile through biometric through MP Online Kiosk.
4. For the application of recruitment in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, along with the profile, all the candidates will also have to register in the Madhya Pradesh Employment Office.
5. Candidates who have registered their profile and employment, now they have to apply which will run from 12/01/2023 to 27/01/2023.
6. Photo / Template Instruction : The photo, signature and handwriting which the candidate has to upload in the High School Teacher Eligibility Test - 2023 will have to be uploaded together in a template. Whose format is given on the last page of the notification. The photo should not be more than 03 months old and the background of the photo should be white, along with the name of the candidate and the date of taking the photo should also be written on the photo.
7. For More Details Read the Full Notification.
