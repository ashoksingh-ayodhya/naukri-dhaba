The Balmer Lawrie Executive Trainee Syllabus 2026 covers two separate question paper patterns - one for candidates applying through B.E./B.Tech (Engineering) positions and another for candidates applying through MBA-equivalent (Management) positions. Both papers are divided into two sections: a core subject section and a common aptitude section covering logical reasoning, data interpretation, quantitative aptitude, and English proficiency. This guide breaks down every topic listed in the official syllabus so candidates know exactly what to prepare for the Balmer Lawrie Executive Trainee recruitment exam 2026.
