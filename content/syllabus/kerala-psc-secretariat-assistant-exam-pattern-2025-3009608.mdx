Kerala PSC Secretariat Assistant Exam Pattern 2025: For the Secretariat Assistant post, the examination will consist of a total 6 of subjects with a maximum score of 100 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Knowledge, Simple Arithmetic, Mental Ability, and Reasoning, General English, Regional Language, Principles of Administration and Management and Constitution of India. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
