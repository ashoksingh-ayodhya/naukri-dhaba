Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released an official notification for Railway RRB Junior Engineer CEN 03/2024 Stage II Answer Key, Re Schedule Answer Key 2025 for 7951 Post. 
A total of **7951** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **29/08/2024**.
Candidates must have RRB JE Eligibility Post Wise Eligibility as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Railway Recruitment Board RRB Online Released Junior Engineer and Other Post Recruitment Advt No. CEN 03/2024 Exam. Candidate Can Apply Between 30/07/2024 to 29/08/2024.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Railway Board Junior Engineer JE & Other Various Post Exam 2024 Under CEN Advt No. 03/2024 Exam Online Form 2024.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
