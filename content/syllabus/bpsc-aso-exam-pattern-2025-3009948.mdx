BPSC Assistant Section Officer Exam Pattern 2025: For the Assistant Section Officer post, the examination will consist of a total 4 of subjects with a maximum score of Preliminary - 150, Mains Paper 1 - 100 and Mains Paper 2 - 150 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Studies, General Science & Mathematics, Mental Ability, and General Hindi. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
