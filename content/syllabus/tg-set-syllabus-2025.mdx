TS SET Syllabus 2025: Osmania University, on behalf of the Telangana State Government, has released the detailed syllabus for the Telangana State Eligibility Test (TS SET) 2025, also known as TG SET. This state-level exam determines eligibility for Assistant Professor/Lecturer positions in universities and colleges in Telangana. Candidates can download the official syllabus PDFs for Paper I and all 29 subjects of Paper II from the official website. This guide covers the exam pattern, subject-wise topics, preparation tips, and recommended books for the TS SET 2025.
