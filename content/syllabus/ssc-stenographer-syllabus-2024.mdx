Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released an official notification for SSC Stenographer Grade C & D Examination 2024 Results, Marks, Final Answer Key, Skill Test Exam Admit Card 2025. 
A total of **2006** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **17/08/2024 upto 11 PM only**.

## How to Apply

1. SSC OTR Instruction : SSC has started one-time registration on its new website SSC.GOV.IN. Candidates who have not yet done OTR registration on the new website will be able to apply for any recruitment only after completing the registration.
2. Staff Selection Commission SSC Are Issued Stenographer Grade C and Grade D 10+2 Recruitment 2024. Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the SSC Steno Post Jobs 2024 Candidate Can Apply Online for 26/07/2024 to 17/08/2024
3. SSC Photo Instruction : SSC has made a big change in the application process. Now the photo of the candidate will be uploaded live, that too through webcam or through the official SSC App. The candidate has to take care that he looks straight ahead in the photo and the background should also be light/white.
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the SSC Latest Recruitment 2024. Application Form in Sarkari Result Latest Job Section.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Hand Writing, Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID, Thumb, Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
9. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
