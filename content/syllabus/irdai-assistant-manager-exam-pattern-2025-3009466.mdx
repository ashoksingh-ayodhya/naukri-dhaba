IRDAI Assistant Manager Exam Pattern 2025: For the Assistant Manager post, the examination will consist of a total Prelims (4) Mains (3) subjects with a maximum score of Prelims - 160, Mains - 300 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are Prelims (Reasoning, English Language, General Awareness, and Quantitative Aptitude), Mains (English, Economic and Social Issues, and Insurance & Management). Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
