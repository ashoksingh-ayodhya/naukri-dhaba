The MPSC Deputy Director and Senior Research Officer Syllabus 2026 covers the recruitment examination for the Group-A (Gazetted) post under the Planning Department. The selection process consists of a written examination worth 200 marks followed by an interview worth 50 marks, and the written paper tests candidates on eight core subject areas including Planning, Economic Analysis, Statistics, Research Methodology, and Governance. This article explains the complete exam pattern, negative marking rules, and a topic-wise breakdown of the syllabus based on the official notification.
