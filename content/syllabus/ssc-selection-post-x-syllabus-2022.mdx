Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released an official notification for SSC Selection Post X Recruitment 2022 Additional Written Exam Result 2023. 
A total of **2065** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **13/06/2022 Upto 11:00 PM Only**.

## How to Apply

1. Staff Selection Commission SSC Are Issued Notification for Selection Various Post IX Recruitment 2021 Candidate Can Apply Between 12/05/2022 to 13/06/2022.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in SSC Selection Post X Recruitment Online Form 2022.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
