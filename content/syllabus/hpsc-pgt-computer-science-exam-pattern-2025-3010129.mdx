HPSC PGT Computer Science Exam Pattern 2025: For the PGT Computer Science post, the examination will consist of a total 7 of subjects with a maximum score of 140 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Studies, Reasoning Ability, Numerical Ability, Teaching Aptitude, English Language, Hindi Language, Concerned Subject (Computer Science)... Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
