KVS TGT Exam Pattern 2025: For the TGT post, the examination will consist of a total 7 of subjects with a maximum score of 180 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General English, General Hindi, General Awareness & Current Affairs, Reasoning Ability, Computer Literacy, and Perspectives on Education and Leadership. It will also assess knowledge in the Subject Concerned (Specialization). Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position.
