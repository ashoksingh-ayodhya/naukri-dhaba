UPSSSC UP Lekhpal Exam Pattern 2025: For the UP Lekhpal post, the examination will consist of a total 4 of subjects with a maximum score of 100 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Hindi, Maths, General Knowledge and Village Society & Development. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
