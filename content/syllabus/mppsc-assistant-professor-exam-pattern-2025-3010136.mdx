MPPSC Assistant Professor Exam Pattern 2025: For the Assistant Professor post, the examination will consist of a total 6 of subjects with a maximum score of 450 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Knowledge, General Studies, Research Aptitude, Teaching Aptitude, Reasoning Ability, Concerned Subject (Based on Discipline)... Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
