The AIIMS CRE Pharmacist Syllabus 2026 covers Pharmaceutics, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutical Chemistry, Pharmacognosy, Hospital Pharmacy, and General Knowledge & Aptitude, spread across separate syllabi for General, Ayurvedic, and Homeopathic Pharmacist posts. The exam is a 90-minute Computer Based Test (CBT) with 100 MCQs carrying 400 total marks, with 1/4 negative marking for every wrong answer. Candidates preparing for this recruitment must understand both the subject-wise breakdown and the exam structure to plan an effective study strategy.

Candidates must have Health promotion; community pharmacy services; patient counselling as minimum educational qualification.
