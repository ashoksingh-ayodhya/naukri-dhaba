Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released an official notification for Haryana HSSC CET for Group C Various Post Online Form 2022. 
The last date to apply is **08/07/2022**.

## How to Apply

1. Haryana Staff Selection Commission HSSC Common Eligibility Test CET Group C 26000 Post Recruitment 2022. Candidate Can Online Registration Till 08/07/2022.
2. It is mandatory for the candidates who want to participate in Group C recruitment to be conducted by HSSC of Haryana or any other state, it is mandatory to qualify the CET exam.
3. Step 1 : First of all, the candidate has to go to the Some Userful Important link in this page, but he will get the link of Apply Online, he will have to click on it.
4. Step 2 : In the page that will open, the candidate will have to enter his mobile number, immediately an OTP will be received on the candidate's mobile, after entering the candidate will reach the dashboard of Haryana CET 2022.
5. Step 3 : Those who are residents of Haryana will have to enter their family identity card PPP id number or Aadhar card number and the candidate of other state will have to enter their own Aadhar card number and name.
6. Step 4 : Now the candidate will have to enter his personal information / education information / experience / all other information on the portal as well as upload his scanned photo and signature / other documents.
7. Step 5 : After filling the complete application, the candidate will have to check the preview very carefully, after that a print out of it will have to be signed and uploaded in it. At the end the fee has to be paid and the final print has to be out.
8. Before applying, read the complete information in the Haryana CET 01/2022 Notification.
