The SSC Stenographer Syllabus 2026 and Exam Pattern form the base of preparation for candidates aiming for Stenographer Grade C and Grade D posts in central government ministries and offices. The exam is conducted by the Staff Selection Commission (SSC) and has two stages: a Computer-Based Test (CBT) and a Shorthand Skill Test. Knowing the syllabus and pattern of both stages together helps candidates plan an effective study strategy and avoid missing important topics.
