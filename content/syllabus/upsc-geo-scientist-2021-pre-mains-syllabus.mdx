Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for UP GEO Scientist Recruitment 2020 Pre Exam Result, Final Result 2021. 
A total of **40** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **27/10/2020 upto 06:00 PM Only**.

## How to Apply

1. UPSC Geo Scientist Examination 2021 Notification Are Issued and UPSC Invited Online Application Form for the UPSC Geo Scientist Recruitment 2021 Candidate Can Apply Online for 07 October, 2020 to 27 October, 2020.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the UPSC Latest Recruitment 2020 Application Form in Naukri Dhaba Latest Job Section.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Hand Writing, Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID, Thumb, Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
7. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
