South Indian Bank Junior Officer Exam Pattern 2025: For the Junior Officer post, the examination will consist of a total 5 of subjects with a maximum score of 100 marks. Sections included in the exam patterns are General Knowledge, Current Affairs, General Science, Logical Reasoning and Mathematics.. Each section contributes to the total score, testing a wide range of skills necessary for the CGL position. Details of the pattern are discussed further below.
