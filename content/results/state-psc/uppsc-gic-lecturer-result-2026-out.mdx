The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) released the UPPSC GIC Lecturer Prelims Result 2026 on 20 July 2026 on its official website, uppsc.up.nic.in. The result covers candidates who appeared for the Lecturer (Male/Female), Government Inter College (GIC) preliminary examination and lists roll numbers of those provisionally shortlisted for the mains exam across various subjects. The UPPSC GIC Lecturer Mains Exam 2026 is scheduled for 30 August 2026.

A total of **1516** posts are available for this recruitment.
