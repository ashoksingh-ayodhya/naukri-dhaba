MPPSC Dental Specialist Result 2026 has been declared by the Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC), Indore, on 16 July 2026. The Commission has released both the Selection List (Letter No. 5593/50/2025/Chayan) and the Marks List (Letter No. 5594/50/2025/Chayan) for the post of Dental Specialist (Dant Chikitsa Visheshagya) under the Public Health and Medical Education Department, against Advertisement No. 54/2024 dated 31.12.2024.
