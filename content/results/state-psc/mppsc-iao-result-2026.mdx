The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has officially declared the Internal Audit Officer (Aantarik Lekha Parikshan Adhikari) Result 2026 on 24 June 2026. The final selection list has been published under Advertisement Number 14/2024 (dated 28.12.2024) for the Mineral Resources Department (Khanij Sadhan Vibhag), Government of Madhya Pradesh. Candidates who appeared in the interview held on 03 June 2026 can now check their selection status and interview marks from the official MPPSC website.
