The Arunachal Pradesh Public Service Commission (APPSC) has declared the Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT) Secondary Level Written Examination Result 2026 on 17th July 2026, for the exam held on 12th July 2026 under Advertisement No. 02/2026. Provisionally qualified candidates across 14 subjects, covering a total of 389 posts, can check their roll numbers in the subject-wise annexure list. The next stage for qualified candidates will be the Interview/Viva-Voce, followed by Document Verification, with dates to be announced separately.

A total of **389** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. Check your roll number in the subject-wise annexure list in the official PDF.
2. Await the official notice for the date of Interview/Viva-Voce (to be announced separately).
3. Upload the required certificates through your OTR (One Time Registration) account when the document upload window opens.
4. Keep all original educational and category-related certificates ready for Document Verification.
5. Eligibility for appearing in the Interview/Viva-Voce is subject to fulfillment of all requisite eligibility conditions for the post, as per Advertisement No. 02/2026 dated 23rd March 2026.
6. Selection of PwBD candidates is subject to disability certificates matching the specifications in the advertisement.
7. The Commission's decision on eligibility for admission to the Interview/Viva-Voce is final.
8. All provisionally qualified candidates will be called for Document Verification, and must upload certificates through their respective OTR.
9. The date for document upload and Document Verification will be notified subsequently.
