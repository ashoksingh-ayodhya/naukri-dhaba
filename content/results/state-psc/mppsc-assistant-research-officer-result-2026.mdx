The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC), Indore, has released the result for the Assistant Research Officer (Sahayak Anusandhan Adhikari) Examination 2024 on 22 July 2026. This result covers Advertisement No. 12/2024 (dated 28.12.2024) for the post of Assistant Research Officer under the Tribal Welfare Department (Janjati Karya Vibhag), Government of Madhya Pradesh. The Commission has published the Selection List (Chayan Suchi) and the Marks List (Obtained Marks List) for candidates selected under the main part (87%) of the total vacancies.
