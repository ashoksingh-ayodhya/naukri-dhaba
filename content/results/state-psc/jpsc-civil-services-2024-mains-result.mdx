Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has released an official notification for Jharkhand JPSC Civil Services Exam Advt No. 01/2024 Recruitment 2024 Download Mains Results for 342 Post.

The last date to apply is **29/02/2024 Upto 5 PM**.
Candidates must have 01 as minimum educational qualification.
