The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has officially released the MPPSC Insurance Medical Officer Result 2026 on 25 June 2026. Candidates who appeared in the interview held from 28 May 2026 to 02 June 2026 can now check their selection status from the official MPPSC website at mppsc.mp.gov.in.

Department has released an official notification for MPPSC Result 2026 Out For Insurance Medical Officer and Assistant Surgeon - Check Selection List. 
A total of **41** posts are available for this recruitment.
