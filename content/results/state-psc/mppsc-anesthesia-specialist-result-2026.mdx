The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has officially released the Revised Selection List for the post of Anesthesia Specialist (Nishchetna Visheshagya) under Advertisement No. 03/2024, dated 24 July 2024. The revised list was declared on 19 June 2026 from its Indore office. This result pertains to the 87% Main Part of 203 total advertised posts under the Department of Public Health and Medical Education, Government of Madhya Pradesh. Candidates who appeared in interviews held between 4 July 2025 and 22-28 July 2025 can now check their selection status.

A total of **203** posts are available for this recruitment.
