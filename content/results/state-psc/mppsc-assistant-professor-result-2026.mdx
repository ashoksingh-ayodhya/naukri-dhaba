The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released the MPPSC Assistant Professor Result 2026 for the post of Assistant Professor, Zoology. The revised selection list and marks list were issued on 8 July 2026, replacing the earlier list dated 15 January 2026 after the Commission noticed a partial error in it. Candidates who appeared for the written exam and interview can check their roll numbers in the official PDF released by the Commission.
