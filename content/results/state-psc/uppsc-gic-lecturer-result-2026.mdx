The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has declared the UPPSC GIC Lecturer Prelims Result 2026 for the Lecturer (Male/Female), Government Inter College Examination-2025, under Advertisement No. A-6/E-1/2025. The result has been released subject-wise on the official website, uppsc.up.nic.in. As of the latest update, results for five subjects Sociology, Geography, Economics, Civics, and Physics have been declared.
