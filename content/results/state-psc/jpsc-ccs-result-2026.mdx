The Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has officially announced the result of the Jharkhand Combined Civil Services (Backlog) Preliminary (PT) Examination-2023, advertised under Advertisement No. 06/2026. The result was published on 24 May 2026 and is available on the official JPSC website. Candidates who have qualified for the PT exam must now apply online for the Mains (Written) Backlog Competitive Examination, scheduled for 25, 26, and 27 July 2026.
