The Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has declared the JPSC ACF Mains Result 2026 for the Assistant Conservator of Forest post under Advertisement No. 03/2024. As per the official press release (Reference No. 2219, dated 07 July 2026), the result of the Assistant Conservator of Forest Main (Written) Competitive Examination has been published on the official website, jpsc.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the Mains exam held from 4 to 12 April 2026 (excluding 5 April 2026) can check their result on the official JPSC portal.
