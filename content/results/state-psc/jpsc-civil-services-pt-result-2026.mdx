The Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has officially declared the JPSC Civil Services PT Result 2026 for the Combined Civil Services (Preliminary) Examination 2025-26 (Advertisement No. 01/2026). The result was released on 2 July 2026, following an order from the Hon'ble Jharkhand High Court dated 30 June 2026. Candidates who appeared for the Preliminary Examination held on 19 April 2026 can now check their qualifying status and download the merit list to find out if they have advanced to the Main (Written) Examination stage.

A total of **103** posts are available for this recruitment.
