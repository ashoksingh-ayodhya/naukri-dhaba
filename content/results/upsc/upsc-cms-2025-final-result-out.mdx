The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has officially declared the Reserve List for the Combined Medical Services Examination (CMSE) 2025 on June 25, 2026. As per a requisition from the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, 30 additional candidates from the Reserve List (Category II) have been recommended to fill the remaining vacant posts. These candidates are in addition to the 812 candidates already recommended in the main result announced on December 22, 2025.
