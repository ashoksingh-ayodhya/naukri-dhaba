Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for UPSC National Defence Academy and Naval Academy NDA II Examination 2025 Download Marks. 
A total of **406** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **17/06/2025 upto 06:00 PM only**.
Candidates must have Candidate Not be Born Before : 02/01/2007; Candidates Not be Born After : 01/01/2010; Age Relaxation Extra as per NDA II Exam Notification 2025 Recruitment Rules. as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Photo Instruction : The photograph of the candidate should not be more than 10 Days old and the name of the candidate and the date of taking the photograph should also be written on the photograph.
2. One time registration has become necessary for all the applications of Union Public Service Commission, so all the candidates will be able to apply only after OTR.
3. Union Public Service Commission UPSC NDA II Recruitment 2025. Candidate Can Apply Between 28/05/2025 to 17/06/2025.
4. Candidate Read the NDA II Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in NDA Apply Online 2025.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
