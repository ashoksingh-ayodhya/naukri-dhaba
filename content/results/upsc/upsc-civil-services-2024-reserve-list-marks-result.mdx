Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for UPSC Civil Services IAS Pre / Forest Service IFS Recruitment 2024, IAS Final Result with Reserve List Marks 2025 for 1206 Posts. 
A total of **1206** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **06/03/2024**.

## How to Apply

1. To apply for Civil Services and Forest Services Recruitment 2024 in UPSC, it will be mandatory for the candidate to do One Time Registration OTR.
2. Photo Instruction : The photo of the candidate should not be more than 10 days old and the name of the candidate and the date of taking the photo should also be written on the photo, the background of the photo should also be white.
3. OBC certificate should be issued after 01 April 2023.
4. Union Public Service Commission UPSC Civil Services / Forest Service IAS / IFS Prelim Exam Latest Jobs Recruitment 2024. Candidate Can Apply Between 14/02/2024 to 06/03/2024.
5. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in UPSC 2024.
6. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
7. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Exam Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
8. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
9. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
