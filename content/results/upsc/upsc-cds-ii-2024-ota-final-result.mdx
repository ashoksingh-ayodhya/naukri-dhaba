Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released an official notification for UPSC Combined Defence Service Exam II Second Examination 2024 Download OTA Final Result with Marks for 459 Post. 
A total of **459** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **04/06/2024**.
Candidates must have Minimum Age : 20 Years; Maximum Age : 24 Years; for Post Wise Details Read Notification.; Age Relaxation Extra as per Rules as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. Photo Instruction : The photo of the candidate should not be more than 10 days old, the name of the candidate and the date of taking the photo should be written on the photo.
2. One Time Registration OTR : One Time Registration System is implemented in UPSC, hence to apply for CDS II, the candidate must have OTR, those who do not have it, should apply only after registering a new OTR.
3. Union Public Service Commission UPSC Are Issued Combined Defence Academy CDS 2 Recruitment 2024 Notification and Invited Online Application Form for the UPCS CDS II Recruitment 2024 Candidate Can Apply Online for 15 May 2024 to 04 June 2024.
4. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the UPSC CDS II Recruitment Application Form in UPSC Latest Defence Vacancies 2024.
5. Kindly Check and College the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
9. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
