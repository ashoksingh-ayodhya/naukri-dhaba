Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment & Promotion Board has released an official notification for Eligibility Criteria. 
A total of **41424** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **17/12/2025**.

## How to Apply

1. Each applicant must register with a unique Email ID and a unique Mobile Number, which will be unchangeable during the examination process.
2. Applicants can complete their registration using Aadhaar, DigiLocker, Driving License, PAN, or Passport.
3. Only the details recorded in the Matriculation/High School certificate will be considered valid for OTR purpose.
4. The Applicant’s Name, Gender, and Date of Birth entered during the registration process must exactly match with the information in the Matriculation/High School certificate.
5. If the Matriculation/High School details cannot be fetched from DigiLocker, applicants can manually enter their information.
