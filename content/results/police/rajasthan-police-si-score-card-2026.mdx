The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the Rajasthan Police SI Score Card 2026 on its official website rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. The score card has been activated for all candidates who appeared in the Sub Inspector (SI) and Platoon Commander written examination. Candidates can now log in to the official portal using their roll number and registration credentials to download their individual score card PDF. The score card contains subject-wise marks and total marks obtained by the candidate in the written examination. This will help candidates assess their performance and plan their preparation for the upcoming Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Interview stages.

A total of **1015** posts are available for this recruitment.
