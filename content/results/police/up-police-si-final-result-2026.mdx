The Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has officially declared the UP Police SI Final Result 2026 on 14 July 2026 on its official website, uppbpb.gov.in. The final result covers the posts of Sub Inspector (Civil Police), Platoon Commander/PAC, and Fire Station Second Officer, and has been prepared for candidates who cleared the written examination, Document Verification (DV), Physical Standard Test (PST), and Physical Efficiency Test (PET) stages. This recruitment was conducted under Advertisement No. 03/2025.
