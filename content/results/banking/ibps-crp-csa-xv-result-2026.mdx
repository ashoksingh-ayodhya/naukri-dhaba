The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) has released the Result of the Online Main Examination for CRP-CSA-XV (Provisional Allotment for UT of Chandigarh) on its official website, ibps.in, on 17 July 2026. Candidates can check their result using the official login link on ibps.in.
