The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), Ajmer, has released the Provisional List of Candidates for Eligibility Checking for the post of Agriculture Research Officer (Agriculture Chemistry) under the Agriculture Department, Competitive Examination-2024. This list is issued only for document verification purposes and is not a final selection list or merit list.


## How to Apply

1. Log in to the RPSC recruitment portal using your SSO ID.
2. Go to My Recruitment → Detailed Form Cum Scrutiny → Apply Now.
3. Fill in the detailed online application form completely and accurately.
4. Submit the form online within the window.
5. After submission, use the print option to take 2 printed copies of the complete detailed application form.
6. Keep both printed copies safely along with your original documents and self-attested copies.
7. This provisional list is strictly for document/eligibility verification and is not a selection or merit list.
8. Document verification and checking of the detailed application form will be carried out by the Agriculture Department, not RPSC.
9. RPSC will not send any separate individual communication to candidates about the verification schedule candidates must watch for updates from the Agriculture Department through the proper channel.
10. If a candidate does not appear on the department's prescribed date for document verification, they will be treated as ineligible and will not be considered in the final result.
11. Eligibility will be checked strictly according to the conditions/rules of the original advertisement; candidates who do not fully meet these conditions will have their eligibility cancelled.
12. After the Agriculture Department completes verification, it will forward the list of eligible candidates to RPSC, which will then issue the result and recommend selected candidates' names for appointment.
