The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) declared the MPSC Rajyaseva Prelims Result 2026 on 22 June 2026 on its official website, mpsc.gov.in. The result is for candidates who appeared in the Preliminary Examination held on 31 May 2026 under Advertisement No. 132/2025, conducted for recruitment to various Group A and Group B posts under the Maharashtra Civil Services Gazetted Combined Examination 2026. The result has been published as a PDF containing the roll numbers of candidates shortlisted for the Mains Examination. Along with the result, MPSC has also released the category-wise cut-off marks, with the Open (General) category cut-off fixed at 98.00 marks.
