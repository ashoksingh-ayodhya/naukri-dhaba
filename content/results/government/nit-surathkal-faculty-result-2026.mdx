The National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal has released the faculty recruitment 2026 shortlist result on 21 July 2026. The shortlist covers applications received against Notification No. 5210-Advt./Faculty-NITK-Estt./2026/B1 dated 04-05-2026, for Assistant Professor and other faculty positions across seven departments. A total of 191 applications have been shortlisted for the next stage of the selection process, which includes a Teaching Presentation, Document Verification, and a Research Presentation and Personal Interview. This article gives you the complete NITK Surathkal faculty result 2026, department-wise shortlisted application numbers, and the full interview schedule.
