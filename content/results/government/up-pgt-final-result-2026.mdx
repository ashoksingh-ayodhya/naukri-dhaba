UP PGT Final Result 2026 has been officially declared by the Uttar Pradesh Education Service Selection Commission (UPSESSB) on 7 July 2026 on its official website, upsessb.up.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the UP PGT written examination held on 9 and 10 May 2026 can now check their subject-wise result and download the official PDF from the commission's portal. Out of 1,92,934 candidates who appeared for the exam, 2,253 candidates have been declared successful and will now move to the interview stage of the UP PGT Recruitment 2026, which covers a total of 624 posts across 18 subjects.

A total of **624** posts are available for this recruitment.
