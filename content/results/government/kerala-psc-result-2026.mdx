Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC) has published three new shortlists this week one for Part Time High School Teacher (Sanskrit), Alappuzha and two for Beat Forest Officer, Malappuram (NCA-OBC and NCA-E/B/T categories). These lists contain the register numbers of candidates provisionally shortlisted for the next stage of selection interview for the teacher post, and Physical Measurement/Physical Efficiency Test for the Beat Forest Officer posts. Candidates can check their register numbers below and find out what to do next.
