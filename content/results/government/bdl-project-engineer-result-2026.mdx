Bharat Dynamics Limited (BDL) has released the result for the recruitment of Project Engineers on a contract basis under Advertisement No. 2026-1. The list of provisionally selected candidates was published on 30 June 2026 following walk-in interviews held at AeSI, Bengaluru on 6th and 7th June 2026. A total of 18 candidates have been selected for the posts of Project Engineer in the Electronics, Mechanical, and Computer Science disciplines.

A total of **20** posts are available for this recruitment.
