Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has released an official notification for Bihar Sakshamta Pariksha Phase 5 Result 2026. 

## How to Apply

1. BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Online Form 2025 Notification Released and Candidate Apply within Last Date.
2. Candidate Read BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Notification 2025 very carefully before Apply the Online Form Application Form.
3. While applying, the candidate will have to fill all the columns carefully so that no mistake can be made like – Name of the candidate, Father’s name, Mother’s name, Date of birth, Address, Qualification details.
4. If upload is asked in the application form, then upload all the documents in the correct size and correct format, be it PDF or JPEG.
5. Before submitting the form, re-check all the columns and documents and submit only if everything is correct.
6. After submitting the form of Bihar BSEB Sakshamta Pariksha Online Form 2025, take a print of it or save it in PDF.
