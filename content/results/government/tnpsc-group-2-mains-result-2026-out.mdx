The Tamil Nadu Public Service Commission (TNPSC) has officially released the TNPSC Group 2 Mains Result 2026 on its website, tnpsc.gov.in. This result is for candidates who appeared in the Combined Civil Services Examination–II (Group 2/2A Services) Mains Exam, held on 15th March 2026. Candidates can now log in using their Registered Number and Date of Birth to view their scorecard, normalized marks, and qualifying status for the next stage the interview/certificate verification round. This article covers the direct result link, the step-by-step checking process, and all the details mentioned on the scorecard.
