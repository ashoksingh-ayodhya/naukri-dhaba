The Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has released the HPSC ADA (Assistant District Attorney) Screening Test Result 2026 for the recruitment held under Advertisement No. 18/2025. The result is for candidates who appeared in the Common Screening Test conducted on 5 July 2026 for 255 Assistant District Attorney posts in the Haryana Prosecution Department. Candidates who qualify will be shortlisted for the Subject Knowledge Test (SKT) scheduled on 9 August 2026.

A total of **255** posts are available for this recruitment.
