Rajasthan Staff Selection Board Recruitment 2025 has released an official notification for Librarian Grade III. 
A total of **548** posts are available for this recruitment.

## How to Apply

1. RSMSSB Librarian Online Form 2025 Notification Released and Candidate Apply within Last Date.
2. Candidate Read RSMSSB Librarian Notification 2025 very carefully before Apply the Recruitment Application Form.
3. While applying, the candidate will have to fill all the columns carefully so that no mistake can be made like – Name of the candidate, Father’s name, Mother’s name, Date of birth, Address, Qualification details.
4. If upload is asked in the application form, then upload all the documents in the correct size and correct format, be it PDF or JPEG.
5. Before submitting the form, re-check all the columns and documents and submit only if everything is correct.
6. After submitting the form of RSMSSB Librarian Recruitment 2025, take a print of it or save it in PDF.
