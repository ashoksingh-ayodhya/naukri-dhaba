The Himachal Pradesh Rajya Chayan Aayog (HPRCA), Hamirpur has released the revised final result for the recruitment of 312 posts of Assistant Staff Nurse (Allopathy) on an engagement basis under Post Code-25026. This revision, issued through an official press note dated 1st July 2026, updates the original result that was declared on 24 June 2026, after the Aayog identified a technical error related to candidates' category and sub-category classification.

A total of **312** posts are available for this recruitment.
