The National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has released the result for the Young Professionals Programme (On Contract) 2025-26. NABARD has published two separate lists: a Select List of candidates chosen for the programme and a Wait List of candidates placed on standby. Both lists identify candidates only by their registration numbers. This page reproduces both lists exactly as issued by NABARD, along with the eligibility, salary, and selection details from the original recruitment notification.

Candidates must have 01 November 2025 as minimum educational qualification.
