JIPMER Senior Resident Result 2026 has been officially declared by the Jawaharlal Institute of Postgraduate Medical Education & Research (JIPMER), Puducherry, on 5 July 2026. The result covers the recruitment to the post of Senior Resident on a regular basis across various departments at JIPMER, Puducherry and Karaikal, conducted through a rolling advertisement for the July 2026 session. Candidates who appeared in the written test and interview held on 2, 3 and 4 July 2026 can now check the department-wise list of selected, ad-hoc and waitlisted candidates.
