Punjab National Bank (PNB) has released the PNB LBO Final Result 2026 on 23rd June 2026 at its official website pnb.bank.in. The final result has been declared for candidates who appeared in the Online Test, Language Proficiency Test, and Interview for the post of Local Bank Officer (JMGS-I). Candidates can download the result PDF directly from the official PNB website or use the direct link shared below. As of now, 205 candidates have been shortlisted for appointment as Local Bank Officers. The remaining results are expected to be announced in phases. The result PDF contains roll numbers and registration numbers of shortlisted candidates.

A total of **750** posts are available for this recruitment.
