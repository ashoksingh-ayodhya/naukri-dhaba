The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has officially declared the BPSC 70th Combined Competitive Examination (CCE) Final Result 2026 on June 20, 2026. The result was announced in Patna by BPSC Chairman Ravi Manubhai Parmar, bringing the curtain down on one of the largest and most closely watched recruitment drives in the Commission's history. Out of 2,035 advertised vacancies, 2,027 candidates have been finally selected, while 8 posts reserved for Persons with Disabilities (PwD) remain unfilled due to the unavailability of eligible candidates. Uttar Pradesh's Shraddha Pandey has emerged as the overall topper with 593 marks. Candidates can check their selection status by downloading the final merit list PDF from the official BPSC website.
