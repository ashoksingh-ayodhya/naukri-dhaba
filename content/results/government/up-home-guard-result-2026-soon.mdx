The UP Home Guard Result 2026 is highly anticipated by lakhs of candidates who appeared in the written examination conducted by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB). The written exam was held from 25 to 27 April 2026, and the provisional answer key was released on 7 May 2026. Objections were accepted until 10 May 2026. Following the review of those objections, UPPRPB is now finalising the result. As per latest media reports and updates, the UP Home Guard Result Date 2026 is expected to fall in the last week of June 2026. No official date has been announced yet. Candidates can check their result on the official website uppbpb.gov.in once it is published.
