DPCC Trainee Merit List 2026 has been released by the Delhi Pollution Control Committee (DPCC) for the recruitment of 150 Technical and Scientific Trainees. The Admin Officer, Mr. Pawan Kumar Jayant, has signed off on the "Merit List of Trainees for 150 Candidates," which is available on the official DPCC website. This article explains how the merit list was prepared, who topped the list, category-wise selection details, and how to check your result.


## How to Apply

1. Visit the official DPCC website: dpcc.delhi.gov.in.
2. Go to the Trainee recruitment/application link on the careers or notifications page.
3. Fill in personal, educational, category, and GATE/NET details.
4. Upload scanned copies of required documents.
5. Submit the form and keep a printout for future reference.
6. Candidates who have previously served as Trainees at DPCC are not eligible for re-engagement.
7. Selected candidates cannot claim any right to regular or permanent employment.
8. Vacancy numbers are tentative and may increase or decrease as per departmental requirements.
9. Candidates should keep checking the official DPCC website for updates on document verification and joining schedules.
