MP Anganwadi Merit List 2026: The Directorate of Women & Child Development (WCD), Madhya Pradesh has released the provisional merit list for Anganwadi Worker (Karyakarta) and Anganwadi Helper (Sahayika) recruitment 2026, Phase-1, covering a total of 2,548 vacancies. The merit list was released on 22 July 2026 and is available for download on the official MP Online recruitment portal, chayan.mponline.gov.in. Separate provisional merit lists have been issued for the general category as well as the Special Caste (Sahariya, Bhariya, Baiga) category, for both Worker and Helper posts. This article explains how to check and download the MP Anganwadi Merit List 2026, along with important dates, vacancy details, eligibility conditions, and the next steps after the merit list.

A total of **2548** posts are available for this recruitment.
