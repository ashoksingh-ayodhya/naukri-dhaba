The wait is finally over for thousands of aspirants who appeared in the Rajasthan Village Development Officer recruitment process. The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially announced the RSSB VDO Final Result 2026 on 24 June 2026 at its official website rssb.rajasthan.gov.in. Candidates who participated in the Document Verification (DV) stage under Advertisement No. 03/2025 can now download the final selected candidates' merit list in PDF format and verify their selection status using their roll number.

A total of **850** posts are available for this recruitment.
