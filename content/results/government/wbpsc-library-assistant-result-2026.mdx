Public Service Commission, West Bengal (PSC WB) released an important update on 21st July 2026 for candidates who appeared for the Library Assistant recruitment in the West Bengal Legislative Assembly Secretariat (Advt No. 14/2022). Through Notice No. 190-PSC(Selection)/1S-03/2022, the Commission has published the roll numbers of candidates declared ineligible to proceed further in the selection process- spread across three categories: failing to secure minimum qualifying marks in English, non-submission of required documents, and lacking the essential "Knowledge of Computer Operation in Library Software" qualification. If you appeared for this recruitment, this article gives you the complete, verified roll number lists so you can quickly check your status, along with everything else officially confirmed about this notice.
