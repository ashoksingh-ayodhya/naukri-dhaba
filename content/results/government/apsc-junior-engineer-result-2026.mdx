The APSC Junior Engineer Result 2026 has been officially declared by the Assam Public Service Commission (APSC) on 7th July 2026 for the post of Junior Engineer (Civil/Mechanical/Chemical/Electrical) under the Public Health Engineering Department (PHED). The Commission has recommended a total of 187 candidates across all four engineering disciplines based on the Screening Test (OMR based) held on 22nd February 2026 and document verification conducted on 22nd, 23rd, and 24th June 2026. Candidates who applied against Advertisement No. 27/2025 dated 11/08/2025 can check their roll numbers in the official result list below.
