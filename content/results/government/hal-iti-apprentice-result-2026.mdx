Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Korwa, has officially released the HAL ITI Apprentice Result 2026 for Trade (ITI) Apprenticeship Training 2026-27. The list of provisionally selected candidates has been published by the Gurukulam Technical Training Institute, HAL, Avionics Division, Korwa, Amethi (U.P.). A total of 100 candidates across multiple ITI trades have been provisionally shortlisted. Selected candidates must appear for document verification between 29 June 2026 and 6 July 2026.
