The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has declared the result for the post of PGT (History) Male and Female in the Directorate of Education, GNCTD, under Post Code 831/24, vide Main Result Notice No. 1036 dated 10 July 2026. The result is based on the Tier-I written examination held on 22 September 2025. A total of 162 candidates have been provisionally nominated against 61 vacancies. Candidates can check their roll numbers, category-wise cutoff marks, and further process below.

A total of **61** posts are available for this recruitment.
