The Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC) has published the shortlist of candidates provisionally eligible for interview for the post of Higher Secondary School Teacher (Junior) Sanskrit - Statewide under Category No. 709/2025. The notification (SL.No. 156/2026/ERIX) was issued on 21/07/2026 from the KPSC Head Office, Thiruvananthapuram. This article gives you the complete, official register number list, cut-off mark, category-wise breakup, and the next steps you need to follow.


## How to Apply

1. Check their register number in the Main List, Supplementary List, or PwD list above.
2. Keep all original certificates and documents ready as listed in the "Required Documents" section.
3. Wait for the official schedule announcing the date, time, and venue of OTR Verification and interview, to be published separately by Kerala PSC.
4. Visit the official Kerala PSC website regularly for updates.
5. Inclusion of a register number in the Short List is purely provisional and subject to verification and scrutiny of the application on an absolute basis.
6. The Short List has been prepared by including candidates who secured the top marks in the Online Examination, based on details furnished by candidates in their application. Subsequent claims will not be entertained at any stage of selection.
7. Candidates who do not produce the required documents within the prescribed time limit, or whose applications are found defective, will be deleted from the Short List without further notice.
8. For this Online Examination, rechecking and revaluation are not allowed. However, a printout of the answer sheet will be issued to candidates who apply for it, on payment of the prescribed fee, after publication of the Ranked List. Detailed instructions on this will be furnished along with the Ranked List.
