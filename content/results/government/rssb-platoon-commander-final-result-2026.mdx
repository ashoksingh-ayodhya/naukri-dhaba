The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB), also known as RSMSSB, has released the result-related documents for Platoon Commander Direct Recruitment 2025 (Advt No. 05/2025) on 17 July 2026. Three separate documents were published on the same date: the provisionally qualified candidate lists after interview for both the TSP and Non-TSP areas, and the meritwise list of selected candidates with cut off marks after interview. Candidates who appeared in the written exam, PET/PST, and interview stages can check their status using the official links below.

A total of **84** posts are available for this recruitment.
