PSSSB Jail Warder, AS, and Matron Final Result 2026: The Punjab Subordinate Services Selection Board (PSSSB) has declared the final result for recruitment to 500 posts in the Jail Department of the Punjab Government. The recruitment, advertised under Advt. No. 07/2025, covers the posts of Warder, Assistant Superintendent (AS), and Matron. The Physical Measurement Test (PMT) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET) results have been declared on 21 July 2026, and candidates can now check their final result and download the result PDF from the official website, sssb.punjab.gov.in.

A total of **500** posts are available for this recruitment.
