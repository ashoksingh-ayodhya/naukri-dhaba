The Bhakra Beas Management Board (BBMB) has published the list of selected apprenticeship candidates for the Dehar Power House Circle, BBMB (PW) Slapper, for the year 2026-27. The result covers six trades: Electrician, D/Man, Fitter, Plumber, Welder, and PASAA. A total of 45 candidates have been selected across these trades.
