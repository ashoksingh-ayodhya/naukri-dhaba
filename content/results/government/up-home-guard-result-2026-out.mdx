The UP Home Guard Result 2026 has been officially released by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) on 28 June 2026 at its official website uppbpb.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the Home Guard Enrolment-2025 written examination, held on 25, 26, and 27 April 2026, can now log in and download their scorecard using their Roll Number or Application Number along with their Date of Birth. A total of 1,07,221 candidates have been shortlisted for the next stage Document Verification (DV) and Physical Standard Test (PST) out of more than 1.92 crore applicants who sat the exam for 41,424 Home Guard (Swayamsevak) vacancies.

A total of **41424** posts are available for this recruitment.
