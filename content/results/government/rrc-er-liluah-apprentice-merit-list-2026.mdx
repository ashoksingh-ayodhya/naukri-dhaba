The Railway Recruitment Cell, Eastern Railway (RRC-ER) has released the RRC ER Liluah Apprentice Merit List 2026 a provisional panel of candidates found suitable for Act Apprentice training at Liluah Workshop and Howrah Division. The list is trade-wise and community-wise, based on verification of original testimonials submitted against Notification No. RRC-ER/Act Apprentice/2025-26. Candidates can check their registration number against the official PDF and must report for Document Verification (DV) on 10 April 2026 at the Liluah Staff Canteen.
