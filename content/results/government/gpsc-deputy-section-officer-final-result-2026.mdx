The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) declared the final result of the Competitive Examination (Main) for the post of Deputy Section Officer, Legal Side, Class-III on 20 July 2026 through Notification No. APE-67/2024-25-Main-CNF. The result relates to Advertisement No. 67/2024-25, and the Main Examination was conducted from 22 to 24 February 2026.

Candidates must have 5% of the marks obtained by the candidate, as per Government Resolution dated 25 February 1980 as minimum educational qualification.
