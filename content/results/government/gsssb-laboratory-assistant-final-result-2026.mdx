The Gujarat Subordinate Services Selection Board (GSSSB), Gandhinagar, has officially declared the Revised Final Result for the post of Laboratory Assistant (Physics Group), Class-3 (Advertisement No. 242/2024-25) on 25 June 2026. The recruitment was conducted for 13 vacancies under the Home Department for the Directorate of Forensic Science Laboratory (FSL). This article covers the complete revised select list (Annexure-A), waiting list (Annexure-B), cut-off marks, and the list of unsuccessful/absent/OPT OUT candidates (Annexure-C).

A total of **13** posts are available for this recruitment.
