The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has declared the final result for the Assistant Inspector of Motor Vehicles, Class-III recruitment examination on 23 June 2026. Candidates can check their merit status, category-wise cut-off marks, and waiting list details from the official annexures published alongside the notification.
