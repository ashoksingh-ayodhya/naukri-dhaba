The Border Roads Organisation (BRO), under its General Reserve Engineer Force (GREF) wing, has declared the written examination result for the post of Vehicle Mechanic against Advertisement No. 02/2025, against a total of 324 vacancies. Out of 5,488 candidates who appeared for the written test, 481 candidates have been shortlisted to appear for the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) and Trade Test (TT). Shortlisted candidates must report at the GREF Centre, Pune, on 24 August, 28 August, or 1 September 2026.

A total of **324** posts are available for this recruitment.
