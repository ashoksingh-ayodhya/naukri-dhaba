Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board (MPESB) has released an official notification for MPESB Excise Constable (Abkari Sipahi) Recruitment Test 2024 Download Result 2026 for 253 Posts. 
A total of **253** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **01/03/2025**.

## How to Apply

1. Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board for the Excise Constable Direct and Backlog Post Recruitment Test - 2024 (For Excise Department M.P.) The applications of Recruitment will be online from 15 February 2025 to 01 March 2025
2. To apply in Madhya Pradesh Professional Board, the candidate has to first create a profile of himself. To create a profile, the candidate wants to have Aadhar card number and also want to have registered mobile number in it, only then there will be E-KYC in the profile, those whose mobile number is not registered, they will have to create a profile through biometric through MP Online Kiosk.
3. For the application of recruitment in Madhya Pradesh Employee Selection Board MPESB, along with the profile, all the candidates will also have to register in the Madhya Pradesh Employment Office.
4. For More Details Read the Full Notification.
