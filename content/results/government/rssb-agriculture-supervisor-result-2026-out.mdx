The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has officially released the RSSB Agriculture Supervisor Result 2026 on 23 June 2026 on its official website rssb.rajasthan.gov.in. The result has been published in PDF format, listing the roll numbers of candidates who have been shortlisted for the next stage of the selection process Document Verification (DV). Along with the merit list, RSSB has also released the Agriculture Supervisor Cut Off 2026, enabling candidates to assess their qualifying status. The written examination for this recruitment was conducted on 18 April 2026.
