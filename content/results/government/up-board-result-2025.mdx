Uttar Pradesh Madhyamik Shiksha Parishad (UPMSP) The result of Uttar Pradesh UP Board Class 10 and Class 12 Examination 2025 is going to be released. This year in 2025, a total of 27.32 lakh candidates appeared in the High School Class 10 examination and a total of 27.05 lakh candidates appeared in the Intermediate Class 12 examination, who are eagerly waiting for the result of the examination. The result of Uttar Pradesh Board UPMSP Result 2025 is to be released in the 25 April 2025. After the result is released, students can check their result by writing their roll number, the link of which will be available on Naukri Dhaba.

UPMSP UP Board Class 10th High School & Intermediate Class 12th Result 2025 has released an official notification for UPMSP UP Board Class 10th High School and Class 12th Intermediate Exam 2025 Result Today at 12:30 PM.
