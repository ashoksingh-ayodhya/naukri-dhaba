The Arunachal Pradesh Staff Selection Board (APSSB) has released the PET/PST result 2026 for candidates who appeared for the Physical Efficiency Test (PET) and Physical Standard Test (PST) for Fireman and Constable posts under the Special Recruitment Drive for Uniformed Services Combined Examination (USCE) 2026. Two separate result PDFs have been published - one for candidates who appeared on 13 July 2026 and one for candidates who appeared on 14 July 2026. Both PDFs are available on the official APSSB website, apssb.nic.in.
