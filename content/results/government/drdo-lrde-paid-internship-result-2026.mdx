The Electronics and Radar Development Establishment (LRDE), a laboratory of the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), Ministry of Defence, Government of India, has released the provisional result for the LRDE Paid Internship 2026-27. The notification was published on 16 July 2026 and lists candidates provisionally selected, along with a waitlist, for a six-month paid internship at LRDE, Bengaluru.


## How to Apply

1. Send a willingness email to [email protected] on or before 24 July 2026. If no willingness is received by this date, the candidature will be cancelled and the seat offered to a waitlisted candidate. No further correspondence on this will be entertained.
2. Report in person to the Director, LRDE, DRDO, CV Raman Nagar, Bengaluru - 560093 on 31 July 2026 for document verification, carrying all required original documents and self-attested copies.
3. On successful verification, the internship will commence from 3 August 2026.
4. The internship duration is 6 months and is considered part of the candidate's degree course.
5. Interns must be physically present at LRDE for a minimum of 15 working days per month during the internship.
6. If a candidate who has submitted willingness fails to join on the specified date, the offer will be treated as automatically cancelled with no further intimation.
7. Unfilled seats will be filled from the waitlisted candidates, who should be ready to report on short notice.
8. Interns will only be granted access to non-classified areas within LRDE.
9. Completion of the internship does not guarantee employment at DRDO.
