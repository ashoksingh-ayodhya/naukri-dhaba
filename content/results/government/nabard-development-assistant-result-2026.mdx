The National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD) has released the Development Assistant Main Exam 2026 results. NABARD has published the provisional select list and provisional wait list for candidates who appeared for the Main Exam held on 12th April 2026. Candidates can check their roll numbers against their respective regional offices and download the official PDF lists directly from NABARD's website.
