The Employees' State Insurance Corporation (ESIC), under the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India, has officially released the Select List for Recruitment of Insurance Medical Officers Grade-II (IMO Gr.-II) 2026. This select list has been prepared using the scores of candidates from the Combined Medical Services Examination 2024 (CMSE 2024) conducted by the Union Public Service Commission (UPSC), as available on the PRATIBHA Setu Portal. A total of 102 candidates have been shortlisted on a provisional basis, subject to document verification. The advertisement for this recruitment was issued on 13 January 2026, and the select list is dated 22 June 2026.
