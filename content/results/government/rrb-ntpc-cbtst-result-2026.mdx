The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the RRB NTPC CBTST Result 2025-26 for Category 4 (Senior Clerk cum Typist) and Category 5 (Junior Account Assistant cum Typist) posts under CEN No. 05/2024 (Non-Technical Popular Categories, Graduate). RRB Jammu-Srinagar published its shortlisting notice on 8 July 2026, listing additional candidates called for Document Verification (DV) and Medical Examination based on their performance in the 2nd Stage Computer Based Test (CBT-2), CBAT, and the Computer Based Typing Skill Test (CBTST). This is in continuation of the Category 1, 2, and 3 shortlisting reports already published earlier.
