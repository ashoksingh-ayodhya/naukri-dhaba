BPSC BSPCB Result 2026 has been officially declared by the Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) on 3 July 2026. The final results cover five posts under the Bihar State Pollution Control Board (BSPCB), Environment, Forest and Climate Change Department, Bihar, including System Analyst, Jan-Sampark Padadhikari, Sahayak Paryavaran Abhiyanta (Assistant Environmental Engineer), Vidhi Padadhikari (Law Officer), and Kaniya Prayogshala Sahayak (Junior Laboratory Assistant). Alongside these, BPSC has also issued a separate court-order compliance result for the post of Assistant Professor, Grammar, at Kameshwar Singh Darbhanga Sanskrit University. Candidates can check their roll numbers, merit list, and category-wise cut off marks in this article.
