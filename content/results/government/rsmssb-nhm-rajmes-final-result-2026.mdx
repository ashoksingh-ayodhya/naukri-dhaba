The Rajasthan Subordinate and Ministerial Services Selection Board (RSMSSB) released the NHM and RajMES Contractual Posts 2025 final result on 09 July 2026. The update covers multiple contractual health department posts, including Pharma Assistant, Senior Counsellor, Community Health Officer (CHO), Compounder (Ayurveda), and Psychiatric Care Nurse. Candidates who appeared for these posts can now check their final selection status, meritwise lists, and document verification (DV) results directly from the official RSSB results portal.
