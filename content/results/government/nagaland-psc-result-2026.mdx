Nagaland PSC NCS, NPS & Allied Services Result 2026 has been declared by the Nagaland Public Service Commission (NPSC) on 6th July 2026. The Commission has released the Mains written examination result and published the roll numbers of candidates shortlisted to appear for the Viva-voce (interview) round. Candidates who appeared for the NCS, NPS and Allied Services (Mains) Examination 2025 can check their qualification status through the official notification available on the NPSC website.
