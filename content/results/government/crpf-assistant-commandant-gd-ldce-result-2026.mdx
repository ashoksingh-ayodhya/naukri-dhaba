The Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF), on behalf of all four Central Armed Police Forces (CAPFs), has declared the final result for the post of Assistant Commandant (General Duty) - AC(GD) - through Limited Departmental Competitive Examination (LDCE) for the Vacancy Year 2024. The result covers selected candidates from CRPF, Border Security Force (BSF), Indo-Tibetan Border Police (ITBP), and Sashastra Seema Bal (SSB). The final result was released on 22 July 2026.
