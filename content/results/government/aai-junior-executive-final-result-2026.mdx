The Airports Authority of India (AAI) has declared the final result for the post of Junior Executive (Fire Services) under Advertisement No. 01/2025/CHQ. A total of 12 candidates have been provisionally selected for appointment based on their performance in the Computer Based Test (CBT) held on 06.05.2025, followed by Application Verification, Physical Measurement Test, Driving Test, and Physical Endurance Test conducted from 17.03.2026 to 20.03.2026. The result was officially released by the Executive Director (HR), AAI, on 01.07.2026.
