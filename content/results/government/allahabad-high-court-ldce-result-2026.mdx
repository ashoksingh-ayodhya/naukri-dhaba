The High Court of Judicature at Allahabad issued a notice on July 9, 2026, under the heading "Limited Departmental Competitive Examination," provisionally permitting a list of serving judicial officers to appear in the UPHJS (Uttar Pradesh Higher Judicial Service) - 2023 Limited Departmental Competitive Examination. The notice was signed by Pawan Kumar Sharma, HJS, Registrar (J) (Computer), I/c Selection & Appointment/Seniority. A total of 303 candidates are named in the attached list.
