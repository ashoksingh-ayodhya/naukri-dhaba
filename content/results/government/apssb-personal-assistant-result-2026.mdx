The Arunachal Pradesh Staff Selection Board (APSSB) has released the result of the Stenography Proficiency Test conducted for the post of Personal Assistant (Stenographer Gr-III), Post Code 12/26, under the Combined Graduate Level Examination 2026. The test was held from 4 July 2026 to 7 July 2026, and the result notice was issued on 8 July 2026 vide Memo No. APSSB-13/5/2026. Candidates whose roll numbers appear in the official list have been provisionally shortlisted for the next stage - the Written Examination - scheduled for 30 August 2026.

A total of **37** posts are available for this recruitment.
