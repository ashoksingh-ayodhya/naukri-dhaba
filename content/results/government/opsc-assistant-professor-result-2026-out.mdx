OPSC Assistant Professor Anaesthesiology Result 2026 has been declared by the Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC) on 4 July 2026. The Commission has recommended 25 candidates for appointment to the post of Assistant Professor (Broad Speciality) in the discipline of Anaesthesiology under the Health & Family Welfare Department. This recommendation has been issued against Advertisement No. 03 of 2025-26 through Notice No. 3865/P.S.C.
