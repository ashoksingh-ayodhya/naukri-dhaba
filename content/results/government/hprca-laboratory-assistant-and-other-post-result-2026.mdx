The Himachal Pradesh Rajya Chayan Aayog (HPRCA), Hamirpur declared the result of the Computer Based Screening Test (CBST) on 20 July 2026 for four different posts. The CBST for all four posts was conducted on 15.06.2026. Candidates whose roll numbers appear in the official result have been provisionally shortlisted for the next stage - document verification - scheduled for 03.08.2026 at 10:00 AM at the HPRCA premises in Hamirpur.
