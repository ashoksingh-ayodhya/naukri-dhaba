The AIIMS Faculty Recruitment Result 2026 has been declared by the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, for more than 460 vacant faculty posts ranging from Assistant Professor to Professor across over 50 disciplines. The result covers selections for AIIMS New Delhi and the AIIMS-Central Armed Police Forces Institute of Medical Sciences (CAPFIMS) campus at Maidan Garhi, and was announced using blockchain digital technology to ensure transparency in the selection process.

A total of **460** posts are available for this recruitment.
