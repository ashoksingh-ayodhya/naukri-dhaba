The Assam Government has officially released the Gunotsav 2025 results on March 27, 2025. Chief Minister Himanta Biswa Sarma announced the results at a special event held at Srimanta Sankaradeva Kalakshetra in Guwahati. This year’s evaluation focused on enhancing the quality of school education across the state.
