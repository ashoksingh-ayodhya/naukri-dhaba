The Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC) has officially declared the written examination results for three major recruitment posts on 25th June 2026. Candidates who appeared for the Group B Non-Gazetted Technical Examination for the post of Tutor under the Health & Family Welfare (H&FW) Department, the Technical Competitive Examination for Junior Engineer (Electrical) under the Public Health Engineering (PHE) Department, and the Competitive Examination for Instructor, ITI under the Labour, Employment, Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (LESDE) Department can now check their results. All three notifications were issued by MPSC from the Mizoram New Capital Complex, Khatla, Aizawl.
