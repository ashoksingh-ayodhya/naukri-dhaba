Sambalpur University has officially released the Sambalpur University Result 2026 for various UG and PG programmes. Students who appeared for the M.Com Final Semester Examination, April 2026 and Integrated B.Ed-M.Ed 6th Semester Examination 2026 can now check their results online through the university's official website.The university has published the latest results for M.Com Final Semester and Int.B.Ed-M.Ed 6th Semester courses. Candidates who appeared for these examinations can access their results and download the provisional marksheet for future academic and admission-related purposes.
