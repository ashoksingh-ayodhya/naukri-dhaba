The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has officially released the List of Eligible Candidates for Application Scrutiny for two major recruitment advertisements - Advertisement No. 124/2025-26 for Deputy Executive Engineer (Civil), Class-2, and Advertisement No. 98/2025-26 for Inspector of Inspection of Building and Other Construction, Class-II. The result lists were published on 25 June 2026 and are available on the official GPSC portal. Candidates who appeared in the preliminary examination can now check whether their seat number appears in the eligible list and proceed with the online document upload process.
