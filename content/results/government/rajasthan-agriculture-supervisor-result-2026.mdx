The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) is expected to release the RSSB Agriculture Supervisor Result 2026 by 24th June 2026 at its official website, rssb.rajasthan.gov.in. The result will be declared for the written examination conducted for the post of Agriculture Supervisor against a total of 1100 vacancies. Once released, candidates can download the result PDF directly from the official RSSB website or use the direct link that will be shared below. The result PDF will contain the roll numbers and names of candidates who have qualified the written exam and are eligible to proceed to the Document Verification stage.

A total of **1100** posts are available for this recruitment.
