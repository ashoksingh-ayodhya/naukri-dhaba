The Haryana Public Service Commission (HPSC) has finalized and declared results for four Assistant Professor (College Cadre) posts under the Higher Education Department, Haryana. All four results were dated 23.07.2026 and are available as separate official PDFs on the HPSC website, hpsc.gov.in. The subjects covered are Mathematics, Psychology, Commerce, and Music (Instrumental). All results are described by HPSC as provisional and subject to document verification and the outcome of pending court cases.
