The Arunachal Pradesh Staff Selection Board (APSSB) has declared the Physical Standard Test (PST) and Physical Efficiency Test (PET) results for candidates who appeared under the Special Recruitment Drive for Uniformed Services Combined Examination (USCE) - 2026. Two separate result PDFs have been released, one for candidates tested on 16-07-2026 and one for candidates tested on 15-07-2026, both declared on 17-07-2026. Candidates can download their result PDF from the official APSSB website and check their roll number to confirm qualification status.
