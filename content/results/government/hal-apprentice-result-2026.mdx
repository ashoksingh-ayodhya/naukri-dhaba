Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad has released the Provisional Allotment Letter for the One Year Apprenticeship Training programme under the NATS Scheme, covering Diploma (Technician), Graduate in General Stream, and Engineering Stream Graduate Apprentices. The letter, numbered HAL/HD/TRG/2026-27/JUL/NATS/DIP-GRAD/INTAKE-1, is dated 16th July 2026 and lists 170 provisionally shortlisted candidates in Annexure-I.

Candidates must have Share Paid by BOAT (₹) as minimum educational qualification.
