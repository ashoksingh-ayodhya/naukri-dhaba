The Delhi Subordinate Services Selection Board (DSSSB) has officially released the DSSSB PGT Geography Result 2026 on 24 June 2026 through Main Result Notice No. 1030. This result covers the post of PGT (Geography) - Male and Female - in the Directorate of Education, GNCTD, and PGT (Geography) in the New Delhi Municipal Council (NDMC) under Post Code 812/23. Candidates who appeared in the Tier-I written examination held on 25 July 2025 and 02 August 2025 can now check their provisional nomination status and category-wise cut-off marks.

A total of **24** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have UR-01, EWS-NIL, OBC-NIL, SC-NIL, ST-NIL (incl. PwBD d&e) as minimum educational qualification.
