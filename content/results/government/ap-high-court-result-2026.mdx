The High Court of Andhra Pradesh at Amaravati has officially released the AP High Court Result 2026 in the form of the third provisional selection list dated 25 June 2026 under ROC No. 72/2025-RC. The results have been declared for six posts under District Judiciary recruitment - Junior Assistant, Office Subordinate, Process Server, Examiner, Field Assistant, and Record Assistant. Candidates who appeared under Notification Nos. 2, 4, 5, 8, 9, and 10 of 2025-RC can now check their provisional selection status district-wise and category-wise on the official website aphc.gov.in. This is the third and latest list released after earlier lists published in December 2025 and April 2026.
