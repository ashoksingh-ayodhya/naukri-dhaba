The RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Main Merit List 2026 has been released by the Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) on 3 July 2026 for eight divisions of the Senior Scientific Officer Competitive Examination 2024. Candidates who appeared for the posts of Senior Scientific Officer in the Toxicology, Serology, Physics, Narcotics, Documents, DNA, Cyber Forensic, and Biology Divisions can now check their main merit list on the official RPSC website.
