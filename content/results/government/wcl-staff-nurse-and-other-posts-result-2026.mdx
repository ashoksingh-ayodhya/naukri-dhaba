Western Coalfields Limited (WCL), a Miniratna Cat-1 Government of India undertaking under Coal India, has declared the result for multiple Trainee and Grade-D/Grade-C posts advertised under Notification No. 583 dated 12.03.2026 and Notification No. 553 dated 31.01.2026. The result includes roll number wise marks obtained by passed candidates for the posts of Jr. Technician (ECG/EEG), Pharmacist (Trainee), Staff Nurse (Trainee), Technician (Pathological) Trainee and Jr. Technical Inspector. WCL has also issued an office order dated 01.07.2026 confirming the final selection of two candidates for the Pharmacist (Trainee) post in T&S Grade-C.
