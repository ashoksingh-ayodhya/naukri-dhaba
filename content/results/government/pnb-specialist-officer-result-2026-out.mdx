Punjab National Bank (PNB) has released the list of candidates shortlisted for interview under the "Recruitment of 30 Specialist Officers under HR Plan 2026-27." A total of 257 candidates have been shortlisted across three posts — Officer (Civil Engineer), Officer (Electrical Engineer), and Officer (Mechanical Engineer). Interviews are scheduled from 30 July to 1 August 2026 in New Delhi. This article explains the shortlist, interview schedule, venue, and the next steps for shortlisted candidates.

A total of **30** posts are available for this recruitment.
