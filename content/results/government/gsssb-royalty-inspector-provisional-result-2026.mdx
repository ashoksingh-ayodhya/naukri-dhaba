The Gujarat Subordinate Services Selection Board (GSSSB), Gandhinagar has officially released the GSSSB Royalty Inspector Provisional Result 2026 on 24 June 2026. All 833 candidates who appeared in the written examination held on 10 February 2026 can now check their obtained marks in Part-A and Part-B through the official website gsssb.gujarat.gov.in. The result has been published as Annexure-A under Advertisement Number 369/202526 for a total of 29 vacancies in the Royalty Inspector, Class-3 cadre under the Industries and Mines Department, Government of Gujarat.

A total of **29** posts are available for this recruitment.
