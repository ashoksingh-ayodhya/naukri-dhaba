The Himachal Pradesh Rajya Chayan Aayog (HPRCA), Hamirpur has declared the result of the Computer Based Screening Test (CBST) for recruitment to the post of Teacher (Public Administration), engaged on an engagement basis, against Post Code 26034. The exam was conducted on 30 June 2026, and the result press note was issued on 21 July 2026. A total of 93 candidates have been provisionally shortlisted for document verification, which will begin on 4 August 2026.

A total of **78** posts are available for this recruitment.
