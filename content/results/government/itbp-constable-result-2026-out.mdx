The Indo-Tibetan Border Police Force (ITBP) has officially released the ITBP Constable Compassionate Ground Recruitment Result 2026. The shortlist has been published for the 1st Phase Recruitment Test conducted at SS Bn, ITBPF, Saboli Camp, Nathupura, Sonipat, Haryana, with effect from 29 June 2026. A total of 149 candidates appear in the Appendix-A shortlist across multiple posts. All shortlisted candidates are required to report to SS Bn ITBP, Saboli Camp, Post-Nathupura, Distt-Sonipat (Haryana) - 131029 on their allotted reporting date at 0700 Hrs.

A total of **105** posts are available for this recruitment.
