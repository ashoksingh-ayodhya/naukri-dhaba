The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the RRB Group D Result 2026 for CEN 08/2024 (Level 1 posts) on 1st July 2026. Candidates who appeared in the Computer-Based Test (CBT) held between 27 November 2025 and 10 February 2026 can now check their qualifying status, zone-wise merit list, scorecard, and cut off marks on the official regional RRB websites. This result follows the resolution of a Telangana High Court case that had earlier delayed the declaration.

A total of **32438** posts are available for this recruitment.
