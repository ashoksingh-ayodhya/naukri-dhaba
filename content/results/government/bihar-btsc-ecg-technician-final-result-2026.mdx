The Bihar Technical Service Commission (BTSC) has declared the ECG Technician Final Result 2026 on 16 July 2026 under Advertisement No. 04/2025. This result covers a total of 242 ECG Technician posts that were part of the larger BTSC Lab Technician, OT Assistant, ECG Technician, X-Ray Technician & Specialist Medical Officer (SMO) Recruitment 2025 for 9,757 posts. Candidates who appeared in the written exam and attended Document Verification (DV) can now check their final selection status, category-wise vacancy details, and cut-off information through the official BTSC website, btsc.bihar.gov.in.

A total of **242** posts are available for this recruitment.
