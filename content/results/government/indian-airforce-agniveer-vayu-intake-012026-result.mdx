## How to Apply

1. Indian Airforce Are Released the Notification for Agniveer Vayu Intake 02/2025 Recruitment 2024. Candidate Can Apply Online Between 07/01/2025 to 02/02/2025.
2. Photo Instruction : Passport size recent colour photograph (taken not before January 2025) of size 10 KB to 50 KB (front portrait in light background without head gear except for Sikhs). The photograph is to be taken with candidate holding a black slate in front of his chest with his name and date of photograph taken, clearly written on it with white chalk in capital letter.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in Agniveer Vayu Intake 01/2026 Online Form 2025.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
