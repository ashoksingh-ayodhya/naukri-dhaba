Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC), District Office Kannur has released the shortlist of candidates provisionally eligible for the Endurance Test for the post of Beat Forest Officer (First NCA: Hindu Nadar), Category No. 432/2025, in the Forest and Wildlife Department, Kannur District. The shortlist (SL.No. 32/2026/DOC) was published on 20-07-2026 and is based on the Common OMR Examination held on 06-05-2026. A total of 71 candidates have been shortlisted.
