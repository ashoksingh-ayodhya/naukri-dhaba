The KVS NVS Tier 2 Result 2026 will be release by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) as of 15 July 2026. The Tier 2 examination for Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti (NVS) recruitment was conducted from 27 to 31 March 2026, and the provisional Tier 2 answer key was released on 13 May 2026 with an objection window that closed on 17 May 2026. Once CBSE finalises the answer key after reviewing objections, the Tier 2 result will be published on the official portals. This page will be updated with the confirmed result date and direct link as soon as it is officially declared.

A total of **15762** posts are available for this recruitment.
