The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), Ajmer, has released the interview shortlist for the Assistant Professor - History Competitive Exam 2023 on 21 July 2026. A total of 36 candidates have been declared "purely provisional" successful for the interview stage, based on the written (objective type) exam result that was announced earlier on 19 March 2025.


## How to Apply

1. Visit the official RPSC website: https://rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in.
2. Download the detailed application form (Vistrit Aavedan Patra) for this interview.
3. Fill the form completely and prepare two physical copies.
4. Gather original and photocopies of all educational, professional, caste, and other relevant certificates.
5. Wait for RPSC's official communication about the exact interview date, venue, and time, and appear with all documents on that date.
6. This shortlist is purely provisional. Being on this list does not guarantee a final interview call - RPSC will first verify eligibility as per the original advertisement's conditions.
7. Only candidates found eligible after verification will be permitted to appear for the interview.
8. The interview date for this specific list has not yet been announced and will be communicated in due course by RPSC.
9. Candidates should regularly check the official RPSC website for updates on interview date, venue, and call letters.
