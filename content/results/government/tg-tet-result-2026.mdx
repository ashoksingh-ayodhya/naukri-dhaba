The Department of School Education, Telangana, is scheduled to release the TG TET (Telangana Teacher Eligibility Test) June 2026 session result today, July 13, 2026, at 2:30 PM, on the official website tgtet.aptonline.in. Candidates who appeared for Paper I, Paper II, or both will be able to check their result and download their scorecard using their Hall Ticket Number and Date of Birth.
