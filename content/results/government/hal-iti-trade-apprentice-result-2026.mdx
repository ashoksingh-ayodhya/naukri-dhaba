Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Avionics Division, Hyderabad has released the provisional allotment list for the One-Year ITI Trade Apprenticeship Training 2026-27 under the National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS). The official letter, dated 30 June 2026, confirms that 167 candidates have been provisionally shortlisted after document verification, with training scheduled to begin on 17 July 2026 at the TTC Building, Avionics Division, HAL, Balanagar, Hyderabad.
