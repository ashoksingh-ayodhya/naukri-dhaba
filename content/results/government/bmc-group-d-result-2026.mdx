The Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC/MCGM) has published the Common Merit List (Result) for Class 'D' (Group D) posts under the General Manager, Deonar Slaughterhouse (Devnar Pashuvadh Griha) establishment, Labour Department. This merit list covers 38 vacant 'D' cadre posts to be filled through direct recruitment, and it lists candidates who appeared for the online examination held on 4 May 2026, conducted against the earlier advertisement No. 802/MC dated 22 January 2026. The merit list itself was released through Notice No. 494/MC dated 14 July 2026, signed by the Chief Labour Officer (Pramukh Kamgar Adhikari), BMC.
