Uttar Pradesh Education Service Selection Commission (UPESSC) has released an official notification for UPTET Result 2026 | UP TET Teacher Eligibility Test. 
The last date to apply is **03/05/2026**.
Candidates must have Bachelor Degree / Master Degree with 50% Marks with B.Ed / B.Ed Special Exam Passed / Appearing (Final Year) OR; Bachelor Degree / Master Degree with BTC Training 2 Year Exam Passed OR; 10+2 Intermediate Exam with 50% Marks and B.LEd 4 Year Course OR; 10+2 Inter Exam with 50% Marks and B.SC Ed Exam Passed; Bachelor Degree in Any Stream with 45% Marks / Master Degree in Any Subject with B.Ed Degree (Passed / Appearing) (As per NCTE Norms) OR; More Details Read Notification as minimum educational qualification.
