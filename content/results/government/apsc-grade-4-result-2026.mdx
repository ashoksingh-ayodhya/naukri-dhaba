The Assam Public Service Commission (APSC) has declared the APSC Grade 4 Result 2026 for the Phase-I Screening Test conducted for recruitment to Grade-IV posts under the establishment of the Commission itself. The result was released on 20 July 2026 in PDF format on the official website, apsc.nic.in. Candidates who qualify in this screening round will be called for Phase-II: Interview/Viva-Voce.
