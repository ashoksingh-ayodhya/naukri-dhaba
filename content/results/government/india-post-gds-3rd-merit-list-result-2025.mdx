Department of Post (India Post) has released an official notification for India Post Gramin Dak Sevaks GDS Schedule I January Recruitment 2025 Third Merit List / Result for 21413 Post. 
A total of **21413** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **03/03/2025**.

## How to Apply

1. India Post GDS Delhi Are Released Gramin Dak Sewak GDS All India Recruitment in Various Recruitment 2025. Candidate Can Apply Between 10/02/2025 to 03/03/2025.
2. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in India Post GDS Jobs Recruitment January 2025.
3. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
4. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Admission Entrance Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
5. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
6. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
