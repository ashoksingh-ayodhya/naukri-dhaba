The Post Office GDS IVA Merit List 2026 has been published by the Department of Posts (DoP) as a Supplementary List-IVA under the GDS Online Engagement Schedule-I, January 2026. The list has been released for eight Divisions across four Postal Circles, including Anand, Bagalkot, Davanagere, Goa, Baramati, Shrirampur, Nagaland, and Dharmanagar, where by-elections had earlier delayed the engagement process.
