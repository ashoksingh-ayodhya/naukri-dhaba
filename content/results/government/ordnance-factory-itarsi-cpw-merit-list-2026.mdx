Ordnance Factory Itarsi (OFI), functioning under Munitions India Limited (MIL) and the Ministry of Defence, has released its Tenure Based CPW (Chemical Process Worker) shortlist for 2026. A total of 828 provisionally eligible candidates have been called for the Trade Test / Practical Test, scheduled from 6 July to 11 July 2026, with reporting time fixed at 8:30 AM each day. Call letters have already been emailed to shortlisted candidates, and the schedule has been organised strictly by roll number.
