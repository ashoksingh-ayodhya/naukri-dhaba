The Kerala Public Service Commission (KPSC), District Office Thrissur, has published the short list of register numbers for the post of Civil Excise Officer Driver (Part I - Direct Recruitment), Category No. 386/2025, in the Kerala Excise and Prohibition Department. Candidates whose register numbers appear in this list are provisionally eligible for the Physical Measurement and Practical Test (T-Test and Road Test), subject to verification of original documents. The notification (SL.No. 26/2026/DOR) was issued on 30 June 2026 based on the Common OMR Examination held on 25 February 2026.
