The Mizoram Public Service Commission (MPSC) has officially declared the Final Result 2026 for the posts of Assistant Grade and Upper Division Clerk (UDC) under various departments of the Government of Mizoram. The result was published on 19th June 2026 through official notification number NON-TECHNICAL/4/2025-MPSC(PE), issued from the Mizoram New Capital Complex, Khatla, Aizawl. Candidates who appeared in the Combined Competitive Examination held on 9th October 2025 and the Personal Interview conducted between 15th May and 19th June 2026 can now verify their selection status in the official merit list published by the MPSC.
