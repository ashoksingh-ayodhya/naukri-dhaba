The Odisha Public Service Commission (OPSC) is set to announce the OPSC OCS Prelims Result 2026 in July 2026 on its official website, opsc.gov.in. Lakhs of candidates who appeared for the Odisha Civil Service (OCS) Preliminary Examination on 7 June 2026 are now waiting for the merit list that will decide who advances to the Mains stage. Once declared, the result PDF will carry roll numbers and qualifying status of shortlisted candidates, along with category-wise cut off marks released separately by the Commission.

A total of **465** posts are available for this recruitment.
