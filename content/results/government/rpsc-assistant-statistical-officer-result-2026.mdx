The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the Result Preamble and Cutoff Marks for the Assistant Statistical Officer (Economics and Statistics Department) Exam-2024. RPSC has published two separate result documents a Main List and a Reserve List both available on the official website rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in. The written examination for this post was conducted on 12 October 2025, and candidates can now download their result PDF and check the cutoff marks for their respective category directly from the official portal.
