The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) is expected to release the RRB Group D Result 2026 in the 4th week of June 2026 at the official website rrbcdg.gov.in, following the disposal of the Telangana High Court case that had earlier put a hold on the result. Over 1.08 crore candidates who appeared for the Computer Based Test (CBT) under CEN 08/2024 for 32,438 Level 1 vacancies can check their merit list, scorecard, and zone-wise cut off marks once the result is declared. This article covers everything candidates need to know the latest court case update, how to check the result, scorecard details, cut off expectations, and the next stage of selection.

A total of **32438** posts are available for this recruitment.
