The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), Ajmer has officially released the Main Merit Lists for three major recruitment examinations on 24 June 2026. The results announced are for the posts of Assistant Professor (Medical Education) - Anaesthesiology, Group Instructor / Surveyor / Assistant Apprenticeship Advisor, and Analyst-cum-Programmer (Deputy Director). Candidates who appeared in the written examination and interview can now check their final rankings, total marks, and selection status from the official RPSC website or through the direct PDF links provided in this article.
