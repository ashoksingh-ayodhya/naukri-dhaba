The GPSC Accounts Officer Result 2026 has been declared by the Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) in the form of an interview eligibility list. On 6th July 2026, GPSC released the roll numbers of candidates who qualified for the interview stage under Advertisement No. 114/2024-25 (Accounts Officer, Class-II) and Advertisement No. 136/2024-25 (Accounts Officer, Class-I, Special Recruitment Drive for Divyangs), based on the Combined Competitive (Main Written) Exam held from 8th to 12th January 2026.

Candidates must have 6th July 2026 as minimum educational qualification.
