The RRC NWR Apprentice Merit List 2026 has been released by the Railway Recruitment Cell, North Western Railway (RRC NWR), for the Ajmer Division under Notification No. 04/2025 (NWR/AA) dated 26.09.2025. The provisional training panel, digitally signed on 23.06.2026 by the Assistant Karmik Adhikari-III, Ajmer, lists selected candidates for Act Apprentice training across ten trades, including Electrician, Fitter, Carpenter, Painter, Mason, Pipe Fitter, and Diesel Mechanic. A total of 184 training slots have been allotted to the Ajmer Division, and reporting for training will take place between 13 July 2026 and 20 July 2026.
