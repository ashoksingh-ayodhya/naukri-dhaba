The Uttar Pradesh Education Service Selection Commission (UPESSC) has officially declared the UP TGT Result 2026 on June 30, 2026, on its website, upessc.up.gov.in. The result is for candidates who appeared in the Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT) written examination held on June 3 and 4, 2026, for recruitment to aided secondary school teaching posts under Advertisement No. 01/2022. Shortlisted candidates can now download their call letters from July 1, 2026, ahead of document verification, which begins on July 9.

Candidates must have July 13, 2026 as minimum educational qualification.
