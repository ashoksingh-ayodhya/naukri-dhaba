The UPSSSC Instructor & Mechanic Result 2026 has been officially declared by the Uttar Pradesh Adhinasth Seva Chayan Aayog (UPSSSC) on 2 July 2026. The result covers the post of instructor, Mechanic (Refrigeration and Air Conditioner) under the Directorate of Training and Employment, Uttar Pradesh, advertised through Advertisement No. 20(7)/2015 (Instructor Samanya Chayan Bharti 2015). Out of 48 advertised posts, 47 candidates have been selected, and the category-wise cut off marks have also been released along with the result.
