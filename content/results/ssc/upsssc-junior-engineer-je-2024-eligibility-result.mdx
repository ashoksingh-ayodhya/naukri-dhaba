Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released an official notification for UPSSSC Junior Engineer JE Civil Recruitment 2024 Exam Eligibility Result, Mains Answer Key 2026 for 4612 Post. 
A total of **4612** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **13/07/2024**.
Candidates must have Click Here as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. There are 02 types to apply online for UPSSSC Junior Engineer Civil Recruitment Advt No. 08-Exam/2024 Recruitment 2024.
2. First: In this, the candidate will have to login with his personal information, the information that has to be given is: PET Registration Number, Date of Birth, Gender, Domicile and Category.
3. Second : In this, the candidate will have to login through his OTP, the information that has to be given is: UPSSSC PET 2023 Registration Number and OTP Password.
4. After login, the complete information of the candidate, his photo and signature will also be visible, the candidate will have to fill the information related to the post he is asking for and the application fee: Rs.25/- will have to be paid.
5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Apply Online / Submit Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
