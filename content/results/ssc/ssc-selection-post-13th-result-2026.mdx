Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released an official notification for SSC Selection Posts XIII Recruitment 2025 Download Result, Final Answer Key for 2423 Matric, Inter and Graduate Level. 
The last date to apply is **23/06/2025**.

## How to Apply

1. SSC Photo Instruction : The candidate will have to take a LIVE PHOTO for which a webcam will be required, there should be background light in the live photo and both the eyes of the candidate should be open and the photo of the candidate should be absolutely straight.
2. Staff Selection Commission SSC Are Issued Notification for Selection Various Post XII Recruitment 2024 Candidate Can Apply Between 02/06/2025 to 23/06/2025.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in SSC Selection Post XII Recruitment Online Form 2024.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
