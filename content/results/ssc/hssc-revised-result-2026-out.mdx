The Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) has released a revised result 2026 in the form of an additional shortlist of candidates for the Physical Measurement Test (PMT), covering the posts of Forester, Forest Guard, Deputy Ranger, Warder, Assistant Superintendent Jail, Constable, and Sub-Inspector. This public notice, dated 29.06.2026, has been issued against Advertisement Nos. 13/2024, 02/2026, and 04/2026. Candidates whose registration numbers appear in the newly released list must download their admit cards and appear for the PMT starting 02.07.2026, as per the official schedule.
