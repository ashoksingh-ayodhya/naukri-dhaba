Staff Selection Commission (SSC) has released an official notification for SSC GD Constable Recruitment 2024 Exam Date for CAPFs, NIA, SSF and Rifleman (GD) in Assam Rifles Examination 2025 Result with Marks. 
A total of **53690** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **14/10/2024 upto 11 PM**.

## How to Apply

1. Staff Selection Commission SSC Constable GD Recruitment 2024, Candidate Can Apply Between 05/09/2024 to 14/11/2024.
2. SSC Live Photo Instruction : SSC has made a big change in the application process. Now the photo of the candidate will be uploaded live, that too through webcam or through the official SSC App. The candidate has to take care that he looks straight ahead in the photo and the background should also be light/white.
3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Recruitment Application Form in SSC Constable GD Vacancy 2024.
4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
6. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If You have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
