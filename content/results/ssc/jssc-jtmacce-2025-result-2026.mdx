The Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) has officially declared the JSSC JTMACCE 2025 Result on 25 June 2026. The result has been published under Advertisement Number 02/2025 for the Jharkhand Trained Secondary Teacher Combined Competitive Examination (JTMACCE) 2025. Candidates who appeared in this examination can now check the subject-wise and category-wise list of selected roll numbers on the official website at jssc.jharkhand.gov.in.
