Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UPSSSC) has released an official notification for UPSSSC Auditor & Assistant Accountant Recruitment 2023 Exam Final Result for 530 Post. 
A total of **530** posts are available for this recruitment.
The last date to apply is **01/08/2023**.
Candidates must have Click Here as minimum educational qualification.

1. There are 02 types to apply online for UPSSSC Auditor & Assistant Accountant Recruitment Advt No. 05-Exam/2023 Recruitment 2023.
2. First: In this, the candidate will have to login with his personal information, the information that has to be given is: PET Registration Number, Date of Birth, Gender, Domicile and Category.
3. Second : In this, the candidate will have to login through his OTP, the information that has to be given is: UPSSSC PET 2022 Registration Number and OTP Password.
4. After login, the complete information of the candidate, his photo and signature will also be visible, the candidate will have to fill the information related to the post he is asking for and the application fee: Rs.25/- will have to be paid.
