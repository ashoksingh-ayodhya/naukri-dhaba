The Odisha Sub-ordinate Staff Selection Commission (OSSSC) has officially released the Phase-II Result for the post of Laboratory Technician 2021 on 24 June 2026. Candidates who appeared in the Written Test and were awarded marks for Contractual Experience can now check their provisional selection status through the OSSSC official website. The results include the Roll Numbers of provisionally selected candidates district-wise, published in Annexure I.
