Staff Selection Commission has announced recruitment for SSC CPO SI Sub- Inspector in Delhi Police and CAPFs 4187 Posts of Delhi Police BSF CISF CRPF ITBP SSB. has released an official notification for Female Candidate. 
A total of **369** posts are available for this recruitment.
Candidates must have 369 Post as minimum educational qualification.

## How to Apply

1. SSC CPO SI Recruitment 2024 Notification Released and Candidate Apply within Last Date.
2. Candidate Read SSC CPO SI Notification 2024 very carefully before Apply the Recruitment Application Form.
3. While applying, the candidate will have to fill all the columns carefully so that no mistake can be made like – Name of the candidate, Father’s name, Mother’s name, Date of birth, Address, Qualification details.
4. If upload is asked in the application form, then upload all the documents in the correct size and correct format, be it PDF or JPEG.
5. Before submitting the form, re-check all the columns and documents and submit only if everything is correct.
6. After submitting the form of SSC CPO SI Recruitment 2024, take a print of it or save it in PDF.
