The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission has officially released the UPSSSC ASO Mains Shortlisting Result 2026 on its official website, upsssc.gov.in. Candidates who applied for the Assistant Statistical Officer (ASO) and Assistant Research Officer (ARO) posts under Advertisement No. 05-Exam/2026 can now check their shortlisting status online. The recruitment is being conducted for a total of 1,565 vacancies - an increase from the originally announced 929 posts.

A total of **1565** posts are available for this recruitment.
