The Teachers Recruitment Board (TRB), Tamil Nadu has released the TN TET Answer Key 2026 on July 15, 2026 at the official website trb.tn.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the TNTET (Special TET) exam held on July 4 and 5, 2026 can download the provisional answer key and response sheet for Paper 1 and Paper 2. The objection window is open from July 15 to July 22, 2026.
