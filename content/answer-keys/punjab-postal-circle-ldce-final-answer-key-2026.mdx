The Office of the Chief Postmaster General, Punjab Circle, Chandigarh, has released the final answer key for the LDCE (Limited Departmental Competitive Examination) / Competitive Examination 2026, held for promotion/recruitment to the post of Postal Assistant/Sorting Assistant under the Departmental Quota of vacancy for the year 2026 (01.01.2026 to 31.12.2026). The examination was conducted on 28.06.2026 (Sunday), and the final answer key of Paper-I was forwarded on 13.07.2026 for publishing on the official India Post website.
