The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) released the provisional MPESB Van Rakshak / Jail Prahari Answer Key 2026 on 22nd June 2026 at esb.mp.gov.in. Candidates who appeared in the Computer Based Test held from 4th to 19th June 2026 for the posts of Van Rakshak, Kshetra Rakshak, Jail Prahari, and Jail Superintendent can now download their answer key and response sheet using their application number and password. The board has also opened an objection window so candidates can challenge incorrect answers before the final key is published.
