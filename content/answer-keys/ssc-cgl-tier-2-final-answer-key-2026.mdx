The Staff Selection Commission released the SSC CGL Tier 2 Final Answer Key 2026 on 17th June 2026, along with individual scorecards and response sheets for the Combined Graduate Level Examination 2025-26. Candidates who appeared in the Tier 2 exam, held on 18th and 19th January 2026, can now log in at ssc.gov.in using their Registration ID and password to download their final answer key, marks, and response sheet. The facility is available until 16th July 2026 (9:00 PM), after which the documents will no longer be accessible.
