The Madhya Pradesh Employees Selection Board (MPESB) has released the MPESB ADDET Answer Key 2026 for the Animal Husbandry and Dairy Technology Admission Test (ADDET), conducted for 1,100 seats across Animal Husbandry Diploma and Dairy Technology courses. The answer key was released on 13 July 2026 on the official website esb.mp.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the exam held on 10 July 2026 can download the MPESB ADDET Answer Key 2026 and response sheet using their login credentials.
