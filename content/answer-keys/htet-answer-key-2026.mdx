The Board of School Education Haryana (BSEH) is set to release the HTET Answer Key 2026 today, 6 July 2026, on its official websites bseh.org.in and htet.eapplynow.com. The provisional key will cover all three levels of the Haryana Teacher Eligibility Test Level 1 (PRT), Level 2 (TGT), and Level 3 (PGT) allowing candidates who wrote the exam on 4 and 5 July 2026 to match their responses, estimate their probable score, and raise objections if they spot an incorrect answer.
