The Jammu & Kashmir Services Selection Board (JKSSB) has released the Provisional Answer Key for the OMR-based written examination conducted for the post of Staff Nurse G-II under the Health and Medical Education Department. The exam was held on 12.07.2026 against Advertisement Notification No. 07 of 2025, Item No. 142. The answer key covers all four question paper series A, B, C and D and candidates who wish to challenge any answer can submit an objection offline within the specified window.
