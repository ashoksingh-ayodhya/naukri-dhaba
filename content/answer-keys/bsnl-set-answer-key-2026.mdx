Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited (BSNL) released the BSNL SET Answer Key 2026 on 10th July 2026 for the post of Senior Executive Trainee (Telecom & Finance). The answer key is available in the form of a response sheet, and candidates who appeared in the Computer-Based Test (CBT) held on 5th July 2026 can download it by logging in with their registration number and password. BSNL has also opened an objection window so candidates can challenge any answer they believe is incorrect, provided they submit supporting documents.
