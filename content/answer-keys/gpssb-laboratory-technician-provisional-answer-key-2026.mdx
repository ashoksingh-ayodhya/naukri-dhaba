The Gujarat Panchayat Service Selection Board (GPSSB) has officially released the Provisional Answer Key cum Response Sheet for the Laboratory Technician recruitment exam under Advertisement No. 04/2026-27. The Computer Based Response Test (CBRT) was conducted on 5th July 2026, and candidates can now check their responses, compare them against the provisional key, and raise objections if needed. The answer key and response sheet are available online from 8th July 2026, 2:00 PM to 13th July 2026, 11:55 PM.
