The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has officially released the MPSC LDCE Paper 1 and Paper 2 Final Answer Keys 2026 on 23rd June 2026. Candidates who appeared in the Limited Departmental Competitive Examination (LDCE) can now download the final answer key PDFs for both papers, verify their responses, and estimate their expected scores. The answer keys cover two separate sets of roll numbers - Paper 1 covers 1547871 to 154787100 and Paper 2 covers 154787101 to 154787200. Questions marked with the # symbol have been officially cancelled and will be excluded from evaluation in both papers.
