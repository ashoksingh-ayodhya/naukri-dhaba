Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the CBT-II Answer Key for NTPC Graduate Level posts under CEN No. 06/2025 on 15 July 2026. The CBT-II exam was conducted on 10 July 2026 for candidates who qualified in CBT-I. This recruitment covers a total of 5,810 vacancies across six post categories. Candidates can check the answer key using their registration number and date of birth on their respective regional RRB website.

A total of **5810** posts are available for this recruitment.
