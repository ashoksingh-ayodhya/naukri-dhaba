UPTET Answer Key 2026 has been released by the Uttar Pradesh Education Service Selection Commission (UPESSC) on 8 July 2026 at the official website, upessc.up.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the Uttar Pradesh Teacher Eligibility Test (UPTET) 2026 on 2, 3, and 4 July 2026 can now download the provisional answer key for Paper 1 and Paper 2, match it with their OMR response sheet, and calculate their probable score. UPESSC has also activated the online objection portal, allowing candidates to challenge any incorrect answer until 14 July 2026 (11:59 PM).
