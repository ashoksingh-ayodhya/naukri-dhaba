The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) will release the RRB NTPC Undergraduate (UG) Answer Key 2026 for the CBT 1 exam in the 4th week of June 2026 on the official regional websites under rrb.digialm.com. Candidates who appeared for the CBT 1 exam between 7th May and 20th June 2026 can download their response sheet and provisional answer key using their registration number and date of birth/password. This article covers the direct download process, marking scheme, objection procedure, and score calculation method for the RRB NTPC UG CBT 1 Answer Key 2026.
