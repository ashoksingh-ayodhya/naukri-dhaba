The Tripura Public Service Commission (TPSC) has officially released the Final Answer Key for the Combined Competitive Examination (CCE) Preliminary 2026 for recruitment to Miscellaneous Posts under the Government of Tripura. The exam was conducted on 01 March 2026 under Advertisement No. 18/2025 dated 04/06/2025. Candidates who appeared in any of the four booklet series -Series A, B, C, or D can now cross-check all 100 answers, identify bonus questions, and estimate their preliminary exam score.
