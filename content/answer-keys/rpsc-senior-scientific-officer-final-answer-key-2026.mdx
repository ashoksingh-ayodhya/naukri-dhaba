The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC) has released the RPSC Senior Scientific Officer Final Answer Key 2026 on 3 July 2026 for the Sr. Scientific Officer (Home Department) Competitive Examination 2024. The final keys cover the Cyber Forensic Division, Narcotics Division, Physics Division, and Serology Division (Part A/Part C and Part B/Part C papers). Candidates who appeared for these papers can now download the PDF from the official RPSC website, rpsc.rajasthan.gov.in, and use it to estimate their score ahead of the result.
