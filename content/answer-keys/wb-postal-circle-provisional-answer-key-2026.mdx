The Office of the Chief Postmaster General, West Bengal Circle (Department of Posts, India) has released the provisional answer key for the LDCE (Limited Departmental Competitive Examination) / CE (Competitive Examination) held on 12.07.2026 (Sunday) for promotion/recruitment to the cadre of Postman, Mailguard and MTS from eligible MTSs/GDSs, for the vacancy year 2026 (01.01.2026 to 31.12.2026). This article gives you the complete, verified details of the notice - including the answer keys for Paper-I, Paper-II and Paper-III (Series A, B, C, D), how to submit objections/feedback, and the last date to do so.
