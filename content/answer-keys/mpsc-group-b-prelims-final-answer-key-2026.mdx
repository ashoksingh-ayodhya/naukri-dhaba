The Maharashtra Public Service Commission (MPSC) has officially released the MPSC Group B Prelims Final Answer Key 2026 on its official portal, mpsc.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the Maharashtra Group B (Non-Gazetted) Services Combined Main Examination can now download the provisional answer key PDF and begin comparing their responses. This is a critical step for all aspirants aiming for Group B posts such as State Tax Inspector, Assistant Section Officer, and Police Sub-Inspector, as it allows them to calculate tentative scores and assess their chances of advancing to the next stage.
