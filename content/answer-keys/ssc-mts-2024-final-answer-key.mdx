1. SSC OTR Instruction : SSC has started one-time registration on its new website SSC.GOV.IN. Candidates who have not yet done OTR registration on the new website will be able to apply for any recruitment only after completing the registration.
2. Staff Selection Commission Are Released Matric Level Multi Tasking Staff MTS Non Technical & Havaldar Exam 2024 Candidate Can Apply Between 27 June 2024 to 31 July 2024
3. SSC Photo Instruction : SSC has made a big change in the application process. 5. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document – Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
6. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form – Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
7. Before Submit the Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
