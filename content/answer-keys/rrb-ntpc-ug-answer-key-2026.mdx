RRB NTPC Undergraduate Answer Key 2026 has been officially released on 27th June 2026 (7 PM). The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has published the provisional answer key along with the response sheet and question paper for the CBT 1 examination conducted under CEN 07/2025. Candidates who appeared in the exam between 7 May and 20 June 2026 can now log in to the official portal using their Registration Number and Password or Date of Birth to download the answer key, compare responses, and raise objections if needed before the deadline of 5th July 2026.

A total of **3058** posts are available for this recruitment.
