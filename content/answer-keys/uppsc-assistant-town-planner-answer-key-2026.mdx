The exam, covering General Studies and Town & Country Planning, was conducted in a single sitting on 7 July 2026, and the answer key was made available on 15 July 2026. Candidates can now match their responses against booklet/set code 2000585 and, if dissatisfied, file an objection with supporting evidence before the deadline.
