The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) has released the UPSC CAPF AC Answer Key 2026 on 22 July 2026 for the Central Armed Police Forces (Assistant Commandants) Examination 2026. The provisional answer key covers Paper 1 (General Ability and Intelligence) and is available set-wise for Set A, Set B, Set C, and Set D on the official website, upsc.gov.in. Candidates who appeared for the written exam on 19 July 2026 can now match their responses, estimate their marks, and raise objections within the prescribed timeline before the final key is issued.
