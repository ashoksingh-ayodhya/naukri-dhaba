The UP Home Guard Final Answer Key 2026 has been officially released on 28 June 2026 by the Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board. Alongside the final answer key, the board has also declared the UP Home Guard Result 2026 and Score Card for all candidates who appeared in the written examination held on 25, 26, and 27 April 2026. Candidates can now access the final answer key and check their result by logging in at the official portal uppbpb.gov.in or the main website at uppbpb.gov.in. This recruitment drive covers a massive 41,424 Home Guard (Swayamsevak) vacancies across Uttar Pradesh, and over 1.92 crore candidates appeared for the examination - making it one of the largest recruitment exercises in the state's history.

A total of **41424** posts are available for this recruitment.
