The HP JBT Teacher Answer Key 2026 has released by the Himachal Pradesh Rajya Chayan Aayog (HPRCA) on its official website hprca.hp.gov.in. This article covers everything you need to know from the direct download link and objection procedure to the marking scheme and the complete selection process that follows.
