The APSC CCE Answer Key 2026 is now available, giving lakhs of aspirants their first real chance to gauge performance right after the Combined Competitive (Preliminary) Examination held on 5 July 2026. Released by the Assam Public Service Commission on the same day as the exam, the provisional answer key covers both General Studies Paper I and Paper II (CSAT), allowing candidates to match their responses, estimate their probable score using the official marking scheme, and get an early sense of their chances of qualifying for the Mains Examination.
