The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has declared the final answer key for two preliminary examinations conducted on 23 June 2026 - Assistant Professor, Sociology (Advt. No. 91/2025-26) and Child Development Project Officer, Female, PwD (Advt. No. 26/2025-26). Released on 15 July 2026, the final answer key lists the correct option for all 200 questions in Series M/A and comes after GPSC reviewed candidate objections to the earlier provisional key.
