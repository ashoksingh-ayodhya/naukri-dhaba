The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) has officially released the DRDO CEPTAM 11 Tier-2 Answer Key 2026 on 18 June 2026. Candidates who appeared in the Tier-2 Computer-Based Test (CBT) held on 15 June 2026 for the posts of Senior Technical Assistant-B (STA-B) and Technician-A (Tech-A) can now log in to the official portal and download their answer key using their Registration Number or Date of Birth.

A total of **764** posts are available for this recruitment.
