Northern Coalfields Limited (NCL), a subsidiary of Coal India Limited, has opened the response/query management window for candidates who appeared in the Computer Based Test (CBT) held on 6 July 2026 for the posts of HEMM Operator (Trainee), Paramedical Staff, and Overseer (Civil). As per Notice No. 250 dated 11 July 2026, candidates can raise queries or objections regarding the questions/answers of the CBT from 11 July 2026 to 15 July 2026 through the NCL website and their individual login portal.
