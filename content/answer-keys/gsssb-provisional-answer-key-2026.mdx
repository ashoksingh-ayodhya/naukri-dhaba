The Gujarat Gaun Seva Pasandgi Mandal (GSSSB), Gandhinagar, has officially released the Provisional Answer Key cum Response Sheet for the CBRT examination held on 25 June 2026. This answer key covers three technical cadre posts: Staff Nurse (Ayurved) Class-3, Junior Geologist (Laghu Bhustarshashtri) Class-3, and Speech Therapist & Audiologist Class-3, under Advertisement Numbers 377/202526, 380/202526, and 385/202526, respectively. Candidates who appeared in the CBRT exam can now view their question paper, their given answers, and the provisional answer key online. The objection window is open from 27 June 2026 at 14:00 hrs to 3 July 2026 at 23:55 hrs.
