The Tripura Public Service Commission (Tripura PSC) has released the Provisional Answer Key for the Written Examination for the post of Lecturer, District Institute of Education and Training (DIET), conducted against Advertisement No. 15/2025. The provisional answer key has been published for 17 subjects, covering Series A, B, C, and D question booklets. Candidates who appeared in the written exam can download the subject-wise answer key from the official TPSC website and match their responses before the objection window closes.
