The Odisha Subordinate Staff Selection Commission (OSSSC) released the OSSSC RI ARI Answer Key 2026 on 3rd July 2026 on its official website, osssc.gov.in. The answer key is for the Combined Recruitment Examination (CRE) Prelims held for the posts of Revenue Inspector (RI), Assistant Revenue Inspector (ARI), Amin, ICDS Supervisor, and Statistical Field Surveyor. Candidates who appeared in the shift-wise CBT exam conducted between 22nd May and 28th June 2026 can now log in with their application number and date of birth to download their response sheet, match answers, and estimate their probable score. Those who spot a discrepancy in the provisional key can raise an objection online until 12th July 2026.

A total of **5012** posts are available for this recruitment.
