The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has published the Provisional Answer Key 2026 for the Assistant Professor recruitment exams held under the Special Recruitment (PwD - Second Attempt) drive. This answer key covers two posts Assistant Professor, Botany (Advertisement No. 95/2025-26) and Assistant Professor, Geography (Advertisement No. 89/2025-26) both for Government Arts, Science & Commerce Colleges under GES, Class-2. Candidates who appeared for the Preliminary Test held on 16 June 2026 can now download the answer key, match their responses, and raise objections within the prescribed window if needed.
