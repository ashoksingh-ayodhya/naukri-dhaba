The Jharkhand Public Service Commission (JPSC) has officially released the Corrected Answer Key for the Jharkhand Combined Civil Services (Backlog) Preliminary Examination 2023, advertised under Advertisement Number 06/2026. Candidates who appeared in the preliminary test held on 10 May 2026 can now download the final corrected answer key from the official JPSC website at jpsc.gov.in. The commission has dropped 2 questions from Paper I and 4 questions from Paper II after reviewing objections submitted by candidates.
