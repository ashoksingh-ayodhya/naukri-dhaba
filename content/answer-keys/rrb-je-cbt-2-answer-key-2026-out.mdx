The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) has released the provisional RRB JE CBT-2 Answer Key 2026 on 10 July 2026 for the Computer Based Test-2 held on 2 July 2026 under CEN 05/2025 for the posts of Junior Engineer (JE), Depot Material Superintendent (DMS), and Chemical & Metallurgical Assistant (CMA). Candidates who appeared in the exam can log in on their respective regional RRB website to download the answer key, response sheet, and question paper, and raise objections till 17 July 2026 (11:50 PM).

A total of **2585** posts are available for this recruitment.
