The Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA) has released the Revised Answer Key 2026 for the Kannada Language Test (KLT), dated July 22, 2026. The test was originally held on July 19, 2026, for recruitment to Group 'A' and Group 'B' posts across various Karnataka government departments, corporations, and boards, along with Civil Police Constable positions. Candidates can download the revised key from the official KEA portal, cetonline.karnataka.gov.in.
