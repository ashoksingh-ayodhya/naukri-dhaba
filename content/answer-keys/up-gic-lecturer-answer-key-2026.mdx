The Uttar Pradesh Public Service Commission (UPPSC) has officially released the UP GIC Lecturer Answer Key 2026 on its official website at uppsc.up.nic.in. The provisional answer key has been published for the Lecturer (Male/Female) Government Inter College (GIC) Preliminary Examination, which was conducted on 14 June 2026 and 17 June 2026 across 67 districts of Uttar Pradesh. Candidates who appeared in the exam can now download the subject-wise answer key PDF, cross-verify their attempted responses, calculate their estimated scores, and if needed, raise objections against any answer they believe is incorrect.

A total of **1516** posts are available for this recruitment.
