Power Grid Corporation of India Limited (POWERGRID) has activated the Answer Key Objection Management Link on its career portal for candidates who appeared in the Computer Based Test (CBT) for the recruitment of Diploma Trainee (Electrical/Civil/Survey), Jr. Officer Trainee (HR/F&A), and Junior Technician Trainee (Survey). As per official Notice-8 dated 2 July 2026, the objection window is open from 3 July 2026 (1700 hrs) to 5 July 2026 (1659 hrs) for candidates who took the CBT held on 1 July 2026. This article covers all officially confirmed details, including the objection window timeline, eligible posts, and how candidates can raise their objections.
