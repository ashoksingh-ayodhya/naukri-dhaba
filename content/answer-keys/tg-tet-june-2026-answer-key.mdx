The Department of School Education, Government of Telangana has released the TG TET June 2026 provisional answer key and individual response sheets on the official website. Candidates who appeared for the Telangana Teacher Eligibility Test can now download both documents, verify their attempted responses against the official key, and raise objections if they spot any discrepancy. The objection window closes on July 2, 2026.
