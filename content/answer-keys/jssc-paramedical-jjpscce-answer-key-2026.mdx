The Jharkhand Staff Selection Commission (JSSC) has released the provisional answer key for the Jharkhand Jail Para Medical Staff Combined Competitive Examination (JJPSCCE) 2025. This exam was held to recruit Para Medical Karmi (paramedical staff) in the jails of Jharkhand State, under Advertisement No. 09/2025. The answer key was published through Important Notice No. 07, dated 23 July 2026, on the official JSSC website.
