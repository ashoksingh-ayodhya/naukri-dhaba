The Rajasthan Staff Selection Board (RSSB) has released the RSSB LDC Answer Key 2026 on 24 July 2026 for the Clerk Grade-II (LDC) and Junior Assistant Joint (Direct Recruitment) Examination 2026. The exam was held on 5 July 2026 in two shifts across Rajasthan. Candidates can now download the Master Question Papers and Provisional Answer Keys for both Paper-I and Paper-II from the official website, rssb.rajasthan.gov.in, and raise objections if they find any discrepancy in the official answers.

A total of **10644** posts are available for this recruitment.
