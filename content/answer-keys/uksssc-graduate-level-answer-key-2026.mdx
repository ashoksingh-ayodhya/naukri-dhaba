The Uttarakhand Subordinate Service Selection Commission (UKSSSC) has released the provisional answer key for the Graduate Level Recruitment Examination 2026. The answer key was published on 16 June 2026 for the exam conducted on 14 June 2026, covering all four question paper sets A, B, C, and D. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now match their attempted responses against the official answers, estimate their probable score, and raise objections within the window specified by the commission. This article covers the direct PDF download link, important dates, the objection fee, and the step-by-step process to challenge any answer you believe is incorrect.
