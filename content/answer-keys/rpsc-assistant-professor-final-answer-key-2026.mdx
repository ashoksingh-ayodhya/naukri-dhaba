The Rajasthan Public Service Commission (RPSC), Ajmer, has officially released the Final Answer Key for the Assistant Professor (Medical Education Department) Competitive Examination 2024 on 22 June 2026. The answer keys are now live on the official RPSC website for two subjects: Geriatric Medicine (BS) and Uro Oncology (SS). Candidates who appeared in these exams can now cross-check their attempted answers against the official key and estimate their expected scores before the result is declared.
