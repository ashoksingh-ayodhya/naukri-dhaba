The Gujarat Public Service Commission (GPSC) has declared the Final Answer Key for two Class-2 preliminary examinations held under the Special Recruitment - PwD - Second Attempt drive. The final key was released on 22 July 2026 for the posts of Assistant Director, GSS, Industries and Mines Department (Advertisement No. 22/2025-26) and Assistant Professor, Automobile Engineering, Government Engineering College, GES, Class-2 (Advertisement No. 61/2025-26). Both examinations were conducted on the same date, and candidates can now match their responses against the officially declared correct answers.
