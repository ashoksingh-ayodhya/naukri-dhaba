The UP Lekhpal Revised Answer Key 2026 is officially out. The Uttar Pradesh Subordinate Services Selection Commission (UPSSSC) released the revised (final) answer key for the Revenue Lekhpal Mains Examination on its official website, upsssc.gov.in, on 1 July 2026. This revised key comes after the commission examined all objections raised by candidates against the earlier provisional answer key, making it the basis on which the final result and merit list will be prepared. Candidates who appeared for the UP Lekhpal Mains Exam on 21 May 2026 can now download the set-wise PDF, match their responses, and calculate their expected score using the official marking scheme.

A total of **7994** posts are available for this recruitment.
