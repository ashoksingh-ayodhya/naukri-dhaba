The Telangana District Court Answer Key 2026 has been officially released by the High Court for the State of Telangana on 6th July 2026. Candidates who appeared for the Computer-Based Test (CBT) held on 24th, 25th, and 28th June 2026 for posts like Junior Assistant, Examiner, Field Assistant, and Record Assistant can now download their response sheets and raise objections until 8th July 2026. The answer key is available on the official website, tshc.gov.in, and requires login with registration details.
