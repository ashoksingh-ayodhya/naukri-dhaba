The Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA) has released the provisional answer key for the Kannada Language Test conducted on July 19, 2026. This test was held for recruitment to Group 'A' and 'B' posts across various Karnataka government departments, corporations, and boards, as well as for Civil Police Constable positions. Candidates who appeared for the exam can now download the answer key from the official website and raise objections, if any, within the specified window.
