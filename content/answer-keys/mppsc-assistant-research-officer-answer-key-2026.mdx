The Madhya Pradesh Public Service Commission (MPPSC) has released the Final Answer Key for the Assistant Research Officer (ARO) Examination 2024, dated 16 June 2026. This final key is available on the official website, mppsc.mp.gov.in, and is the document candidates need to calculate their final probable score before the result is declared. Unlike the provisional key released earlier, this final answer key has already incorporated all approved objections, meaning the answers listed here will be used for actual result preparation.
