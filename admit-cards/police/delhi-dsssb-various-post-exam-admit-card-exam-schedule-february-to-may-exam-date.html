How to Download Admit Card

  1. Visit the official website (https://www.crpf.gov.in/Recruitment.htm) or use the Download Admit Card link on this page.
  2. Log in using your Registration Number / Roll Number and Date of Birth or Password.
  3. Verify all details on the admit card — name, photo, exam centre, reporting time.
  4. Download the admit card and take a clear colour printout on A4 paper.
  5. If any detail is incorrect, contact the exam authority immediately before the exam date.
  6. On exam day, carry the printed admit card along with a valid photo ID (Aadhaar / PAN / Passport / Voter ID).
