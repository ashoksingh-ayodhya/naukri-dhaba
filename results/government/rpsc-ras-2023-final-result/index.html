How to Fill : Rajasthan Public Service Commission RAS Online Form 2023 has released an official notification for Rajasthan RPSC RAS / RTS Recruitment 2023 Result with Marks, Mains Result with Marks, Final Result 2026 for 905 Post.

A total of 905 posts are available for this recruitment.

The last date to apply is 31/07/2023.

How to Apply

  1. One time registration is necessary for all the recruitment of Rajasthan Public Service Commission RPSC - so you can apply only after OTR.
  2. Rajasthan Public Service Commission RPSC RAS / RTS Recruitment 2023, Candidate Can Apply Between 01/07/2023 to 31/07/2023
  3. Candidate Read the Notification Before Apply the Vacancies Application Form in Govt. Jobs of RPSC Latest Govt. Recruitment Online 2023.
  4. Kindly Check and Collect the All Document - Eligibility, ID Proof, Address Details, Basic Details.
  5. Kindly Ready Scan Document Related to Recruitment Form - Photo, Sign, ID Proof, Etc.
  6. Before Apply Online / Submit Application Form Must Check the Preview and All Column Carefully.
  7. If Candidate Required to Paying the Application Fee Must Submit. If you have Not the Required Application Fees Your Form is Not Completed.
  8. Take A Print Out of Final Submitted Form.
