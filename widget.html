How to Embed

Basic Embed (Light Theme, 5 jobs)

<script src="https://naukridhaba.in/widget.js"></script>

Dark Theme, 8 jobs

<script src="https://naukridhaba.in/widget.js" data-nd-limit="8" data-nd-theme="dark"></script>

Options

AttributeValuesDefaultDescription
data-nd-limit1–205Number of jobs to show
data-nd-themelight / darklightWidget color theme
